2 TBsp. soy sauce
1 TBsp rice vinegar
2 TBsp thai sweet chili sauce
1 TBsp. dark sesame oil
2 garlic cloves, minced.
1 salmon fillet

Place salmon ina large shallow glass baking dish.
Combine remaining ingredients. Blend well.
Pour over salmon turn salmon to coat thoroughly.
Let stand at room temperature 15 minutes to marinate
Heat oven to 450F. Line baking pan with foil. Place salmon in pan. Bake 12 to 18 minutes until fish begins to flake.
