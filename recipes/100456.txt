Easy Fruit Cookie Tarts
Make everyone smile with a cookie tart to call their own! Decorate each frosted tart with each person's favorite fresh fruit.

8 cups Original Bisquick? mix  
1 1/3 cups sugar 
1 1/3 cups butter or margarine, softened 
4 eggs 
2 packages (3 ounces) cream cheese, softened 
2/3 cup sugar 
2 teaspoons vanilla 
1 1/2 cups whipping (heavy) cream 
4 cups assorted cut-up fresh fruit 
4 tablespoons apple jelly, melted 


 
 1.  Heat oven to 375Â§F. Stir Bisquick, 2/3 cup sugar, the butter and eggs in large bowl until soft dough forms. Pat dough into sixteen 3-inch circles on ungreased cookie sheets.  
2.  Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until edges just begin to brown. Remove from cookie sheet to wire rack. Cool completely, about 30 minutes.  
3.  Beat cream cheese, 1/3 cup sugar and the vanilla in small bowl with electric mixer on low speed until smooth. Beat in whipping cream on high speed until stiff peaks form. Spread over tarts to within 1/4 inch of edges. Place fruit on top; brush with jelly. Store covered in refrigerator.
