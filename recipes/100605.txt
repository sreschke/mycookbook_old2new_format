Mix: 1 cube melted butter
     14-16 ounces peanut butter
     1 pound powdered sugar
Add: 3 1/2 cups Rice Krispies
Make into one inch balls
Melt: 6 ounces chocolate chips
      1/2 bar parafin wax
      4 oz Hershey bar
Using a toothpick, dip balls into chocolate mixture, place on waz paper.
