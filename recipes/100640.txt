3  lbs chicken wings
Pinch salt and pepper
1  C brown sugar
1/4  C all-purpose flour
1/2  C water
1/4  C vinegar
1/4  C soy sauce
1  T ketchup

Cut off wing tips and discard.  Cut wings apart at joint.  Place on foil-lined baking sheet.  Sprinkle salt and pepper over top.  Bake in 350-degree oven for 30 minutes.

Put sugar and flour into saucepan.  Stir thoroughly.  Add water, vinegar, soy sauce and ketchup.  Stir and cook over medium heat until mixture boils and thickens.  Remove from heat.  When wings have baked for 30 minutes, brush liberally with sauce.  Bake 10 minutes more.  Brush with sauce again and bake 10 minutes more.  If chicken is tender, remove from oven.  If not, brush with sauce once more and continue to bake until done.  Serve hot  

Makes about 36 pices.
