Creamy Swiss Chicken Breasts
Replies: 11

4 whole chicken breasts, split, skinned, and boned (8 halves)
8 slices Swiss cheese
1 can cream of chicken soup (can use celery or mushroom, if desired)
3/4 teaspoon seasoned salt (I prefer Lawry's)
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder (not salt)
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1/4 cup dry white wine (not cooking wine) or apple juice
1 cup herb stuffing mix, crushed
1/2 stick butter or margarine, melted

Grease a 9x12 baking dish and arrange chicken pieces in a single layer. Top each with a cheese slice.
Mix soup with salt, garlic powder, pepper, and wine or apple juice.. Spoon evenly over the cheese slices. Sprinkle with the crushed stuffing mix. Drizzle melted butter over the stuffing. Bake at 350Ã¸ for 45 to 55 minutes or until browned. 8 servings.
