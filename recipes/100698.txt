
1 lb. ground beef
1 onion, finely chopped
1 garlic clove, minced
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can tomato soup
1 soup can of water
1 8-oz. can tomato sauce
16 oz. thin spaghetti* cooked and drained (or other pasta)
1 pkg. sharp cheddar cheese, shredded

Brown beef, onion, and garlic. Add soups, water, and tomato sauce. Let simmer 20 minutes. Add cooked spaghetti and 1 cup of cheese. Pour into a casserole dish and top with more cheese. Bake at 400 25 minutes.

*Can substitute any other pasta if desired.
