Creamy Lasagna

1 lb ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 14 1/2 oz can tomatoes, cut up
1 6oz can tomato paste
1/3 cup water
1 garlic clove, minced
1 tsp dried oregano leaves, crushed
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
8 oz cream cheese, cubed
1/4 cup milk
8 oz lasagna, cooked
1 6 oz pkgs part skim mozarella slices
1/2 cup kraft parmesan cheese

Brown meat in large skillet. Drain. Add onions and cook until tender. Stir in next 7 ingredients. Cover, and simmer for 30 minutes.
Combine cream cheese and milk in a separate pan, and cook, stirring over low heat until smooth
In 13 x 9 glass baking dish, layer half of noodles, meat mixture, cream cheese mixture, mozarella slices, and parmesan. Repeat layers. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
