1 lb dry Northern beans, soaked and drained
7 cups chicken broth
2 med onions, chopped
1 T oil
2 4oz cans chopped green chilis
1T cumin
1/2 tswp oregano
1/4 tsp cloves
1/4 tsp cayenne
4 cups diced cooked chicken
2 cloves garlic
3 cups Monterrey Jack cheese

Combine beans, broth, 1/2 the onions and garlic in large sauce pan. Boil for 3 - 3 1/2 hours.
Saute rest of onions. Add chilis and seasonings. Add bean mixture.  Add chicken .  Simmer one hour.
