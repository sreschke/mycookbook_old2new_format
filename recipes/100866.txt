
Apple Pudding Cake

2 eggs
2 c. sugar
2 sticks butter, melted
1 tsp. salt
2 tsp. soda
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
2 tsp. cinnamon
2 c. flour
4 c. peeled and sliced apple

Prheat oven to 325 degrees. Combine eggs and sugar; beat well. Add melted 
butter mixing thoroughly; add salt, soda, nutmeg, cinnamon and flour, mixing 
well. Add apples and stir. Spread in greased and floured 9x13 inch pan. Bake 
for 1 hour. Serve warm or cool with ice cream or whipped cream. 12 servings
