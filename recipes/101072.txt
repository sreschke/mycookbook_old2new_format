
4 pound pork roast (can use chops or steaks too) up to six pounds
1/2 pkg dry onion soup mix
1 can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 can Pepsi (NOT DIET)

Place meat in crockpot, sprinkle with soup mix. Stir together Pepsi and
mushroom soup and pour over the top. Cook on low 6-7 hours. The gravy from
the sauce is delicious on potatoes or rice.
