Seafood Lasagne 

1/2 c. butter
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1/2 c. flour
1/2 tsp. salt
2 c. milk
2 cans chicken broth
1/3 c. dry white wine
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. dried basil
2 c. mozzarella cheese, part skim milk, shredded
1/2 c. green onions, chopped
15 lasagna noodles, UNCOOKED
1 c. cottage cheese, lowfat
2/3 c. shrimp, cooked and cut in bite size pieces
2/3 c.scallops, cooked & cut in bite size pieces
2/3 c. crabmeat, cut in bite size pieces
1/2 c. Parmesan cheese

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Heat butter in a large saucepan over low heat until melted. Add the garlic. Stir in the flour and salt. Cook, stirring constantly until bubbly. Remove from heat.

Stir in milk, broth and white wine. Return to stove and heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil for 1 min. Add pepper, basil, mozzarella cheese and green onions. Cook over low heat until cheese is melted, stirring constantly.

Spread 1 1/2 c. of the sauce into an ungreased 9x13" pan. Top with 5 lasagna noodles, overlapping as needed.

Spread the cottage cheese over the noodles. Spread another 1 1/2 c. of the sauce over the cottage cheese and top with another 5 lasagna noodles. Spread the seafood over this layer and top with another 1 1/2 c. of the sauce. Cover with the last 5 lasagna noodles and top with all of the remaining sauce. Sprinkle with the Parmesan cheese.

Bake, uncovered, for 35-45 mins. or until the noodles are tender.

Let stand for 15 min. before cutting.

You can used canned seafood,drained.
