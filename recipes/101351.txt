1 1/2 pounds of sliced zucchini
1 bunch green onions
salt and pepper
In Saucepan bring to boil and cook until just tender

In large bowl combine 15-16 crackers crushed
add 1 cup of milk
1-2 cans chopped green chilis
1 small jar pimentos(optional)
1 cup grated cheese.

Combine in casserole dish(or crockpot) sprinkle with cheese.

Bake 350 30-40 minutes
