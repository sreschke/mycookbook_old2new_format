A gramma's Cheesecake Pie! 

First part of ingredients:

12 oz. cream cheese

2 whole eggs

3/4 cup white sugar

1 tsp. pure vanilla extract 
====================================
Second part of ingredients:

1 cup sour cream

1/2 tsp. pure vanilla extract

3 1/2 Tbsp. white sugar 

1 graham cracker or vanilla wafer pie crust 

Pre-heat oven to 350ø 

Blend until smooth, first four ingredients. Pout into the pie crust. 

Shake to be sure it sets in the middle.

Bake 20 minutes at 350ø 

Let this stage cool for five minutes while you.... 

Blend the toppping ingredients, pour over the slightly cooled first step mixture,

 bake 10 minutes at 350ø then cool and chill at least 4-5 hours, or over night before serving. 
Not Needed Extra: 

After the pie has 4-5 hour-cooled you may want to cover it with your favorite fruit topping (blueberries, cherries, etc.) from the can, but just the fruit, not the juice. Then chill for another 4-5 hours before serving.
