1 lb. Kielbasa sausage, sliced
2 16 oz. cans pork & beans
1/2 c. barbecue sauce
1/2 c. chopped onion
1 tsp. salt
1/4 lb. Velveeta cheese, cubed
8 oz. tube buttermilk biscuits
Grated Cheddar cheese

Preheat oven to 375F. Brown sausage in small amount of butter, drain if necessary. Add beans, barbecue sauce, onion and salt. Simmer 5 minutes while stirring. Stir in cubed cheese. Pour into baking dish. Cut biscuits in half circles and put around the outside of
the dish. 

Bake 20 minutes. Top with Cheddar cheese and return to the oven til melted.
