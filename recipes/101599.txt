2 Tbsp peanut oil 
1 tsp minced garlic 
1 lb beef cut for stir-fry 
1 pinch red pepper flakes 
3/4 lb sea scallops 
1 pinch red pepper flakes 
1-1/4 cup sliced green onion 
1 Tbsp water 
1 pinch fresh ginger 
1 pinch white pepper 
1 tsp salt
 
Heat oil in wok or large skillet until almost smoking. Add garlic, beef & scallops. Stir-fry 30 seconds. Add remaining ingredients. Stir-fry beef & scallops another 20-30 seconds. 
Serves 4.
