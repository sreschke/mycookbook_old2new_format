4 lbs pickling cukes. 
1 large head dill 
6-8 large cloves garlic sliced 
2 tbls. mustard seed (optional) 
1 tsp. alum (optional, available at pharmacy) 
1 tsp or 2-3 hot peppers 

Brine:
8 cups water 
2 cups white vinegar
1/2 cup pickling salt

Put first set of ingredients into a clean quart jar. Boil brine ingredients. Cool, pour into jar with other ingredients.Keep lid loose, invert every day, keep in cool spot. After 3 weeks, strain brine, put pickles in quart size jars for easier keeping with brine in refrigerator. 
Will keep at least 6 months.
