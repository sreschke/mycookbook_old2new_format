sliced raw potatoes
1/3 cup rice
about 1 pound or 1 1/2 pounds ground hamburger
sliced onions
carrots, thinly sliced
1 quart tomatoes
1 Tablespoon salt
1 teaspoon sugar

Place a thick layer of sliced potatoes in a buttered baking dish. Sprinkle rice over potaotes and then place ground hamburger over potatoes. Put a thick layer of sliced onions then a layer of carrots. Pour tomatoes that been seasoned with the salt and sugar. Bake 2 1/ 2 hours in a moderate oven---325 F. to 375 F.

NOTE: *This was my Grandma Williams's six layer dinner recipe. The recipe is at least 43 years old. The William family is my mother's family.* (3-3-01)
