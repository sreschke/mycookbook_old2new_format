 
  

 



 
 
Servings:  8
Prep time:  10 minutes
Bake/Cook time:  20 minutes


Carbohydrates: 1 grams 
 
Net Carbs: 1 grams 
 
Protein: 7 grams 
 
Fat: 11.5 grams 
 
Calories: 134 

   
  
Serve this savory dip with assorted controlled carb veggies or crackers.

1 package (8 ounces) cream cheese 
2 tablespoons cream 
2 green onions, finely chopped 
2 teaspoons prepared white horseradish 
1 can (4 1/2 ounces) lump crab meat, rinsed and drained 
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese 

1.Heat oven to 375?ยง F. Butter a small, shallow baking dish. Mix cream cheese, cream, green onion and horseradish until well combined. Fold in crab meat.
2.Transfer mixture to prepared dish. Smooth top; sprinkle with cheese. Bake 20 minutes until hot and bubbly.
