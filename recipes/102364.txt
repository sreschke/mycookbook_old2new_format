Fresh green beans
1 package bacon

1. Snip the ends of the green beans and leave them long.
2. Wash thoroughly.
3. Take a bundle of 5 to 6 beans and wrap bacon in a spiral from one end of the bundle to the other.
4. Place the wrapped beans on a rack over a baking pan. The pan will catch the grease.
5. Cover all  with foil and bake for one hour or until beans are done.
6. Remove the foil and allow bacon to brown for a few minutes.
7. Serve with your other holiday favorites!!
