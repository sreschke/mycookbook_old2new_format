
Cuisine: Spanish
Prep Time: 30 Minutes
Cooking Time: 40 Minutes
Yield: 8 Servings
Source: Cullinary Council Chef Shelley Young, EG Culinary Council 


 


  1/2 pound(s) Spanish chorizo, sliced 
 2 tablespoon(s) Olive oil 
  1   Onion, medium, chopped 
 2 clove(s) Garlic, chopped 
 2 cup(s) Rice 
 1 1/2 teaspoon(s) Saffron threads 
   to taste Salt 
    to taste Black pepper, freshly ground 
 2   Roasted red peppers, cut into strips 
 1 cup(s) Spanish cooking wine 
 5 cup(s) Chicken stock 
  1 pound(s) Mussels, scrubbed 
  1 pound(s) Clams, scrubbed 
  1 pound(s) Shrimp, peeled and de-veined; tail on 
  1/2 pound(s) Petite frozen peas 
  3 tablespoon(s) Fresh parsley, chopped, as garnish 

 

--Instructions:

Preheat oven to 350øF. In a large paella or saut‚ pan, brown chorizo in olive oil. Add onion and saut‚ until translucent. 
Add the garlic and rice; toss to coat. Add saffron to rice and season with salt and pepper. 
Scatter peppers on top and pour in wine and stock. Bring to a boil on stovetop. 
Place in preheated oven and bake for 10 minutes. 
Arrange mussels, clams, shrimp, and peas on top of rice. 
Cook until shells open and shrimp are pink, about 10 minutes longer. Garnish with parsley. 
Season rice to taste with salt and pepper and serve immediately.
