1 1/2C Flour
1C Brown Sugar
1/2C Cocoa
1tsp Baking Soda
3/4C Milk
2/3C Shortening
3 Eggs
1tsp Vanilla
1C(6oz)Chocolate Chips
 Grease bottom of 10" electric skillet.Combine all except chocolate chips in mixer,beat 4 minutes at medium speed.Pour batter in cold skillet.Sprinkle with chocolate chips.Cover,open steam vent.Bake 275* for 60-70 minutes.Unplug skillet,remove lid and let cake cool.
