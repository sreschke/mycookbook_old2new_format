Ingredients

3/4c. (1-1/2 sticks) butter or margarine, melted.
1-1/2c. sugar
1-1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
3 eggs
1/2c. Hershey's Cocoa
3/4c. all-purpose flour
1/2tsp. baking powder
1/4tsp. salt

Instructions

1.  Heat oven to 350F.  Grease 12" pizza pan or 13"x9" baking pan.  If using disposable pizza pan, place on cookie sheet to bake.

2.  Use only a spoon to beat melted butter, sugar, vanilla and eggs in a large bowl.  Stir in dry ingredients until well blended.  Spread into pan.

3.  Bake 20 to 22 min. or until top springs back when touched lightly.  Serve plain or sprinkle hot brownie with Hershe's chocolate or white chips, Reese's Peanute Butter Chips, Mounds Coconut, SKOR or Heath Toffe Bits, or get creative, and add your own toppings!
