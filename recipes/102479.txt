Hot Cocoa Mix

2 cups non-fat dry milk powder
1/2 cup low-fat powdered non-dairy creamer
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
1 tbs Equal Measure or 12 packets Equal sweetener or 1/2 cup sugar
3/4 tsp ground cinnamon (optional) 

Stir together all the ingredients. Cover and store in airtight container. For each serving, add 3/4 cup boiling water to 1/3 cup cocoa mix; stir to dissolve. Makes 2-2/3 cups mix (enough for 8 six-ounce servings).
