EASY SKILLET SUPPER

2 slices crisp cooked bacon, crumbled
2 tsp. olive oil
1/2 c. chopped onion
1/4 c. chopped green bell pepper
1/4 c. chopped scallions (green onion)
1 lb. red potatoes, cubed
6 large eggs, lightly beaten
1/4 c. skim milk
1/4 tsp. black pepper
1/2 tsp. salt
3 oz. shredded 2% cheddar cheese

1. In a large nonstick skillet, fry bacon. Remove bacon to paper towels to drain. To bacon grease add olive oil. Heat oil; add onion, green bell pepper, scallions, and potatoes. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until potatoes are tender, about 20 min.

2. Add eggs, milk, pepper, and salt; cook, stirring frequently, until eggs are just set, about 3 min. Remove from heat and sprinkle in cheese and bacon. Stir well.

3. Serve immediately.

Serves 4 at 8 POINTS per serving.
