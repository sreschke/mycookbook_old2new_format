Malibu Jello

Ingredients:

1 pkg Peach Passion Fruit Jello
1 cup water
1 cup Mailbu Rum

Boil 1 cup water and add to peach passion fruit jello, stirring until dissolved about 2 minutes. Add 1 cup Malibu rum and pour into shot glasses or 1 ounce paper cups. Chill for four hours. Enjoy!!!
