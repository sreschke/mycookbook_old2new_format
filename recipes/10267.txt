1 1/2 c. cook cut up chicken
1 can (4 oz.) sliced mushrooms, drained
1 clove garlic crushed
2 T. oil
1 can chicken broth
1 (9 oz.) package french cut green beans
1 t. salt
1/2 t. basil leaves
dash of pepper
1 1/2 c. Minute Rice

Saute chicken, mushrooms and garlic in oil 2-3 minutes.  Add remaining ingredients except rice and bring to a full boil.  Stir in rice, remove from heat.  cover and let stand 5 minutes.  Makes 4 servings.
