5 tablespoons baking mix
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese 
1/4 teaspoon salt 
1/8 teaspoon pepper 
2 eggs, beaten 
2 tablespoons butter 
2 cups zucchini, grated 
  
1.  Blend the baking mix, parmesan, salt, pepper. Add eggs. Mix zucchini into batter. 
  
2.  Grease hot pan or griddle with butter. Pour batter into pan and fry 3 minutes on each side. Serve warm. 
  
Make 8 to 10 fritters.
