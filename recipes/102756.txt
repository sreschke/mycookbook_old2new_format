3 eggs
1/3 cup sugar
2 large tart cooking apples, pared and sliced
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1/4 cup pecans or walnuts, chopped
1 teaspoon cinnamon

1.  Heat oven to 450 degrees. 

2.  Combine baking mix, milk, eggs, and 1 teaspoon sugar. Mix well. 

3.  In 9 to 10-inch ovenproof skillet, saute apples in butter over medium heat until tender. Remove from heat; sprinkle with nuts. Pour batter evenly over apples and nuts. 

4.  Combine remaining sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle over batter.

5.  Cover skillet with lid or foil. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes until pancake is puffed and sugar is melted.  Loosen side of pancake from skillet. Cool slightly.  Cut into wedges.
