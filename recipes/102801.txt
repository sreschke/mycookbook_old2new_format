1 cup sugar

1/4 cup flour

1/4 cup butter, melted

1/8 teas salt

2 eggs separated

1 cup evaporated milk

Juice and pulp from 2 lemons

1 9inch pie shell

Combine sugar, flour, butter, salt, and egg yolks. Beat until smooth. Add lemon juice and pulp. Add milk, beating slowly. Beat egg whites until stiff. Fold them in. Bake pie shell at 350 for 5 minutes. Remove from oven and pour filling into shell. Bake 40 minutes longer at 350 or until firm. When pie is cut there will be a delicate cake on top of the filling.
