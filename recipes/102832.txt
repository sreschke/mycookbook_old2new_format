2 10 oz pkgs frozen chopped spinach, thawed
15 oz ricotta cheese
1 cup Parmesan
2 tablespoons fennel seeds
2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil 
3 garlic cloves, minced
Salt and pepper
3 1/2 cups spaghetti sauce
32 jumbo pasta shells, cooked 
Parmesan 

Squeeze spinach dry. Put in a large bowl. Add ricotta, 1/2 cup Parmesan, fennel, basil and garlic to bowl. Season with salt and pepper;  blend.

Preheat oven to 350F. Spoon 1/2 cup spaghetti sauce over bottom of  9x13 dish. Fill each pasta shell with spinach mixture. Place shells,  filling side up, in dish. Spoon remaining sauce over shells. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup Parmesan. Cover loosely with foil and bake til heated through, about 30 minutes. Serve, passing additional Parmesan separately. 

Serves 6.
