In a Bowl Mix the following:

1/8 C Wine Vinegar
1/8 C Balsamic Vinegar
1/4 C Olive Oil

ADD & stir in well each ingredient as it is put in the mixture:
Each seasoning is put in according to YOUR taste so measurements are not listed.

Onion Powder
garlic Salt
Oregano Crushed
Black Pepper
Basil
MIX IN WELL>>>

Now add 1/4C Lemon (Fresh squeezed is best)

Cover and let set for a day...

Enjoy!
