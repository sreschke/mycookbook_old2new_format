2 1/2 cups (11 oz.) Brazil nuts
2 cups (8 oz.) dried apricots 
1 1/2 cups (8 oz.) pitted dates 
2 cups (16 oz.) candied cherry-pineapple mix
1 cup baking mix
1/2 cup sugar
3 eggs
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla

Heat oven to 300 degrees. Line loaf pan 9 x 5 x 3 inches with foil grease. Mix all ingredients thoroughly. Spread in pan. Bake until wooden toothpick inserted in center comes out clean, about 1 3/4 hours. Remove from pan gently, remove foil. Cool.  Soak in brandy.
