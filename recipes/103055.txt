1 cup baking mix
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup oatmeal
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/8 teaspoon cloves
1 egg, beaten
3 tablespoons water
2 teaspoons vanilla
1/4 cup chopped nuts
1/4 cup cinnamon baking chips

1.  Stir together mix, sugar, oatmeal, and spices.  

2.  Combine water, vanilla, and egg; add to dry ingredients. 

3.  Fold nuts and cinnamon chips into dough. Drop by teaspoonfuls onto a greased baking sheet. Bake at 400 F for 10-12 minutes or until browned.
Makes 24 medium-sized cookies.
