1 lb. ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1 small can tomato paste
barbecue sauce
3 cups baking mix
2 tablespoons cornmeal (optional)
2/3 cup milk
2-3 tablespoons butter, melted
3 eggs

1.  Cook and stir ground beef (seasoned with seasoned salt and granulated garlic) and onion; drain.  

2.  Stir in tomato paste and barbecue sauce. 

3.  Generously grease 12-cup Bundt pan.  Mix remaining ingredients; beat 30 seconds.  Spread 2 cups batter in bottom and 2" up side and center of pan.  Spoon beef mixture onto center of batter.  Drop remaining batter by teaspoonfuls 1/2" apart onto beef mixture.  

4.  Bake in 375F oven until golden and firm, about 25 minutes.  Invert on serving plate.

High Altitude:  Heat oven to 400F.
