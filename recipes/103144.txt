Pork tenderloin, pounded thin
salt 
pepper
oil
flour
egg,whipped
breadcrumbs

mix flour, salt and pepper.  Flour tenderloins, dip in egg mixture, then dip in breadcrumbs.

In electric skillet, add oil, brown tenderloins.

Serve with mashed potatoes.  Can make gravy from pan drippings.
