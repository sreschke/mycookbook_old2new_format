1 can Campbells' Fiesta Chili Beef Soup
6 flour tortillas
Shredded cheddar cheese

Spoon 2T soup down center of each tortilla. Top with cheese. Fold tortilla around filling. Place seam-side down on a microwave-save plate and cook on high 2 minutes.

Makes 6 tortillas
11 WW points/2 tortillas
