4 sliced tart apples
2/3 c brown sugar
1/2 c flour
1/2 c oats
1/3 c soft butter
3/4 tsp cinnamon
3/4 c tsp nutmeg

  Heat oven to 375. Arrange apples in oil-sprayed 8" square baking pan. Mix remaining ingredients; sprinkle over apples. Bake 30 minutes until brown and tender. Serve warm, with whipped cream or ice cream.
  Serves 6.

  *Can also try using apricots, cherries, peaches, or pineapple.
