2 1/2 cups (1 package) round butter flavored crackers
3/4 cup butter
3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 package (12oz) milk chocolate morsels
1/2 cup chopped pecans
multicolored candy sprinkles

Preheat oven to 350. Place crackers in non-stick foil lined 9 x 13 pan. Bring butter and brown sugar to a boil in medium saucepan, stirring constantly. Cook 3 minutes. Pour this onto crackers. Bake 5 minutes. Turn oven off. Sprinkle crackers with chocolate morsels and let stand in the oven 3 minutes or until chocolate melts. Spread melted chocolate evenly over crackers. Top with pecans, then sprinkles. Cool and break into pieces. Serve in pretty paper cones tied with ribbon for a colorful and fun presentation.
