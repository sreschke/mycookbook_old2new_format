1 1/2 lbs ground beef
1 can stewed tomatoes, drained
2 tabelspoons dried minced onion
1/4 teaspoon dried parsley flakes
1 cup any flavor bbq sauce
1 deluxe macaroni & cheese dinner
1 cup flavored croutons
2-4 slices American cheese

Prepare macaroni & cheese dinner according to package directions. Brown ground beef & drain. Add minced onion, parsley, tomatoes, bbq sauce & simmer for 5 minutes. In casserole dish sprayed with Pam, layer meat mixture, then macaroni-cheese topped with croutons then covered with slices of American cheese. Place under broiler 2-3 minutes until cheese is melted.
