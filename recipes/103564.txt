Cottage Pie   
by Somerfield
Serves 4     
 Preparation Time 30mins to 1 hour   
 Cooking Time 30 mins to 1 hr    
 Ingredients

1 tbsp sunflower oil
1 onion, peeled and finely chopped
1 clove garlic, peeled and crushed
500g/1lb2oz lean beef or lamb mince
2 tsp tomato pur‚e
400g/14oz canned tomatoes
1 beef stock cube
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
salt and freshly ground black pepper
600g/1lb5oz potatoes, peeled and halved
25g/1oz butter
1 tbsp whole milk
1 leek, finely sliced and blanched for 2 minutes
50g/1?oz cheddar cheese
butter for dotting 
Method

1. Heat the oil in a large heavy-based saucepan. Add the onion and garlic and cook until soft. 
2. Add the meat and allow to brown.
3. Add the pur‚e, tomatoes, stock cube and the Worcestershire sauce.
4. Season to taste with salt and freshly ground black pepper then cook on a slow heat for about 20 minutes.
5. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F/Gas 4. 
6. Meanwhile, cook potatoes in boiling salted water until soft. 
7. Drain and mash with the butter and milk until smooth and creamy. 
8. Place the meat in a large ovenproof dish. Top with the blanched leek and grated cheddar cheese.
9. Finish with the mashed potato. Fork over and dot with a little butter.
10. Place in the preheated oven for 20 minutes.
11. Remove from the oven and place under a hot grill for about 3 minutes until brown and crispy.
