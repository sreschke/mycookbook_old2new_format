1-CARTON (8-OZS] DAIRY SOUR CREAM
2-TBLSP. KIKKOMAN SOY SAUCE
1-1/2 TO 2 TBKSO, SNIPPED FRESH CHIVES
HOT BAKED POTATOES

COMBINE SOUR CREAM AND SOY SAUCE,STIRRING TIL BLENDED.  STIR IN CHIVES AND SPOON ON BAKED POTAOTOES.  (CAN USE LEFTOVER TOPPING AS DIP WITH CUT-UP VEGGIES, CRACKERS OR CHIPS) MAKES 1 1/4 CUPSS.  

FROM THE KITCHEN OF PAM HUGHES
