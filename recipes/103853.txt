8 skinless, boneless chicken thighs
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp peper
1 tsp veg oil
1 T minced garlic
1 c chopped fresh parsley
1/4 chopped kalamata olives
2 tsp capers
1 (14 oz) can diced tomatoes, undrained

1. Sprinkle chicken with salt and pepper. Heat oil over med-high heat. Add chicken, cook 4 min on each side. Remove and keep warm.
2. Add garlic to pan;saute 30 secs. Add remaining ingred; scrape pan to loosen browned bits. Return chicken and accumulated juice to pan; reduce heat and simmer 5 min or until chicken is done.

Serves 4
