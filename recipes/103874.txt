5 lbs pork spare ribs
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 cube beef bouillon
3/4 cup boiling water
1 tsp oil
1 can pineapple,crushed,undrained
1/4 cup brown sugar,firm packed
1 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp garlic powder
2 tbs Worcestershire
2 tbs cornstarch
1/4 cup cold water

Cut ribs in individual portions; sprinkle with 1 teaspoon salt. Place on a rack in a shallow baking pan; bake in 375F  oven til almost tender, 1 1/2 to 2 hours. Drain off fat.

Meanwhile, dissolve bouillon in boiling water in saucepan. Add oil, pineapple, brown sugar, onion powder, garlic powder, Worcestershire sauce and remaining salt; bring to     a boil. Reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes.

Combine cornstarch with cold water in a small bowl; stir into sauce. Cook and stir til thick Spoon sauce over drained, baked ribs. Increase oven temperature to 425F; bake til nicely glazed, about 30 minutes.
