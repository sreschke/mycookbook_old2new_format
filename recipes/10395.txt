Here's a wonderful punch adaptation of the Fuzzy Navel cocktail.  This is a sparkling, flavorful punch that you will want to use all year long.

9 cups orange juice with pulp
3 cups peach schnapps
1 bottle(750 ml) brut champagne, chilled
Crushed ice or frozen ice ring

Combine the oranage juice, peach schnapps, and champagne.  Pour into a punch bowl and add crushed ice or frozen ice ring.

Yield: l gallon of punch
