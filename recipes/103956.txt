ingredients 
1 pound small red potatoes 
1 pound small yellow potatoes 
1 fresh jalapeno pepper, sliced 
12 ounces baby carrots 
4 cloves garlic 
1 (10 ounce) package frozen white corn 
1/4 cup butter 
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese 
salt and pepper to taste 
 
  
 
Directions 
1    Place red potatoes, yellow potatoes, jalapeno pepper, carrots, and garlic cloves in a large pot. Cover with water and bring to a boil over high heat. Cook 15 to 20 minutes, until potatoes are tender. Drain water from pot.
 
2    Stir in corn and butter. Mash the mixture with a potato masher until butter is melted and potatoes have reached desired consistency. Mix in cheese, salt, and pepper. Serve hot.
 
Makes 4 servings
