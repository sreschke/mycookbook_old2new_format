This is the recipe that my family has used for 3 generations.

Christmas Cookies
1 c Margerine
2 c Sugar
2 Eggs
1 t Vanilla
1 c Sour Cream
5 c Flour
1 t Baking Soda
2 t Baking Powder
1/2 t Salt

+++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++++
Cream together margerine, sugar, eggs, and vanilla. Add in sour cream. Sift together remaining dry ingredients and mix into sugar mixture. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before rolling out on a floured surface.
