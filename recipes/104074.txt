2 eggs boiled
3 large potatoes boiled with skin
- peel off skin
- mash and add salt & yellow food colour
- sliced egg into 4 peices 
- mix little flour with little food colour (yellow) thick enough to stay on
- make potatoes into balls make a hole put 1 slice egg in and cover 
- put the mixed flour on the potatoes and deep fry
- take out when golden brown
