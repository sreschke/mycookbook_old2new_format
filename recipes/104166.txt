1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
1 small onion, chopped
1/3 cup evaporated milk
1 egg
1-1/2 pound ground chuck
1/2 pound pork sausage
4 - 5 bacon slices
1/3 cup water

Preheat oven 375 degrees.  In large bowl, mix 1/2 can soup, bread crumbs, onion, egg, milk and salt.  Add ground chuck and sausage.  Mix well.  Place meat mixture in pan.  Put bacon on top.  Bake 35 minutes.  Drain.  Add remaining 1/2 can of soup on top.  Bake 35 - 40 minutes.
