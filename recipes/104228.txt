This is a quick and delicious salad that you can make for lunch or a light dinner. It is healthy, very tasty and does not take much time to prepare. Enjoy! 

Serving: 5
Prep Time: 25 minutes minutes
Cook Time: 75 minutes
Total Time: 135 minutes

1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice 
2 cloves garlic, minced 
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme 
1/4 teaspoon salt 
1/4 teaspoon pepper 
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper 
1/2 cup non-fat plain yogurt 
3 cups cubed, cooked chicken 
1 cup thinly sliced green onions 
1/2 cup diced green bell pepper 
1/2 cup diced celery 
3 cups diced fresh tomatoes

1. Combine lemon juice and next 5 ingredients. Beat in yogurt. Gently fold chicken and next 3 ingredients into dressing. Chill. Just before serving, fold in tomatoes. 

Based on individual serving.

Calories: 188
Total Fat: 3 g
Carbohydrates: 10 g
Protein: 29 g
