2/3c olive oil
1 pint cherry tomatoes 1/2'd
1/3c lemon juice
1/2 lb. mushrooms, sliced
1 tsp. oregano
1 can (5 3/4 oz. drained pitted ripe olives, 1/2'd)
1 can artichoke hearts, drained & 1/4'd
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
Romaine lettuce leaves

In small bowl, stir together olive oil, lemon juice, oregano, garlic, salt & pepper.  In large glass bowl, put tomatoes, mushrooms, olives & artichokes.  Pour olive oil mix over all.  Toss, refrigerate overnite or several hours in covered bowl.  

Serves 6
