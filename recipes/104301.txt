1 small onion, chopped
2 large carrots, peeled & thinly sliced
1 med zucchini, sliced 1 inch thick
1 c. chopped spinach
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 (15 oz) cans kidney beans, drained
2 (14.5 oz) cans vegetable broth
1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
1 t. dried basil leaves
1/2 t. dried oregano leaves
1/2 t. salt
1/4 t. coarsely ground black pepper
2 c. cooked elbow or shell macaroni
1/4 c. grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:

Combine all ingredients except macaroni and Parmesan cheese in stoneware.  Cover; cook on Low 8 to 9 hours, or on High 4 to 5 hours.  Stir in macaroni.  Serve sprinkled with cheese.
