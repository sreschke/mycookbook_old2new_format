Little Meat Loaves
2/3 cup dry bread crumbs
1 cup milk 
2 eggs 
1 1/2 lb ground beef 
1/4 cup chopped onion 
1 tsp of salt 
1/2 tsp sage dash of pepper
Set oven at 350 degrees. Allow children to lightly grease individual muffin pans. Put crumbs in a large bowl. Add milk. Beat eggs in small bowl (children can take turns). Stir into milk mixture. Add rest of ingredients. Mix together with a spoon. Allow each child to place serving in a muffin cup. Round off tops. Bake 45 minutes.
