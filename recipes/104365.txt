1lb.ground beef,1 large chopped onion,1 med.chopped green pepper,1 can Swanson Beef Broth,1 can (8 oz.) tomato sauce,3/4 cup uncooked regular rice,1 Tbls.chili powder,1/2 tsp. garlic powder.In skillet cook beef,onion and pepper until beef is browned and vegetables are tender;stir to seperate meat.Spoon off fat.Stir in broth,tomato sauce,rice,chili powder,and garlic powder.Heat to boiling.Cover,cook over low heat 2o min. or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed,stir occassionally.Serve with cheese and sour cream if desired.
