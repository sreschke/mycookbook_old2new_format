1 8-ounce container sour cream 
1/3 cup mayonnaise 
1/2 cup fresh cilantro -- chopped 
1 tablespoon sugar 
1 teaspoon lime peel -- grated 
1/4 teaspoon cumin 
1/8 teaspoon salt 
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice 

In medium bowl, combine all ingredients; mix well. Cover; refrigerate 1 to 2 hours to blend flavours. 
Serve with cut-up fresh vegetables. 
Source:"Pillsbury Holiday Appetizers & Desserts Dec 1998 #214" Yield:"1 1/2 cups"

Andy's notes: Mix well by hand - do not use food processor, as it breaks down the structure and thins out the dip. Leave out the sugar and taste before adding any. Add the lime juice a teaspoon at a time to get the consistency and flavour you like. Serve with veggies and/or corn chips.
