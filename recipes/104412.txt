Baked Parmesan Filet of Halibut 

Category: Seafood
Preparation Level: moderate


1 cup plain low-fat yogurt
1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
2 tsp. lemon juice
1 small onion, finely minced
1 tsp.salt 
1 small dried red chilli pepper,seeded and crushed
2 firm halibut fillets, rinsed and dried 
1 tsp. paprika 

Preheat oven to 350. Place yogurt, cheese, lemon juice, onion, salt and chilli pepper in a medium sized bowl, and mix thoroughly. Coat an 8 inch square baking dish with nonstick cooking spray. Place fish in baking dish. Pour the yogurt mixture over the fish,and sprinkle with paprika. Bake for 20 minutes.
