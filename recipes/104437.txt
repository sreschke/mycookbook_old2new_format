Boil 1 lemon slowly for 10 minutes. Cut lemon in half and extract juice with a lemon squeezer. Put juice into an ordinary drinking glass. Add 2 tbs of glycerine. Stir the lemon juice and glycerine well through. Fill up the drinking glass with honey. Stir with a spoon before taking. Take a tsp when you're coughing and if you have a coughing spell at night take it.
