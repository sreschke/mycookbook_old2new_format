3/4 cup salad oil
1 envelope Hidden Valley Ranch Milk Recipe Salad Dressing Mix
1/2 tsp. dill weed
1/4 tsp. lemon pepper
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
12 - 16 oz. plain oyster crackers

Whisk together all but crackers.  Pour over crackers, stir to coat.

Place on baking hseets and bake at 275 for 15-20 minutes.

Makes 11-12 cups.
