1 chicken, cut up
1 lb pork loin, cut in cubes
Chicken stock or water to cover 
2 cans hominy 
1 chile or 1 tsp red pepper flakes
salt and pepper to taste

Garnishes
2-3 limes cut in wedges
10 radishes, sliced
1 small lettuce, shredded
1 onion, chopped
oregano 
 
In a large pot, place the chicken, pork, and chile, and cover with stock (or water).  Simmer til chicken is falling off the bone. Remove chicken and remove skin and bones, return to pot.  Add the hominy and cook for about 15 minutes, or until pork is done and tender.  Season.

Serve in large bowls, making certain that each gets pork, chicken and hominy.  Place the garnishes in bowls and allow each guest to garnish their own soup.
