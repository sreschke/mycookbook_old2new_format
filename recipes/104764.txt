

SHARI'S QUIK LASAGNA


1 pkg lasagna noodles (10 count)
1 1/2 lb ground beef (cooked & drained)
1 block motz cheese
1/2 cup Parm cheese
16 oz cottage cheese
1 jar of your favorite speghetti sauce


Place 5 uncooked noodles in bottom of 13 X 9" pan. Sprinkle 1/2 of the ground beef over noodles.  Then pour half of the jar of sauce over beef.  Place half of the motz cheese on top of sauce.  Pour on half of the cottage cheese on top of motz cheese.

Place 2nd layer of noodles, hamburger, sauce, motz cheese, cottage cheese and pour on the parm cheese.

Bake uncovered for about 45 minutes in 350 degree oven.
