1 (14 oz.) package caramels
2/3 cup evaporated milk
1 box german chocolate cake mix
3/4 cup margarine, softened
1 cup pecans, chopped
1 (6 oz) package semi-sweet chocolate chips

Combine 22 caramels and 1/3 cup evaporated milk on top of double boiler. Stir mixture until completely melted. Combine cake mix, remaining 1/3 cup of milk and softened margarine. Blend until mixture holds together. Stir in pecans. Press one half of the cake mixture in bottom of 9x13-inch pan that has been sprayed with Pam. Bake for 6 minutes @ 350 degrees. Remove from oven and sprinkle semi-sweet chocolate chips on top. Pour melted caramels evenly over the top of the chocolate chips. Put remaining cake mixture over top of the caramels. Bake at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes. Cool before cutting into squares.
