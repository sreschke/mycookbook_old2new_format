1 lb 3 oz. Sweet Potato peeled and cubed
1 1/4 cup plain yogurt
3/4 cup packed, dark brown sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
5 egg yolks
1 9 inch frozen pie shell
1 cup toasted peacans
1 tblsp maple syrup

Put the cubed potato's in a steamer basket in a large pot of water. (Allow no less than two inches of space between the water and the basket.) Steam the potato's for about twenty minutes, or until fork tender.

Mash the steamed potato's in a bowl

Place the potato's in a standing mixer's bowl. Attach the paddle piece . Add the yogurt, cinnamon, nutmeg, brown sugar, egg yolks and salt to taste. Beat well.

Pour the mixture into the pie shell.

Put on a baking sheet

Sprinkel peacans over the top and drizzle the maple syrup.

Bake for 50-55 min.
