1/4 C heavy cream
2 T liqueur (grand marnier,etc)
6 oz semisweet chocolate pieces
4 T unsalted butter cut in small pieces
1/2 C unsweetened cocoa (for rolling in)

Bring cream to a boil over medium high heat and boil until reduced by half.  Pemove from heat, stir in liqueur and chocolate pieces.  Return pan to low heat, stirring constantly until chocolate is melted. Remove from heat and whisk in butter until smooth.  Place in a shallow dish and chill 40 minutes. Scoop out and roll in 1" balls. Roll in cocoa
