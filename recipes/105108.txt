

3 Egg whites
1/8 teaspoon cream of tartar
1/2 teaspoon Vanilla
Flavored Syrup or sweetner to taste
Cinnamon - Optional

Beat the egg whites and Cream of Tarter until stiff peaks form. slowly
add your sweetners/cinnamon while beating. Lightly spray a Corningware dish
w/ Cooking Spray and dump your mixture into the bowl. Bake at 375 degrees
for about 10 minutes or until browned on top.
Comes out with almost an Angel Food Cake consistency.
Tastes good with butter.

57 Calories (kcal); 0g Total Fat; (0% calories from fat); 11g Protein; 2g Carbohydrate; 0mg Cholesterol; 165mg Sodium

NOTES : Counts for syrup/sweetener and cinnamon not included in totals.
