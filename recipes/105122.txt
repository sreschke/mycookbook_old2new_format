6 - 8 small zucchini, sliced in 1/2 inch pieces
4 tomatoes, chopped
1 small onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp. dried oregano
1 tbsp. fresh basil, chopped
1/4 cup olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese

Combine all ingredients except Parmesan cheese together in a large bowl and mix well. 

Place in a greased baking dish, sprinkle with
Parmesan cheese and bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
