
1 package pudding-included chocolate cake mix
1 cup water
3 eggs
1 cup Hellmann's mayonnaise (any variety, or so they claim)

Grease and flour 2 9" round cake pans.  In large bowl with mixer
at low speed, beat cake mix, water, eggs and mayonnaise just until
blended.  Beat at medium speed 2 minutes.  Pour into prepared pans.
Bake in 350 oven 30 to 35 minutes or until cake springs back when
touched lightly in center.  Cool in pans on wire racks 10 minutes.
Remove from pans; cool completely on racks.  Fill and frost as
desired.
