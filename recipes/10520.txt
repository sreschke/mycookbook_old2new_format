1 1/2 cups sugar
1/2 cup egg substitute
1/4 cup margarine, melted
3 tbsp Kahlua (coffee flavored liqueur)
1 1/4 cups sifted cake flour
1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 cup finely chopped walnuts
cooking spray

Preheat oven to 325.  Combine first 4 ingredients; stir well. Combine flour, cocoa, and baking powder; stir into sugar mixture. Fold in walnuts. Spoon into a 9 inch square baking pan coated with cooking spray. Bake at 325 for 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into center comes out clean.  Cool on a wire rack.
