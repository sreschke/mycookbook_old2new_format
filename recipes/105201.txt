1 lb ground turkey
1 large onion, finely chopped
1 green pepper, finely chopped (opt)
3-5 or more cloves garlic, quartered
1 28-oz and 1 14-oz can Italian whole tomatoes
2 16-oz cans kidney or pinto beans
1/2 tsp salt (or more to taste - I like to use Krazy Janes salt)
2 bay leaves
2 T chili powder
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp allspice
(if you want it hot, add 1 T paprika)

1. Crumble turkey finely into pan.
2. Add onion and green papper, and saute until tender. (Add garlic in last few minutes to prevent from browning.)
3. Add the rest of the ingredients and simmer for at least 45 minutes.

There are so many wonderful flavors in this dish that no one will never guess that it's made with ground turkey.
