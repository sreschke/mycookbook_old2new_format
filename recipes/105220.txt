3 Cups Loosely packed fresh basil leaves
3 large cloves garlic
1/2 cup pine nuts
1 Cup freshly grated Parmesan*
1/2 to 2/3 cup olive oil
salt to taste

Combine all of the ingredients in a food processor or blender, adding enough olive oil to make a thick, smooth sauce. Add salt to taste. Add to hot pasta. Toss to combine and serve right away with fresh garlic bread and a crisp salad.

Makes about 2 Cups
