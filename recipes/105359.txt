1 C  butter, room temperature
1 C  brown sugar
1  whole egg yolk
2 C  flour
1 t  vanilla
1 C  white chocolate, broken up
1 C  dark chocolate, broken up
1 C  pecans, chopped

Grease a 9x13-inch pan.  Cream butter and sugar together, add yolk and vanilla, mix well.  Add flour and mix until dough forms.  Spread in pan.  Bake at 350 for 15-20 minutes, until golden brown.  Remove from over.  Sprinkle chocolate bits over the crust and cover.  Let chocolate melt and then spread while still warm.  Add nuts to top.  Cool and cut into squares while slightly warm.

Makes 12 servings.
