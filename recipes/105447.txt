1-1/2 lbs. lean hamburger meat, browned
1 onion, chopped
1 bell pepper, chopped
5-6 small cloves of garlic, minced
1 (14 oz.) can diced tomatoes
2 (6 oz.) cans tomato paste
1 can (8 oz.) tomato sauce
water as needed to thin sauce
1 can of mushrooms
1 package Lawry's spaghetti sauce mix
1 package thin spaghetti

Brown hamburger meat with onions. Place in crock pot or large pot and add all other ingredients. Stir thoroughly and cook on low for a couple of hours. Add water as needed to thin the sauce to your desired consistency.

NOTE: May add additional Italian seasonings, basil, oregano and parsley flakes to taste.
