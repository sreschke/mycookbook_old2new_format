Beef Marinade

This is a great marinade for steaks, I especially enjoy this for Shish Ka-bobs.

1/2 env. dry onion soup mix
2 TBS sugar
1/2 C ketchup
1/4 C vinegar
1/2 C water
1/4 C salad oil
1 TBS dry mustard
dash of hot sauce (optional)

Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 min. Cool. Pour over meat and refrigerate overnight or for several hours.
