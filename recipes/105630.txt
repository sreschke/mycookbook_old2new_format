1 prebaked italian bread shell crust, cubed
1-1/2 cups diced fresh tomatoes
1/2 cup thinly siced fresh basil
1/2 cup italian salad dressing, divided
7 cups ready to serve salad(spinash,lettuce etc)
1 small green pepper, julienned
1 cup sliced pepperoni
1 cup chredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/2 cup sliced ripe olives

In a large salad bowl, conbine bread cubes, tomatoes, basil and 1/4 salad dressing; let stand for 5 min. Add the salad gresns, green pepper, pepperoni, mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese and olives. Add remaining salad dressing and toss to coat.
