Banana Chocolate Chip Cake 

6 oz semisweet chocolate chips
2 c all-purpose flour
3/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking powder
1/2 c butter, softened
1-1/4 c sugar
2 eggs 
1 cup mashed banana  
1/3 c sour cream 
1 tsp vanilla extract 

Grease and flour a 9-inch tube pan or bundt pan. Combine flour, Baking soda, salt and baking powder in a bowl, set aside. 
Cream the butter in a large mixing bowl. Add the sugar gradually, beating at high speed until light and fluffy. Add eggs and beat well. 
Combine banana, sour cream and vanilla in a bowl. Add to egg mixture alternately with the dry ingredients, beginning and ending with the dry ingredients and beating at low speed until well blended after each addition. Stir in chocolate chips. Pour batter into the pan and bake at 350Ã¸F for 50 to 60 minutes.
