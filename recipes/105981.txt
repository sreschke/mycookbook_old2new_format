Basic Uncooked Icing 

2-1/4 c confectioners' sugar, sifted if lumpy 
1 tbs butter, softened 
3-4 tbs liquid (water, milk, coffee, lemon juice, orange juice, or other fruit juice 
1 tbs grated citrus rind (optional) 

Place sugar, butter, and 3 tbs liquid in mixing bowl; beat until very smooth. The icing should be thin enough to pour, but not so liquid that it runs off the cake. If it is too thick, add more liquid; too thin, beat in more confectioners' sugar. 
Makes enough for: tops and sides of 8-9" cake, or 18 cupcakes
