Brown Sugar Cookies

1 c. brown sugar
1 c. crisco
1/4 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. baking powder
2 c. flour
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 egg 
1 c. nuts (most times--make without nuts) 

Mix together, roll in small balls and place on pam sprayed cookie sheet. flatten with fork. bake at 350 till light brown they spread thin----so they cook fast.
