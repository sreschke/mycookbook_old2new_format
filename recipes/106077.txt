2 c. small shell macaroni
2 tbsp. butter
2 tbsp. minced onion
1 cn. mushroom soup 
1 lb. ground beef
1 tsp. celery salt
freshly ground black pepper
3/4 c. milk

Cook nacaroni , drain. Heat butter and saute onion over low heat until soft. Add beef and cook, string constantly, until browned. Add seasonings, soup and milk and blend well. Add macaroni and heat until piping hot.
