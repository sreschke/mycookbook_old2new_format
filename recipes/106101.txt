1 beef flank steak (about 1 pound) or 1 pound of 
      pork steak 
1 envelpoe onion soup mix
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1/4 cup lemon juice
1/4 cup water
2 cloves garlic minced
1 tsp. grated lemon peel
1 tsp. ground cumin
1/2tsp dried oregano
1/4tsp pepper
1 medium onion, thinly sliced
 Green Red and or yellow peppers, julienned
1 T cooking oil
8 flour tortillas, warmed

Place steak or pork in a glass dish.  Combine the next nine ingredients; pour over meat.  Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for 4 hour or overnight.  Drain and discard marinade.  Grill meat over hot coal until it reaches desired doneness, about 4 minutes per side for medium.  Meanwhile, in a skillet, saute onion and peppers in oil for 3-4 minutes or until crisp tender.  Slice meat into thin slices, across the grain; place on warm tortillas. Top with onion and pepper mixture.  If desired serve diced tomtoes, shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa, and guacomole along with the fajitas.
