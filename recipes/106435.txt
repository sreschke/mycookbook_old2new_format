1 cup flour
1 stick butter
1 cup sugar
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
3/4 cup milk
1 large can cherry fruit filling

Heat oven to 400 deg.  Slice butter into cake pan.  Place it in the oven to melt.  Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and milk together then pour on top of butter.  Spoon fruit over batter.  Bake until crispy brown, about 25 minutes.  Peaches, huckleberries, pineapple can be used for the fruit.
