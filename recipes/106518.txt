Corned Beef With Mustard Sauce

6-8 lbs corned beef brisket
2 cloves garlic
4 bay leaves
   whole cloves
1/3 cup brown sugar, packed
1 Tlbs prepared mustard
1/3 cup catsup
3 tlbs vinegar
3 Tlbs water

cover corned beef with water and simmer with the garlic and bay
leaves5-6 hours or until tender
   Cool in liquid. Drain (save juice fr veggies) , stud the fat side
with cloves and cover wth mustard sauce made by blending remaining
ingredients. Bake uncovered in slow oven at 275 degrees F. about an hour
basting occasionally with the sauce
