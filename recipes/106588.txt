1/4 cup olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup Chardonnay
1/4 cup finely chopped fresh Italian (flat-leaf) parsley
1 teaspoon dried crumbled oregano
2 cans minced clams
Juice from 1/2 fresh lemon
Salt, freshly ground black pepper, and crushed red pepper to taste
1 bunch green onions, chopped

12 oz. linguine
3 tablespoons butter
1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan

In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat and briefly cook garlic. Add wine, parsley, and oregano; stir and cook about 1 minute. Add clams with their liquid, lemon juice, and salt and peppers to taste. Cook over medium heat about five minutes; add green onions and cook for a few more minutes or until onion is limp.

At the same time, cook linguine according to package directions; drain. Add butter to pasta pot and melt. Return linguine to pot and coat with butter. Add clam sauce and Parmesan, stirring well to coat.

Serves 4.
