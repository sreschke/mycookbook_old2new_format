2 20 OZ CANS CHICKEN BROTH
1/4 CUP CORN STARCH
1 CARROT, MINCED
GARLIC SALT TO TASTE
PEPPER TO TASTE
2 SGGS
1 GREEN ONION

IN A SAUCE PAN,HEAT BROTH, STIRN IN CORN STARH ADD MINCED CRROT,GARLIC SALT AND PEPPER,BRING TO A BOIL, TUN OFF FLAME AND COOL SLIGHTLY.
IN ANOTHER BOWL BEAT 2 EGGS WELL, ADD EGGS TO HOT SOUP[ MIXTURE,SLOWLY, STIRRING CONSTANTLY WITH A WIRE WHISK, ADD FINELY CHOPPEED GREEN ONION
SERVES 4
