1 cup Marechino Cherries
1 cup manderine oranges (need 2 cans)
1 cup crushed pinapple (1 big can)
1/2 cup coconut (1 cup optional)
1/2 cup pecans (1 cup optional)
1 cup mini marshmellows 

2 cups coolwhip topping 

drain and dry off fruit (use paper towel!!!) ... put 1 cup ingredients into bowl ...
mix in coolwhip topping ... refrigerate (overnight if possible)

Very simple ... Very good ... Everyone enjoys!!!
