1 lb	Crabmeat (cleared of all shells)
2	Eggs
2 t	Mustard
2 T	Mayonaise
2 T	Butter, softened
2 T	Onion, minced
1 t	Old Bay Seafood Seasoning
Salt & Pepper to taste

1. Mix ingredients until well blended.
2. Form into patties.
3. Fry until golden.

Makes 1 dozen small crabcakes.
