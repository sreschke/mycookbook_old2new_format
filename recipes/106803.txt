1 cup sugar
2 eggs
2 cups flour
13 1/2 oz can crushed pineapple (not drained)
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/2 cup coconut
1/2 cup chopped pecans
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. salt

Topping:
1/2 cup melted butter
1/2 cup whipping cream (not whipped)
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla

             * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * * *

Combine sugar, vanilla, and eggs in large bowl.  Beat 2 minutes at medium speed.  Add flour, soda, salt and pineapple.  Mix 1 minute and pour into greased 12 x 8 inch pan
Top with mixture of brown sugar, coconut and pecans.  Bake at 350 degrees for 45 - 50 minutes.

Topping:  Melt 1/2 cup butter, add 1/2 cup whipping cream, 1/2 cup sugar and 1/2 tsp vanilla.   Pour over warm cake.
