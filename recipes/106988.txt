2 c water
16 oz can red kidney beans
1 chopped onion
1 chopped green pepper
1 c uncooked rice
1 1/2 tsp salt

  Drain beans, reserving liquid. Cook and stir onions and peppers in skillet. Add enough water to reserved bean liquid to make 2 c. Add to pan with beans, rice, and salt. 
  Heat to boiling, stirring occasionally. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 14 minutes. Remove from heat; fluff with fork; cover and let steam 5-10 minutes. 
  Serves 6.

  * Can add 1 sliced kielbasa, if desired, when adding rice.
