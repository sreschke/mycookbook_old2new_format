1 unsliced loaf french bread
1 # gr. beef
1 can (10 3/4oz.) cheddar cheese soup, undiluted
1 med. green pepper chopped
1 celery rib chopped
1 Tbs. worcestershire sauce
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. pepper
4 slices process american cheese, halved

Cut off top of bread. Carefully hollow out bottom of loaf, leaving a 1/2 inch shell. Cut removed bread into small cubes. Set aside. In skillet, brown beef & drain. Add soup, gr. pepper,celery, worcestershire sauce, salt & pepper. Cook & stir for 3-4 min. Stir in reserved bread cubes. Spread mixture into bread shell. Top with cheese. Replace bread top. Bake on ungreased baking sheet at 350 for 6-8 min. or till cheece is melted.
