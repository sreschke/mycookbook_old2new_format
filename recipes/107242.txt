4 tbsp margarine
2/3 cup non dairy creamer
4 1/2 tbsp flour (or cornstarch or potato starch)
20 oz. frozen broccoli or spinach, thawed and drained
2 tbsp onion soup mix
2/3 cup mayonnaise
3 eggs
corn flake crumbs on top and bottom

Beat eggs.  Mix with all ingredients except corn flake crumbs.  Cover bottom of a 9 in pie pan or a 8 inch square baking dish with crumbs.  Add quiche mixture.  Top with corn flake crumbs.  Bake in 350 oven for 50 minutes.(?)

To quadruple recipe, triple only the mayonnaise, non-dairy creamer and eggs.
