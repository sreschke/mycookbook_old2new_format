
1  Egg, well beaten
2  Sticks of soft Oleo
1  Cup Sugar
2  Cups plain flour
1  tsp Soda
¬  tsp. Salt


Beat the egg.  In a separate bowl beat Oleo and Sugar. Add the beaten egg and all dry ingredients. Mix well. On an ungreased cookie sheet drop 1 tsp. for each cookie,pressing a pecan half on the top of each.  Bake at 350ø until light brown.  8 to 10 minutes.
