Recipe: Cucumber Yogurt Raita 

Makes about 1? cups 

- 1 tablespoon finely minced red onion or shallot 
- 1« cups yogurt, preferably Greek-style 
- 1 small clove garlic, peeled and minced 
- 1 tablespoon grated fresh gingerroot 
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh mint 
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice 
- ¬ teaspoon salt 
- ¬ teaspoon ground black pepper 
- 1 unpeeled cucumber, seeded and coarsely grated 
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds, toasted 

1. Put the onion in a small bowl and cover with cold water. Let sit 10 minutes, then drain and rinse. Put into a paper towel and squeeze out excess moisture. 

2. Combine onion with yogurt, garlic, ginger, mint, lemon, salt and pepper. Grate the cucumber and stir into the yogurt. Cover and refrigerate up to 2 hours. Just before serving, stir some of the cumin seeds into the raita, and sprinkle the remaining on top. 

Note: Toast the cumin seeds in a small, dry skillet over medium heat just until fragrant. Transfer to a bowl and cool before using. 

From "Grilling" by Eric Treuille and Birget Erath.
