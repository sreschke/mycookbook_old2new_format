Ingredients:

  2  lbs. LEAN ground beef (or ground turkey)
  1 Qt. water
  2 med. onions, Chopped
  2  -  8 oz. cans tomato sauce
  4 cloves garlic, minced
  2 Tbsp vinegar
  1 lg. bay leaf
  1/2 tsp ground cloves
  2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  1 tsp cinnamon
  1 tsp allspice
  1 tsp. cayenne pepper
  1 tsp ground cumin seed
  5 Tbsp chili powder
  1/2 oz.  unsweetened chocolate (or tsp. cocoa)
  1  1/2 tsp. salt
  1 can chili hot beans (or kidney beans)

Directions:

  In a 4 - quart pot, add ground meat to water.  Stir until it is separated into a fine texture.  Boil slowly for 1/2 hour.  Add all the other ingredients.  Stir to blend and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and simmer uncovered about 3 hours.  You can cover the pot for the last hour if chili is thick enough.  Refrigerate overnight to skim off fat if desired.  Add a can of
chili hot beans to 1 cup of the chili, heat, and mix with remainder of the chili.  Serve  with spaghetti, grated cheese and chopped onion.
