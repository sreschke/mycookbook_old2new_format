              CRUST:
   2      cups          flour
   1      cup           brown sugar -- packed
     1/2  cup           butter -- softened
   1      cup           pecan halves -- (can used chopped)
                                        
                        CARAMEL LAYER:
     2/3  cup           butter
     1/2  cup           brown sugar
                                        
   1      cup           chocolate chips

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

CRUST: 

Combine flour, brown sugar and butter; mix well. (I used my food processor.)
Pat firmly in a 9 x 13- inch pan. Sprinkle pecans on top.

CARAMEL LAYER:

In a heavy saucepan over medium heat, cook butter and brown sugar,
stirring
constantly. 
Bring to a boil and let cook for 30 seconds to 1 minute longer.
Quickly, pour caramel evenly over crust. (before it "sets")

Bake 18-22 minutes or til bubbly. Remove from oven and immediately
sprinkle
chocolate chips over top. 
Allow to melt 2-3 minutes; swirl to create a marble effect. Cool completely
before cutting.
