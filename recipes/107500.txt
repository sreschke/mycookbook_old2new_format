
 Marinade for many meats

 1 onion, grated
 1 to 2 garlic cloves, crushed
 1/4 cup lemon juice
 1/4 cup soy sauce
 1 tsp. pepper
 few drops tabasco sauce (optional)
 1 tsp ground coriander
 2 Tbsp brown sugar
 2 Tbsp vegetable oil

 Mix all ingredients well.  Let meat marinade several hours or
 overnite.  Great with shish kabobs, city chicken, steak or even
 chicken.  Double or triple recipe with ease if serving large
 quantities.
