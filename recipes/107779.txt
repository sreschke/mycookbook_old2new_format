Amish Peanut Butter  -  Spread 

1 Cup light corn syrup
1/2 Cup creamy peanut butter
1/4 Cup Marshmallow Creme
	
Stir all the ingredients together in a mixing bowl until well-mixed. Put the spread into a covered bowl and store in the refrigerator. You need to serve the peanut butter spread at room temperature. Can be used as a bread spread and as ice cream topping. Makes about 1 1/2 cups. 	

Source: Midwest Living Magazine

My Personal Added Note: This is a popular Amish food item. Sometimes it is known as "church spread" for it is common to eat this spread on bread after most Amish church services has ended.
