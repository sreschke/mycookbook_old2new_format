2 1/2 c. flour
1 tbsp. instant yeast
1 c. warm water
2 tbsp. vegetable oil
1 tsp. sugar
1 tsp. salt
dash of pepper
1/4 tsp. oregano
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1/4 tsp. thyme
1/3 c. Kraft Italian dressing
1 c. grated mozzarella
1/4 c. parmasan


In medium bowl, mix water, oil, sugar, flour and yeast. Let rise 30 minutes. Spread dough on greased cookie sheet.

In small bowl, mix italian dressing with pepper, oregano, garlic and thyme. Spread this mix on top of dough. Sprinkle cheeses on top and bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes.
