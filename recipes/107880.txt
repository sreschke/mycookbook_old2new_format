15 pounds Ground Beef
5 cups Ketchup (40 ounces)
1/2 cup Vinegar
3 16-ounce cans Tomato Sauce
3/4 cups Mustard
1 3/4 cups Brown Sugar
2 tablespoons Black Pepper
3 tablespoons Salt
3 cups Water
1 1/2 cup Dried, Chopped Onion

Brown hamburger, drain.  Combine remaining ingredients in large canner, simmer 20 minutes.  Makes 96 1/2 cup servings.  Fills 2 6 quart ice cream pails.  Freezes well.
