1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup parmesean cheese
1/2 cup breadcumbs
1/4 cup fresh chopped parsley
1.5 lbs. ground beef
1 TBS minced garlic
1/8 tsp. red chile flakes

Preheat oven to 375. Combine the above and shape into about 25 ping pong sized balls. Place on a cookie sheet or jellyroll pan and bake 10 minutes. Add to your favorite spaghetti sauce and simmer for an additional 10 minutes. **SO GOOD**
