
 
4 TBSP. butter, divided
2 TBSP. all-purpose flour
1/2 cup milk
6 large eggs, beaten
1 lb. pkg. bacon
1 (12-inch) pizza shell (such as Boboli)
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
 
Heat 2 TBSP. butter in saucepan.  Add flour, cook 1 minute.  WHISK in milk and cook until mixture boils and thickens.  Cool.  Heat remaining butter in small skillet.  Add eggs.  Cook and stir eggs until soft scrambled.  Cool.  Cook bacon until crisp; drain and crumble.  Combine milk sauce with eggs.  Spoon onto pizza shell and top with bacon and cheese.  Bake at 400'F for 20 - 30 minutes or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
