Overnight  Eggnog  French  Toast - 
  Small Recipe 
5 eggs 
1 1/2 cups of half & half , or milk 
1/4 Cup of Sugar
1/4 tsp of nutmeg 
1tsp. Vanilla 
3/4 tsp. rum extract 
12 /34 inch thick slices of French Bread 

Large Recipe 
9 eggs
3 cups of half & half or milk 
1/3 cup of sugar 
1/2 tsp. nutmeg 
2 tsp. vanilla 
1 1/2 tsp. rum extract 
24 3/4 inch thick slices of French Bread (1 1/2 lb. loaf)

powdered sugar
 
Grease two  15x10x1 inch baking pans. 

In large bowl , combine eggs, half and half, sugar, nutmeg, vanilla,and rum extract. Beat until well blended.  

Arrange bread slices in greased  baking pans.  Pour  egg  mixture  over bread in pans.  
Lift and  move bread slices  until all  mixture is absorbed.  
Cover with foil, refrigerate overnite or freeze up to 1 week. 
Heat oven to 500*. 
Remove bread slices from refrig or freezer(do not thaw),remove foil .  
Bake 1 pan  at  500*  for  15 minutes or until golden brown.
Sprinkle  with  powdered sugar.      
Repeat with second pan.  24 slices.
*tip  if both pans are baked at same time , switch pans in oven  halfway thru 
baking  time ....
