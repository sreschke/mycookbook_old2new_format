1 8oz. okg. cream cheese (softened)
8 oz. sour cream
1/2 c. mayonaise
1/2 c. sliced green onions
2 tsp. Tabasco hot pepper sauce
1/2 c. sliced almonds
1 tsp. lemon juice
10-12 oz. canned crab
dash garlic salt
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. 
Mix all ingredients
Spoon into greased 2 qt. baking dish
Bake 25-30 min. or until golden brown around edges
"You won't be crabby when you eat this!"
