STREUSEL TOPPING:       
1/3 C Bisquick             
1/3 C Brown sugar; packed      
1/2 t groundn cinnamon             
2 T firm margarine or butter

COFFEE CAKE:             
2 C Bisquick
2/3 C milk or water
2 T gran. sugar
1 egg

HEAT: Oven to 375.  Grease 9" round pan.  Mix Streusel Topping until crumbly; set aside.  STIR: Coffee Cake ingred. until blended.  Spread in pan; sprinkle with Streusel Topping.
BAKE:18 to 22 min. until golden.
