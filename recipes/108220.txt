1 1/2 c. cooked, chopped chicken
1 c. shredded Cheddar
1 c. frozen corn, thawed and drained
1 1/4 c. milk
1 pkg. taco seasoning mix
3/4 c. Bisquick 
4 oz. can chopped green chiles
3 eggs

Heat oven to 400.  Grease 10-inch pie plate.  Mix  chicken, corn, and seasoning mix; spread in plate. Sprinkle with chiles and cheese.  Beat remaining  ingredients until smooth with a hand mixer.  Pour into pie plate.  Bake until knife inserted in center comes out clean (25-30 minutes).  Cool for 5 minutes.  Serves  6-8.
