4   large shallots, diced
3/4 cup dry white wine
3 T white wine vinegar
2 t. dried tarragon
4 small avocados, peeled and chopped
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 T lemon juice
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t. freshly ground pepper
Garnishes:  lemon cups, avocado slices

Cook first four ingredients in a small saucepan over medium-high heat 8 to 10 minutes or until liquid is reduced to about 2 tablespoons.  Cool.

Process avocado, next four ingredients, and reduced misture in a food processor until smooth, stopping to scrape down sides.  Chill up to 2 days, if desired.  Garnish, if desired.  Make about 3 cups.
