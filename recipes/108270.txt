First: 
2 4-oz. cans whole chilis
12 4(long)x1(wide)x1/2-inch(deep)wedges Montery Jack cheese

Second:
Slit one side of chilis and take out seeds & veins,
then roll 1 wedge of cheese inside. Place in a buttered 9x13-inch baking dish, slit-side down.

Third:
to 1 1/2 cups milk, add 4 large eggs, beaten, 1/4 cup flour, and salt and pepper to taste. Can add hot sauce to taste as well.

Bake in 350 degree oven for 40-45 minutes. Serve hot with sour cream and/or guacamole. Serves 6-8.
