3 T olive oil
1 jalapeno--seeded and chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 lb shrimp
1/2 lb crab meat
8 oz spaghetti
1/2 cup scallion, chopped
1/4 cup grated parmesan

Heat oil in large skillet.  Add jalapeno and garlic and saute 2 minutes.  Add shrimp and crab and saute 2 more minutes.  Remove from heat and keep warm.
Boil pasta and drain.  Drizzle with 1 T oil.  Add green onion, grated cheese and shrimp mixture.  Toss to mix.  Good served in taco bowl.
