20 saltine crackers
1 lb ground beef
1/2 pound ground pork
1 cup milk
1 egg
1 small onion, quartered
5 sprigs parsley
1 t salt
1/4 t pepper
1/2 cup celery
1/4 t sage

Crumble crackers in blender.  Place in bowl with meat.  Add remaining ingredients to blender, process until chopped.  Pour over meat mixture and mix thoroughly.  Place in loaf pan.  Bake at 350 for 1 1/2 hours or until done.
