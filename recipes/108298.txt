3 cups flour
1/4 tsp salt
2 eggs
1 Tbsp baking powder
1 1/2 cups dried cranberries
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tsp baking soda
3/4 cup buttermilk
3/4 cup butter
rind of 2 lemons, zested

Mix dry ingredients together.  Beat butter and sugar until fluffy, add eggs one at a time.  Mix well.  Add dry ingredients, then add buttermilk.  Fold in cranberries and lemon zest.  Mix well.  Drop on cookie sheet, using ice cream scoop.  Brush tops with buttermilk and sprinkle with sugar.  Bake at 350 for 22 minutes.
