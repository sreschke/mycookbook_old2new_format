1  Rabbit
2 cups of Vinegar
2 cups of Water
Salt and Peper
3 Bay leaves
1/2 cup of Butter
1 cup of Sour Cream

Dress out rabbit and cut in pieces. Make vinegar tea  with vinegar, water, salt, pepper, and bay leaves.  Arrange rabbit pieces in a deep bowl and cover with tea. Turn rabbit 2 times a day for 2 days (in the fridge!). Drain but  keep the tea. Melt butter in a deep, heavy skillet,  add rabbit pieces and fry to dark gold. Slowly add tea to cover the rabbit. Simmer one hour then remove  from  stove and add the sour cream.
