INGREDIENTS:

1lb bacon (fried)
1lb hamburger (browned)
1lb can red beans
2-1lb cans baked beans (I use Vancamps)
1pkg frozen small lima beans
1 onion diced
2C ketchup
2C brown sugar
6T vinegar
4T worcestershire sauce
4T dry mustard

PREPERATION:

Brown hamburger with onion and drain. Cut bacon into
small pieces. Put everything into a slow cooker and heat on high for about 3hrs. This lets the flavors blend. You can cook longer on low if desired. Stir occasionally.
