3 cups unpared zucchini sliced VERY thin
1 cup Bisquick
1/2 cup finely chopped onions
1 clove garlic minced
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup vegetable oil
2 tbs snipped parsley or flakes
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp seasoned salt
1/2 tsp dried oregano
2 eggs slightly beaten
dash pepper
Mix all together & put in greased 9x13 pan.  Preheat oven to 350*.  Bake for 25-35 minutes until golden brown.  Cut into 2"x1" squares.  Serve hot or cold.
