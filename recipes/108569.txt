2 qt water
2 lb chicken necks, skinned and trimmed of fat
1 16oz can sliced tomatoes
1 cup sliced celery
1 cup sliced onion
1 cup sliced carrots
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
6 tbs quick-cooking medium barley
1 bay leaf
1/4 tsp dried marjoram

Heat water to boiling; add chicken.  When boiling again, skim foam from surface.  Add remaining ingredients.  Cover and simmer for 50-60 minutes.  Remove chicken and chill them quickly.  When cool enough to handle, remove meat from bones; return meat to soup.  Before serving, skim fat from surface and remove bay leaf.  Makes 4 meal-size servings, approx. 210 calories/serving.
