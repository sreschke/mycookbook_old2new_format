salt and pepper to taste
3lbs. beef round, cut into 1 1/2" cubes
1/2 c. seasoned flour (1/2 tsp each salt and  pepper)
1/3 c. vegetable oil
2 large onions, cut into thick rounds
4 carrots, cut into 1" lengths
1 1/4 lbs. small red potatoes, skinned and quartered
3 ribs celery, cut into 1" lengths
4 c. beef broth
2 T. tomato paste
2 T. Dijon mustard

Coat meat w/ flour and brown in hot oil.  Reduce heat to med-low.  Drain off all but 1 T. pan drippings.  Add onions, carrots, potatoes, celery to pot.  Cook 5 mins, until lightly browned (stir once or twice).  Add broth, tomato paste, and mustard, stirring to scrape up browned bits.  Bring to a boil.  Return browned meat to pot.  Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for approx. 2 hours, stirring occassionaly to prevent sticking.  Stew is ready when meat is tender and sauce is slightly thickened.

Cooking Time - approx 2 1/2 hours
8 servings - 279 calories per serving
