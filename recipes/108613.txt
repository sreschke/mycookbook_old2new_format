1/2 c. white vinegar
1/4 c. water
HEAT THE ABOVE IN A SMALL SAUCEPAN. THEN STIR IN THE FOLLOWING AND HEAT UNTIL DISSOLVED:
1/4 c. sugar or Splenda
1 1/2 t. Accent
2 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
1/2 t. garlic powder

MIX TOGETHER IN A BOWL THE FOLLOWING:
2 cans bean sprouts, drained
2 T. diced pimento
2 T. onion, chopped 
2 T. green pepper, chopped

POUR COOKED DRESSING OVER SPROUT MIXTURE.  ADD 1/2 CUP VEGETABLE OIL.  TOSS & CHILL.  OVERNITE OR LONGER.
