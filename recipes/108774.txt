
1 lb bulk sausage, browned, drained and crumbled 
4 eggs 
2 1/2 c milk 
1 1/2 c shredded cheddar 
8 ea slices white bread 
1 can cream of mushroom soup 
1/2 c milk 
1 t mustard
Grease large, flat baking dish. 
Completely cover bottom of dish with bread slices, allowing them to rise along sides slightly for crust. 
Spread cooked sausage to cover. 
Spread cheese to cover. 
Mix eggs and 2 1/2 cups milk and mustard - pour over casserole. 
Refrigerate overnight*. 
Next morning, preheat oven to 375. 
Mix soup and milk and cover casserole. 
Bake for l hour 15 minutes or until completely set. 
Yield 5 servings
