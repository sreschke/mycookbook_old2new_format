1 1/2 lbs. ground beef
1/2 C chopped onion
1 can tomato soup
1 can chicken gumbo soup
3-4 squirts ketchup
1 squirt mustard
all seasonings including chili powder
1/4 C brown sugar

Brown beef and onion; drain fat.  mix in remaining ingredients; simmer
Serve on hamburger buns or hoagie rolls
