This is for a three layer cake but I just make 2 and have enough batter left  for a dozen cupcakes :-)

Cake
 2 cups sugar
1 1/2 cups oil
4 large eggs
2 cups flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
2 teaspoons baking soda
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
3/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1 lb fine grated carrots 
1/2 cup chopped pecans 
1/2 cup golden raisins

Frosting
4 cups powdered sugar
2 8-oz packages cream cheese, softened
1 stick butter, softened
4 teaspoons vanilla 

cake:
Heat oven to 325F. Grease three 9" cake pans. Line bottoms  with wax paper. Lightly grease wax  paper. Beat sugar and oil til combined.  Add eggs 1 at a time, beating well after each addition. Sift in flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon and nutmeg. Stir in carrots, pecans and raisins.

Pour into prepared pans. Bake until toothpick put in center comes out clean, about 45 minutes. Let cool. 

frosting:
Beat all ingredients til smooth and creamy.

Place a cake layer on platter. Spread with 3/4 cup frosting. Top with another cake layer. Spread with 3/4 cup frosting. Top with remaining cake layer. Using spatula, spread remaining frosting over sides and top.  Serve cold. For 10.
