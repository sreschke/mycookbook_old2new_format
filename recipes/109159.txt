12 oz. pkg. flat egg noodles
1 pkg. frozen broccoli
1 quart half & half
1 lb. velveeta
1/2 cup onions (browned in butter)

Cook noodles as directed on package. Shortly before noodles are done, add broccoli, half & half, velveeta (cut into small pieces) and onions. Cook on medium heat until cheese melts.

Note: use a big pan and plan on lots of leftovers.
Enjoy!!
