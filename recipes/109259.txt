1 pkg. boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 medium sized can tomatoes
1 small can green chilies
1 tsp. chili powder
1 tsp. oregano
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 tsp. dried minced onion
1/2 tsp. cumin powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper

Pour canned tomatoes and green chilies into crock pot.  Place chicken breasts on top of tomatoes and chilies.  Sprinkle on seasonings.  Cook in crock pot until meat is tender and falls apart (about 6-8 hours on low).  Serve with tortilla shells, refried beans and spanish rice.
