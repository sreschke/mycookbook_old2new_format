1/2 Lb Chicken breasts, cubed
5 T soy sauce
2 T sugar
wine
1/2 inch ginger, chopped
1 can of pineapples

heat wok with cooking oil. add ginger. then add chicken and stir until it's white on all sides. then add soy sauce, wine, and sugar. finally, add one can of pineapples and simmer until ready to serve.
