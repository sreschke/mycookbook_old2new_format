Smoked turkey or chicken is also good.

1/4 cup bottled salsa plus 4 teaspoons
3 tablespoons mayonnaise
3 scallions, sliced thin
2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
4 turkey slices (or more)
4 slices sourdough bread, toasted
1 package (6 ounces) sliced Monterey jack cheese
1 avocado, sliced

Preheat broiler.  Combine 1/4 cup salsa,mayonnaise, scallions and cilantro in bowl. Place toast slices on cookie sheet. Top with turkey slices; coat with salsa mixture; top with cheese.  Broil 1 to 3 minutes, until cheese begins to brown.  Top sandwiches with avocado and remaining 4 teaspoons salsa.  Makes 4 sandwiches.
