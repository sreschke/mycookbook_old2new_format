2 cups self-rising flour
1/3 cup shortening
2/3 cup buttermilk
1 quart briarberries (blackberries)

Combine flour and shortening, add buttermilk and mix well, add berries.

Pour into a baking pan and bake 20 to 30 minutes (until nicely browned) in a 450øF/235øC oven.

Sauce:
1/3 cup water
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup butter

Mix in a small pot and bring to a boil.
Serve with pudding hot from the oven.

This is Mama's version of a recipe she got from my Grandmother Kate.
