2- (230gram) packages of San Franciscas or Buerre Blanc cookies
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1 cup melted butter
2 (300gm) packages cream cheese
3-4 cups powdered sugar (bloom suiker)
4 tablespoons lemon juice
1 Liter of verse slag room

1. Crush cookies to make crumbs (I put in blender)

2. Add the 1/2 cup granulated sugarand the melted butter to the cookie crumbs and mix.

3.Press crumbs mix into a 9 x 13" pan

4.Beat the cream cheese until creamy then add the powdered sugar, the verse slagroom and lemon juice
continue to mix until the cream thickens.

5. Fold the cream cheese mixture onto the cookie 
crust.

6. Chill until firm and top with favorie fruit filling.
