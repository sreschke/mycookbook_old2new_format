
   1/2 Cup of Maragine     2    Tablespoons Sugar
 1     Cup Flour           1    Teaspoon Vanilla
 1     Pinch of Salt       1    Cup Pecans Chopped
       Powdered Sugar


 Melt maragine and add sugar, flour, Vanilla
 and Pecans.  Mix.  Roll into fingers - about 
 2- inches long.  Bake, let cool, roll in 
 Powdered Sugar.


 BAKE: AT 325 DEGREES FOR 20 MINUTES.
