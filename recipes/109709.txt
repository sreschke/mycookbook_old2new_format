INGREDIENTS:
3 cups cooked diced sweet potatoes 
2 tablespoons butter 
1/2 cup warm milk 
1/2 teaspoon salt 
miniature marshmallows 
1/2 cup chopped peanuts
PREPARATION:
Add butter and salt to warm milk; stir until butter is melted. Blend with potatoes; beat until light and fluffy. Add more milk, if needed. Pile potatoes into a buttered casserole. Preheat oven to 375Ã¸. 
Bake casserole 20 minutes. Generously top with miniature marshmallows and chopped peanuts. Return to oven and heat until marshmallows are lightly browned.
Sweet Potato Casserole serves 6 to 8.
