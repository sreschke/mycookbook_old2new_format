4 		Chicken Breasts halves -( boneless) Cut to make 1/2 thickness so you end up with 8 				thin pieces
1 		Orange, remove zest and jullienne strips, squeeze and reserve juice
1/4 C	Marjorine, softened
1		Garlic clove, crushed and finely chopped
1		Onion, halved and thinly sliced

Sauce breasts in marjorine for 5 minutes on each side (medium heat). Add remaining ingredients and finish cooking. Chicken should be slightly browned and most of liquid has reduced.

Prep time 15 minutes.
Cooking time 15 minutes.
