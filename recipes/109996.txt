Sesame Chicken with Yogurt-Honey Dipping Sauce 
Category: Kid Stuff
Preperation Level: easy

Carole Kotkin is Food Editor for Wine News Magazine, Manager of The Cooking School at Ocean Reef Club in Key Largo. She is co-author of
MMMiami?Tempting Tropical Tastes for Home Cooks Everywhere, Henry Holt Publishers. 

Kids will love this fun-to-eat version of fast-food chicken nuggets.

1 cup unseasoned bread crumbs
3 tablespoons sesame seeds
4 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
1 pound boneless, skinned chicken breasts, cut into 1-1/2-inch pieces
2 egg whites, beaten
2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
1-1/2 tablespoons olive oil

Sauce:
1 cup plain yogurt
1 tablespoon honey
1 tablespoon Dijon mustard

Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Spray baking pan with nonstick vegetable-oil cooking spray.
Combine crumbs, sesame seeds and Parmesan cheese in a shallow bowl. Combine egg whites and mustard in a shallow bowl. Dip chicken in egg whites, then in crumbs. Place in prepared baking pan. Sprinkle with olive oil. Bake for 15 minutes or until cooked through, turning nuggets over once. Meanwhile combine sauce ingredients in a small bowl. Serve with chicken. Serves 6.
