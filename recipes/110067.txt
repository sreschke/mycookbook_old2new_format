2 (7oz) cans artichoke bottoms, drained
1 (4oz) can diced green chilies
1 cup mayonaise
1 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
1 can drained crabmeat (optional)

                  * * * * * * * 

Mash artichokes and mix together with remaining ingredients.   Bake in a small casserole at 350 degrees for 40 minutes (or heat in microwave).  Serve hot or room temperature with crackers.  I really like this with fretos.
