2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
8 oz (2 containers) Better n Eggs
3/4 cup low fat yogurt
2/3 cup softened margarine
2/3 cups unsweetened cocoa
1/2 cup low fat milk
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda

1.In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar and all other ingredients until blended together. Beat at high speed for 2 to 3 minutes. Spread batter in greased and flour dusted 13 x 9 baking pan. Bake at 350 for 30 to 35 minutes. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then remove and cool on wire rack. Cut into 15 pieces. Can be served with cream topping, low fat ice cream or raspberry sauce.
1 serving--Calories 250 Sodium 220mg Protein 5.5g
Cholesterol 5mg Total Fat 10g
