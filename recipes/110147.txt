Preheat oven to 350. Combine and set aside:
1 cup flour
2 Tablespoons wheat germ
1/4 cup whole wheat flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt

Cream: 
1 cube butter, softened
scant 1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/4 cup brown sugar

Add 1 egg and 1 Tablespoon vanilla

Mix the 2 above mixtures together. Add 1 cup chocolate chips. Drop spoonfuls of dough and bake for 10 minutes.
