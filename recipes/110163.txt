1 chopped red pepper 32 oz chicken broth
2 cans corn - undrained
1 can Rotel - tomatoes and green chili
1 can ranch style beans undrained
1 can diced tomatoes - undrained
2 chicken boulion cubes
10 oz white chicken meat shredded
8 oz velvetta cheese cut into cubes

Sauteed red pepper in olive oil until tender.
Add all ingredients except cheesein larger soup pan. Heat.  Just before serving add cheese and stir until melted.
