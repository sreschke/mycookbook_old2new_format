1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup onion, diced
1/2 cup celery, diced
1/2 cup carrots, diced
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1-1/2 tablespoons cornstarch
2 - 14-1/2 cans chicken broth
4 cups milk, room temperature
1/8 teaspoon soda
1 - 2 pound processed cheese, cubed (amount depends
  on how cheesy you want the soup)
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon pepper
1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 tablespoon dried parsley
paprika

Melt butter in heavy saucepan.  Saute' vegetables until tender.  Stir in flour and cornstarch.  Cook until bubbly.  Add stock and milk gradually, blending into smooth sauce.  Add soda and cheese cubes; stir until thickened.  Season with salt and pepper, add parsley.  Before serving heat thoroughly in double boiler.  Do not let boil.  Garnish with paprika.
