Linguini or angel hair pasta
Canned tomatoes, chopped
Onion, chopped
olive oil
Basil and oregano, chopped
red pepper flakes
medium shrimp, tail on, p&d
salt and pepper

Cook and shock pasta.  Refrigerate for service.

Saute onion in olive oil.  Add tomatoes, salt, pepper and red pepper flakes.  Simmer until tomatoes are tender, breaking apart as they cook.  Saute shrimp in olive oil and small amount of red pepper flakes.  Cook until barely done.  Reheat pasta and toss with sauce and shrimp.
