A See's recipe

3 ? 6 oz. pakage chocolate chips
1 ? 7 oz or 8 oz bottle marshmallow whip
1 « to 2 cups nuts
1 « cubes butter, but up
2 tsp. vanilla

Mix in large bowl and have ready.  

Mix in large pot, bring to boil, 4 « c. sugar, 1 large can evaporated milk, keep at rolling boil, under slow fire for 10 minutes, stirring constantly.  Pour hot mixture over ingredients and stir until everything is melted well mixed.  Pour into large pan making candy about 1" thick.  Cut into squares when cooled.

Makes 5 lbs.
