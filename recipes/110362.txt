2 1/2 cups cornstarch
1 1/4 cup water
1/2 cup ginger, minced
1/2 cup garlic, minced
3 3/4 cup sugar
2 1/2 cup soy sauce
1 1/4 cup white wine
2 1/2 cups rice wine vinegar
8 cups chicken stock
15 lbs chicken, dark meat, deboned, in large chunks
1 1/4 cup soy sauce
2 Tbsp white pepper
5 eggs, lightly beaten
3 3/4 cup cornstarch
1 1/4 cup flour
10 cups green onion, cut on bias
60 small dried hot peppers
Korean Chili Sauce to taste

Combine water and cornstarch.  Add ginger, garlic, sugar, soy sauce, white wine, vinegar and chicken stock.  Stir until sugar dissolves.  Refrigerate until needed.

Mix chicken, soy sauce and pepper.  Stir in eggs.  Add cornstarch and flour and mix until chicken pieces are evenly coated.  Deep fry in small batches until crispy.  Drain on paper towels and hold warm.

Place small amount of oil in wok and heat until hot.  Add green onion and peppers and stir-fry briefly.  Add sauce and bring to boil.  Season with Korean Chilic Sauce to taste.  Cook until sauce thickens.  Stir in chicken pieces and serve immediately.

Yield:  3 hotel pans
