Flour
salt
pepper
cayenne pepper
paprika
onion powder
garlic powder
celery seed
poultry seasoning
buttermilk
chicken pieces

marinate chicken in buttermilk overnight.  Season flour with spices.  Dredge chicken in flour and place on rack to set.  When flour has been moistened, dredge again in flour mixture.  Deep fry at 350 until light brown, place on sheet pan on racks and bake at 350 until done.  Re-fry in deep fryer until golden brown and crispy.
