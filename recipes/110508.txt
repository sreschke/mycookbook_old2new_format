preheat oven to 340ø 
Place in small container and set aside:

2-1/3 cup flour
1-1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt

1 cube butter 
1 cup sugar (or « cup Splenda + « cup sugar)
1« Tbsp lemon  rind
2 tsp lemon juice
2 lge eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp almond extract
1 cup cut-up or whole almonds

In electric mixer beat together butter and sugar til light and fluffy. beat in eggs one at a time beating well after each.  Add the flour mixture and mix in well. Beat in the vanilla and almond extract. Stir in the almonds.

Form 3 equal size pieces of dough about 2" wide, 3/4" high and about 7 or 8 " long placed side by side in the parchment covered large cookie sheet.
Bake 25-28 min until lightly brown.

Remove from oven. Cut each into 3/4" diagonal pieces. Place cut-side up down on cookie sheet and bake at 325ø for about 15 min.  Take out and turn each piece over and bake another 15 minutes.  Should be very crispy and light.  Freezes very well.
