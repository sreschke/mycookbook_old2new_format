2 cups flour 
2 cups old-fashioned oats 
1 teaspoon soda 
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon 
1/2 teaspoon salt 
1 cup butter 
2 cups brown sugar 
2 cups peanut butter 
3 eggs 
2 teaspoons vanilla 
Instructions:
Preheat oven to 350. Cream butter and brown sugar. Add eggs, vanilla and peanut butter. Add dry ingredents. Roll by tablespoon-full into balls. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
