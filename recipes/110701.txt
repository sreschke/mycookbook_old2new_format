Serves 6
Prep time: 15 min.
Cook time: 40 min.
----------------------------------------------------
Ingredients:

1/4 cup butter
1 onion, chopped
1 bell pepper, chopped
1 - 10 oz. can cream of mushroom soup
1 - 14.5 oz. can tomatoes with green chiles
2 cups chopped cooked chicken
10 - 6 inch corn tortillas
2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese
1 cup sour cream
1 TBS. milk
1 small jalapeno pepper, chopped
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
----------------------------------------------------
Directions:

     Preheat oven to 325.  Melt butter in a large skillet; add onion and pepper.  Cook until tender, about 5 min.  Stir in soup, undrained tomatoes and chicken.  Tear tortillas into bite-size pieces.
     Spray a large casserole dish with cooking spray.  Layer tortillas, soup mixture and Monterey Jack cheese in prepared casserole.
     Mix sour cream, milk and jalapeno ina small bowl.  Spread over top of casserole.
     Bake chicken until bubbly, about 40 min.  Sprinkle with parsley.  Serve immediately.
----------------------------------------------------
If you can't find tomatoes with jalapenos, make it yourself.  Combine a can of stewed tomatoes with either a chopped jalapeno or a can of chopped green chiles.

For an authentic flavor, garnish with finely chopped cilantro.

You can prepare this through step 3 up to 4 hours ahead of time.  Refrigerate until ready to bake.
