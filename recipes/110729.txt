3  ears sweet corn,husks and silk removed
2 Whole eggs
2 Egg Whites
1 cup low-fat cottage cheese
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp freshly ground black  pepper
2tbs Extra -virgin olive oil
1 cup finely chopped yellow onion
3/4 cup finely chopped red bell pepper
1  cup finely chopped yellow summer squash
1 stem fresh rosemary9leaves only)
Nonstick cooking spray(olivr oil flavor)
1.Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.Place thesweet corn onto a hot grill.grill for 10 minutes,turning frequently until the corn the corn is lightly browned on at least 2 sides.Remove and let cool.Stand the ears on end and hold firmly.Cut down the lenghth of the ears with a large knife,stripping the cooked kernals from the cob.set the kernels aside.There should be about 2 cups.
2.In a mixing bowl,whisk the whole eggs and the egg whites together.Fold in the cottage cheese,flour,salt and pepper.Let the batter stand.
3.In a large nonstick skillet over a medium -high heat,warm the olive oil for 1 minute.Add the onion,red pepper,squash and rosemary leaves and cook,stirring frequently,for 5 minutes.Add the corn and cook for for 2 minutes,stirring once.Coat a 9-inch glass or stonewear pie plate with non stick spray.Pour half of the batter into the bottom of a pie plate.Spoon the vegatables and corn evenly into the pire plate.Pour the remiaining batter in a circle over the vegetables.Place the pie on the middle shelf of the oven and bake for 45 minutes.Remove from the oven and let stand for 5 minutes.Remove from the oven and let stand for 5 minutes.Cut into wedges,and serve.
Makes 6 servings
