Makes 6 jumbo muffins Prep 15 minutes.Bake 375 degrees for 30 minutes
Crunch Topping:
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1/3 cup packed light brown sugar
3 tbs unsalted butter,chilled,cut up
1/2 cup pecans,chopped
Muffins:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
1/4 tsp salt
2 ripe medium bananas  mashed
1 large egg
1/3 cup milk
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/3 cup packed light brown sugar
1/2 cup(1 stick)unsalted butter,melted
1. heat oven to 375 degrees.Coat bottoms of one jumbo 6 -cup muffin pan,or line with muffin cup liners.
2.Crunch Topping:In a medium-size bowl,combine flour and brown sugar.With two knives, cut butter until mixture coarse crumbs.Stir in pecans;set aside.
3.Muffins:In large bowl,whisk together flour,baking soda,cinnamon,nutmeg and salt.
4.In a medium-size bowl,whisk together bananas,milk,both sugars and butter until well blended.Make a well in the flour mixture.Stir until ingredientsare just moistened.
5.Spoon 2/3 cup batter into each muffin cup;evenly crumble Topping over each.Bake at 375 degrees for 30 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.Remove pan to cool for 5 minutes.With metal spatula,release muffins;remove from rack  to cool.
