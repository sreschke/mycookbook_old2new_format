5 lb. small boiled potatoes, cold, sliced
1/2 C oil
1/2 C vinegar
1/4 C sugar
1 tsp salt
2 TB onion, chopped fine
2 celery stalks, diced
1/2 tsp mustard

Mix together.  Best served at room temperature same evening when made in morning.  Can keep at room temperature another day.  Safe for picnics without mayonnaise.
