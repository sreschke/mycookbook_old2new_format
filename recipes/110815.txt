32 large chicken wings 
2 1/2 ounces butter, melted 
3 1/2 tablespoons hot sauce 

Place the chicken wings on a cookie tray and place in a 425 degree oven. Bake for 50 minutes. (A bit longer for more well done).

Place melted butter and hot sauce in a large bowl, toss the wings in the sauce until evenly and well coated.
