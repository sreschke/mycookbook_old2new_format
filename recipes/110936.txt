1 lb ground beef
1 26 oz jar spaghetti sauce
1 15 oz can tomato sauce
1 24 oz cottage cheese
1/4 C parmesan cheese
1 1/2 t dried basil
1 egg
1 pkg lasagna noodles
2-4 C shredded mozzarella cheese

In fry pan, brown the ground beef, drain and set aside.
Combine cottage cheese, parmesan cheese, basil and egg; mix well.
Spray 9x13 or lasagna pan with cooking spray or grease pan.
Spread 1 1/2 C tomato sauce/meat mixture in bottom of pan.
Cover with 1 layer of uncooked lasagna noodles.
Top noodles with 1/2 of the cottage cheese mixture.
Sprinkle with 1/2 mozzarella cheese (1-2 Cups).
Then, 1 more cup (or so) of the tomato sauce mixture, noodles, rest of the cottage cheese, remainder of the mozzarella cheese, finish with the remainder of the tomato sauce mixture.
Cover pan with foil, shiny side down.
Bake at 350 degrees, 60-70 minutes.
Let stand 10 minutes to set the tomatos and noodles.
Enjoy!
