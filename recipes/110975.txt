  
  
3 tbsp (45 mL) butter

1 1/2 to 2 cups (375 to 500 mL) finely diced vegetables (zucchini, peppers, onions, mushrooms, tomatoes, etc.) 

8 eggs, beaten

2 tbsp (30 mL) chopped fresh herbs, your choice

Salt and freshly ground pepper, to taste



6 large, thin crˆpes, homemade or store-bought

6 to 12 slices ham

11/2 to 2 cups (375 to 500 mL) Medium Canadian Cheddar cheese, shredded


 
 
 
  
  
In a frying pan over medium heat, melt 2 tbsp (30 mL) butter. Add vegetables and cook for 4 or 5 minutes. In a bowl, stir together beaten eggs, herbs, salt and pepper. Melt remaining butter in same pan and cook egg mixture, as an omelette or as scrambled eggs. 



Cover each crˆpe with ham. Divide vegetables and eggs among crˆpes, placing on one edge of each crˆpe. Sprinkle with Medium Canadian Cheddar cheese and roll up. Place in an ovenproof dish lined with parchment paper. Cover and refrigerate. 



At serving time, preheat oven to 350øF (180øC). Cover stuffed crˆpes and reheat for 12 to 15 minutes. Serve with your favourite fresh fruit. 



Variations: 

Replace fresh herbs with dried herbs, using about 1/3 as much. 

For a flavour burst, replace Medium Canadian Cheddar with Old Canadian Cheddar.   
  

To keep crˆpes soft, brush lightly with water before reheating.
