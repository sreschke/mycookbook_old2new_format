Add protein to this dish with diced chicken, turkey, or tofu. 

Serving: 4
Prep Time: 10 minutes minutes
Cook Time: 5 minutes
Total Time: 15 minutes

1/4 cup honey 
1/4 cup prepared stir-fry sauce 
1/4 to 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flake 
4 tsps vegetable oil 
2 cups small broccoli floret 
2 cups mushroom 
1 small onion, cut into wedges and separated 
1 medium carrot, cut diagonally 

1. In small bowl, combine honey, stir fry sauce and crushed red pepper flakes; set aside. 

2. In wok or large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat; add vegetables and toss while cooking, about 2-3 minutes. 

3. Add honey sauce, stir until all vegetables are glazed and sauce is bubbly hot, about 1 minute. 

4. Serve as a vegetable side dish or over steamed rice or noodles for a main dish. 

5. Makes 4 servings. 

6. Substitution tip: Peanut oil may be substituted for vegetable oil.

Based on individual serving.

Calories: 168
Total Fat: 4 g

Protein: 2 g
