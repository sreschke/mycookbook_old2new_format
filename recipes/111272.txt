WRITING CHOCOLATE

1 CUP CHOCOLATE CHIPS
2 TEASPOONS CANOLA OIL

     In a small bowl or measuring cup, combine the chocolate chips and the oil.  Heat in the microwave on high for 3 minutes.

GANACHE

3 TABLESPOONS CORN SYRUP
6 OUNCES HEAVY CREAM
12 OUNCES DARK CHOCOLATE, CHOPPED INTO SMALL PIECES
1/2 TEASPOON VANILLA EXTRACT

     In a small saucepan, combine the corn syrup and heavy cream.  Bring to a simmer and addd the chocolate.  Stir until smooth.  Remove from the heat and add the vanilla extract.
