1pound ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup chopped green pepper
1 can (28 oz. ) tomatoes
1 ( 8 oz. ) can tomato sauce
2 tsp. chili powder
1 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. cayenne pepper
1/8 tsp. paprika
1 (15 oz. ) can drained kidney beans
In large skillet, cook and stir meat, onion and green pepper until meat is brown and onion is tender. Drain off fat. Stir in chopped tomatoes with liquid and remaining  ingredients . cook for 20 minutes then add beans, cook till heated through.
