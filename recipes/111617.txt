1 (16oz) can cut green beans
1 (16oz) can cut yellow wax beans
1 (16 oz) can red kidney beans
1 (16 oz) can garbanzo beans
1/4 cup chopped green pepper
1 med. onion thinley sliced
1/2 cup salad oil
1/3 cup sugar
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. pepper

Rinse beans well; drain.  combine beans, green pepper and onion.  Combine remaining ingredients; add to bean mixture.  Toss well and chill overnight.  Store leftovers in refrigerator.  
Yield 10-12 servings.
