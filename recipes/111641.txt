
                        Beef Bourguignon, **Best**

Recipe By     : Best Recipes From The Backs Of Boxes, Bottles, Cans, &
Jars
Serving Size  : 4    Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : Beef

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
     3/4  Pound         Mushroom -- Sliced
     1/4  Cup           Butter
   4      Slices        Bacon -- Chopped
   2      Pounds        Boneless Beef -- In 2" Cubes
   2      Tablespoons   Flour
   2      Cloves        Garlic -- Crushed
   6      Ounces        Tomato Paste -- Divided
   1 1/4  Cups          Dry Red Wine -- Or More
   2                    Beef Bouillon Cubes
   2      Tablespoons   Sugar
     1/4  Teaspoon      Salt
     1/4  Teaspoon      Thyme
   1      Small         Bay Leaf
   4                    Peppercorns
     1/2  Pound         Onions -- Quartered And Sliced

In large pot, saute mushrooms in butter; remove mushrooms and set aside.

 Fry bacon until crisp; remove and set aside. Add meat to drippings and
brown well.

Blend in flour. Add garlic, 2 tablespoons tomato paste, wine, and
seasonings. Cover and simmer for 2 hours, stirring occasionally. Add
onions, mushrooms, and bacon; simmer 1 hour longer. Add additional wine
if liquid has evaporated as well as the remaining tomato paste.  .
Garnish with cherry tomatoes and serve over rice if desired.
