2 cans mixed nuts
2 cans peanuts
1 pkg pretzels
1 pkg cheese nips
1 pkg Cheerios
1 pkg Crispix
 
Mix above together in large roasting pan and drizzle the following on top and toss:
1 lb melted butter
2 1/2 t. seasoned salt
2 1/2 t. celery salt
1 3/4 t. garlic salt
1 1/2 t. onion powder

Put in over at 200 degrees, stirring every 30 minutes.  Bake for 2 hours.
