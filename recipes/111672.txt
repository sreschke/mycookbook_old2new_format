1 cup catsup
1/2 cup vinegar
1 cup sugar
6 T. soy sauce
1 tsp. prepared mustard
1 tsp. garlic salt

Mix all ingredients and pour over cut up chicken in dripper pan.  Bake, covered, at 250 for 3 hours.  Put in some baked potatoes if desired.  Or serve with rice pilaf.

Enough sauce for 2 dripper pans of chicken pieces.
