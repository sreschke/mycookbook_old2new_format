Pecan Pie
3 eggs, slightly beaten
1 cup white sugar
1 cup white Karo
1Gbs flour
2 Tbs melted butter
1tsp vanilla 1 cup chopped pecans

Mix all ingredients and bake in an unbaked pie shell @ 350 for about 45 minutes or until done, which is with a slightly jiggly 2 inch wide spot in the center. Pie will continue to cook after it is removed from the oven.
