Preheat oven to 450. Combine 2 cups bisquick, 2/3 cup buttermilk and 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese with a wooden spoon until a soft dough forms. Beat vigorously 30 sec. drop by heaping tablespoon onto an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 8-10 mins. Or until golden brown.

Combine 1/4 cup butter or margerine, melted, and 1/2 tsp. garlic powder; brush over warm biscuits befor removing from cookie sheet. Serve hot. Makes 8-10 biscuits.
