1 lb ground turkey or beef
1/8 tsp fresh shredded ginger
2 green onion, chopped
1/2 cup finely chopped cabbage
1 tsp cornstarch
1 1/2 tsp soy sauce
1 pkg wrappers
1 can chicken broth

DIPPING SAUCE
1/4 cup rice vinegar
2 Tbs soy sauce
1 Tbs fresh shredded ginger
1/2 tsp hot pepper sauce
1/4 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp sesame oil

Mix 1st 6 ingredients. Place large spoonful of uncooked mixture in a wrapper and fold,crimping edges (moisten edges to help seal)
Pour about 1 Tbs of oil in a large skillet. Place about 5 pot stickers in pan and cook 'til lightly browned (about 5 mins) Don't turn. Pour about 1/3 cup broth in the pan and cover. Lower heat and cook about 10 mins until broth is absorbed. Remove potstickers and place them in a shallow dish. Cover loosely and keep warm while cooking the rest of the potstickers. Serve warm with dipping sauce.
