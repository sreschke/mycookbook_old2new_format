3 cups all purpose flour 
1 1/2 cups warm milk
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1tsp oil
 Vegetable oil for deep frying
Stir together flour,baking powder,and salt in a large bowl. Knead until smooth on a lightly floured surface,Rub a little oil over the dough . Cover, let sit 30min. Pull off 10=12 equal pieces ,pat or roll ouy to form 8 in circles . Heat 2 inches of oil in Dutch oven,or deep fat fryer to 380 degrees .Drop 1-2 pieces of dough at a time ,gently keeping them immersed with tongs until golden brown,about 2 min on each side, remove with slotted spoon,drain on paper towels. Keep warm in 200 degreee oven 
Cover with powered sugar and honey  or chile and chesse, or like a Taco
