
                         Ingredients: 
                         
                           
                              1/3 cup pitted dates, chopped fine
                              1/2 cup boiling water (1/2 cup or so)
                              1 cup all-purpose flour
                              1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
                              1 teaspoon baking powder
                              3/4 cup shortening
                              1/4 cup white sugar
                              1 teaspoon vanilla extract
                              2 cups cornflakes
                         
                         
                         Directions: 
                        
                         1
                              In small cup, cover chopped dates with boiling water and let soak for 10-15 minutes. Drain liquid and
                              discard. 
                        
                         2
                              Combine flour, cocoa and baking powder and set aside. 
                        
                         3
                              In a large bowl, cream shortening and sugar until light and fluffy. Stir in dates and beat in vanilla
                              extract. Gradually blend in the dry ingredients. Fold in cornflakes. 
                        
                         4
                              Break off pieces of dough (walnut sized). Roll into balls and place 1-1/2 inches apart on ungreased
                              cookie sheet. 
                        
                         5
                              Bake 12-15 minutes or until firm. Cool on wire racks. 
                                                                                    Makes 3 - 4 dozen
