2 lb hamburger
2 cans chili hot beans
1 lb pasta:  wagon wheels, macaroni, etc.
2 cans tomato sauce
1-2 tsp EACH:  cumin, onion salt, garlic powder, chili powder

Brown hamgurger, drain.

Cook pasta, drain.

Add spices, beans, tomato sauce, and hamburger.  Simmer 15 min. over low heat.
