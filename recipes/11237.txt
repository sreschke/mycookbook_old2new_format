 The Adobo is a seasoning for meats, pork, fish and poultry.  Excellent for trying something different.

INGREDIENTS:
      6 CLOVES OF GARLIC
      2 TEASPOON OF SALT
      2 TEASPOON OF OREGANO (GROUND)
      1 TEASPOON OF WHOLE PEPPER (black)

    In a Mortar, blender or food proccesor, put all ingredients.  Make sure everything is well blend. It will become a paste.  Rub in any meat you want. It is better if it is left on for a few hours. Bake, fry or roast. The smell and flavor will be great!
