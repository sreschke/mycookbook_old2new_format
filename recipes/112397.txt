
Make a selection of New World and Old World vegetables from this list of ingredients or roast them all. 

1 small bulb of fennel, sliced into 6 pieces lengthwise
3 small eggplants about 5 inches long, sliced in half lengthwise or 1
medium eggplant cut in 5" x 2" chunks
3 small zucchini, sliced in half lengthwise
3 yellow squash, sliced in half lengthwise
6 large mushrooms, left whole
6 small yellow onions, unpeeled, left whole
6 new potatoes, left whole or halved
12 cloves garlic, peeled
2 red or yellow bell peppers, quartered
1/2 cup olive oil
1/2 cup chopped fresh herbs, mixed (oregano, thyme, chives, parsley)
1 tablespoon Kosher salt
freshly ground pepper

Preheat oven to 375 degrees.  Arrange vegetables in a baking dish, coating them with oil and sprinkling with herbs, salt and pepper.  Place in oven
and bake until tender to the fork, about 40 minutes. Serves 8.
