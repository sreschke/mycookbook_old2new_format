Serves 10

20 slices thin sliced brown bread
60ml milk
1 egg

FILLING
200 g bacon rashers, choped
300 g mushrooms, sliced
1 clove garlic, crushed
2 tbls flour
250 ml reduced fat milk
fresly ground black pepper, to taste

Preheat oven to 170C degres.  Lightly oil patty tins.
Cut bread into 8cm rounds or trim crusts from each slice.  Flatten lightly with a rolling pin.
Beat milk and egg together and brush on both sides of bread.
Place bread in patty tins, pressing down well.  Bake for 15 mins or until firm and golden brown.  Cool.
Gently cook bacon, mushrooms and garlic until soft.  Stir in flour and cook, stirring for 2-3 mins.  Gradually blend in milk.  Continue cooking, stirring until thick.  Add pepper.
Divide the filling into prepared breadcases and place on baking tray.  Bake in preheated oven for 10mins.  Serve hot.
