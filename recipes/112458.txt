8 (4oz.) salmon fillets
1/4 c. peanut oil
1/4 c. soy sauce
1/4 c. balsamic vinegar
1/4 c. green onion, chopped
1 T. brown sugar
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 t. ground ginger
2 t. crushed red pepper flakes
1 t. sesame oil
1/2 t. salt

1. Plaace salmon fillets in a med. glass dish.  In a med. bowl combine peanut oil, soy sauce, vinegar, green onions, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, red pepper flakes, sesame oil, and salt.  Whisk together well and pour over fish.  Cover and marinate in the refrigerator for 4 to 6 hrs. 
2. Preheat grill for high heat and lightly oil rack.
3. Cook the fillets on the prepared grill 5 inches from coals for 10 min. per inch of thickness, measured at the thickest part, or until fish just flakes with a fork, turning fillets halfway through cooking time.  8 Servings
