1 1/2 C SUGAR
2 C WATER
2 C VODKA
2 C BRANDY
2 TEA ALMOND EXTRACT
1/4 TEA VANILLA

In medium saucepan, combine sugar & water.  Bring to a boil and reduce heat & simmer, uncovered, for 10 mins.  Stirring occasionally.  Remove from heat and cool completely.  Stir in Vodka, Brandy, almond extract and vanilla.  Transfer to tightly covered bottles.

Makes 5 1/2 cups.

This recipe came from J.B.'s mother, Linda Henning.
