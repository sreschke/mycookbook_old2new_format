2 lbs. of beef sirloin steak; sliced
1/2 lb. fresh mushrooms; sliced
2 medium onions; thinly sliced
1 clove garlic; minced
1/4 cup butter
1 cup water
2 tsp. instant beef bouillon
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2 cup water
1/4 cup flour
1-1/2 cup sour cream


In skillet, saute mushrooms, onions and garlic in the butter until onions are tender; remove all from skillet and set aside.  Brown beef strips in the same skillet.  Stir in the 1 cup of water, the bouillon, salt and Worcestershire sauce; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer for about 15 minutes.  Stir together the 1/2 cup of water and the 1/4 cup flour; stir it into the beef mixture.  Add the onion/mushroom mixture and bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Before serving add 1-1/2 cups of sour cream to the beef mixture.  Heat, but do not boil.
