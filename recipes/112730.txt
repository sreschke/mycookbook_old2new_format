1 can 8oz tomato sauce
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
2 pounds ground meat/turkey/chicken or mixture
1 small onion finely chopped
1 small carrot shredded
1 cup whole wheat bread crumbs
3/4 cup skim milk
1/4 cup fresh parsley chopped
3/4 tsp salt
2 large eggs slightly beaten
1/4 cup Parmesean Cheese
2 tsp Barbeque sauce
1 tsp brown sugar
Garlic powder to taste or fresh Garlic

Mix tomato sauce and Worcestershire set aside 
Mix remaining ingredients & half cup sauce mixture-make loaf and the rest of the sauce goes on top
BAKE  1 hr 10 minutes
