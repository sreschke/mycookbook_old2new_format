2 pounds lean beef
1  cup dry breadcrumbs
1/2 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
2 eggs
1 tsp. salt 
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. dried oregano
freshly ground pepper
1/4 cup olive oil
In large bowl, Combine all ingredients except oil; blend thoroughlly. Use 1 T of mixture to form each ball. In 12 inch skillet, heat the olive oil and fit only half as many meatballs as skillet  can hold, leaving room to turn balls.Saute until evenly browned oon outside and cooked but still juicy in center. Remove with slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
Makes 60 tto 70 meatballs
