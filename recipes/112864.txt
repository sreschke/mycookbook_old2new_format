1 bunch of chives. (A nice handfull if you are picking.)
Whir in food processor.
Add some very good quality EVOO.
Add the juice of 1/2 a lemon.
Add lemon zest.
Add some nice salt.
Whir till smooth and lovely.

Good as a plate base.
Good on roasted potatoes or other veg.
Good on salad.
Probably good with eggs.
Just lovely really.
