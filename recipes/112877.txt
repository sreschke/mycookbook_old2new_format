1/2 cup sugar
4 tsp cornstarch
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 cup apple cider
1 Tbs lemon juice
2 Tbs butter


-In a small saucepan stir together sugar, cornstarch, and cinnamon. Then stir in apple cider and lemon juice.
-Cook and stir mixture over medium heat until thickened and bubbly.
-Stir in butter until melted

makes about 1 1/3 cups
