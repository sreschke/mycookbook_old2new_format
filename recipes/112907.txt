salt and pepper	
8 jumbo pasta shells	
1 1/2 lbs. of riccotta cheese	
1 lbs. of mozarella, diced	
1/2 cup grated parmigiano 	
1 cup shredded asaigo	
1/4 cup parsley	
2 tblspoons extra virgin olive oil	
3 cloves of garlic, chopped	
1 small onion, finely chopped	
6 or 7 fresh basil leaves, torn	
1 can 28 oz. of crushed tomatoes

Pre-heat oven to 450, bring large pot of water to boil. Add salt to water along with pasta and cook shells for 12-15 min, they should be softened but still undercooked at the center. Drain pasta and cool. Combine riccotta and 1/2 of the aisaigo cheese. Add parsley and stir. Add oil, garlic, onions to a pan and saute for 5 min. Add tomatoes, salt and pepper and let simmer for 5 min. then add basil leaves. Pour small amount of sauce into bottom of a medium caserole dish and fill shells with spoonfulls of the cheese mixture and place seam side down. Top shells with remaining sauce and cheese mixture. Cook 6-8 minutes.
