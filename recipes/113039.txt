1 TBLSPN vegetable oil
1 1/2 lb cubed beef stew meat
4 med. carrots, cut into 1/2 in. slices (2 cups)
3 med. red potatoes, unpeeled, cut into 1/2 inch
  cubes (3 cups)
1 large onion, cut into 1 inch pieces 
  (1 1/2 cups)   
1 med. satlk celery, cut into 1 in. pieces
  (1 cup)
3 c vegetable juice (tomato or V-8)
3 TBLSPNS quick-cooking tapioca
1 TBLSPNS beef flavor instant bouillon
2 teaspns Worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspn pepper

In 12 in. skillet, heat oil over med.-high heat. Add stew beef: cook 4 to 6 min., stirring frequently, until browned on all sides.

In 4-5 quart slow cooker, mix browned beef and all remaining ingrediants.

Cover; cook on low setting 9-10 hours.
