1 top loin pork-chops,4-6 oz each
1/8 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp Italian Herb Seasoning
Dash of salt and pepper
1 can(16-oz) no-salt added tomatoes
4 oz fresh mushrooms,sliced
1/4 c grated low-fat cheese(Swiss is good)

Preheat oven to 375. Trim all visible fat off chops
and place them in roasting pan. Sprinkle with seasonings. Drain juice from tomatoes into pan over 
pork chops. Reserve tomatoes. Bake chops for 30 minutes. Cover chops with mushrooms,tomatoes and cheese. Bake another 30 minutes.

Serves 4-2 1/2 oz meat and vegetables;6g fat;181 cal;54mg chol;3g fiber;213mg sodium
