Tortilla De Banana - (Banana omelette)

1/4 cup milk
3 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley
1/2 teaspoon salt
Dash of cayenne pepper
6 eggs, separated
4 bananas, sliced

Melt butter in skillet and saute bananas about 5 minutes, turning them frequently. Remove the bananas from skillet and set them aside. Beat egg yolks with cayenne pepper, salt, and milk. Beat egg whites until they are stiff and fold the egg yolks and egg whites together. Transfer to a buttered 7 x 10 inch shallow casserole dish and arrange banana slices on top. Bake for 20 minutes in a 350 degrees F. oven until lightly browned. Sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve from the casserole dish. Makes 4 servings.

Note: This recipe came from Colombia, South America.
