Dressing:
2 Tbsps creamy peanut butter
1 small clove garlic, chopped
1/2 tsp grated ginger
1/2 tsp lemon juice
1/2 tsp soy sauce
1/2 tsp honey
1/4 tsp turmeric
hot pepper sauce to taste
@1/2 cup water or enough to make consistency of a thick dressing

Salad:
Use pregrated slaw mixtures.  Can add red peppers, scallions etc.  Also oriental vegetable mixtures ( frozen.  Just defrost what you need in microwave a few seconds and dry well with paper towels.)
