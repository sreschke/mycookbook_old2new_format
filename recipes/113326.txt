 Serves 8 
2 pounds asparagus, trimmed 
2 tablespoons plus 2 teaspoons oriental sesame oil 
1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon rice vinegar 
1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon soy sauce 
1 teaspoon sugar 
Salt and pepper to taste 
Sesame seeds, toasted 
 
Bring a large skillet of water to the boil. Add asparagus and cook until just crisp-tender, about 4 minutes. Drain. Rinse with cold water and drain well. Pat dry with paper towels. 
 
Arrange asparagus on a serving platter. Whisk together the sesame oil, rice vinegar, soy sauce and sugar. Season to taste with salt and pepper. 
 
Spoon dressing over asparagus. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve. 
 
Note: Asparagus and dressing can be prepared 1 day ahead. Cover separately and refrigerate until ready to serve.
