                  Ingredients
2 hard boiled eggs, 1/2 green pepper, 1garlic
bulb(optional)*,1/4 C sugar, 1/2 jar/bottle chili
sauce, 1t salt, 1pt. HEILMAN'S mayonise.

                     Directions
Coarse or finely chop eggs,pepper and garlic*.
Mix all together. After use store in jar in refrig.
