Ingredients:

1 Carrot
2 Sprigs of Parsley
1 Stalk of Celery
2 Small onions sliced
1 Teaspoon Salt 
2 Cups of Water
1/2lb of fresh mushrooms sliced
           or 
2 4oz cans of mushrooms
1 Cup of Water or Mushroom liquid
1 Teaspoon dill weed
1 Teaspoon chopped parsley
1 Tablespoon of flour 
1/4 Cup of cold water 
1/2 Cup Sour Cream or Whipping Cream

Directions:

1. Cook carrot, parsley, celery, 1 onion and salt in 2 cups of water for 20 minutes, strain; discard veggies.
2. Cook Mushrooms and other onion in 1/2 cup water for 8 minutes, add to vegetable broth along with 1/2 cup water, dill and Parsley.
3. Mix flour with 1/4 Cup cold water. Stir into soup.  Bring to soup to a boil, cook and stir for 3 minutes and remove it from the heat.  
4. Beat in sour Cream and serve hot.
                (Makes four Servings)
