Healthy Sole Filet with Almonds  
From: Toutoune, Anjou, Quebec, Canada  
Comments: Excellent, healthy lunch or supper!  
Servings: 4  


Ingredients Preparation 
1 egg, slightly beaten 
1/4 cup [60 mL] low fat milk 
1 1/3 cups [330 mL] whole wheat breadcrumbs 
1 teaspoon [5 mL] oregano or basil 
1/4 teaspoon [1 mL] sea salt 
1/4 teaspoon [1 mL] pepper 
1 pound [454 g] sole or haddock fillets 
2 teaspoons [10 mL] lemon juice 
2 tablespoons [30 mL] water 
1 tablespoon [15 mL] melted margarine or butter 
Minced blanched almonds 
Minced green onions 
 Preheat oven to 425øF [220øC]. 
Into a shallow, large enough to dip fish fillets, bowl, beat together egg and milk. 
Onto a plate, mix together breadcrumbs, oregano or basil, sea salt and pepper. 
Dip fish fillets into milk mixture; then coat with breadcrumbs mixture. 
Lay coated fish fillets side-by-side onto a greased [with a non-stick spray or shortening] baking sheet. 
Into a small bowl, mix together lemon juice, water and melted margarine or butter. 
Pour lemon juice mixture all over fish fillets; sprinkle with minced almonds and green onions [see below], to taste. 
Bake into preheated oven, uncovered, for approximately 10 minutes, until fish flesh is longer translucent. 
If desired, wait after baking to sprinkle fish fillets with green onions.
