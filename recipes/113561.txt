1 bunch broccoli
8 oz Velveeta cheese - cut up into small pieces
6 oz low fat evaporated milk 
2 cans Campbell's cream of potato soup

Boil or steam broccoli until almost done (still a little bit crisp).  Pour off water and save.  Cut up broccoli leaving heads intact.  

Mix soup and milk on low and add pieces of cheese while constantly stirring.  Add broccoli and small amounts of the broccoli water until you have desired consistency.

If you want, you can slice stems and cook them in water until tender.
