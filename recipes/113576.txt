Makes 8 sservings;  Great for a busy day!

1 container (15oz.) ricotta cheese
2 eggs beaten
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1box (16 oz.) ziti pasta, cooked and drained
1 jar pasta Sauce.
1 cup. shredded mozzarella cheese.

combine ricotta cheese, eggs and Parmesan cheese; set aside.

In another bowl, thoroughly combine pasta and pasta sauce.

In slow cooker pour in some pasta sauce to coat the cooker then spoon 1/2 of the pasta mixture; evenly top with ricotta cheese mixture, then remaining pasta mixture.  Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese cook on low  and enjoy your busy day!!

It desired add hamburger or sausage to it or additional pasta sauce. 

This Recipe you can add whatever you like!
