3/4 cup butter (melted)
1/2 cup bread crumbs
2 Tbsp parmesan cheese
2 tsp italian seasoning
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1 tsp garlic
4 chicken breast halves
1/2 cup dry white wine
1/3 cup chopped green onion
1/4 cup chopped parsley

Preaheat oven to 350.  Combine bread crumbs, parmesan, and spices.  Dip chicken in melted butter and coat with bread crumb mixture.  Place in ungreased baking dish and bake 45-50 minutes or until done and tender.  Add green onion, wine and parsley to remaining butter.  When chicken is done, pour butter mixutre over chicken in pan and return to oven for about 5 minutes or until sauce is hot.
