3/4 c sugar
1/3 c olive oil
2 eggs (or egg substitute)
1 1/2 c mashed ripe banana
1/3 c water (scant)
1 2/3 c flour
1 t baking soda
1/2 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
1/2 c chopped walnuts
2 T wheat germ

Heat oven to 350 degrees. Grease 9 x 5 loaf pan or 4 - mini loaf pans. Combine all wet ingredients and mix well with whisk. Stir in dry ingredients and combine until moistened. Stir in nuts and wheat germ. Pour into prepared pans and bake until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean, about 55 - 65 mins. For mini pans check after 25 mins. Remove from oven and let cool for 5 mins. before loosening from sides and pan and turning out. Let cool on wire rack completely before slicing.
