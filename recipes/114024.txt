2 1/2 - 3 lbs chicken
2 cups chicken broth
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 stick melted margarine
1 cup Bisquick
1 cup buttermilk
1/4 t pepper
1/2 t salt
1/2 - 1 bag of mixed vegetables (optional) 

Cut cooked chicken (a rotisserie chicken is great) into small chunks and place in a casserole dish. Put vegetables on top of the chicken.

Mix broth and soup in a saucepan and bring to a boil. Pour over chicken, and vegetables. 

In another bowl, combine margarine, pepper, salt and bisquick. Add buttermilk. Mix thoroughly to form batter. Pour over chicken, broth, and vegetable mixture.

Bake at 425oF. for 25-30 minutes.
