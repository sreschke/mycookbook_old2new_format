2 - 3 lbs pork tenderloins
Rub: 1 T each dried thyme, garlic powder and cumin
2 T chili powder
1.5 t salt
1 t fresh ground pepper
Season tenderloins with the rub and grill over moderate flame until internal temperature reaches 160 degrees (about 20 mins)

Tomato relish:
2 T olive oil
1 green pepper, chopped
1 med onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 T rosemary, chopped
3 med ripe tomatoes, or 1 - 14oz can, chopped
2 T red wine vinegar
1 T sugar (or to taste)
Salt & pepper
Saute pepper and onion in oil until tender. Add remaining ingredients and simmer until liquid is reduced.

Arrange sliced pork on a platter and surround with the tomato relish. Serves 6 - 8
