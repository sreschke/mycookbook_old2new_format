4 Chicken Breasts

Preheat oven to 350.  Place chicken in large baking dish add about 2 inches of water.  Pour olive oil on the chicken breasts and  sprinkle with Italian Seasonings and minced garlic.  Cook in the oven for 1 1/2 to 2 hours until done.  Debone and shred chicken and set aside.  Strain broth, keeping 1 cup of broth.  You may want to freeze the remaining for other recipes.

1 Can of Cream of Chicken Soup
1 cup of Chicken Broth
Shredded Chicken

Combine above ingrediants and pour into a 9 X 12 baking dish.  If you perfer a thicker crust, place in 9 X 9 dish and reduce heat and add to cooking time-usually about 90 minutes.

1 Cup of Milk
1 Cup of Self Rising Flour
1 stick of melted margarine

Whisk together the above ingrediants and pour over chicken mixture.  Place in preheated 350 oven for 60 to 90 minutes until crust in golden brown.
