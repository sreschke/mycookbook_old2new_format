2 lb hamburger
3 stalks celery
1 can tomato soup
1/2 can water
2 tsp chili powder
4-5 dried chilies (seeds only)
8-10 slices processed cheese
chopped onion
chopped tomato

In large skillet, brown hamburger slightly.  Add chopped onions and celery and continue cooking until meat no longer pink. Addchili pepper seeds and chili powder, tomato soup, and tomatoes; simmer for 30 minutes. Pour the entire skillet full over cooked shell macaroni.  Stir well.  Top with cheese slices and bake in 350F oven until cheese melted.
