1 small cucumber
1 crushed garlic clove
1 cup yoghurt
2 tsps lemon juice
1tsp fresh mint
salt to taste
Peel cucumberthinly, grate and place in strainer to drain off exdess liquid
Blend grated cucumber, garlic into yoghurt, add lemon juice and mint
salt to taste
Serve with fish or fried zucchini and eggplant
