Tumeric gives this simple rice dish its bright yellow color.   

3 cups water
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon ground tumeric
1 cup frozen corn kernels, thawed
1-1/2 cups long-grain rice

Combine water, salt, cumin, cinnamon, and tumeric in medium-size saucepan.  Bring to a boil.  Add corn.  Reheat to boiling.  Stir in rice.  Cover;
reduce heat to simmer.  Simmer for 18 minutes. Remove from heat and let stand covered for 5 minutes.
