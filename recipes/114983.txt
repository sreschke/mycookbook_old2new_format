Preheat Oven: Med-high

Parmesan cheese
Butter-flavored Pam 
chicken
avacado
or just about anything SB-friendly in your fridge

In a small skillet, with heat on med-high, spray butter flavored Pam generously. Sprinkle a generous amount of Parmesan cheese to layer the bottom. 

As it is cooking, top the cheese with chicken, veggies, or just about anything...shrimp is good... 

Cook until the cheese bottom is a dark golden brown. 

With spatula, fold in half like a taco.

Note: cheese has a salty flavor, so do not add any salty ingredients.
