From Oct 2006 Gourmet Magazine
Serves 6
20 min. active, 45 min. start to finish

3 tbsp. unsalted butter
3/4 c. thinly sliced shallots (2 large)
1 tsp. chili powder
3/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. black pepper
2 lb. carrots, cut diagonally into 3-in pieces
1/3 c. pure maple syrup
1 c. dry red wine
2 tsp. cider vinegar
2 tbsp. chopped fresh dill

1. Heat butter in a 12-in heavy skillet over moderate heat until foam subsides, then cook shallots, stirring occasionally, until softened, about 4 minutes.

2. Add chili powder, salt, and pepper and cook, stirring, until chili powder is very fragrant, about 1 minute.

3. Add carrots, maple syrup, and wine and simmer, covered, stirring occasionally, until carrots are very tender, about 20 minutes.

4. Add vinegar and boil, uncovered, until liquid is reduced to a glaze, 3 to 5 minutes.

5. Remove from heat and stir in dill.
