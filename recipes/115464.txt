1 1/2 lbs bonelss,skinless chicken breasts
1 med onion,sliced
1 green bell pepper,sliced
1 can(4-oz)sliced mushroooms,drained
1/2 tsp salt(opt)
1/8 tsp pepper
1 can sugar-free orange soda pop

Preheat oven to 350. Arrange chicken in 3-quart 
casserole dish and top with remaining ingredients.
Cover and bake for 35-45 minutes,or until chicken in tender. Uncover and bake 10 more minutes.

Serves 6;160 cal
