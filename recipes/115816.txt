8 country style boneless pork ribs
4 tblsp. sugar
2 tblsp. salt (you might want to cut back on salt)
4 cloves garlic, finely chopped (I used garlic oil)
1 cup catsup
4 tblsp. Hoison sauce
2 tblsp. dry white wine (I used cooking sherry)

Mix ingredients in bowl, set aside a half cup of sauce, marinade ribs in bowl covered with plastic overnight turning several times.  Place on broiler rack in single layer, brush with sauce, bake at 400 degrees for half hour, turn, brush with sauce and bake a half hour more or til done.
