1 1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup cocoa
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup butter, softened
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
10 oz jar maraschino cherries
6 oz chocolate chips
1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk

Stir together flour, cocoa, salt, baking powder and baking soda.  In mixer bowl beat together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.  Add egg and vanilla; beat well to combine.  Gradually add dry ingredients and beat until well blended.  Shape dough into 1" balls and place on ungreased cookie sheet.  Press down center of dough with thumb.  Drain cherries, reserving juice.  Place a cherry in the center of each cookie.  In a small saucepan combine chocolate chips and condensed milk;  heat until chocolate is melted.  Stir in 4 tsp of cherry juice.  Spoon about 1 tsp frosting over each cherry, spreading to completely cover.  Bake at 350 for 10 minutes.  Cool on wire rack.
