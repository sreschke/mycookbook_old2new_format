4 tablespoons margarine
1 1/3 cup sugar 
1 cup milk 
1 egg, beaten 
1 1/3 cups flour 
2 teaspoons baking powder 
3/4 teaspoon cinnamon 
3/4 teaspoon nutmeg 
1/4 teaspoon salt 
1/2 teaspoon vanilla 
1+ cups blackberries 

Preheat oven to 325F. Grease 12 muffin tins heavily (or use liners). Cream margarine and sugar together. Add egg, baking powder, spices, salt, vanilla, and half the flour, mix well. Gently fold in remaining flour. Divide batter evenly among muffin tins, and place a few blackberries on the top of each. Bake 20-30 minutes.  Let cool 5-10 minutes before removing from tins.  

Makes 1 dozen
