
1 lg. or 2 med. cabbage, shredded
1 1/2 lbs. loose fresh sausage
5 or 6 potatoes, diced
Salt & pepper to taste 
 

Brown Susage in a large pan,
add 1 large cabbage shredded or chopped.
Add 5 potatoes diced, and add your pepper and salt.
Add about 1/3 cup of water, and Simmer.
