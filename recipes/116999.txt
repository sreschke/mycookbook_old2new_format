1/2 c. butter
1 c. water
1 c. flour
4 eggs
1-2 cartons Cool & Creamy pudding
1 can Betty Crocker chocolate frosting

Bring butter & water to a boil. Add flour all at once. Stir until it leaves sides of pan. Let cool 1 minute. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each egg. Drop by Tbs. onto greased cookie sheet, 2" apart.

Bake at 450 for 20 minutes, then at 325 for 20 minutes. Cool. 

Cut off tops, remove membranes & fill with pudding. Replace tops and frost with chocolate frosting. Refrigerate.
