1/4 cup butter
1 bottle (8 oz) commercial Italian dressing
juice of 2 lemons
1/4 tsp freshly gound black pepper
3 lbs large shrimp, peeled with tails intact

Melt butter in a 13 X 9 X 2 inch baking dish.

Add dressing, lemon juice and pepper.

Add shrimp to sauce, mixing well.

Bake at 325 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes, stirring several times.

Serves 6 to 8.
