1 (7oz.)can solid white tuna
1/2 cup minced onion
1/2 cup minced celery
1/4 cup minced greep pepper
6 slices French bread, 1/2 inch think
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/2 lb. Cheddar cheese, diced
4 eggs, slightly beaten
1 cup milk
1 (8oz.) can Hunt's Tomato Sauce with Cheese
1/2 tsp. seasoned salt. 

Drain oil from tuna into small skillet.  Add onion, celery and green pepper and saute until onion is soft.  Add tuna and toss to mix; set aside.  Spread bread slices with mayonnaise, cut into small cubes.  In a buttered 1 1/2 quart casserole, arrange layers of half the bread cubes, half the tuna mixture and half the cheese.  Combine eggs in a bowl with milk; stir in Hunt's sauce graduated and added seaoned salt, mix well.  Pour slowly over casserole, pressing gently to immerse all ingredients; let stand 10 minutes.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hours until golden brown and puffed on top.  Serves 6.
