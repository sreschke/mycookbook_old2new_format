2 eggs beaten,
2 lbs ground beef,
1 package Stove Top stuffing mix for chicken,
1 cup water,
1/2 cup Kraft original barbcue sauce, divided.

Place beef, stuffing mix, water and eggs and 1/4 cup barbecue sauce in large bowl.  Mix.

Shape meat mixture into an oval loaf in a 13-9 inch baking dish.  Top meatloaf with remaining 1/4 cup barbecue sauce.  

Bake 1 Hour at 375.  Useing a meat theomoter at 160 degrees
