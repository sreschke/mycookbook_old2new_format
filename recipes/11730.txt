From grandma Lathan as taught to Debbie Cole
from deep in the Missouri  ozarks

1 1/2 cups corn meal/ fresh ground as possible
1/2 cups flour
1/2 t soda
1/2 t baking powder
1 t salt
1/2 cup sugar
 Mix all dry ingredients  /set aside

mix following 
1 cup cream style corn
1 medium onion,chopped
2 jalapeno peppers,or (1 can green chillis peppers
1/2 pound (8oz) cheddar cheese,shreded
1/2 cup oil
2 beaten eggs
1/2 cup buttermilk
 combine above with the dry ingredients until
moist /pour into a preheated cast iron skillet that 
has been coated with oil(bacon)
 bake in preheated 450 oven for 25-35 minute 
till crust is browned/and  knife  can be inserted
and come out clean. invert pan over cooling rack
pop corn bread from pan. allow to cool on rack
out of pan to prevent sweating and to form dry crispy crust/serve hot. great with a pot of beans
