Batter
1 Cup flour
1 t. salt
1 t oregano
1/8 t pepper
2 eggs
2/3 cups milk

Topping
1 1/2 lb. ground beef or sausage browned and drained well.
1 cup thinly sliced pepperoni
1 cup chopped onions
1 4 oz can mushroom stems and pieces drained
1 cup pizza sauce or make your own (8 oz tomatoes sauce 1-2 t. oregano and 1/4 t pepper)
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

1. Lightly grease and flour a 12 or 14" pizza pan or a cookie sheet
2.  Prepare batter by mixing until smooth, pour batter into pan, tilt pan so batter covers bottom evenly.
3. Arrange hamburger, onion and mushrooms on top, bake on low rack of oven at 425 degrees for 25-30 minutes
4.  Remove from oven, drizzle with pizza sauce and sprinkle with cheese.  Return to oven for 10-15 minutes longer.
