serves 8
3 c. oatmeal
1 qt. low fat buttermilk (or skim milk soured with 1 T. lemon juice per cup
1 c. flour
2 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. Salt
Egg substitute to equal four eggs
Directions:
Combine. The batter will be rather thin. Cook on hot griddle (spray if desired) until golden brown.
