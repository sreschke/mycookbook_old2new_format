1/2 C reduced-fat margarine (70% vegetable oil spread), softened.
1 c all-purpose flour
1/2 c confectioners sugar
1/2 c chopped walnuts. 

FIRST LAYER
     1 pkg. (8 oz. fat- free cream cheese, softened
      1 c (8 Oz) nonfat sour cream
       1 carton (8 oz) frozen light whipped topping,         thawed.

SECOND LAYER
        3 c cold skim milk
         2 pkgs. (1 oz. each) sugar-free instant                    pistachio-pudding mix.

TOPPING
          1 carton (8 oz)  frozen light whipped            topping, thawed  
           2 tab. ground waltnuts

In a mixing bowl, cream the margarine. Add flour and sugar, blend until crumbly. Stir in walnuts.  Press onto the bottom of a 13 x9x2 in baking dish coated with nonstick cooking spray.  Bake at 375 for 10-12 min or until set.  Cool,  In a  mixing bowl beat cream cheese and sour cream.  Fold in whipped topping. Spread over the crust. In another mixing bowl, combine milk and pudding mixes, beat on low  speed for 2 minutes.  Spread over first layer.  Carefull spread whipped topping over second layer.  Sprinkle with walnuts.  Chill at least 1 hour.
1 Serving equals 153 calories.
