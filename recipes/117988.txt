2 Basil leaves(torn)
2 egg whites
1/4 cup fresh mozzerella cheese(torn)
2 slices of bread
Butter

Butter the ends of the bread and put basil and and cheese inside. Cook until the bottam is slightly browned and flip over. To the same to this side.

Cook egg in the same pan. Chop up when you flip it. 

When the egg is done, put it inside the sandwich.
