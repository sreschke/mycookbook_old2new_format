1/2 cup shortening
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg
1 cup mincemeat
2 cup self rising flour

Cream shortening & sugar until fluffy.

Add egg & beat well. 
Add mincemeat, mixing well. 
Combine flour. 
Mix well.

Drop by teaspoonful onto greased cookie sheet.

Bake @ 350* for 18-20 minutes.

(5 dozen)
