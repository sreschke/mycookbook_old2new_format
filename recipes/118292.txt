1 can of kraut
1 large onion sliced and seperated
3 or 4 tablespoons brown sugar
2 cups tomato juice

Mix and put in baking dish.

2 lbs. hamburger
3/4 cup rice
  salt and pepper
1 small onion,chopped fine

Make meatballs the size of a walnut.  Place them all over the top of your kraut and cover.
Bake in 350* oven for an hour.
Wrap a couple small baking potatoes in foil and bake in same oven another hour.
