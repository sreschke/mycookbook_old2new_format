
Serves six.

8 ounces Emmentaler cheese, shredded
8 ounces Gruyere cheese, shredded
2 tablespoons flour
1 clove garlic, cut in half
2 cups white wine
1 ounce kirsch (optional)
Freshly ground black pepper to taste
Dash nutmeg
1 pound French bread, cut into cubes

Place cheese in a large food storage bag. Sprinkle with flour and toss to coat. Rub the inside of the fondue pot with the cut edge of the garlic. Let sit a few minutes. Add wine and bring to a boil. Slowly add cheese to the wine, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon. Stir in kirsch, if desired. Add pepper and nutmeg. Stir to blend well. Serve immediately. Place pot on holder with burner and adjust flame. Dip and swirl bread in cheese.
