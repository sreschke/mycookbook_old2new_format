4 med red potatoes, tinly sliced
1 small onion, thinly sliced & separated into rings
3 T butter, melted
1/4 cup grated Parm cheese
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1/8 tsp garlic powder

Place 1/2 of the potaoes in greased 2 qt baking dish.  Top with onion and remaining potatoes, drizzle with butter.  Sprinkle with cheese, salt, pepper and garlic powder.  Bake, uncoveres at 450 degrees for 25-30 minutes or until potatoes are golden brown and tender.

Serves 4
