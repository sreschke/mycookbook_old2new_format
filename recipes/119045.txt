2 cups rhubarb,cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces 
2 cups sliced fresh strawberries 
1 1/2 cups sugar 
3 1/2 Tbsp instant tapioca 
1 pinch salt 
1 1/2 tsp ground nutmeg 
 
Preheat oven to 425 degrees. 
Mix the rhubarb, strawberries, sugar, tapioca, salt and nutmeg until the fruit is well coated. Pour filling into the crust and dot with butter. Cover with top crust, seal edges and cut three 1 inch slits in top of crust. 

Bake pie at 425 for 20 minutes. Reduce temperature to 375 and bake for an additional 20 minutes. Let pie cool before cutting.
