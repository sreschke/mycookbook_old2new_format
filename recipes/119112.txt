2 cups milk
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 Tbsp melted butter
1/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1 cup rolled oats
1 cup chopped apple  (FINE!!!)

1/2 cup raisins and 1/2 cup chopped walnuts if desired.

Grease the inside of the crockpot.  Mix ingredients.  Cover.  Low speed through the night.  Serve with milk.
