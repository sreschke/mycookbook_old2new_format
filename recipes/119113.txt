Grease 9x13 pan
1st. layer: 8 slices of bread, cubed
2nd layer: 2c. sharp cheese, grated
3rd layer: 1 lb. of bacon, browned and crumbled
or sausage cooked.
mix 4 eggs
2 c. milk
3/4 tsp. perpared mustard
salt and pepper
beat slightly and pour over the 3 layers. refrigerate over night.
next day:
mix one can of cream of mushroom soup and 1 1/2 cup milk, pour all over and bake uncovered 1 1/2 hours at 300 degrees.
