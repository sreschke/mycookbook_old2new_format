1 whole chicken (cut into pieces)
3 tbsp. chopped onion
2 tbsp. soy sauce
1 tbsp. minced ginger
1 tsp. minced garlic
2 tbsp. honey
1/4 cup thinly sliced green onions

In small bowl combine the onion, soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and honey.  Mix together and spoon over chicken and marinate in covered dish for one hour in fridge, turning pieces once.  Preheat oven to 425 degrees, bake chicken for 30 minutes.  Turn pieces over and top with green onions, then continue baking for 15 minutes, or until done.
