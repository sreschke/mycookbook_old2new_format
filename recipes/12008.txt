Makes 8 servings

Use frozen pie crust.  

Filling
2 chicken breasts
4 large eggs
1 « cups light cream
1/4 teaspoon dried thyme
1/8 teaspoon white pepper
« cup shredded white Cheddar cheese (about 2 ounces)
1.  Cook chicken in a medium skillet.
2.  In a small bowl, whisk together the eggs, cream, thyme, and pepper.  Pour into crust.  Cut chicken into bite size pieces.  Sprinkle the egg mixture with the chicken, and cheddar cheese.  Bake until golden and custard is set, about 30 minutes.  Serve warm.
