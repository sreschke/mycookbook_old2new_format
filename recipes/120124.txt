Nonstick cookig spray or melted butter
4 eggs
1 cup milk
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1 medium Jonathan or McIntosh apple,peeled and shredded
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1 loaf  French bread,cut into 12(1 inch)cut into12(1 inch)slices
1/2 cup chopped pecans
2 tbs melted butter
Maple syrup
1.Coat the bottom of a 13-by 9-inch with nonstick cooking spray or brush with melted butter.
2.Combine eggs,milk,sugar,cinnamon,apples and vanilla.Pour egg mixture over bread.Turn bread over to coat other side.Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate overnight
3.Preheat oven to 425 F.Sprinkle pecans over top of bread slices and drizze with melted butter.Bake 20 to 25 minutes or intil puffy.Serve with maple syrup.Serves 4 to 6
