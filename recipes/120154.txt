Grilled Chicken Fahitas

chicken breast, chopped, (any amount)
mushrooms, chopped (any amount)
onions, chopped (any amount)
green peppers, chopped (any amount)
cheese, shredded, (any amount)
tortillas (any amount)
salsa (any amount)

Grill chopped up chicken breast. (Outside grill is best or sautee in pan.) Cook till almost done inside.) Then add chopped mushrooms, onions, and green peppers. Cook till little brown. Heat tortillas. Add shredded cheese. Fill tortillas with everything. Eat with salsa. 

Recipe Source: Sheila Bratcher
