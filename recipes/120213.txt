1 lb boneless pork shoulder
1 Tbs cooking oil
1 lb tiny new potatoes,quartered
1 cup chopped onions
2 fresh poblano peppers,seeded and cut into 1 inch pieces
1 fresh jalapeno pepper,seeded and chopped
4 cloves garlic,minced
2 inch stick cinnamon
3 cups canned chicken broth
1(141/2 -ounce can diced tomatoes,undrained
1 tbs chili powder
1 tsp dried oregano, crushed
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1/4 cup snipped fresh cilantro or parsley
Hot cooked basmati rice or long grain rice
Green onion strips(optional)
1.Trim fat from pork.Cut pork into 1 inch cubes.Brown pork ,half at a time,in hot oil in a large skillet.Drain off fat.Combine potatoes,onions,poblano peppers,jalapeno pepper,garlic,and stick cinnamon in a 3 1/2 or 4 -quart slow cooker.Add pork.Combine broth,tomatoes,chili powder,oregano and black pepper;pour broth mixture over all.
2.Cover;cook on low heat setting for 8 to 10 hours or on high heat setting for 4 to 5b hours
3.To serve,discard stick cinnamon.Stir in cilantro or parsley.Serve stew over cooked rice and if desired garnish with green onion strips.Makes 6 servings
