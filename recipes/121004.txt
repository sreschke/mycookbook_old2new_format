2 cans whole corn (drained)
1 tbs granulated sugar
2 eggs
1 tbs butter or margarine
1 tsp salt
4 tbs self rising flour
2 cups milk (I use skim)
In a medium mixing bowl beat eggs, add sugar, salt, and flour, beat until creamy. Add milk. Fold in corn and pour into a cassorole dish sprayed with pam.  Melt butter and pour on top.  Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour, stirring every 10 minutes for the  first 30 minutes.  Uncover the last 20 minutes to brown.
