Prep Time: 30 minutes

Cook Time: 30 minutes

Ingredient List:
turkey broth and giblets (if leaving out giblets, you can use chicken broth instead)
2-3 bags of stuffing crumbs (Pepperidge Farms, Orowheat, or Gibson all are fine)
1 stalk of celery
1 bunch of scallions
1 small white onion
4 tbsp butter
1 green bell pepper
1/8 lb dried cranberry
1/4 cup raisins (optional)
1 cup white wine
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 apple (optional - any kind)
seasoning salt

Directions:
1) If using giblets, take this step the night before-- Combine the neck, liver, and heart (not the tail) taken from the turkey in a small saucepan with 4 cups of water, 1 clove of garlic. Bring to a boil and simmer for 1 hour and 30 minutes. The next day, separate giblets from turkey broth, remove nerves and fat and chop up.

2) Chop scallions, celery, 1/4 onion, and bell pepper. Heat large skillet with butter, add vegetables and  and seasoning salt and cook for 5-10 minutes.

3) Chop apple and add to vegetables along with the chopped giblets at the last minute.

4) Warm up turkey broth

5) In a large bowl, mix stuffing with grilled vegetables, cranberries, raisins, wine, and brown sugar. Add turkey (or chicken) broth until moist.

6) Bake in a 350 degree oven for 30-45 minutes.
