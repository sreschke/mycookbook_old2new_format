Ingredients:
4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves (about 1 1/4 lbs.)
1 egg, slightly beaten
3/4 cup Italian seasoned dry bread crumbs
2 oz thinly sliced prosciutto
26 oz jar pasta sauce
4 oz fresh mozzarella, thinly sliced
8 oz spaghetti, cooked and drained

Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  Dip chicken in egg, then bread crumbs, coating well.  In 13 x 9 baking dish arrange chicken.
Bake uncovered 20 mins.  Top chicken with prosciutto, then 1 1/2 cups pasta sauce.  Top with mozzarella.  Bake an additional 10 mins or until chicken is no longer pink.  Serve over hot spaghetti tossed with remaining pasta sauce.
