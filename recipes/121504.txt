1 LB linguine
4 tbs butter, divided use
4 tbs extra virgin olive oil, divided use plus more for drizzling
1 lg shallot finely diced
5 cloves garlic, sliced
Pinch red pepper flakes
20 lg shrimp, about 1 lb
Kosher salt and black pepper
1/2 C dry white wine
Juice of 1 lemon
Zest of l lemon
1/4 C finely chopped parsley

Cook pasta in salted, boiling water for about 6 to 8 minutes or until not quite done.  Drain pasta, reserving 1 cup water.

Meanwhile, in large skillet, melt 2 tbs butter in 2 tbs olive oil over medium-high heat.  Saute the shallots, garlic and red pepper flakes until shallots are translucent about 3 to 4 minutes.  Season the shrimp with salt and pepper, add them to the pan and cook until they have turned pink, about 2 to 3 minutes.  Remove shrimp from pan, set aside and keep warm.

Add wine and lemon juice to skillet and bring to a boil.  Add 2 tbs butter and 2 tbs oil.  When the butter has melted, return the shrimp to the pan along with the lemon zest, parsley and cooked pasta and reserved pasta water.  Stir well and season with salt and pepper.  Drizzle over a bit more olive oil and serve immediately.

Serves 6
