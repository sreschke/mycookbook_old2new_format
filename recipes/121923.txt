2 cups sugar
2 cups brown sugar
2 cups heavy cream
2 tbsp unsalted butter

1. mix the creme and the two sugars together in a sauce pan.

2. place the sauce pan over med heat and bring to a boil. cook until a candy therm reaches 240 f.

3. remove from the heat and cool for 30 mins.

4. stir in vanilla and butter and beat until the mixture loses its shine.

5. pour the sucre a la creme into a square pan and cool to room temp.

to serve:

cut them sucre a la creme into 1 inch thick squares
and with a lg glass of cold milk.
