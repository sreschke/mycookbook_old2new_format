1 lb Goya 16 bean soup mix
1 medium onion chopped
2 stalks celery chopped
1 large carrot chopped
1 packet Goya ham concentrate or 2 Goya chicken bouillon cubes
1 bay leaf
salt and pepper to taste

Soak and drain beans.  In a large saucepan, combine beans and remaining ingredients with six cups of water.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, cover and simmer until beans are tender.  Serves 8 to 10.

Can add ground beef, chicken or turkey.
