6 tbs. butter
1/2 c. sliced mushrooms
2 tbs. minced onion
1tsp. fresh lemon juice
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/8 tsp. salt
2 2tsp. snipped fresh parsley
1 lb. beef tenderloin, cut into 8 slices

Melt 4 tbs. butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add mushroom,onions,garlic,lemon,Worchester sauce and salt cook stirring until mushrooms are tender.  Stir parsley pour the sauce into small metal bowl or saucepan cover and keep warm. Melt remaining butter in skillet.  Cook steak over medium-high turing once 3 to 4 minutes per side. Serve with mushroom sauce.
