1 cup all purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. allspice
1 tsp. salt
1 egg
1/2 cup oil
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup grated zucchini
1/2 cup milk

Measure into large bowl all seven dry ingredients.  Stir thoroughly.  Make a well in the centre.
In small bowl beat egg until frothy.  Mix in oil, sugar, zucchini and milk.  Pour into well in dry ingredients bowl stirring only to moisten.  Batter will be lumpy.  Fill greased muffin cups 3/4 full.  Bake in 400 oven for 20 - 25 minutes.  Let stand 5 minutes before removing from pan.  Serve warm.  Makes 12 large muffins.
