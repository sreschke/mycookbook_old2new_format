14 oz. bag chow mein noodles
2 tsp. oil
1 lb. lean ground beef
2 cups sliced onions
4 cups canned tomatoes
4 tsp. sugar

Stir fry ground beef and onions in oil until meat is browned.  Salt and pepper.  Drain excess fat.  Add tomatoes and sugar and bring to boil.  Add noodles and cook over medium heat for 10 minutes.
