Vegetable cooking spray
4 small boneless,skinless chicken breasts
1 can chicken broth
2 c uncooked minute rice
1 pkg frozen broccoli,thawed
3/4 lb(12 oz) velveeta,cut up

Spray skillet with cooking spray and brown chicken 
4 minutes on each side,until cooked. Remove chicken and set aside. Add broth;bring to boil. Stir in rice,broccoli and cheese. Cook until rice is done. Top with chicken. Serves 4
