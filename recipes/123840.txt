Ingredients:

Sweet Potatoes/Yams
Butter
Brown Sugar
Marshmellows

Fill 14" pyrex with boiled, sliced sweet potatoes - wedges (Grandma Donohue - Yams)

Melt butter and mix in brown sugar until desired thickness and taste. 

Spoon over potatoes.

Cook at 250' untill potatoes look candied. 

Also, can place on stove low and spoon over potatoes until candied.Option - You can melt marshmellows on top at well.
