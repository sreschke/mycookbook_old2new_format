2/3 cup butter, softened
1 package german chocolate cake mix
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
1 tub creamy coconut pecan ready-to-spread frosting
1/4 cup milk

Heat oven to 350 degrees.  Lightly grease 9X13 pan.  Cut butter into cake mix in a medium bowl, using a pastry blender.  Press half of the mixture in bottom of pan.  Bake 10 minutes.  Sprinkle chocolate chips over baked layer; drop frosting by tablespoonfuls over chocolate chips.  Stir milk into remaining cake mixture.  Drop by teaspoonful onto frosting layer.  Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until cake portion is slightly dry to touch and cool completely.  Cover and refridgerate until firm.  Cut into bars.  Cover and refridgerate any remaining bars.  Makes 48 bars.
