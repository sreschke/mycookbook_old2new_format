               Napoleatino Style:

5# Potato    or     2# Potato                               
                     
3 Medium onions     1 1/2 Medium onions
2 Can pitted black olives.  1 Can pitted black olives
Peppers: Cherry, hot? Your choice                                
1/3 Cup White vinegar  1/4 Cup White Vinegar
2Tbsp  Garlic Salt  1Tbsp Garlic sallt
1tsp pepper (coarse)  1 tsp pepper (coarse)
Olive Oil, your call  Olive oil, your call
/////////////////////////////////////////////

Boil potatoes with skin till done, but not mushy.  
Let cool then remove skin. Cut into fourths, then half again
Combine the above in with the potatoes, be sure to FOLD in the ingredients
Cover tight and refrigerate for 1 day
Next day turn potatoes taste salad.  add the required ingredients  to your taste!
//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
Sicilian potato Salad:

Mushrooms,
