This recipe was given to me by the cook at Barrett House in Albuquerque, NM. It was given to her by her grandmother. This dish is excellent either hot or cold. Serves 4.

1 can salmon (2 cups)
4 eggs
1/2 cup bread crumbs
4 tablespoons butter
salt and pepper

Pick salmon into small bits. Beat eggs well. Mix all ingredients together. Put in buttered pan. Set pan in water and bake until knife comes out clean. Bake at 350 degrees for 1and 1/2 to 2 hours.
