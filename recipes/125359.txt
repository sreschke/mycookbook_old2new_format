3/4 c bottled salsa
1/4 c unsweetened coconut milk
1 green onion, chopped (2T)
1/2 tsp finely shredded lime peel
1 T lime juice
1 T chopped fresh cilantro or parsley
1 tsp red curry paste
1 tsp grated fresh ginger
1 tsp soy sauce
1 clove garlic, minced
4 chicken breasts

In blender combine salsa, coconut milk, onion, lime peel and juice, parsley, curry paste, ginger, soy and garlic.  Process till smooth.  

Remove 1/3 c to drizzle, cover and chill.

Place chicken in plastic bag and pour remaining marinade over.  Regrigerate for 1 to 2 hours.
Drain chicken and reserve marinade.

Grill chicken, brush with marinade used for marinating!  Drizzle with unused marinade in refrigerator.
