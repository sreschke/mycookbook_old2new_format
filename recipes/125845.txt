Ingredients:
2 cups of all purpose flour
1/4 cup of sugar
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup milk
1 egg beaten 
1/3 cup vegtable oil
3/4 cup mashed banana
1/2 cup chopped walnuts

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Grease a 12 cup muffin pan. Sift flour,sugar,baking powder,and salt into a medium bowl; make a well in center.

2. Mix milk, egg, oil, banana and walnuuts in a small bowl. Pour mixture into well. 

3. Mix batter just until moistened; do not overmix.

4. Spoon batter into prepared muffin cups. Bake until tooth pick inserted in center comes out clean or until muffins are golden brown, about 15 mins. 

5. ENJOY!!
