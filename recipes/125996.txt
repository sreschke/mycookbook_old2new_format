1 can (7 1/2 oz) refrigerated buttermilk biscuits
1 tbsp butter or margarine, melted
4 1/2 tsp parmesan cheese, grated
1 tsp dried oregano
8 oz brown-and-serve sausage links

On a lightly floured surface, roll out each biscuit into a 4 inch circle; brush with butter.  Combine parmesan cheese and oregano; sprinkle over butter.  Place a sausage link in the center of each; roll up.  Cut each widthwise into four pieces; insert a toothpick into each.  Place on an ungreased baking sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees for 8-10 minutes or until golden brown.

Yield:  approx 4 dozen

(recipe taken from Taste of Home cookbook.  Very Good!)
