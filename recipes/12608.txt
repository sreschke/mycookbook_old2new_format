CHICKEN CONTINENTAL  (2 ingredients)

Bread chicken  cutlets (egg and crumbs - I use flavored crumbs)
Do not saute the cutlets.  Put them on flat oven pan, top with pat of butter and bake about 12 minutes at 375.  Remove and add thick slice of muenster cheese and return to oven for 10 minutes, or so - or til cheese is browned and bubbly.

BON APPETITE!!
