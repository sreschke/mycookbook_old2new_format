1 8 oz cream cheese
1/4 cup butter
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1 can 4 oz mushroom pieces, chopped fine
2 cups of cooked chicken-bite size
3 tablespoons chooped green onion
2 packages of cresent rolls

Mix soft butter and cream cheese with pepper.  Fold in mushroom, chicken and onion.
Unwrap rolls.  Seperate them so they are 8 triangles.
Put 1/4 cup of mixture onto each triangle.  Take the wide end (not the pointed end), roll up and press edges together to seal.  Put on an ungreased cookie sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 min.
