Makes: 4 servings 
Preparation Time: 15 minutes 
A classic combination in a mouthwatering sandwich. Juicy heirloom tomatoes are layered with fresh mozzarella cheese, fresh basil, then drizzled with a mixture of olive oil, balsamic and coarse sea salt. Crisp cooked applewood-smoked bacon and crunchy romaine is added for dynamic flavor and texture.

4 FRANCISCO French 6-inch Sandwich Rolls, lightly toasted 
¬ cup olive oil 
2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar 
2 medium heirloom tomatoes (or regular tomatoes), thinly sliced 
2 balls of fresh mozzarella cheese, approximately 1 pound 
Coarse sea salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste 
« cup loosely packed fresh basil leaves 
8 slices applewood-smoked bacon, cooked crisp 
2 cups torn romaine lettuce 
Mix olive oil and vinegar in a shallow dish. Dip tomato slices to coat. To assemble arrange tomato slices on the bottom half of the FRANCISCO rolls. Pour any extra olive/balsamic mixture on the cut-side of the top half of FRANCISCO roll. Place lettuce, then bacon on bottom of each roll and top with alternating slices of tomato, mozzarella cheese, and basil. Sprinkle with sea salt and freshly ground black pepper. Place the top half of FRANCISCO roll over the filling.
