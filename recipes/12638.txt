Preheat oven to 375. Grease Jelly roll pan.  Beat 3 eggs with 1 cup sugar till mixture is lightly fluffy and creamy. Add 2/3 cup pmpkin, 1 teaspoon baking soda, 3/4 cup flour and 1 teaspoon cinnamon. mix well. Pour into prepared jelly-roll pan.  Sprinkle with chopped nuts and bake 11-15 min. turn onto foil dusted with confectioners sugar. Roll up like jelly roll. cool.  When cake has cooled un roll and spread with well creamed mixture of 1 8oz pkg cream cheese, 1 cup confectioners sugar 2 tablespoons butter and 1 teaspoon vanilla.  Carefuly re-roll and tightly wrap in foil and keep cool till time to serve.
