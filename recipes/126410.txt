THIS IS THE MOST DELICIOUS AND MOIST PUMPKIN BREAKFAST CAKE YOU'VE EVER EATEN!  IF YOU WANT, YOU CAN FROST IT WITH CREAM CHEESE FROSTING AND IT'S DESSERT!

4 large eggs, set at room temp 30 minutes

Meanwhile...
SIFT together:
2 cups flour(SIFTED before measuring) 
2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp ground cloves
2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg

When eggs are at room temp beat them rapidly, then add 
2 cups sugar...beat till fluffy and light, then add
1 can pumpkin and 
1 cup vegetable oil alternately at slow speed on mixer

When mixed add flour mixture slowly till blended
chopped nuts (1/2 c) if desired, folded in now

Bake for 1 hour at 350 in a well-greased bundt pan
