1/2 lb. (8 oz.) bulk pork sausage
2 c. frozen shredded hash brown potatoes
1 can 10 oz. rotel (drained)
1/2 lb. (8 oz.) velveeta, cut up
6 eggs

Brown sausage in large nonstick skillet on med. heat and drain.  Add potatoes and tomatoes and cook 5 min.  Top with velveeta.

Beat eggs with 2 T. water with a whisk in a bowl.  
Pour evenly over ingr. in skillet. Cover and reduce heat to low.

Cook 10 to 12 min. or until egg mixture is set in corners, and velveeta is melted.  Uncover and let stand 5 min. before cutting in wedges.

6 servings
