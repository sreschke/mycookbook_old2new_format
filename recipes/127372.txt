
1 lb. ground beef 
1/4 cup cup chopped onion 
1 12 oz. bottle of chili sauce or ketchup 
2 Tbsp. vinegar 
2 Tbsp. brown sugar 
1 Tbsp. worcestershire sauce 
salt & pepper to taste 

Brown meat & onion together. Drain off excess grease. Mix in chili sauce or ketchup. Add vinegar, brown sugar, worcestershire sauce, salt & pepper. Simmer for 1/2 hour. Serve on hamburger buns. 
 
OR:

?	1/2 white onion 
?	3 cloves garlic 
?	3 tablespoons olive oil 
?	1 2/3 cups ketchup 
?	1/2 cup white vinegar 
?	1/3 cup brown sugar 
?	1/3 cup Worcestershire sauce 
?	2 teaspoons chili powder 
?	1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper 

Directions

Put olive oil in a sauce pan on medium heat and cook onion and garlic until they are soft. 

Then add the white vinegar, ketchup, brown sugar, worcestershire sauce, chili powder, and cayenne pepper. 

Let this simmer for 30 minutes.
