leaves of 1 large brunch of fresh basil, weighing about 2 + 1/2 oz. (65g) about 2 packed cups
1/3 cup pine nuts
1 garlic clove
1/3 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
1 cup olive oil 
sal tand pepper

1) Pick the basil leaves off their stems and drop them in the blender.
2) Saute pine nuts in a tbsp of olive oil until brown. 
3) Add the pine nuts, garlic, parmesan cheese.
4) Pulse, add oil, pulse, add oil.
