2 cups + tbsp of milk
7 tbsp unslated butter
3 tbsp granulated sugar
1 (1/4) oz package active dry yeast
5 cups all-purpose flour
1 tbsp salt

1) In a small saucepan, combine the milk, 3 tbsp butter, and sugar.  
2) Place over medium-high heat and cook, stirring, until the butter has melted.  
3) Remove from the heat and cool to about 110 degees F.  
4)Scatter the yeast over the surface of the liquid and set aside until foamy, about 10 minutes.  
5) Meanwhile, in a large bowl, whisk together the flour and salt and set aside.  
6) Pour the milk and yeast mixture into the bowl of flour and mix until soft ragged mixture is formed.
7) Transfer to a well-floured work surface and knead by hand until a soft elastic dough is formed. About 10 minutes.
8) Transfer to a lightly oild bowl, cover with a kitchen towel, and place in a warm spot.
9) Leave for two hours
10) Preheat over to 400F.  
11) Divide the dough into 12 equal pieces.  Using your hands, round each dough piece into a ball.
12) Butter a baking deal, place the dough balls in the dish, then cover it for two hours.
13) Melt the remaining butter and liberally brush the rolls with it.  
14) Bake until golden brown, about 25 minutes.
