 BISQUICK BLUEBERRY MUFFINS
 Makes 12 Medium Muffins
 
 1  egg
 2 c.  Bisquick Baking Mix
 1/3 c.  sugar
 2/3 c.  milk
 2 T.  vegetable oil
 3/4 c.  blueberries

Heat oven to 400 degrees. Grease bottoms only of 12 medium muffin cups or line with paper baking cups. Beat eggs slightly in medium bowl; stir in remaining ingredients except blueberries just until moistened. Fold blueberries into batter. Divide batter evenly among cups. Bake 12.
 minutes or until golden brown.
