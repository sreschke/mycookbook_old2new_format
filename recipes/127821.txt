6 eggs
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
2 cups whole milk
2 cups all purpose flour
1 tbl fresh parsley
6 tbl prime rib drippings or veg oil

Preheat oven to 425. Beat eggs, salt and pepper in a large bowl with a hand mixer on medium speed until foamy, about 1 min. Add flour and milk to eggs just until blended, scrape sides of bowl if necessary. Whisk in parsley by hand. Heat drippings or oil in a 10" cast iron skillet over high until it smokes. Pour batter into pan, transfer to oven, and bake until sides are puffed, browned and crisp, 25-30 min. Cut pudding into wedges and serve immmediately. Serves 12
