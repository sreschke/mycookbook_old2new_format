1 12 oz pkg. hot Italian sausage
1 12 oz pkg. mild Italian sausage
3 tblsp olive oil
1-1/2 cups chopped onion
3 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. pepper
4 red potatoes, unpealed and diced
4 cans chicken broth
3 cups water
4 cups julienne Kale
1/3 cup heavy cream

Brown and crumble sausage in a skillet then drain off any excess oil.  Saute onion in olive oil till onion is just starting to brown.  Add garlic and saute for no more than 2 minutes.  Add pepper, broth, water and potatoes.  Bring to a boil and continue boiling for about 5 minutes.  Reduce heat and allow to simmer for about 30 minutes.  Then add browned sausage and continue to simmer.  When ready to serve, add cream and kale.
