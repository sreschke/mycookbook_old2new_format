2 pkg. chopped frozen spinach, cooked cooled and drained
3 eggs
4 tbsp. flour
1/4 lb. american cheese, cubed
1/4 lb. brick cheese, cubed
12-16 oz. small curd cottage cheese
1/4 c. margarine, melted
salt and pepper to taste

Melt butter. Mix with eggs and flour.  Add cottage cheese.  Mix with spinach and cheeses.  Bake 1 hour at 350 uncovered.

note:  can assemble ahead..leftovers reheat great in the microwave.
