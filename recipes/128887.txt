1 c. butter
1 3/4 c . sugar
3 large eggs, beaten
5 large bananas, mashed
2 1/2 c. flour
2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 c. mini chocolate chips

Cream together butter and sugar.  Mix eggs and bananas in separate bowl.  Sift together flour, baking soda and salt.  Alternatively add a little banana mixture, then flour mixture to the creamed butter until all is added and mixed well.  Stir in chocolate chips.  Pour into greased and floured loaf pans.  

Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
