Crumb together 1/4 cup butter, 1/2 cup sifted flour and 1/2 cup broun sugar. Set aside.
  Sift together 2 cups flour, 2 teaspoons baking powder. 1/2 teaspoon baking soda, and a dash of salt.
  Cream together 1/2 cup butter and 1/8 ounce cream cheese.  Add 1 1/4 cups sugar, 2 eggs and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
  Alternate mixing dry ingredients with 1/2 cup milk, beginning and ending with dry ingredients.  Mix ln butter and cream and put in a 9x13 inch pan.
  Sprinkle crumb mixture and 1/2 cup nuts over cake.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 40 minutes.
   Serve with strawberries and Cool Whip on top.
