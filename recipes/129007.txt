Toffee Bar Brownies: (Aunt Janice)

1 Box Brownie mix, mix according to package directions.

3-6 oz. Symphany candy bars (the real big ones) you want the one with almonds and toffee.

In a 9X13 baking pan, put tin foil in and up the sizes, spray with Pam.  Put Â« the brownie mix in the pan and spread to cover pan.  Place the candy bars on top of the brownie mix in the pan, you might need to break off one row of one of the candy bars.  Add the rest of the browning mix on top of the candy bars, spread.

Bake per directions on the pan.  Cool completely, lift foil from pan and cut brownies into squares.
