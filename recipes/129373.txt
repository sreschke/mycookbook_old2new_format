2 cups diced onion
1 cup frozen corn
1 tbl garlic minced
1 jalapeno, seeded and diced
2 tbl olive oil
1 tbl tomato paste
1 tbl chipoltle chiles in adobo sauce, minced
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp oregano leaves
1/2 tsp ground cumin
1/2 tsp coriander
4 cups chicken broth
1 can diced tomatoes
2 cups cooked turkey
1 cup canned black beans, drained
1/4 cup scallions, sliced
2 tbl minced cilantro
juice of 1/2 lime
shredded jack cheese
sour cream
sliced avacado
tortilla strips

Sweat onion, corn, garlic and jalapeno in 2 tbl oil in large pot over medium heat, 4 -5 min. Stir in tomato paste, chipoltles and seasonings, cook 1 min. Add broth, tomatoes, turkey and beans and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium low and simmer 10 min. Finish soup with scallions, cilantro, lime juice and salt to taste. Top each serving with cheese, sour cream, avacado and tortilla strips. 8 cups.
