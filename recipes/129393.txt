Ingredients for 4 Servings

4 ea Boneless top loin pork chops, diced
12 oz Jar of pork gravy
2 tbsp Ketchup
8 small Red potatoes, diced
2 cups Frozen mixed vegetables

Procedure: 

1. In large skillet, brown pork cubes; stir in gravy, ketchup and potatoes;
cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
2. Stir in vegetables; cook for 10-15 minutes longer, until vegetables are
tender.
