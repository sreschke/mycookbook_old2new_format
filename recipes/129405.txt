5 or 6 thick chops           dash of pepper
1/3 cup brown sugar          3 tblsps of water
dash of salt                 1 sliced onion
1/3 cup ketchup

Brown chops on both sides. Season with salt and pepper. Transfer to baking dish. Place a slice of onion on top of each chop. Blend brown sugar and water and ketchup.Pour over chops. Bake uncovered in 350 oven for 45 minutes. Uncover and bake for an extra 15 minutes.
