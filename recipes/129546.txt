1/3 cup heavy cream
1 tbl light corn syrup
3/4 cup semi sweet chocolate chips

Simmer cream and corn syrup for the chocolate sauce in a small saucepan over medium high heat. Pour mixture over chocolate, let stand for 2 min, then whisk until smooth. To serve, slice cooled tart into wedges, place on serving plate, dust with powdered sugar and drizzle with chocolate sauce.
