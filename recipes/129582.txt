Ingredients 
1 fillet norhtern pike

2 cups milk

2 eggs

2 cups flour

2 tsp garlic powder

 
Directions 
Heat oil to 300+ degrees. Cut fillet into chunks. Lighty beat eggs. Mix all ingredients togerher. Dip fillet chunks into mixture. Put in oil and let fry till golden brown.
