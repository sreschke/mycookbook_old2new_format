BATTER:
1 cup margarine
2 cups sugar
2 eggs
1 cup sour cream
1/2 tsp vanilla
2 cups sifted cake flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
TOPPING: (mix together well)
3/4 cup finely chopped nuts
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 cup brown sugar

Cream together margarine, sugar, and eggs.  Fold in sour cream and vanilla carefully, then sift dry ingredients.  Spoon half the batter onto greased & floured 10" tube pan, cover with half the topping then repeat.  Bake at 350 for 55 to 60 minutes.  Cool almost completely before removing from pan.  Sprinkle top with sifted confectioner's sugar.  Serve cold.  It is best served day after making.

Topping:
3/4 cup finely chopped nuts
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 cup brown sugar

mix together well.
