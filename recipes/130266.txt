Take 4 balls of tin foil and toss them in the crockpot.

Take a whole chicken (depending on the size of your crockpot) and 
wash it inside and out.
Sprinkle generously with Lawry's seasoned salt.

Put flat side down on the tin foil balls. Close lid and cook on high 
for 4 hours. This is important - do not cook on low.
