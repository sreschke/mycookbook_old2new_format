1  Tall can evaporated milk
1  Cup oil
1  teas. salt
1  teas soda
1  Palm full salt
1/4  Cup water
3  Cup flour


Mix all ingredients together and beat until glossy. Put on floured surface and work in a little more flour.  Let set.  This is a good time to drink a cup of coffee. Pinch off to make a ball and roll out to fill. Fry in hot oil

Makes about 30 pies
