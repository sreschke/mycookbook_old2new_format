You Will Need:

For the Pork
150g pork loin, batted flat with a meat mallet.
1 tbsp fresh parsley chopped
1 tbsp fresh chives chopped
1 tbsp fresh coriander chopped
100g white breadcrumbs
50g flour, seasoned with salt and pepper.
2 whole free range eggs beaten
2 tbsp olive oil

For the Garlic Mushrooms
1 tbsp olive oil
25g unsalted butter
1 clove garlic finely chopped
1 small onion finely chopped
100g button mushrooms
salt and freshly ground pepper.

Method:
Preheat the Oven to 22OC
For the pork, place the fresh herbs and breadcrumbs into a bowl and mix together.
Place the flour into a bowl and dredge the pork loin in the flour. Dip the floured pork into the eggs then roll in the herbed breadcrumbs to coat.
Dip into the egg mixture and breadcrumb once more.
Heat the oil in an ovenproof frying pan over a high heat. Add the pork and fry for 2 mins on each side until crisp and golden. Transfer into the oven and roast for 5 mins or until completely cooked through.
For the mushrooms, heat the oil and butter in a saucepan over a medium heat. Add the garlic and onion and fry until soft.
Add the mushrooms and cook until soft, season to taste.
To serve, arrange the mushrooms onto the middle of a serving dish and place the pork on top.
