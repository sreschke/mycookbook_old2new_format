	
1 lb. ground beef
3 stalks celery, chopped 
1 medium onion, chopped

Combine and brown lightly.  Do not add salt  or seasonings.  Combine in 2 quart casserole the following:  1 can mushroom soup, 1 can cream of chicken soup, 1 1/2 cups of water, 1/4 tsp. pepper, 1 Tbs. soy sauce, 1/2 cup, uncooked rice (Minute or regular).  Add meat mixture; stir.                        
1-  Bake 1/2 hour covered at 350  .   
2-  1/2 hour uncovered at 350  .
After cooked add 1 3 oz. can chow mein noodles.  Push half of noodles down into casserole.  Sprinkle remaining noodles on top.
3-  Cook 15 minutes longer.
