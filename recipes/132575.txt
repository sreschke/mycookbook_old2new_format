1 9" pie crust (thawed)
3 cups dark brown sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla
3 cups pecan halves
1 1/2 sticks butter
5 large eggs

In a pan, toast 1 cup of pecans until nuts are darker in coller.  In a food processor, grind toasted nuts finely.  Add butter to pan, and heat on medium until slightly brown.  Stir in brown sugar until melted and remove from heat.
In a large bowl, whisk eggs until frothy.  Fold in the ground pecans, vanilla, butter mixture and mix well.
Pour mixture into crust.  Arrange the remaining pecan halves on top.  Place pie on a cookie sheet and bake at 350 for 45 minutes until center is puffed and brown.  Cool completely.
