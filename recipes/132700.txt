1 box yellow cake mix
1/8 teaspoon soda
1/4 cup flour
2 eggs
1 cup plus 2 tablespoons water
1 cup mashed banana
2 tablespoons orange zest

Blend dry ingredients together, then add remaining ingredients.  Beat for 3 minutes at medium speed.

Pour into 2 greased and floured loaf pans.  Bake at 350 for 45-50 minutes.
