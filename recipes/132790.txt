Ingredients:

1/4 c. of Vegitable oil
1 (18.25-oz.) chocolate cake mix
2 large eggs
1 (3.9 oz.) Instant chocolate pudding mix
1 c. semi-sweet chocolate chips
Cool Whip
1 1/4 c. water

Heat oven to 350 degrees. Pour oil into 9 x 13-inch pan to coat bottom. Put cake mix, 1 1/4 cups water,eggs,pudding mix, and chocoate chips into pan. Mix thoroughly with fork. Spread batter evenly and bake 35 t0 40 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center of cake comes out clean. Cut and serve with Cool Whip.
