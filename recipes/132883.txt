CHEESE & SPINACH PUFFS

1 10 oz pkg frozen chopped spinach	
« cup chopped onion
2 slightly beaten eggs
« parmesan cheese
« cup shredded cheddar cheese

1/8 tsp garlic powder
1 8 « oz pkg corn muffin mix
Cook spinach according to package & drain well pressing out all excess liquid.  Add onion.
In another bowl, combine eggs, cheeses, salad dressing, butter & garlic powder.
