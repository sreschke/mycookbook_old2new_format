1 lb fresh broccoli cut into spears or 1 pkg (10 oz) frozen broccoli spears cooked and drained
1 1/2 cups cubed cooked chicken or turkey.
1 can Campbells new cream of broccoli soup.
1/3 cup milk
1/2 cup shredded chedder cheese.
1 Tbsp butter melted
2 Tbsp dry bread.

In a 9 inch pie plate or a shallow casserol arrange broccoli, top with chicken.  Combine soup and milk and pour over chicken. Sprinkle with cheese.
Combine butter and bread crumbs and sprinkle over cheese.

Bake at 450 for 15 minutes or until hot.
