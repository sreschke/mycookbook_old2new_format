4 egg whites
1 1/2 cup confectioners sugar
1/2 pound dates
1/2 pound shelled walnut meats
vanilla

Beat egg whites until stiff. Add confectioners sugar. Fold in coarsley cut nuts and dates; cut fine. Drop by tsp onto a greased floured cookie sheet. Bake in a 325 degree oven for 15 to 20 min. until just firm.
