1-1/2 lbs. boneless chicken breast (any chicken will do - cook longer for bone-in
1/2 C milk

Coating mixture
1/4 C flour
1 t salt
1/8 t black pepper
1 t sage
1 t garlic powder
1 t thyme
1 t marjoram
1 t oregano

3-4 T butter
1 T parsley


Preheat oven to 375
Pound chicken breasts to flatten. Soak in milk while mixing the coating mixture.
Put coating mixture in large zip-lock and shake to blend. Shake bag until chicken is well coated.
Place chicken in casserole dish and dot with pats of butter and sprinkle with parsley. Bake uncovered 20-25 minutes.
