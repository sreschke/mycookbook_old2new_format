8-10 medium potatoes
1 8oz, cream cheese, softened
1 small carton sour cream
1 C shredded cheddar cheese
salt & pepper to taste

Boil potatoes until tender, drain.  In a separate bowl mix sour cream and sour cream.  In large bowl mash potatoes then add cream mixture beating until light and fluffy.  Add cheddar cheese and salt & pepper.  Put in 9 x 13 casserole dish and top with  8 - 10 pats of butter and paprika (optional).  Bake 325 for 25 minutes.
