In Large pan combine:
2 C Sugar
1 Lb (4 sticks) Butter
Cook 20 Min's on med. heat just to crack stage. Remove from heat and poor into sided cookie sheet.
Stick in refrigerator to cool.

Melt 1 Lg Hershey sweet choc. bar (4.4 oz) in double broiler. Pour over hardened toffee. Cover immediatly with 1 C chopped walnuts.

I like to use organic sugar - sifted. Really Good
