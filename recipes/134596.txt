Ingredients

1 chuck roast
salt
pepper
onions
potatoes
carrots
Liption onion soup mix

In a large pot of water add the following:  
1 chuck roast
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 potatoes, cut into bite size pieces
2 onions, cut into bite size pieces
carrots
1 package of Lipton Onion soup mix.

Cook on stove for 1 - 2 hours, until the meat is tender and vegetables are tender.

Once done, you can thicken the sauce by mixing flour and water and adding it to the sauce.
