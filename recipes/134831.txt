2 cups sugar
2 cups flour
2 tsp soda
2 eggs
1 20oz. can crushed pinapple
1 cup chopped nuts

Mix together and pour into greased and floured pan and bake for 35 minutes at 30 degrees

Frosting:
2 3 oz package cream cheese
2 cups powdered sugar 
1/2 stick butter
1 tsp vanilla

Cream cheese and butter and add powder sugar and vanilla
