SMOOTHIES
Orange Pineapple Smoothie
1 sweet orange peeled and sliced 
1/2 cup sliced fresh pineapple
1/2 cup banana
1/2 cup orange-pineapple juice
1/2 cup ice

SWEET GREEN GOOD MORNING SMOOTHIE
1 cup lime sherbet
1 fresh ripe kiwifruit, peeled and chopped
2/3 cup seedless green grapes
1 cup ripe honeydew melon,peeled,seeded and chopped
1/2 cup fresh cucumaber,peeled,seeded and chopped
1/3 cup pineapple juice
1/4 cup heavy (whipping)cream
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Makes 2 servings

Place lime sherbet,chopped kiwi, grapes, honeydew melon, cucumber,pineapple juice, whipping cream and vanilla extract in blender container. Place lid on blender and blend at medium-high power for 1 minute. Stop to see if all ingredients are blended smooth. If not, blend for another 20 to 30 seconds. Pour into glasses and serve at once. 
*This is a nice change for a summer breakfast. This one is also a nice afternoon refreshment.
