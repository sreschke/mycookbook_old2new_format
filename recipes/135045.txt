1lb. Asparagus cut into 2" pieces
2 small potatoes cut into cubes
1 small onion chopped
1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup flour
1 cup whipping cream
6 ounces brie (rind removed)
1/2 cup white wine

Saute asparagus, potato and onion in butter until tender.
Mix flour with one cup broth until smooth. Add to asparagus and stir until well blended. Stir in remaining broth, cream and wine.
Simmer 15 minutes.
When cool blend until smooth.
Return to pan and add the cubes of brie. Simmer until cheese has melted. ( sometimes the brie does not melt properly. in this case sieve the soup to take out the lumps )
