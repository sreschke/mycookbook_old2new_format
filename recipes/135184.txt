9 ounces lasagna noodles (about 9)
1 TBSP olive oil
1 medium onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 cans tomato puree
1 15oz can diced tomatoes
1 cup grated carrot
1 TSP fresh oregano (or 1/2 TSP dried)
1 TSP fresh thyme (or 1/2 TSP dried)
16 ounces low-fat ricotta cheese
1 egg
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
12 ounces part-skim mozzarella cheese, shredded
1 10-ounce package frozen spinach, thawed and pressed to remove liquid

1. Preheat over to 375 degrees. Boil noodles according to package directions (or use noodles that can be used w/o precooking).

2. Heat oil in skillet over medium heat. Add onion and garlic and cook until golden. Add tomato sauce, puree, carrot, oregano, and thyme. Simmer 10 minutes.

3. Mix ricotta, egg and 1/3 cup of the Parmesan cheese in a large bowl.

4. Spread 1 cup of tomato sauce mixture in the bottom of 9" x 13" x 3" lasagna pan. Place a single layer of noodles over sauce. Spread with half the ricotta cheese mixture and 1/3 of the mozzarella.

5. Mix spinach into remaining ricotta cheese mixture. Build another layer, using 1.5 cups sauce, 3 noodles, all of the spinach/ricotta mixture, and half of the remaining mozzarella. 

6. Finish with a final layer of noodles, sauce and the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Bake, covered with foil, until bubbly, 50 - 60 minutes. Remove foil and continue baking 5 minutes until cheese is melted. Let stand 10 to 15 minutes before serving.

IF Rating: 120; Fat 17 g; Carbs 37 g
