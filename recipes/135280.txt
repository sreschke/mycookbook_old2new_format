Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
1 lb. lean ground beef 
1/2 c. chopped onion
	Brown. Drain well.
1 envelope taco seasoning
1 can corn, drained
salt and pepper to taste
	Add and mix well. Spread into bottom of 10" 
	quiche or pie pan sprayed with non stick 
	spray. Top with shredded cheese.

3 eggs or egg substitute
1 1/2 c. milk
3/4 c. Jiffy or Bisquick mix
	Whisk together or mix well.  Pour over 
	cheese/meatmixture. Bake for 30-40 
	minutes.  Add more shredded cheese on top 
	and return to oven for a few more minutes 
	to melt. Serve with salsa, lettuce, ripe 
	olives, tomatoes, or other toppings 
	if desired.
