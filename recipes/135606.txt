1 pkg. yellow cake mix
1 pkg. lemon jello
1 c. very hot water
4 eggs, well beaten
2/3 c. vegetable oil
1 T. lemon extract
1 c. confectioner's sugar
1 lemons

Dissolve jello in the cup of hot water.  To the cake mix, add the beaten eggs, oil and extract.  Blend well.  Add jellow and stir until well mixed.  Bake in a greased and floured bundt pan at 350 for 40-50 minutes until firm and brown on the top.

Glaze icing--squeeze the juice from the lemons and grate the rind.  Mix in 1 cup of confectioners sugar.  Perforate the top and sides of the cake with a fork. Pour mixture over the top of the warm cake slowing allowing time to soak in cake
