
 
3lbs. Ricotta
2 c. sugar
9 eggs  
1 pint light cream 
1/2 c. flour 
1 tsp. vanilla extract 
1 tsp. lemon extract  
   
Beat  the eggs until thick. Beat in the ricotta until well blended. Add the sugar and beat well. Add the vanilla, lemon and cream and flour. Mix well. Grease a baking dish well and pour the cheese mixture in. Before putting in the oven, decorate with thin orange slices. Bake at 350 for 1 1/2 hours.
