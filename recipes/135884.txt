2lbs. of marinated in "very terryaki" Sirloin Steak
1 whole head of Garlic
1 lg. yellow onion
3 tablespoons of jarred jalapenos
5 yellow jarred chili peppers
1 lg. can of diced tomatoes
1 sml. can of tomato sauce
2 med. cans of kidney beans
1 lg. can of Pinto beans

Start with preparing the base by adding 2 tablespoons of of jalapeno juice from jar of jalapenos,searing onions, garlic.

Add all ingredients (excluding meat)to crock pot, cook for 4 hours on low.

cook sirloin steak in broiler to "rare"
