1 1/2 lb. ground beef
1/2 C rice
1/2 C milk
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 tsp onion, minced
1/4 tsp celery salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Mix all together and form into balls. Brown on all sides in hot skillet.  Cook in barbecue sauce plus additional water as needed to cook rice on rice package.  Check to see if additional water is needed.  Cook 1 1/2 hours if using regular rice, 30 minutes if using instant rice or till rice is done.
Cook additional rice and spoon over.  See Barbecue Sauce.
