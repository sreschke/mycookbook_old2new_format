6 Servings 
(good stuffed in peppers after cooked)

5 med yellow squash- sliced (1 3/4 lbs)
1 med onion chopped
1 LG egg lightly beaten 
1 (6 oz) pkg mexican style cornbread mix
1 (8 1/4 oz) can cream style corn
1/2 cub chunky picante sauce
2 TBSP. butter- softened
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper

Boil squash and onion in water til tender-- mash with fork-- add beaten egg-- add remaining ingredients-- stir til blended spoon into lightly greased 1 1/2 or 2 qt baking dish-- bake at 350 for 30-50 minutes depending on depth of dish and size until its set
