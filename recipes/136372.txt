1 box German Chocolate Cake Mix
3/4 cup melted butter
1/2 cup evaporated milk
14 oz Kraft carmels
1 cup pecans
1 cup chocolate chips

Mix cake per instructions on box.  Pour 1/2 batter in cake pan.  Bake 15 minutes @ 350 degrees.

Melt carmels with 1/2 cup evaporated milk and add butter.  Pour over baked cake

Add nuts and chocolate chips-then add remainder of cake batter.  Return to oven and bake an additional 20 minutes.

Can add more nuts on top of cake batter if desired.
