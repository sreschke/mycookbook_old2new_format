4 oz penne salad
4 oz Zesty Italian Dressing
1/4 c diced Roma tomatoes
2 T sun dried tomatoes
1 clove of garlic
1 oz crumbled feta cheese
1 tsp minced cilantro
1 tsp minced basil
1/2 c green soybeans (thawed)

Cook pasta. Drain and cool. Add remaining ingredients and stir well. Allow flavors to blend for 12 to 24 hours.
