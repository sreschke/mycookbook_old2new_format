1 can sweetened condensed milk 14 oz
1 12 oz whipped topping
1 21 oz can cherry pie filling
2 medium firm bananas cut into chunks
1 8 oz can crushed pineapple
1/2 cup chopped nuts
4 or 5 sliced fresh strawberries.

combine milk and topping until well blended. fold in the rest of the ingredients and chill. Makes 10 servings.
