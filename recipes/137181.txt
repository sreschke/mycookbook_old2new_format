Campbell's recipe

1 lb Ground Beef
1 Can (10 3/4 oz) Campbell's Tomato Soup
1/2 Cup Salsa
1/2 Cup Water
6 flour tortillas (6") but into 1" pieces
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

1.  Cook beef in skillet until browned, pour off fat
2.  Stir in soup, salsa, water, tortillas.  Heat to a boil.  Reduce heat to low and cook for 5 min.  Stir.  Tope with cheese.  Makes 4 servings.

Creamy Mexica Fiesta:  Stir in 1/2 cup sour cream with soup.
