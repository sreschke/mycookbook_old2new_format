2 cups shelled and cleaned prawns
1 cup unsweetened yogurt (lightly beaten to remove lumps)
* 1/2-3/4 cup cashew nuts (substitute with blanched almonds) 
* 2 tsp white poppy seeds (substitute with black ones)
* 2-3 tbsp fresh coriander leaves
* 3-6 green chillies
1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
1 tsp garam masala powder (powder equal quantities of cinnamon, cloves & cardamom)
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1-2 tbsp oil

Grind together the ingredients marked with * to a smooth paste - this is the green paste.
Mix together yogurt, salt, turmeric powder, ginger-garlic paste and garam masala powder, and marinate prawns in this mixture - keep aside for 30 minutes.
Heat oil in a pan, add the prepared green paste and stir constantly for 2 minutes.
Add the marinated prawns along with the marinade, and cover the pan with a lid.
Simmer until the prawns are cooked.
Adjust sesaoning and serve hot.
