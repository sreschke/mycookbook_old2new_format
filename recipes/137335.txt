1 can of cranberry sauce (whole or jellied)
1 bottle of red russian dressing
1 package of lipton onion soup mix
chicken drumsticks/chicken breasts/ or both

Pre-heat the oven to 350*

In a bowl combine the cranberry sauce, red russian dressing, and lipton onion soup mix. Stir until well blended.

Place the chicken in a glass baking pan and pour the sauce over the chicken, coating both sides.

Bake the chicken in the oven at 350* for 45 minutes. Turn the oven up to 425* and bake for another 30 minutes.

Best if served with rice and a vegetable of your choice. Pouring the excess sauce over the rice is good as well.
