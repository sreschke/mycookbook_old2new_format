1 (9oz.) graham cracker crust
1 (8 oz.) package cream cheese (softened)
1 tablespoon grated lime zest
1 cup fresh lime juice
1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
1 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
1 (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed (optional)

DIRECTIONS
In a large bowl, with mixer cream softened cream cheese and condensed milk.  With a rubber spatula scrape cream cheese down from sides and bottom of mixing bowl.  Beat until smooth consistency. Pour in lime juice and lime rind.  Add in pudding mix scraping sides and bottom of bowl.  May add few drops of green food coloring at this point.  Pour into graham cracker crust.  Chill at least 2 hours before serving. Garnish with additional whipped topping if desired.
