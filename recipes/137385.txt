Heat oven to 350 degrees. 

Place sliced apples, pears, or peaches or whole berries or pitted cherries in a buttered 8-inch baking pan. 

With a pastry blender: blend 
1/2 cup butter 
1/2 cup quick oats 
1/2 cup flour
1 cup brown sugar 
until you have a coarse, crumbly mixture.  

Bake approximately 40 minutes at 350 degrees. 
Serve warm topped with whipped cream or vanilla
ice cream
