6 Bonless, skinless chicken halves (about 2 lbs)
1 envelope Good Seasons Roasted Garlic or Italian Salad Dressing Mix
1/2 cup grated Parmesan Cheese
___________________________________________________

(1) Mix envelope and cheese
(2) Moisten chicken in water; dip in dressing mixture and place in shallow baking dish
(3) Bake at 400* for 20 to 25 min or untill cooked through
