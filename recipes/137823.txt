Ingredients:

1 can eagle brand condensed milk
8 oz cream cheese
3 cups milk
large box instant vanilla pudding
1 large cool whip
1 box vanilla wafers
3 or 4 bananas

Instructions:

In a large bowl, whip cream cheese and Eagle Brand milk until smooth.  Add milk and pudding.  Stir or beat.  Add cool whip and beat.

In bowl of your choice, start on with layer of cookies, layer of bananas, and layer of pudding.  repeat ending with top layer of cookies.  Refrigerate.

I add about 3 tablespoons of sugar and a little of vanilla even though the recipe doesn't call for it.
