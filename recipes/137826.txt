mix together:

3 cups of mashed yams (canned is fine--I use Martindale brand)
)
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/3 stick butter
12 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla
sprinkle 3 or 4 good shakes of powdered cinnamon in the mix

topping:

1 cup chopped pecans
1 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup all-purpose flour (not self rising)
1/3 stick butter

bake 35 minutes at 350 degrees (gently shake the dish when you take it
out of the oven--should not be too runny---but be careful also and don't
cook til it is too hard----remember it will harden a little when cools
