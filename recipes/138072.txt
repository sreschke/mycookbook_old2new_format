Chicken Enchiladas

2 cups chicken or turkey, finely chopped
1 cup plain yogurt
1 cup cottage cheese   
1/4 cup chopped onion                 
2 teaspoons chilli powder
2 teaspoons sugar
2 teaspoons cornstarch
2 Tablespoons snipped up parsely
2 cloves garlic
12 6-inch corn tortillas

Mix all of the ingredients together in bowl other than the tortillas. Spray 12 x 7 1/2 x 2 baking dish with nonstick cating or lightly oil. Fill tortillaa with mixed ingredients. Place in pan seam side down. 

Mix the following ingredients and pour over the top.

1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
1 can  (14  ounces) diced green chilli peppers, rinsed and drained
2 teaspoons sugar
1/2 teaspoon ground coriander

Sprinkle with 1/3 cup Monterey Jack cheese. Bake at 350 F. covered with foil for 20 - 25 minutes.

Recipe Source: Sheila Bratcher
