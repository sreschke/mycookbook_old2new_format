1 medium onion, diced
1 pound ground beef
1 package taco seasoning mix
1/2 package ranch dressing mix
1 large can tomatoes, stewed and diced
1 can Rotel tomatoes (original)
2 15 oz. cans Ranch Style Beans
2 15 oz. cans pinto beans
2 15 oz cans corn

Saute diced onion in pot on stove top. Add ground beef and cook til done. Add the taco seasoning and 1/2 of the ranch dressing packet. Add stewed tomatoes, beans and corn, with juices. Stir well and continue to cook over a low heat until thoroughly heated and flavors are combined.
