Spray 11 x 13 with Pam.  Preheat oven to 350.

Prepare StoveTop as directed on box.  Spread along the bottom of the pan.

Add 2 - 3 chicken breasts, or 3 cans of shredded chicken on top of stove top.

Mix 1 can of cream of chicken w/1 can of milk, pour over the chicken.  Top with shredded cheese if desired.

Bake ~30 minutes.
