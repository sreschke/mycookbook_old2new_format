1 cup finely chopped red onion
1/4 cup thinly sliced fresh basil
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 - 1lb salmon filet, skinned and chopped
1 tablespoon hot sauce
1 large egg white
cooking spray
4 rolls or 8 slices focaccia
lettuce, tomato & condiments if desired

Combine first 5 ingredients in large bowl. Combine hot sau and egg white in small bowl; add egg white mixture to salmon mixture, stirring well to combine.

Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions, shaping each into 1/2 thick patty. Heat large nonstick pan over med high heat. Coat pan with cooking spray. Add salmon patties & cook 3 min each side or until desired degree of doneness. Serve on TOASTED focaccia.
