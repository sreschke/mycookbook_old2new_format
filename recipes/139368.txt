4 boxes Jiffy mix - bake and crumble

Melt 1 stick margarine in skillet and add:
4 stalks celery, chopped
1 apple, diced
1 small onion, chopped
Toward the end of sauteing, add 3 whole eggs and stir for a few minutes

Mix together:
Crumbled cornbread
Celery mixture
4 cans clear chicken broth
1 tsp sage or poultry seasoning
salt & pepper

Bake at 350 for 30-40 min or until golden brown.

Makes 2, 9 x 12 pans and freezes well

Gravy:
2 cans clear chicken broth
few tablespoons flour
2-3 chopped boiled eggs
chopped boiled giblets
