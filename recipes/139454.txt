2c. SUGAR
1/4c. COCOA
1/2c. MILK
1 STICK MARGARINE
1t. VANILLA
1/2c. PEANUT BUTTER
3c.QUICK OATS
     MIX SUGAR, COCOA, MILK AND MARGARINE.  COOK ON MEDIUM HEAT FOR 11/2 MINS. AFTER IT STARTS TO BOIL. REMOVE FROM HEAT AND ADD VANILLA, PEANUT BUTTER, AND OATS. MIX WELL. DROP BY TEASPOONFULS ON WAX PAPER.
