Chicken & Wild Rice Casserole

6 pounds cooked chicken breasts (I used 4)
1 cup water
1 cup dry sherry (or cooking sherry)
1/2 tsp curry
1 medium onion, coarsely chopped
1 1/2 cups celery, coarsely chopped
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 pound mushrooms, sliced
1/4 cup margarine (or butter)
2 small boxes prepackaged long grain & wild rice mix, prepared accord. to directions, using broth from cooked chicken (or chicken broth)
1 cup sour cream (lowfat, or I use plain yogurt)
1 can cream of mushroom soup (use Campbell's Healthy Request lower salt)
slivered almonds

Place chicken in pan with water, sherry, curry, onion, celery, and salt.ÿ 
Bake 1 hour.ÿ Let cool.ÿ 
Saute mushrooms with butter. 
ÿAdd cooked rice mixture to mushrooms, then add sour cream and soup.ÿ Cut chicken into chunks and combine with rice mixture.ÿ 
Place in greased 9x13 pan.ÿ 
Top mixture with slivered almonds 
Bake for 1 hour.
