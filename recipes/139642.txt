1 pkg. Kraft Deluxe Macaroni & Cheese Dinner
1 lb. ground beef
1 pkg. Talco Bell Taco Seasoning Mix
3/4 cup Sour Cream
1 1/2 cup Shredded Cheddar Cheese
1 cup Taco Bell Thick N' Chunky Salsa (optional)

Heat oven to 400 degrees.  
Prepare dinner as directed on package.  While macaroni is cooking, cook meat with taco seasoning as directed on package. 

Stir sour cream into prepared dinner; spoon 1/2 into 8 inch square baking dish.  Top with layers of meat mixture, 1 cup cheese and remaining dinner mixture; cover with foil. I also add some crushed tortilla chips to it as well for the crush.

Bake 15 minutes; top with salsa and remaining cheese.  Bake, uncovered, 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
