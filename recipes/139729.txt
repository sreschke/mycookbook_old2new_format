Chicken Tenders
Hot Hooter Buffalo Sauce
Pkg. 10" flour tortilla shells
Pkg. 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
Lettuce (Romaine)
Fresh Tomatos
1 1/2 to 2 cups Flour

Put flour in bowl and add salt, pepper, cajun seasoning.

Roll chicken tenders in flour mixture. 
Put grease in skillet, drop in tenders.

Microwave Tortilla shells for 20 seconds

Microwave Hooters sauce for 30 seconds

Dip tenders in sauce and place lenghwise in shells and top with desired toppings.  Roll up and enjoy.
