3/4 Cup Finely Crushed Cornflakes
1/2 tsp. Basil
1/2 tsp. Garlic Powder
1/2 tsp. Freshly Ground Pepper
1/4 tsp. Salt
1/4 tsp. Oregano
2 Whole Boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 Egg, beaten
1 Tbls. Butter, melted

Preheat oven to 400 degrees.  In shallow bowl, combine cornflakes, basil, garlic powder, pepper, salt and oregano.  Dip chicke in egg, then in crumbs.  Place chicken on a rack in baking pan.  Drizzle with butter.  Bake 20 minutes 

Serves: 4
