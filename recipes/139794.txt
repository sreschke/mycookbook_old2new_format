2 Flour tortillas (6 inch)
1 Tbls. Miracle Whip dressing
1/4 tsp. chili powder
4 slices Shaved Oven Roasted Turkey Breast
1 slice Bacon, crisply cooked, drained and crumbled
1 Kraft singles, halved

Preheat, grill to medium heat.  Spread tortilas with dressing, sprinkle with chili powder

Layer half each of the turkey and bacon and 1 singles half on half of each tortila.  Fold over tortilas to cover filling. 

Grill 2 to 3 minutes on each side or until quesadilas are golden brown on both sides and Singles are melted.
