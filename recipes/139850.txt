1 pkg. Chicken Stovetop stuffing
1/2 lb. sausage
1 egg
1 Tbls. minced garlic
1 tsp. parsley flakes
1 - 2 lbs. bacon

Prepare stuffing according to package directions
Let cool

Add rest except bacon

Cut bacon strips into 3 strips

Wrap bacon around stuffing mix

Cook at 375 degrees until bacon is crisp (30 - 50 minutes)
