2 lbs. Chicken
2 Cans Cream of chicken
2 Cans Rotel Tomatoes
1 Box Velvetta - small cubes
1 Bag Tortilla Chips (10 oz.)

Boil chicken.

In pan, simmer cream of chicken soup, rotel and velvetta until melted. 

In casserole dish, crumble bag of tortilla chips.  Then pour mixture over top.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes.
