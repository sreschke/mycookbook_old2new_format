3 cups mashed sweet potatoes
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1/3 cup milk
1/2 cup butter, softened
Beat together above ingredients until smooth; spread in 9 x 13 pan.

Combine: 
1 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup flour
1/3 cup butter, softened
1 cup pecans, finely chopped
Sprinkle on top of potato mixture.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
