4 c. sliced, pared baking
apples (about 4 medium)
2/3 to 3/4 c. packed brown
sugar
1/2 c. all-purpose flour
1/2 c. regular oats
3/4 tsp. ground cinnamon
3/4 tsp. ground nutmeg
1/3 c. margarine or butter,
softened

Heat oven to 375 degrees. Grease baking pan, 8 x 8 x 2-inches.
Arrange apples in pan. Mix remaining ingredients with fork.
Sprinkle over apples. Bake until apples are tender and topping
is golden brown, about 30 minutes. Serve with cream, ice cream
or hard sauce, if desired. Serves 6.
Cherry Crisp: Substitute 1 can (21 ounce) cherry pie
filling for the apples and use lesser amount of sugar.
Peach Crisp: Substitute 1 can (29 ounce) sliced peaches, drained, for the apples and use lesser amount of sugar.
