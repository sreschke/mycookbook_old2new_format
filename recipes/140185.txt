Brown 1lb. ground beef, 1 chopped onion      
2 cloves garlic or 1 tsp.garlic powder
16oz. stewed tomatoes
15oz. tomatoe sauce
4oz. green chilies
2 c. water
8oz. salsa
1 can kidney beans (juice and all)
1 can pinto beans (juice and all)
1 pkg. taco seasoning
mix all together. Simmer 3 hrs. or in crockpot on low all day. 

Serve with taco chips or crackers. You may also garnish with sour cream, or grated cheese.
