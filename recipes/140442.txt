
     2 cups flour
     4 teaspoons baking powder
     pinch salt
     4 tablespoons butter
     1 cup milk
     butter -- softened
     brown sugar
     cinnamon

     Mix flour, baking powder and salt. Cut in butter until the consistency of oatmeal. Add
     milk gradually until mixture forms a ball. Roll out on a floured surface, spread with
     softened butter and sprinkle with brown sugar and cinamon. Roll up and pinch seam
     together. Cut into 12 pieces. Bake at 400* for 10-15 minutes, or until bottoms brown.

     - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - 

     NOTES : So very easy, no waiting for the dough to rise. Mix them up in 10 minutes or
     less!
