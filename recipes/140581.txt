1/2 cup vegtable oil
4 squares unsweetened chocolate melted  (4 oz)
2 cups sugar
4 eggs
2 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup confectioners sugar (do not add to blend - use to roll dough balls in)

Mix oil, chocolate, sugar.  Blend in one egg at a time.  Add vanilla.  Stir in flour, baking powder and salt.

Chill several hours.  Heat oven to 350.   Roll into balls w/ confectioners sugar.  Cook on ungreased cookie sheet 14-15 minutes.
