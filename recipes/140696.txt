Serving Size  : 4   

Venison steaks
1 1/4  teaspoons     garlic salt
1/2  teaspoon      pepper
2      tablespoons   soy sauce
1      tablespoon    ketchup
1      tablespoon    vegetable oil
1/4  teaspoon      crushed oregano

Mix all ingredients except steak in large shallow 
container.  Add meat, cover and marinate 4 hours in 
refrigerator.  Remove from marinade and broil or 
barbecue to desired doneness.
