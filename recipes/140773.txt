Medium Crock Pot
1 tablespoon Grated ginerroot
1 tsp minced garlic
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1 cup fat free beef broth
1 tbs reduced sodium teriyaki sauce
3/4 lb pork tenderloing
1 1/2 lb sweet potatoes, peeled and quartered
2 onions, cut into wedges

Combine gingerroot, garlic, pepper and teriyaki in small bowl. rub over the pork. Pour broth into crock pot and add the pork(half if necessary).  arrange potatoes and onions around the edges.

cover and cook on low until the pork is cooked through and a meat thermometer registers 160, 6 to 8 hours.  let rest 10 minutes befor slicing.
