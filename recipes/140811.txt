1 1/2 lbs beef steak, cut 1/4" thick
2TB butter or Marg.
1 clove garlic, crushed or minced
1TB onion
1/2 tsp salt
sprinkle of cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp celery seed
2TB Mustard
1c strong beef broth (dissolve 2 cubes in 1 c. boiling water)

1. cut steak into 2x 1/2" strips; brown in hot butter
2. Add a mixture of remaining ingredients, stir to mix
Cover and simmer 25 to 30 or until meat is fork tender. Serve immediately. (6 serivings)
