8 to 10 slices bread, torn in pieces
5 eggs
2 cups milk, divided
1/2 tsp. ground mustart
1/4 tesp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 pound bulk pork sausage
1 can (10-3/4 ounces) condensed cream of mushroom soup, undiluted 
1/2 cup shredded Gruyere cheese
1/2 cup shredded Swiss chees

Arrange bread in an ungreased 13-in.x9 in. baking dish. Whisk together the eggs, 1-1/2 cups milk and seasonings; pour over bread and set aside.

Cook sausage over medium heat, drain.  Spoon over bread. Combine soup, cheeses and remaining milk; spread over sausage

Bake, uncovered, 350 degrees for 45-50 minutes or until the top is puffed and center appears set. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving.
