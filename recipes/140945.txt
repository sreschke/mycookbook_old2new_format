1 shole garlic bulb
3 tesp. olive oil, divided
2-1/2 pounds sweet potatoes (about 4 large), peeled and cut into 1/2-inch slice
2 large onions, cut into wedges
6 cups chicken broth, divided
2 Tblsp. minced fresh parsley
1 Tblsp. minced fresh thyme or 1 tsp. dried thyme
1/4 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper

Remove papery outer skin from garlic bulb (do not peel or separate cloves). Cut top off bulb; brush with 1/2 tsp oil. Wrap in heavy-duty foil.

Place sweet potatoes and onions in a 15-in. x 10-in baking pan coated with cooking spray. Drizzoe with remaining oil; toss to coat.

Bake garlic and vegetables 425 degrees for 30-35 minutes or until tender. Cool for 10-15 minutes.

Place 1-1/2 cups broth, parsley, thyme, salt and pepper in a blender. Squeeze softened garlic into mixture, cover and process until smooth. Transfer toa large saucepan.

In batches, process the sweet potatoes, onions and remaining broth until smooth; add to garlic mixture. Cook, stirring occasionally, until heated through.

Yeild: 10 servings (2-1/2 quarts)
