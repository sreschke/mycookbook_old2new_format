1 bag frozen hash browns
1/2 cup melted butter
1/4 tsp pepper
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 can cr chicken or mushroom soup
1 pt sour cream
1 cup crushed corn flakes
1/2 stick melted butter
shredded cheddar cheese

Thaw hash browns, place in 9x13 pan. Pour 1/2 cup butter over potatoes, sprinkle with salt and pepper. Mix in onions.  Mix soup and sour cream together and spread over potatoes.  Sprinkle with cheese and corn flakes. Pour 1/2 stick melted butter over all.  Bake 350 for 45 mins.
