4 or 5 pound chicken
3 Tsp salt
1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup flour
2 cups milk
2 bunches broccoli
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
3/4 cup Hollandaise sauce
1/2 cup heavy cream, whipped
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
1 jigger sherry
salt to taste

Place chicken in a pot with enough water to cover. Add salt. Bring to a low boil and simmer until tender. When chicken is cool, remove skin and slice meat..
In a medium saucepan, melt the butter and slowly whisk in flour. Add milk, stirring until sauce is thickened and smooth. Set aside and keep hot. Simmer broccoli in salted water until tender. Drain well and keep warm. Cover a heat proof serving platter with broccoli, then layer sliced chicken on top..
Combine Hollandaise sauce with cream sauce. Add whipped cream, sherry and worcestershire sauce. Pour over chicken and broccoli. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Place under broiler until cheese is well browned.
