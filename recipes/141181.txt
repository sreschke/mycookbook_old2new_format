6 eggs
2 cups Milk
1 lb. Sausage, cooked & drained
3-6 slices of cubed bread (cover bottom of dish)
1 Tbs. Mustard
2 cups shredded cheese

Line dish (9X13) with bread cubes (spray with Pam or butter)
Mix all other ingredients and pour over bread. Let sit overnight in refrigerator. Bake for 1 hour at 325 degrees.
