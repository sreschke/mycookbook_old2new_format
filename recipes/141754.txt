Ingredients:
3 cups uncooked Rotini pasta
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1 pound boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
1 shallot, sliced
1/4 cup finely-chopped red bell pepper
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 (28 oz.) can crushed tomatoes
1/4 cup heavy whipping cream
1 Tbsp. Sweet Italian Herb Blend Seasoning
2 cups packed fresh baby spinach
Parmesan cheese (optional)

Directions:
1. Cook pasta according to box directions; drain well, set aside and keep warm.

2. In large Dutch oven, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add chicken, shallot, bell pepper, and garlic; saute for 7 minutes or until chicken is done. Add tomatoes, cream, and herb blend, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, stir in pasta and spinach. Serve immediately with Parmesan cheese, if desired.
