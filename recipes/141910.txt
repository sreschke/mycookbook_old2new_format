Unsalted butter, room temp for pan
3/4 cup sugar, plus more for pan
1 1/2 lbs. fresh whole milk ricotta cheese, pureed in a food processor until smooth
6 lg eggs, seperated
1/4 cup all pupose flour
Finely grated zest of 1 orange or 2 lemons
1/4 tsp. salt

1. Preheat oven to 375. Generously butter & sugar a 9-inch springform pan (3 in. deep). Whisk together ricotta, egg yolks, flour, 6 Tbs. sugar, the zest, & salt in a large bowl.
2. Whisk egg whites with a mixer on low speed until foamy. Raise speed to high, & gradually add remaining 6 Tbs. sugar, whisking until stiff, glossy peaks form, 3-4 minutes. Gently fold 1/3 of the whites into ricotta mixture using a rubber spatula until just combined. 
3. Pour batter into pan, & bake until center is firm & top is deep golden brown, about 1 hour. Let cool on a wire rack for about 10 minutes. Run a knife around edge of cake; release sides to remove from pan, & let cool completely.
