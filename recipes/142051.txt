Prep: 10 minutes
Bake: 35 minutes
Serves: 8

2 cans (10 3/4 oz. each) Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup 
1 cup milk 
2 cups frozen peas 
2 cans (10 oz. each) tuna, drained
4 cups hot cooked medium egg noodles 
2 tbsp. dry bread crumbs 
1 tbsp. butter, melted

Stir soup, milk, peas, tuna and noodles in 3-qt. casserole.

Bake at 400Ã¸F. 30 min. or until tuna mixture is hot and bubbling. Stir tuna mixture.

Stir bread crumbs and butter in small bowl. Sprinkle over tuna mixture. Bake 5 min. or until bread crumbs are browned.

Serve with steamed cut green beans. For dessert serve fresh orange wedges.
