Heat oven to 375.

Cook 1C chopped broccoli in skillet sprayed with cooking spray for 8-10 min. Stirring occasionally. Set aside.

Line 9" pie plate with 1 ready to use refigerated pie crust. Fold over edge.

Beat 5 eggs, .33C miracle whip, .33C milk.

Stir in cooked vegetables, 6oz. Velveeta cut into .5in. cubes.

Pour into crust.

Bake 40 min.
