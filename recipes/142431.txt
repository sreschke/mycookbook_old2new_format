1  1/2 c flour
1 T sugar
1/2 t baking powder
1/2 t salt
2 c milk
2 eggs
2 T margarine/butter melted
1/2 t vanilla

Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.  Stir in remaining ingredients.  beat with hand beater until smooth.  Lightly butter 6-8 inch skillet; heat over medium heat until bubbly.  Por scant 1/4 cup of the batter into skillet; immediatly rotate skillet until thin film covers bottom.

Cook until light brown. Run wide spatula around edge to loosen; turn and cook other side until light brown.  Stack crepes, placing waxed papaer between each.  Keep covered.
