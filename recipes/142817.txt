1 tbsp vegetable oil
3/4 pound pork tenderloin
2 pkg pork top ramen
1 1/2 c water
1 red bell pepper, cut into 3/4 inch pieces (1 cup)
1 c broccoli flowerets
4 green onions, cut into 1 inch pieces (1/2 cup)
1 tbsp fresh parsley
1 tbsp soy sauce

Heat oil in skillet.  Cook veggies until tender crisp. Add pork, stir fry about 5 minutes until pork is no longer pink.  Break apart noodles, stir noodles, seasoning, and remaining ingredients into pork.  Heat to boiling.  Boil 3 to 4 minutes until noodles are completely softened.
