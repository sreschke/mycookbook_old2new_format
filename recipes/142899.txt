Cut dressing ingredients in half.

2 oz. peeled grated ginger root
3/4 cup freshly squeezed lime juice (about 6 limes)
1/4 cup sugar 
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1 teaspoon soy sauce (more to taste, later)
splash of sesame oil
1/4 cup store bought pickled ginger root (to taste)
Julienned green cabbage and carrots
Chopped cilantro
finely crushed peanuts or Asian fried shallots for garnish.

Mix the dressing ingredients in a glass jar.  Can store in frig for awhile.  Shake or stir it up before using.  Pour over the vegetables.  Sprinkle the nuts or shallots over the top.
