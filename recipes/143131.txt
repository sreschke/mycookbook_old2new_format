Ingredients:

9 oz. flank steak
2 plum tomatoes, cut into eighths
1/4 cup sliced onion
1 small clove garlic, crushed
5 cups chopped romaine lettuce
4 tbsp low-fat balsamic vinaigrette
3 tbsp crumbled blue cheese

How to Make It:

Preheat a grill pan on medium high.  Season both sides of the steak with salt and pepper, and cook it about 6 minutes on each side.  Remove the steak, let it rest for 3 to 5 minutes, and then slice it diagonally across the grain into thin strips.  In a large bowl, mix the tomatoes, onion, and garlic.  Add the meat and lettuce, pour on the vinaigrette, and toss well to completely coat everything.  Top with the cheese.  Makes 2 servings.

Per Serving: 
310 calories
32 grams protein
15 grams carbohydrates
15 grams fat (6 grams saturated)
4 grams fiber
630 milligrams sodium

Power Side - Parmesan Squash

Ingredients

4 zucchini
4 yellow squash
2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
salt and pepper

How to Make It

Slice the zucchini and yellow squash into round disks.  Arrange them in a steamer and steam for 5 to 7 minutes.  Remove, pat dry with a paper towel, and toss with the cheese.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.  Makes 4 servings.

Per Serving

70 calories
6 grams protein
13 grams carbohydrates
1.5 grams fat (0.5 grams saturated)
4 grams fiber
65 milligrams sodium
