6 graham crackers, crushed
1 cup plus 3 Tbsp sugar, divided
3 Tbsp. butter or margarine, melted
5 pkg (8 oz. each) cream cheese, softened
3 Tbsp. flour
1 Tbsp. vanialla
1 cup sour cream
4 eggs
1 can 21 (oz.) cherry pie filling

Heat oven to 325F.  Line 13-9-inch pan with foil. Mix crumbs, 3 Tbsp. sugar and butter; press onto bottom of pan. Bake 10 min.

Beat cream cheese, sugar, flour and vililla until blended.  Add sour cream; blend. Add eggs, 1 at a time, mixing on low speed until blended.

Bake 40  min. or until center is almost set.  Cool completely.  Refrigerate 4 hours. Use foil to lift cheesecake from pan.

Top with pie filling and serve. Makes 16 servings.
