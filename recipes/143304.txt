Preheat oven to 375F, rack in the middle position.

1 cup melted butter (2 sticks)
1 cup white sugar
2 beaten eggs
1 tsp vanilla
3 tablespoons maraschino cherry juice
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 cups chopped pecans
2 cups flour (not sifted)

approximately 2 cups corn flakes, crushed (measure before crushing)
1 small jar of maraschino cherries for garnish

Melt the butter and add the white sugar. Then add the eggs. Stir it all up with the vanilla, cherry juice, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Add the chopped pecans and the flour, mix well. 

Crush the corn flakes and put them in a small bowl. 

Roll dough balls with your hands about the size of unshelled walnuts. Roll the dough balls in the crushed corn flakes and place them on a greased cookie sheet, 12 to a standard sheet. Smush them down a bit so they will not roll. 

Cut the cherries into quarters and place one on the top of each cookie. Press the cherry down with the tip of your finger. 

Bake at 375F for 10 to 12 minutes, or until nicely browned. Cool on the cookie sheet for 2 minutes, then transfer to wire rack to finish cooling.

Yield: 6 to 7 dozen
