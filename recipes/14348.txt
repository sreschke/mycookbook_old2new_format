2 pounds medium shrimp, shelled and deveined
3 green onions, cut into 1 inch pieces
3 Tbsp lemon juice
1 1/2 tsp salt
3 Tbsp butter
3 whole chicken breasts, boned and cut in half
3/4 pound thinly sliced mushrooms
1 1/3 cups water
1/3 cup flour
1 envelope chicken flavored bouillon
1 1/2 cups half and half cream
3/4 cup champagne

Combine shrimp, green onions, lemon juice and salt. Set aside. In a skillet, melt butter and cook chicken breasts until well browned and fork tender. Remove chicken to a platter and keep warm.

In the same skillet, cook shrimp mixture until shrimp turn pink. Place on platter with chicken and keep warm.

In a small bowl, combine water, flour and bouillon. Stir this mixture into the hot liquid in the skillet until blended. Gradually add half & half and champagene. Cook, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens and boils. Stir in mushrooms and heat thoroughly. Pour over chicken and shrimp.
