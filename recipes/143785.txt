1 cup sugar
3 tbsp butter
2 1/2 tbsp cornstarch
1 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp apple pie spice
1 cup water
4-5 cups peaches pealed and sliced

  Combine all ing. bring to a boil pour into deep dish pie crust and bake @ 425 on bottom rack for 30 min. 
  Pie has top crust w/holes poked into it.
  Pastry - 2 crust of Pat Kenedys pie crust.
