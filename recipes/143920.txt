4 oz of pancetta diced
1 head of brocolli, cut into florets
9-12 oz pasta
1/4 cup butter
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tbsp lemon zest
2 tsp lemon juice
2 tsp fresh thyme
1/2 cup parmesan cheese

saute pancetta till almost crispy; remove from pan and drain on paper towel.

cook broccoli in large pot of salted boiling water till crisp tender. remove with slotted spoon; add pasta to same boiling water.

drain pasta, return to same pot; add next 5 ingredients, toss to coat. add pancetta, broccoli & cheese; season with pepper
