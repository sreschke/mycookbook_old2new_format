6 oz prosciutto
1/2 lb mushrooms, caps only
1 large shallot finely chopped
2 tbsp butter
3 sage leaves/or 1/2 tsp dried sage
14 oz (appx) straw and hay pasta
1 cup heavy cream
1/4 cup chopped parsley
1/4 cup grated cheese

cut pros. into matchstick pieces; slice mushrooms.

heat (med high) butter, shallots, and sage in a nonstick pan,till butter is melted.. Add mushrooms; cook for 1 min, then ad pros.

cook pasta till al dente.
reserve 1 cup of pasta liquid 

add cream to pan; simmer for 1 min. stir in cheese and parsley, remove from heat.

drain pasta, and return to pot. pour sauce over pasta, and thin as needed with pasta water.
