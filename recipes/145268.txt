First Layer:
2 cups crushed pretzels
4 Tablespoons sugar
3/4 cups melted butter
Mix and spread in a 9x13 inch pan. Bake at 400 degrees for 6 minutes.  Let cool.

Second Layer:
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1/2 cup sugar
9 oz Cool Whip
Mix and spread on cooled crust.

Third Layer:
1 (6 oz.) strawberry Jello
2 (10 oz) pkgs. frozen strawberries
Dissolve Jello in 2 cups of boiling water. Add frozen strawberries. Chill until slightly thickened. Spread over cream cheese mixture. Chill.
