2 cups dark brown sugar  2 sticks butter  
1/2 c. light karo syrup  1 t. salt  1 t. baking soda
6 quarts of popped corn
Boil brown sugar, butter, syrup, and salt for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and add baking soda.  Stir well over popped corn.  Spread in 2- 9x13x2 cake pans.  Place in 200 degree oven for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.  Remove from oven and pour onto wax paper.  Separate and cool.  Stir in covered container to keep crisp.
