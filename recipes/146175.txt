Several slices bacon in large skillet.  Cook until crisp.  Remove bacon add 1 green pepper, 1 med onion diced to hot oil and saute add 4T chili powder, 1 can diced tomatoes and heat through.  Add 1 head of shredded cabbage.  Heat but not to long stir so everything is thoroughly mixed.  Remove from heat serve warm sprinkle crushed bacon on top.
