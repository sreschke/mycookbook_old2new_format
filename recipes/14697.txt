1 can crescent rolls
2 oz. cooked chicken
1 oz monterey jack cheese
8 oz. mushrooms
 Separate dough into 4 rectangles. Press perfs to seal. Press each into a 5x6 rectangle. Place 1 slice of cheese, slice of chicken and 1 TBsp. mushrooms in center. Fold dough in half forming a square; pinch to seal. 
Bake at 375 on Pizza Stone for 20-25 min. Serve with Gravy if desired.
