2 tbsp olive oil
4 (5-6oz) boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 cups sliced mushrooms
1 large onion, thinly sliced
1 (28 oz) can diced tomatoes undrained
1 (7.5 oz) jar marinated artichoke hearts, drained and quartered
1 tsp hot sauce
2 cups fresh baby spinach leaves

1. In a large skilled heat oil over medium-high heat. Season chicken with salt and pepper to taste. Add chicken to skilled; cook 4 minutes per side or until browned. Transfer to a plate; set aside.
2. Add mushrooms and onion to skillet; cook over medium heat until onion is tender. Stir in tomatoes, artichokes and hot sauce. Return chicken to skillet. Simmer, uncovered, 20-30 minutes or until an instant-read thermometer inserted into chicken registers 165f.
3. Stir in spinach; cook just until wilted. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and serve.
