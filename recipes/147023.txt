1 Large can Evaporated Milk
1/8 cup Butter
4 cups Sugar
2 Pkgs Chocolate (6 oz each)
1/2 Jar Marshmellow Cream

combine milk/butter/sugar in sauce pan
Cook to 234 degrees, or until it forms soft ball in cold water
Stir often and remove from heat
Fold in Chocolate and Marshmellow
Add chopped nuts if desired
Pour into greased 8x13 pan
Refrigerate until firm
Cut into pieces
ENjoy
