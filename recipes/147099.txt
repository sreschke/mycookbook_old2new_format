1 can (28oz.) crushed tomatoes
4 cloves garlic
3 tsp. dried oregano
1/2 tsp. Kosher salt
1/2 tsp. red pepper flakes.
2 containers (15oz. each) ricotta
2 cups (8 oz.) grated moarella
1/4 cup grated parmesan
12 lasaga noodles
5 oz. baby spinach (optional)
1 pound browned ground beef.

In a medium bowl combine the tomatoes, garlic, oregano, salt, red pepper, and browned ground beef.
In a seperate bowl, mix the ricotta, 1 cup of the the mozzarella and Parmesan.

in the bottom of a 5-6 quart slow cooker, spread a thin layer of the suace. Top with 3 of the noodles (breaking to fit as necessary). Spread about 1 cup of the sauce over the noodles and layer with 1 1/2 cups of the the cheese mixture. repeat twice more with the noodles, sauce and cheese mixture. Top with remaining 3 noodles, sauce and 1 cup mozzarella.

cook on low, covered until the noodles are tender-especially in the center of the lasagna. About 3 - 3 1/2 hours.
