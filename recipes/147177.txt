BIEROCKS

1 lb. ground beef
1  large onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 1/2 tsp. salt (more or less, to taste)
1/2 head cabbage shredded
1 tube refrigerator Crescent rolls

Brown beef with onion and garlic; drain. If beef is very lean, you may need to add a tablespoon of olive oil for best browning. Thinly slice the cabbage, as you would for cole slaw. Add it to the drained beef, brown. 

Using Crescent roll dough, place 2 tablespoons of the mixture on each dough square. Bring 4 corners together and pinch the ends.

Place pinched side down on greased baking sheet.

Bake at 375Ã¸F for 20 minutes, or until brown.
