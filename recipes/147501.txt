Preparation Time: 20 minutes*     Cook Time:9 minutes* 

Ingredients:

1 1/2 cups of milk
1 (3.3-oz.) package vanilla instant pudding mix
1/2 (8-oz.) container fat-free whipped topping 
4 ripe peaches, halved, pitted and peeeled
3 tbsp. butter 
3 tbps. packed brown sugar
1 1/2 tsp. fresh lemon juice 
1 tsp. pure vanilla extract 
Praline-coated pecans or praline cookies 

Steps:
1. In a medium bowl and using an electri mixer beat milk and pudding mix 2 minutes or until thickened. Stir in whipped topping; mix well. Refrigerate, covered, until well-chilled. 

2. Coat grill rack with nonstick cooking spray. Preheat grill to medium (300 to 350 degrees). Place peaches on grill. Grill 3 to 4 minutes per side. Transfer to a cutting board; chop peaches. Place in a large bowl; set aside. 

3. In a deep, 1-quart microwave-safe bowl combine butter and sugar. Microwave on HIGH 30 to 60 seconds, carefully stirring for every 30 seconds. (Mixture will be very hot and sticky.) Stir in lemon juice and vanilla; let cool 5 minutes. Add to peaches ; stir to coat. 

4. In each of 4 parfait glasses layer 2 1/2 tablespoons of the peaches and 1/4 cup pudding mixture; repeat layer twice. Garnish with praline pecans, and serve. 

Serving:4

* chill time and 5 minutes cooling time extra.
