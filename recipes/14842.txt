1/2 lb beef
1/2 lb italian sausage
1 onion
1 can (29 oz) tomato sauce
1 can (6 oz) tomato paste
6 oz can water
1 tsp basil
1 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp garlic powder

Brown beef with onion until fully cooked. Drain.  Add remaining ingredients and simmer for 15 minutes.

Makes 4 servings
