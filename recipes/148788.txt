Corn Casserole*
Yield: 4 Servings
2 tbsp butter 1 tsp salt
2 tbsp flour 1/4 tsp pepper
1 cup milk 1 Tbsp sugar
1 can corn, drained
2 extra large eggs, beaten
Preheat oven to 350Ã¸F. Melt butter in a sauce pan. Mix in flour and stir until thickened. Slowly add milk; stir constantly. Add remaining ingredients to pan and remove from heat. Pour into a lightly greased 9x9 casserole dish. Bake for 25 minutes or until mixture has set and slightly browned.
