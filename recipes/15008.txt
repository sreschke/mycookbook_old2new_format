Barbecups
Servings: makes 6 cups.
A half pound ground beef
1/4 c. barbecue sauce
1 tsp. minced onions
1 tsp. brown sugar
A can of your favorite biscuits
1/2 c. shredded cheddar

Brown ground beef in large skilet. Drain and add barbecue sauce,onions brown sugar. Set aside.  Separate and place 6 biscuits in muffin tins pressing dough up sides of cups.  Spoon in meat mixture.Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake at 400 for 10 to 12 min.
