3 Tbsp fat
2 medium onions, sliced
1 clove minced garlic
3 1/2 pounds pot roast
2 tsp salt
1 cup thick sour cream
1 cup water
4 or 5 oz pkg noodles

In a Dutch oven lightly brown onion and garlic in fat. Push to one side of pan. Rub meat with salt and brown well. Combine sour cream with water and pour over meat. Top with browned onion, cover and cook over low heat or in 325 oven for 2 1/2 hours, or until tender. Cook noodles. Remove roast from pan and make gravy from drippings. Season to taste with salt pepper and paprika.
