1 butternut squash (about 2 1/2 pounds), peeled, halved lengthwise, seeded, and cut into 1 inch cubes

1/4 cup packed dark brown sugar
3 TBSP melted butter
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Preheat oven to 425 degrees.  Line rimmed baking sheet with aluminum foil and coat lightly with cooking spray.

Toss ingredients together and transfer to baking sheet. Bake for about 45 minutes and stir every 15 minutes..
