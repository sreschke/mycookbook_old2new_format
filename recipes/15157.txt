 Double Chocolate Treasures

Categories    : Cookies

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
  12      ounces        semisweet chocolate chips
     1/2  cup           margarine
     3/4  cup           sugar
   2                    eggs
   1      teaspoon      vanilla
   2      cups          quick-cooking oats
   1 1/2  cups          flour
   2      teaspoons     baking powder
     1/4  teaspoon      salt
     1/2  cup           powdered sugar

Oven to 350 degrees. In heavy saucepan over low heat, melt 1 cup chocolate
chips. Stir until smooth; cool slightly. Beat together margarine and sugar
until light and fluffy. Blend in eggs, vanilla and melted chocolate. Add
combined dry ingredients except powdered sugar. Stir in remaining chocolate
chips. Shape dough into 1" balls; roll in powdered sugar, coating heavily.
Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 10-12 min. Cool 1 min. on cookie
sheet; remove to wire rack. Store in airtight container.
