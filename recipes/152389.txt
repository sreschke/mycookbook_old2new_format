2 cups dry bread crumbs (I think she meant real bread)
4 cups milk scalded
2 eggs
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 c raisin's (optional)

Preheat oven 350

Soak bread crumbs in milk until soft.  Beat eggs until light.  Add sugar, vanilla, nutmeg and raisins (if using).  Mix thoroughly with bread mixture.

Pour into greased baking dish and set in pan of water.  Bake at 350 for one hour or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.
