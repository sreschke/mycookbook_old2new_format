1. Preheat oven to 350.  Grease 9 inch round cake pan.
2. Beat together egg yolks and vanilla.  Beat in half of sugar.
3. Beat together egg whites and salt.  Beat remaining sugar until stiff.  
4. Folk yoke into egg whites.  Sift flour over mixture.  Pour batter into prepared pan.  
5. Bake cake until top springs back when lightly pressed, 25 minutes.  Loosen cake by running a metal spatula around sides of pan.  Invert cake onto wire rack.  Remove pan, leaving waxed paper on cake.  Turn cake right side up.  Cool completely on rack.  
6. Prepare filling, by mixing ingredients in a saucepan.  Bring to a boil over medium heat.  Boil 1 minute whisking constantly.  Strain through a fine sieve into a bowl.  Press plastic wrap on surface.  Chill for 30 minutes.  
7. Cut cake horizontally in half.  Place bottom layer on a serving plate.  Spread evenly with filling.  Top with remaining cake layer.  
8. To prepare glaze, in a saucepan, bring sugar, corn syrup, and water to a boil over low heat, stirring constantly, until sugar has dissolved.  Remove from heat.  Add chocolate; let stand for 1 minute.  Whisk until smooth.  Gradually pour glaze over cake, allowing it to drip down sides.  Let stand until glaze sets.
