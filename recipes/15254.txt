Yield: 2 dozen 

12 oz Twizzlers; chocolate flavor      
4 oz Milk chocolate candy melts 

  Cut twizzlers into 1 1/2" pieces.  Slice each piece in half lengthwise. On waxed paper place 4 legs (pieces) on each side and then drop 1 tsp melted candy in middle for body.  Use a toothpick to
smooth to uniform circle and connect all candy pieces.  Cool completely before removing from waxed paper.  

Source:  Gifts That Taste Good  (ISBN 0-942237-09-9)
