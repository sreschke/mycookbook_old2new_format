Ingredients
 
 40 Saltines
 2 Sticks of Butter
 1 Cup of Sugar(can use brown sugar)
 2 Cups Chocolate Chips
 2 Cups Chopped Pecans or Walnuts

Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Place crackers side by side in a foil lined cookie sheet. Combine the butter and sugar in a sauce pan. Bring to a boil , stirring constantly. Cook for 3 minutes and then pour over crackers. Bake for 5 minutes. Sprinkle the chocolate chips over baked crackers and let stand for 5 minutes. Then spread melted chocolate over the top and sprinkle with nuts. Chill in the refrigerator until firm. Break into pieces and store in a covered airtight container in the refrigerator.
