2 1/4 c flour
1/2 tsp salt
2 tbs sugar
1 c cold cubed butter
4 tbs ice water

Use food processor to combine 1st 4 ingredients and bring butter to pea size pieces then add water 1 tablespoon at a time until dough forms a ball.  Divide into two equal balls and place in saran wrap , form into disc and refriderate for 30 minutes.
