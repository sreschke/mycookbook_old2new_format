1 pkg (3 oz) orange jello
1 pkg (3 oz) cream cheese
1 can (8 1/2 oz) crushed pineapple
1 cup whipping cream

Drain pineapple, reserving juice.  Add water to juice to make 1 cup; heat to boil.  Add boiling liquid to Jello and cream cheese; beat with rotary beater until smooth.  Cool; stirring occasionally.  Whip cream to stiff peaks.  Fold cream and pineapple into cooled Jello/cream cheese mixture.  Chill until firm.  8 servings.

Note: you can add a can of drained mandarin oranges along with the pineapple.
