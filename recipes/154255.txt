topping:
1 1/4 c - 2 c chopped pecans
1 1/2 t cinnamon
1/2 c Brown sugar
2 T maple syrup
up to 1 stick butter (optional)

2 eggs beaten
2 c milk
dash salt
1 t vanilla
1 t brown sugar
1 T sugar
1/2 t cinnamon

mix all together.  Cube up cinnamon raisin bread, pour mixture over bread, soak overnight.  Combine topping mixture, brumble over top, bake at 350' for 40 minutes.
