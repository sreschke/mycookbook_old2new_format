3 eggs
2/3 c. buttermilk
2 c. sugar
1 T. flour
1/2 c. melted butter
1 1/2 t. vanilla
Pinch of salt
Deep dish pie crust

Beat eggs then combine with buttermilk.  Add other ingr. and blend well.  Pour into pie shell.  Bake 10 min. at 275* then increase oven temp. to 300* and bake an additional 1 hr. 15 min. or until center no longer jiggles.  Pie will get golden brown and start to crack on top, this is normal.
