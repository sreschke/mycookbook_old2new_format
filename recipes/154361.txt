Angel Food cake (this can be purchased from deli)
8 oz cream cheese
2 Cups Powdered Sugar
Small Container Cool Whip
Quart Strawberries
Fruit Glaze (small plastic container)
9 x 13 pan

Tear into pieces 1/2 of Angel Food Cake and put in bottom of pan
Mix the Cream Cheese, Powdered Sugar and Cool Whip together and put on top of the angel food cake.
Then layer the other half of angel food cake in pieces on top to the cream cheese mixture.
Top all of this with the strawberries and fruit glaze mixture and refrigerate.    Crystal Taylor
