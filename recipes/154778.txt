2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 onion, chopped
4 cloves fresh or roasted garlic, minced (I used a jar of roasted garlic)
80 oz chicken broth, 2 1/2 cartons of the 32 oz
10 oz can rotel tomatoes, mild
7 oz can diced green chilis 
4 medium bonless skinless chicken breasts; cooked, shredded, and lightly seasoned with salt and pepper
2 1/2 cups cooked rice of choice, i used instant white
1/4 cup fresh lime juice
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cumin
pinches of kosher salt, fresh cracked black pepper and lawry's garlic salt with parsley to taste
1 bunch of fresh cilantro, chopped
fresh diced avocados, drizzled with lime juice
1 cup shredded cheese
tortilla chips

1. PLACE OIL INTO A LARGE DUTCH OVEN OVER MEDIUM HEAT. SAUTE ONION FOR 5 MINUTES AND THEN ADD GARLIC; COOK FOR 1 MINUTE! STIR IN CHICKEN BROTH, TOMATOES, GREEN CHILIS, CHICKEN BREAST, RICE, LIME JUICE, AND SEASONINGS. COOK FOR 5 MINUTES; TASTE AND SEASON ACCORDING TO LIKING. RIGHT BEFORE SERVING ADD CHOPPED CILANTRO.

2. SERVE SOUP WITH FRESH DICED AVOCADO, SHREDDED CHEESE AND CRUSHED CHIPS IF DESIRED.  IF YOU CAN HANDLE THE HEAT, SQUEEZE SOME SIRACHA OVER THE TOP...YUM YUM!!!
