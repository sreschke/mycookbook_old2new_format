2 cups flour
1 1/4 cups sugar
2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt
2 Cups grated carrots
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 cup nuts
1/2 cup coconut
1 apple, peeled and grated
3 eggs
1 cup oil
2 tsp vanilla

Mix dry ingredients.  Stir in carrots, raisins, nuts, coconut and apple.  Beat eggs with oil and vanilla.  Stir into dry ingredients until combined.  Spoon into cupcake holders.  Bake 350 for 20 minutes.
Makes 24 medium muffins
