 
Small package of presifted flour (2 lbs)
2 cups of warm water
2 packages of Fleischmann's ActiveDry yeast
1/2 cup of sugar
1/4 cup crisco shortening
1 egg
1 tsp of salt

If possible use a Kitchen Aid mixer using the Bread blade 

1.  Put in mixing bowl 2 cups of warm water,yeast, salt and 1/2 cup sugar and mix and knead 

2.  Add 1/2 of the flour and mix until it is blended

3. Add Crisco and one egg  Mix really good

4. Add the rest of flour  After it is mixed it will be perfect but not sticky to the touch.  

Cover with a wet towel and refrid.  It will rise but you can bake anytime.  You can have in refrid for a week. 

**  Make into rolls and let rise for at least 15 minutes or so   Then bake at 350 for 15 minutes or until golden brown
