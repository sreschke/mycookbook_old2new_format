4 cups (1 lb) shredded hash browns 
2 cups whipping cream
1 1/2 cups shredded cheese divided
1/4 cup sliced green onions
1 tsp salt 1/4 tsp black pepper

1)  Preheat oven to 350. Spray 4 1 cup ramekins with cooking spray.
2)  Combine all ingredients with 1 1/4 cup cheese & mix well.
3)  Sponn mixture into ramekins.  Sprinkle with remaining cheese.  Bake 35-45 mins.
