1 Lb Large shrimp
1 tsp Sugar
1 Tbs Soy Sauce
1 1/2 Tbs Cornstarch disolved in 3 Tbs of water
3 Tbs Peanut oil
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 Cup Chicken broth
1 5 oz can sliced water chestnuts
2 Cups Snow peas
1/2 Large onion cut in half crosswise and small wedges
2 Small celery stalks sliced thinly on diagonal

Remove shells from shrimp and slice in half through their length.
Mix the sugar, soy sauce, cornstarch and water.
Blanch the celery if less crunch is desired
Heat oil in wok over high heat and add salt. Turn all the shrimp at once into the wok and stir and cook until shrimp starts to turn white, about 1 to 2 minutes. Add broth, water chestnuts, pea pods, onion and celery. Cover and cook for 1 1/2 minutes; remove cover once to stir.
Remove cover and add the soy sauce mixture. Stir for 30 seconds, or until sauce is slightly thickened. Serve at once.
