
Marcia Connorton 

l lg pkg raspberry jello

Mix with 1 3/4c hot water, chill till cool & thick

2c fresh cranberries

1 apple

1 orange

1/2 c sugar

1 8oz can crushed pineapple, drained

1c chopped nuts

1c celery




Grind cranberries, orange with peel, & apple together.  

Add sugar & stir till disolved.  

Fold into jello

Add nuts, pineapple, celery

Refrig till firm.
