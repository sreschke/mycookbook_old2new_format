Makes 4 servings
1 cup plain yogurt
3 tbs sugar
1 cup whipping cream
2 tsp gelatin
3 tbs hot water

Mix yogurt and sugar in bowl. In different bowl, mix whipping cream until it has the same texture as the yogurt. Put hot water in small bowl and add gelatin while mixing to avoid clumping, continue stirring until gelatin is completely melted. Pour water and gelatin mix into yogurt, stirring quickly to keep gelatin from clumping up. Fold in whipped cream and pour mixture into glass cups. Chill for an hour. Serve with whipped cream and fruit on top and enjoy!
