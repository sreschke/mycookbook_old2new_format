1 lemon
Salt
Garlic
1 lb green beans, trimmed
Olive oil

DO AHEAD OF TIME AND HAVE PAN READY.

Juice lemon and add juice to boiling water with salt. Boil green beans until just al dente. Put in ice bath to cool down and drain.

Put olive oil in pan with 5-6 cloves of garlic sliced thin. Zest lemon into oil. Put salt/pepper. Let sit. When ready to cook, add beans and warm!
