6 Eggs
2 Sticks of Butter
3 Cups of Sugar
8 oz sour cream
3 Cups of Flour
Pinch of Salt
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp lemon
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp almond

Preheat Oven to 325.  Cream butter and mix in sugar.  Beat eggs two at a time into butter and sugar mixture.  Add flavoring.  Put salt and soda in first cup of flower.  Beat in one cup of flour.  Half of sour cream.  One cup of flour.  2nd half of sour cream.  Last cup of flour.  Bake in greased pans a 325.  Large stem pans take about 1:30.  Long loaf pans take a little over an hour to 1:20. Place greased wax paper in bottom of pans.
