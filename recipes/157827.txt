Fish Tacos with Chipotle Cream and/or Lime Parsley Crema 

Fish:
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
1/4 teaspoon salt
Freshly ground black pepper
Smoked paprika, cumin, coriander (if you want)
1 pound white flaky fish fillet, like tilapia or halibut

Chipotle cream:
1/2 cup plain nonfat yogurt
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
2 teaspoons+ chipotle pepper, in adobo sauce (or more if you like)
8 (6-inch) corn tortillas
1 1/2 cups shredded green cabbage or lettuce
1/2 cup fresh corn kernels 
1/4 cup fresh parsley leaves

Lime Parsley Crema:
1/4 cup thinly sliced green onions
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
3 tablespoons fat-free mayonnaise
3 tablespoons reduced-fat sour cream
1 teaspoon grated lime rind
1 1/2 teaspoons fresh lime juice
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 garlic clove, minced

Garnishes
Thin sliced cabbage
Fresh corn
Thin sliced red onion
Lime wedges


Directions

In small bowl, whisk together oil, lime juice, salt & pepper. Pour over fish fillets and let marinate for 20 minutes. Put yogurt into strainer lined with paper towel and place over  bowl to drain and thicken for 30 minutes.

Remove fish from marinade and grill on nonstick grill pan over medium-high heat until cooked through, about 3 min per side. Set fish aside on a plate for 5 minutes.

In small bowl combine thickened yogurt, mayonnaise, and chipotle pepper.

Heat the tortillas on grill pan for 30 seconds on each side.

Flake fish with fork. Top each tortilla with 1 tablespoon of crema. Top with fish, cabbage, corn and parsley and serve with lime wedges.

NOTE: MAKE THE CHIPOTLE CREAM & CREMA EARLY IN THE DAY.
