INGREDIENTS
================================
3/4 cup fine cornmeal (do not use coarsely ground cornmeal)
1/2 cup flour
1/4 cup corn starch
1 teaspoon garlic powder
2 teaspoon black pepper
2 teaspoon paprika
1/2 teaspoon cayenne
1/4 teaspoon celery seed
2 TB Konriko Cajun seasoning

Oil for frying (use peanut oil if you can)

DIRECTIONS
======================================
Place fish in milk, buttermilk, milk + vinegar, or kiefer. Marinate if you wish.

Remove fish from milk and while wet put in ziplock bag with mix. Shake bag to completely coat the fish. Deep fry in fryer @ 400F until golden brown.

Remove fish and let drain onpaper towels or cooling rack and then serve.
