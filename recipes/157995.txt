Half Lemon
3 - 4 drops of Honey

Half lemon and add 3 - 4 drops of honey.  Rub the lemon on your face, emphasize the blackheads prone areas like the nose, chin, etc.  Leave the lemon and honey mixture on your face for 5 minutes, then wash it with cold water.  You will see the results immediateley.  Additionally, lemon juice will also fade other marks/spots on your face and honey will moisturize.
