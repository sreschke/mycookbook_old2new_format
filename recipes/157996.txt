1 Graham Cracker crust
2 - 14 oz cans sweetened condensed milk
1 small container of Cool Whip
1 pkg mini chocolate chips


1.  Peel paper from cans of sweetened condensed milk.  Place cans on their sides in a large pot and cover completely in cold water.  Slowly bring pot to a roiling boil for 3 hours making sure to turn cans every 30 minutes or so. 

2. At the end of the 3 hours, allow cans to cool for 1 hour (at least). This is the most important part.  

3. Open cans and spread into pie pan.  Cover with Cool Whip. Sprinkle with Chocolate Chips all over the top. 

4. Refrigerate for 8 hrs.  Delicious!
