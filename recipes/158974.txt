ingredients:
1 pound bacon, cut into thirds
1 pound lil smokies
1 stick butter
2 cups brown sugar

preheat oven 375 degrees

cut bacon into thirds and wrap each smokie.
place all the wrapped smokies in a single layer in a baking dish.
then melt the butter and then add 1 cup of brown sugar, stir until well blended.
pour butter and brown sugar mixture on the smokies and bacon.
take the other cup of sugar, sprinkle over the smokies
bake them for about 15-20 minutes and then turn the heat up to 400 degrees for about 5 minutes or until bacon is crispy.
