This is a recipe I used when I was working as a caterer.

3 heads shredded Boston lettuce
1 1/2 cups beef, in julienne
1 1/2 cups boiled ham, in julienne
1 1/2 cups celery root, in julienne
12 halved artichoke hearts
1 1/2 cups poached chicken, cubed
capers
chopped chives

Mix everything but capers and chives. Then sprinkle top of salad with capers and chives. Serve with an herb type dressing, or with a Crab Louis dressing.
