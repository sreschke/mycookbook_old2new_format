4 lbs Potatoes
2 cups of any kind of salt
1/2 a stick of salted butter.
Water

Put enough water in a pot so that it will cover the potatoes and bring to a boil. Once boiling add the salt then the potatoes and boil for 20 minutes. Shut off and let sit for up to 30 minutes. 

Put the potatoes in to a bowl and add the butter. Mix to melt and serve.
