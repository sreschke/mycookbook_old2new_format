Ingredients


2 tablespoons canola oil
1/2 cup (heaping) golden or brown raisins
1 large white onion, halved and thinly sliced (about 2 cups)
6 ounces chopped escarole
1 pound fresh baby spinach
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon garam masala

 
Preparation

1. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add oil to pan; swirl to coat. 
2. Add raisins and onion to pan; cook for 3 minutes or until onions are lightly browned. 
3. Add half of escarole to pan; cook for 2 minutes or until greens wilt. Repeat with remaining escarole. 
4. Add a third of spinach to pan; cook for 2 minutes or until spinach wilts. Repeat in 2 more batches with the remaining spinach. 
5. Stir in salt and garam masala.
