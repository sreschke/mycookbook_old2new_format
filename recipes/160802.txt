Makes about 2« dozen

Ingredients:

3 pounds chicken wingettes (about 30)

Instructions:

Sprinkle chicken with a dash of salt and pepper. Broil 4-6 in. from the heat for 5-10 minutes on each side or until golden brown. Transfer to a greased 5-qt. slow cooker.

Combine the ketchup, brown sugar, vinegar, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, garlic, liquid smoke if desired and remaining salt; pour over wings. Toss to coat.

Cover and cook on low for 3 ¬ to 3? hours or until chicken juices run clear. Sprinkle with sesame seeds if desired.
