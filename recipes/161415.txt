2 cups sugar
1 cup flour
2/3 cup cocoa
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking powder
2 eggs
1 cup oil (canola or veg)
1 tsp vanilla
3 tbsp chocolate syrup

Mix dry ingredients together first with/whisk.  Add wet ingredients and mix together with spoon.  
Put in a 13 x 9 pan
Cook at 350 degrees for approx 20 - 30 minutes until knife comes out mostly clean.  This will make them chewy.

Icing:
1 stick butter
1/3 cup milk
4 tbsp cocoa
1 box powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla 

Combine butter, milk and cocoa on stove and bring to a boil. Add sugar and vanilla.  Mix together. Pour over immediately or it will harden.  

This is very good.
