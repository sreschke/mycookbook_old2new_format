Ingredients

3 ounces prosciutto or bacon (about 6-8 slices)
3 cups 1% low-fat milk
3 cups unsalted chicken stock
3 tablespoon all-purpose flour (heaping)
1.5 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
1.5 teaspoons grated lemon rind (2-3 large lemons)
3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
9 ounces 1/3-less-fat cream cheese (1 container)
1 lb uncooked whole-grain linguine
3 cups chopped carrots (8-10 large carrots)
3 cups cup frozen English peas 
3/4 cup shaved Parmesan cheese
6 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley

Directions:

Place prosciutto or bacon in single layer on a plate lined with paper towels. Cover with another layer of paper towels. Microwave at HIGH until crisp. Let stand 2 minutes; crumble into large pieces.

Bring milk, stock, and flour to a boil in a large skillet over medium-high.Whisk. Cook, stirring often, until thickened, about 5 min. Stir in pepper, rind, salt, and cream cheese. Reduce heat to low; keep warm.

Bring water to a boil in pot. Add pasta and carrots; cook 4 minutes. Add peas; cook until pasta is al dente, about 3 minutes. Drain pasta mixture; add to sauce in skillet, and toss to coat. Sprinkle with prosciutto, Parmesan, and parsley.
