2 lbs ground beef or turkey
3 tsp minced garlic
1 large yellow onion
2 1/2 cups water
1/2 cup beef broth
1/3 cup olive oil
2 (15 oz) cans tomato sauce
2 (15 oz) cans diced tomatoes
1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
1 tablespoon adobo seasoning
3 bay leaves
1 tablespoon salt
1/2 tablespoon pepper
2 cups elbow macaroni, uncooked
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions:

In a large pan sautâ€š your ground meat in skillet over medium high heat until half cooked and then remove from heat. 

Add garlic, onions, olive oil and cook until meat is fully cooked. 

Add water and broth, tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, Italian seasoning, bay leaves, seasoned salt, pepper and adobo seasoning.  Mix well

Lower heat and cover and allow to cook for about 20 minutes, stirring occasionally. 

Add in the uncooked elbow macaroni to the skillet, stir well until everything is combined. 

Cover once again and allow to simmer for about 30 minutes. 

Once cooked, remove the bay leaves.

Add only the cheddar cheese and mix until combined. 

Add mozzarella right before serving.
