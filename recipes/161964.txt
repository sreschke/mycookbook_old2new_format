6 Boneless Chicken Breasts
1/2 Cup All Purpose Flour
1/4 Cup Vegetable Oil
1 Cup Orange Juice
1/2 Cup Chili Sauce
2 Tablespoons Soy Sauce
1 Tablespoon Dark corn Syrup
1 teaspoon prepared mustard
1 or 2 cloves garlic, crushed
1 Large green pepper, cut in strips 
Orange slices
Parsley sprigs

Dredge chicken in flour; brown in oil  in a heavy skillet over med heat turning once. transfer chicken to a lightly greased 12 x 8 x 2 baking dish. Set Aside.  Drain off drippings. Combine next 7 ingredients in skillet, mixing well.  Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer 5 minutes, stirring occasionally . Pour over chicken; cover and bake at 350 degrees for 30 to 35 minutes or until tender.  Garnish with orange slices and parsley.
