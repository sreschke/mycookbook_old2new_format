olive oil
1 1/2 cups Gf flour
8 cups diced white onion
butter
8 cups chicken tinga
10 cups veggie stock
2 cups tomatillo chile
8 carrots chopped
4 poblano roasted & chopped
heat oil in stock pot. Add onions & teaspoon salt. Cook 10 minutes. Add carrot & poblano & cook 15 minutes more.. Add butter until melted. Add flour (cook roux 6-8 minutes) Add half of the stock & stir. Add remaining stock, tomatillo chile & chicken.
****As a special serve over potato cake
