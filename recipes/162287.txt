2 pks boneless chicken breast skinned and cut in chunks

(mix together the following:
3/4 cup of Italian seasoned bread crumbs
3/4 cup parmesan cheese
2 tsp seasoned salt
1 1/2 tsp each of Italian season, paprika, and basil.
1/4 tsp thyme
)
 1 cube butter- melted
mix together the above and add melted butter

in another bowl beat 2 eggs and 2 tbsp of water
dip chick in egg mixture and then in crumb mixture and put on cookie sheet bake 9 min each side at 350 degrees.
