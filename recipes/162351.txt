1 tbsp ground chile powder
1 1/2 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp granulated garlic
1/2 tsp granulated onion
1/2 tsp dried oregano, rubbed
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Method:
Measure all of the ingredients into a small bowl and whisk with a fork. Keep in a airtight container.

This recipe makes 1/4 cup of seasoning. You may use the whole recipe for 1 pound of ground beef or half of the recipe (2 tbsp per pound of meat) depending on your taste.

Serves 6-12 depending on your taste. 1 carb per 2 teaspoons of seasoning or 1/2 carb per 1 teaspoon of seasoning. 

To Use:
Brown 1 pound of ground beef in a pan. Add 2 tbsp - 4 tbsp (half of the recipe or the whole thing) to the ground beef along with 2 tbsp of tomato paste and 1/2 cup beef broth. Simmer gently until most of the sauce evaporates. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper. Carb counts will depend on how much seasoning you use and how big your serving is.
