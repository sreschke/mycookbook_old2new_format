1 tbsp. olive oil
1/3 cup thinly sliced red onion
8 oz. fresh strawberries, quartered
« cup pinot grigio or similar white wine
¬ cup honey
1 tsp. Sriracha chili sauce
1 (14-oz.) Camembert or Brie cheese round
2 tsp. finely chopped fresh chives
&#8539; tsp. sea salt 
1 baguette, thinly sliced and toasted

1.  Heat oil in a medium saucepan over medium-low heat. Add onion, and cook, stirring often, until translucent and fragrant, about 3 minutes. Add the strawberries, and cook until the berries are tender and soft, 4 to 6 minutes. Add the wine, honey and Sriracha; cook, stirring often, until thickened, 8 to 10 minutes.

2. Place the cheese on a parchment paper, and heat in microwave or oven until warm.

3. Place warm cheese round on serving platter; top with warm strawberry sauce. Sprinkle with chives and salt, & serve with the toasted baguette slices.
