4 thick pork loin chops, bone-in
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 TBSP olive oil
1 cup unsalted chicken broth, or water

Season pork chops with salt and pepper.

Select Saute button to heat Instant Pot.  When "Hot" is displayed, add olive oil.

Add the seasoned pork to the Instant Pot and sear both ides making sure the chops do not overlap, approximately 3 minutes on each side.  

Remove the pork from the Instant Pot and set aside.

Add the broth to the Instant Pot, scraping any bits from the bottom with a wooden spoon.

Place the trivet in the bottom of the Instant Pot and lay pork chops on top of the trivet.  They can overlap.

Cover pot, lock on the lid, and set the steam release valve to Sealing.

Select the Pressure Cook button and set the cook time to 5 minutes.

When complete, all the pot to sit for 5 minutes, and then do QR.

Remove chops and trivet.  Add cream of mushroom soup and cream of chicken soup to the liquid in the pot.  Turn on saute.  Make a slurry of 1/4 cup cold water and 1/4 cup cornstarch.  Add to liquid.  Bring to a boil.  When thickened, return chops back to the liquid.  Set instant pot to warm.
