6 Boneless skinless chicken breasts
2 tablespoons margarine, butter
1/2 cup parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon orgeno
1 teaspoon parsley flakes
1/4 cup bread crumbs
1/4 teaspoon paprike
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Combine buter, cheese,orgeno, and parsley into a paste and spread on the chicken. Season the bread crumbs with paprike, salt, and pepper and coat he chicken. place in a 15x10x1 baking pan sprayed with non-stick cooking spray. Bake at 400' for 25-30 minutes or until tender.
