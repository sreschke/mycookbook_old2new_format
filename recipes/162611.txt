1 ready crust graham cracker pie crust
1 package (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened
1/3 c. sugar
1 c. sour cream
2 tsp. vanilla
1 (8 oz.) thawed Cool Whip
Fresh strawberries for garnish

Beat cream cheese until smooth. Gradually beat in sugar. Blend in sour cream and vanilla. Fold in Cool Whip blending well. Spoon into crust. Chill until set (at least 4 hrs.). Garnish with strawberries or other fruit.
