2-1/2 to 3# boneless pork shoulder roast (you can use beef if you want I'm sure)

1-1/2 c. beef broth

1/2 c. sliced green onions

1 tsp. dried basil

1 tsp. dried marjoram

1/2 t. salt

1/2 t. pepper

1 bay leaf

6 medium red potatoes cut into 2" chunks

4 medium carrots cut into 2" chunks

7-8 fresh mushrooms quartered

1/4 c. flour

1/2 c. cold water

Browning sauce



Place roast in slow cooker. Add broth, onions and seasoning. Cover and cook on high for 2 hours. Add potatoes, carrots and mushrooms. Cover and cook on low for 6 hours or until vegetables are tender. Remove meat and vegetables. Keep warm. Discard bay leaf. In sauce pan combine flour and water until smooth, stir in 1-1-/2 c. cooking juices. Bring to boil. Cook & stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Add browning sauce. Serve with roast and vegetables.
