2 Sticks of Butter (room temp).
4 Cups Powder Sugar.
Pinch of Salt.
2 Teaspoons Vanilla Extract.
1 to 2 Tablespoons Milk.

Method.

Combine the butter, sugar and salt the mixer bowl ( use the paddle attachment) mix on low speed. Mix till mostly incorporated add vanilla and milk.

Icing can by made into different flavors.
