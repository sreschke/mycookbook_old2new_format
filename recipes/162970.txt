1 pie pre-made crust / you can make your own
3 eggs beaten
1 cup milk
2 tbsp flour
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
1 cup cheddar cheese , shredded
1/2 onion chopped fine
1 cup of asparagus sliced into one Ince pieces
1 tbsp olive oil or any oil
1 tbsp butter

preheat oven 350 decrees,  
place pie crust in pan ( can used fluted pan).  
in medium bowl, beat eggs, milk, salt, pepper and flours. set aside. 
in small skillet hear oil, and butter.  add onion and asparagus.  cook until soft about 3-5 min.
when cooked turn beat off and let cook slightly.
place shredded cheese in the bottom of pie crust.  add cooled asparagus/ onion mixuture.  pour egg mixture on top of cheese.
bake for50-60 min or until done.  let rest 10 min before slicing.

can add 1/2 cup mushrooms and bacon when cooking asparagus mixture.  or use cooked bacon and add to asparagus.
