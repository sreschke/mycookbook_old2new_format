 Now don't be afraid to try this one.. 

1 pint ripe strawberries washed and halfed
1 tblsp balsamic vinegar( yes vinegar)
2 tblsp granulated sugar
1 tsp cracked BLACK PEPPER
1 pint vanilla ice cream
  
   Be brave... and continue...
combine strawberries , vinegar, sugar and pepper approximately 15 minutes in advance.. In bowl scoop  4oz. ice cream and distribute the berry mixture evenly over the top .. then serve in a ice party glass and ENJOY the ooh's and aah's from your guests...
    A REAL SHOW STOPPER AT THE END OF A SPECIAL MEAL...
