1 box white cake mix
1 - 4 serving box of vanilla instant pudding
1 cup all purpose flour
1 cup sugar


1 cup sour cream
1 & 1/2 tsp Cookie Nip
1 cup water
3 large eggs

1/2 cup funfetti Jimmies

Preheat oven to 350.

Whisk together dry ingredients.

Add wet ingredients and mix.

Fold in sprinkles

Scoop into cupcake liners using 1/4 cup scoop.  

Bake 15 - 18 minutes.

Remove from oven, remove from muffin pan to cool.  


Cupcake Buttercream

2 sticks butter, softened
2 pounds powdered sugar (1 bag)
2 tsp cookie nip

2/3 cup salt water (1 tsp salt in 1 cup warm water)

Extra Jimmies

Mix all ingredients.  Pipe onto cooled cupcakes.

Makes approx 2 - 2 1/2 dozen cupcakes.
