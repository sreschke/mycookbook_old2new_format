2T olive oil.                             5 cloves garlic
1 tsp thyme.                           1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp basil.                          1/3 C parmesan
Salt n pepper.                        2 T butter
Small potatoes halved
 
Mix all but butter. Toss with potatoes. Place on greased cookie sheet. Bake 400. 25 min.
