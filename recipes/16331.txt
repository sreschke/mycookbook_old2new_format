STEAMED CHINESE FISH (Barbara Ganzer) 

3-4 fish fillets (i.e. scrod, bass) 
Steam in oven steamer (or poach in 2 inches of water in frying pan) until flaky [10 minutes on stiove, 20-30 minutes in oven] 

Top with: 
1. grated carrots 
2. sliced scallions 
3. cut and steamed broccoli 
4. red pepper 
(other vegetables can be substituted if you like) 

Pour about 3-4 tbs of tamari over fish. Heat 1/3 c oil in a small pan, and pour over top to make fish sauce sizzle.
