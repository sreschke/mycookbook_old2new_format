SWEDISH MEATBALLS (Jill Woolman) 

Sauce
1 large onion, sliced
1/2 c raisins
1/2 c brown sugar
1/3 c ketchup
1/4 c vinegar
1/4 tsp salt 

Mix above ingredients and bring to a boil. Lower heat to simmer for 5 minutes. Add meat balls, cover and cook for 1-1/2 hours. Shake pot occasionally. 

Meatballs 
2 lbs ground beef
1 egg
1 tsp salt, or other seasoning
1/4 tsp pepper
1 small onion, grated
1 heaping tbs oatmeal
3 tbs warm water to soften oatmeal
2 tbs ketchup 

Mix above ingredients and make meatballs the size of a walnut. Add gradually to the sauce while simmering. 

NOTE: I double the sauce and use ground chicken cutlets.
