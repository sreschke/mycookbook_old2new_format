CABBAGE SOUP (Faye Sanders) 

Bones
Flanken 
1 medium cabbage, shredded (raw or par boiled) 
1 medium onion, chopped 
1/2 large can whole tomatoes
2 ribs celery,diced
3 c tomato juice
salt, pepper
sour salt to taste
2 tbs brown sugar
1 small apple, diced
1-2 cans tomato sauce 

In a large soup pot, place bones and flanken and cover with water almost to top of pot. Boil for 1/2 hour, skimming frequently. Add remaining ingredients except cabbage. Boil for 15 minutes. Add cabbage and boil additional 30 minutes or until cabbage is soft enough. Adjust sugar and sour salt to taste.
