4 chicken breast halves, skinned
1/4 cup plus 2 tbs lemon juice
1/2 cup butter or margarine, melted
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp poultry seasoning
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground black pepper 
Place chicken in lightly greased 12x7x2-inch baking dish. Combine remaining ingredients; pour over chicken. Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees 
F. for 1 hour or until chicken is tender, basting frequently. Serve over hot cooked rice or flat noodles. Serves 4.
