   CABBAGE, CARROT, BEAN SOUP

1 c. dried pinto beans
1-1/2 c. chopped onion
2 garlic cloves, chopped
1/4 c. chopped fresh dill
1 bay leaf
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
3-1/2 c. peeled, diced sweet potatoes
4 c. thickly sliced cabbage
1 c. sliced carrots
1 c. sliced yellow squash
1/4 c. chopped fresh parsley

Place the beans in lrg pot, add enough cold water to cover them and 
refrigerate them overnite.

Rinse and drain beans and return them to pot.  Stir in the onions, 
garlic, 3 tbls of dill, the bay leat, salt and pepper.  Add the 
potatoes and 2 qts of water and bring to boil over med high heat.  
Reduce the heat to med low, cover the pot and simmer for 35-45 min., 
or til beans are tender.

Add the cabbage, carrots and squash and return the soup to a boil.  
Cook for another 5-10 min, or til carrots are tender.  Remove and 
discard the bay leaf, then stir in the parsley.  Ladle the soup into 
bowls and garnish with remaining dill.

MAKES 4 SERVINGS
Per serv:  360 cal., 1 gm fat, 188 mg sodium, 50 gm carb, 16 gm 
protein, 190 mg calcium, 6 mg iron
