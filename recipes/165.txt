chicken breasts
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
2 large lemons
2 scallions-green onions
2 tsp cornstarch
1 cup cold water
3 tbsp oil

cut chicken into thin strips and season with salt and pepper. set aside.  in small bowl grate 1 tsp of colored zest and squeeze 3 tbsp juice from one of the lemons.  set aside.  cut the second lemon into thin slices and halve the slices.  thinly slice onions. dissolve cornstarch in 1 cup cold water.

heat the oil in pan and add chicken and fry until cooked through-2-3 min.  add lemon zest, juice, slices, and onions.  stir in cornstarch mixture.  bring to a boil, stirring until the sauce is translucent and thickened, 1-2 min.  serve with rice or thin pasta.
