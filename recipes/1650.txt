Spray crock pot with non-stick cooking spray.

Place the following in cockpot: 
1 zucchini-sliced (1/4")
1 yellow squash-sliced (1/4") 
2 carrots- thinly sliced 
1 1/2 cups fresh Mushrooms-sliced 
10 oz. frozen Broccoli cuts
4 Green Onions-sliced 
1 Clove Garlic-minced 
1/2 tsp. basil 
1/4 tsp. salt 
1/2 tsp. pepper 
1 Cup (fresh or dried)grated Parmesan cheese.  
Cover and Cook on High 2 hours  or Low for 4 hours.

Cook 12 oz. fettuccine per package directions; drain.  Add cooked fettuccine plus 1 Cup (4 oz.) Mozzarella Cheese (shredded), 1 Cup Cream and 2 Egg yokes to crock pot.  Stir to blend well.  Allow to heat 15 - 30 minutes.  

Can be kept on Low for 30 minutes for serving.
