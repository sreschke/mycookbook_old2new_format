1 egg well beaten
1/4 cup orange juice
1/2 cup honey
3 tbls. light cream
Dash of salt
1-8oz. jar marachino cherries well drained
1 qt shredded cabbage.

Combine egg, orange juice and honey in top of double boiler.  Cook over hot water until thickened stirring occasionally, then cool.  Add cream, salt and cherries, blend well.  Chill thoroughly.  Toss with shredded cabbage and serve.
