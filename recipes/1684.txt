4 c. slice apples
2 c. flour
1-2 c. sugar (half brown sugar)
2 t. baking powder
1 t. salt
2 eggs, beaten
2/3 c. margarine, melted
1 t. cinnamon

Place apples in greased 9x13 pan.  Mix next 5 ingredients; sprinkle over apples.  Pour melted margarine overall.  Sprinkle with cinnamon.  
Bake at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes to a golden brown.
