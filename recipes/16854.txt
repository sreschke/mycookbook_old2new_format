1 1/2 cups quinoa 
1/4 cup golden raisins 
1/3 cup rum or apple juice 
1 14-ounce can light coconut milk 
2 cups fat-free milk 
1 cup sugar 
1 1/2 tsps. finely shredded lemon peel 
3/4 tsp. ground cinnamon 
1/2 tsp. vanilla 
1 8 - ounce carton nonfat vanilla or lemon yogurt 
slices of fruit

Cook quinoa and 2 cups water in a large saucepan to boiling.  Reduce heat and cook, covered, 10 minutes 
more or till tender but not mushy. Drain off any water. 
Meanwhile, combine raisins and rum or apple juice in a small bowl; set aside.  Add coconut milk, milk, sugar, 
lemon peel, and cinnamon to quinoa.  Bring mixture to boiling; reduce heat to med-low.  Cook and stir, uncovered, for 20 to 25 minutes or till thickened.  Remove
from heat; stir in raisin mnixture and vanilla.  Transfer to 
a large bowl.  Cover and chill at least 4 hours or overnight. 
To serve, stir in nonfat vanilla or lemon yogurt.  Top with slices of fruit, if desired.    Makes 8 servings.
