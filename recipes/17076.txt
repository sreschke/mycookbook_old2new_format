8 potatoes, peeled & thinly sliced
1 onion
1 lb fully-cooked ham, cut into 1 in. cubes
1 pkg (1oz) country -style gravy mix
1 can cream of mushroom soup
2 cups water
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Combine potatoes, onion & ham in lightly greased crockpot
Combine gravy mix, soup & water.  whisk until combined.
Pour over potato mixture..
Cover; cook on low 7 -10 hrs or (high 3-4 hrs)
Top with cheese during last 30 minutes
Serves 10
