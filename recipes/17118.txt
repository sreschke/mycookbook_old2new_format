2/3 cup dry bread crumbs
1 cup milk
11/2 lbs ground beef
2 eggs (beaten)
1/4 cups onion (grated)
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp sage
dash pepper

Soak bread crumbs in milk,add meat, eggs,onion,and seasonings. Mix well. Form into loaf pan and cover with piquant sauce (below).

PIQUANT SAUCE

3 TBl brown sugar
1/4 cup catsup
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp dry mustard

Mix all ingrediences and spoon on top of meatloaf.  Bake 350 11/4 hours
