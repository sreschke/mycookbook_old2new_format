8 Chicken Legs
3 tbls Butter
2-3 heaped tsps American Mustard (or whatever you prefer)
Salt & Pepper (optional)
Breadcrumbs

Place chicken in greased ovenproof dish.
In a saucepan (or microwave) melt butter; add mustard and seasoning.  Cook 1 min.  Using pastry brush, spread mustard mix over chick liberally and cover thinly with bread crumbs.  Bake in moderate oven until cooked - approx 40 mins.  Delicious served with Hot Potato Salad.

Serves 4
