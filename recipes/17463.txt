 AVOCADO DELIGHT
    
    
    4 avocado halves 
    
    1 large grapefruit, peeled and sectioned
    1/2  pound crab meat
    1 bottle Thousand Island Dressing 
    1/4 lb. small shrimp (cooked)
    
    Lightly cover avocado halves with lemon juice to prevent browning. Place each half on plate with lettuce leaf. Fill hollow with grapefruit pieces.
Mound crab on top. Pour Thousand Island dressing over mound. Place shrimp on top in design. Garnish plate with fresh fruit.
