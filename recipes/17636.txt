Beer Bread

3 cups plus 2 teaspoons self-rising flour, divided 
3 tablespoons sugar 
3 tablespoons margarine, melted 
1 (12-ounce) can beer 
Vegetable cooking spray 
 
Coat a 9 x 5-inch loaf pan with cooking spray, and lightly dust with 2 teaspoons flour; set aside. Combine the remaining 3 cups flour and sugar in a bowl; stir well, and make a well in the center of the mixture. Add the melted margarine and beer, and stir just until moistened. Pour the batter into prepared pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Let cool in pan 5 minutes on a wire rack; remove from pan, and let cool completely on wire rack.
