1 pk. flour tortillas
2 lbs. cooked hamburger
1 can enchilda sauce
1 can refried beans
1 8oz. pk. shredded cheese


Brown hamburger, drain, add enchilda sauce and refried beans to meat mixture, stirring well.  Add a spoonful of mixture to a tortilla and roll, place seam down in a casserole dish.  Top enchilda's with remaining mixture(if any left over) and then top with cheese, bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes.
Serve with shredded lettace and taco sauce.
