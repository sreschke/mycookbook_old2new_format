2 tbsp cornstarch
1/4 c. brown sugar
1 c. water
3 c. fresh washed blackberries
1 tbsp  butter
1 c. flour
1/2 cup sugar
1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 c. milk
1/4 c. soft butter

Mix cornstarch, brown sugar and water and microwave until thickened. Add 3 cups berries and 1 tbsp. butter.  Pour into baking dish about 9 x 9 inches.  Set aside while mixing together the dry ingredients.  Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.  Cut in 1/4 cup butter.  Add milk then pour
or spoon on top of berries.  Can sprinkle top with nutmeg and sugar.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
