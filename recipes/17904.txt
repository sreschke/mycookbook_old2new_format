APRICOT MUSTARD

1/2 cup Dijon mustard 
1/4 cup plus 1 T. apricot preserves, stirred until smooth

Place the mustard in a medium bowl.  Gradually add the apricot preserves, stirring to break the lumps (apricot bits
may remain) and tasting as you do.  Stop when the mixture tastes good to you.  Makes about 3/4 cup. 

HONEY-GINGER MUSTARD

1/2 cup Dijon mustard 
1/4 cup honey 
ground powdered ginger or grated fresh ginger root 

Place the mustard in a medium bowl.  Add the honey gradually, adjusting according to taste.  Add ginger to 
taste.  Makes about 3/4 cup. 

MAPLE MUSTARD 

1/2 cup Dijon mustard 
1/4 cup pure maple syrup, or to taste 
Dash black pepper, (optional; to taste)

Place the mustard in a medium bowl.  Add the maple syrup gradually, adjusting according to taste.  Season with black pepper if you like.   Makes about 3/4 cup.
