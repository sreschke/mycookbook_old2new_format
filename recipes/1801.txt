4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
2-3 Tbsp. Dijon mustard
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
2 Tbsp. minced fresh rosemary
Pepper to taste
1/2 c. grated Parmesan cheese
Place chicken in greased 11x7x2" baking dish.  combine mustard & garlic powder; spread over the chicken.  Sprinkle with rosemary & pepper.  Top with cheese.  Bake, uncovered, at 350 for 45 minutes or until juices run clear.
