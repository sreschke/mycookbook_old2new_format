1 lb ground beef
1 can (16 oz) tomatoes
2 tsp sugar
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1 can (8 oz) tomato sauce
1 pkg (5 oz) medium egg noodles
1 pkg (3 oz) cream cheese, cubed and softened
1 carton (8 oz) sour cream
6 green onions, chopped
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Cook ground beef in a large skillet until brown, drain.

Drain tomotaoes, reserving juice; cut tomatoes into quarters.  Add tomatoes, tomato juice, sugar, salt, pepper, and tomato sauce to ground beef; heat to boiling.

Reduce heat and simmer 20 minutes.

Cook noodles according to package directions; drain.

Add cream cheese to hot noodles, mixing well.
Add sour cream and green onion, stirring well.

Alternate layers of meat mixture and noodle mixture in a lightly greased 2-1/2 quart casserole, beginning and ending with meat mixture.

Top with cheddar cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Serves 6.
