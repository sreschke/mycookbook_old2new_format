(Tort Czekoladowy Niepieczony) 

1/2 lb. butter
1 cup sugar
1/2 lb bittersweet chocolate, grated 

TOPPING 
2 cups (1/2 lb) walnuts or hazel nuts (filberts), finely ground 
1 cup sugar
3-4 tbs. heavy sweet cream 

Beat butter until very light and add sugar and grated chocolate and continue creaming until thoroughly blended. Chill in a buttered removable-bottom layer pan until mixture sets. Make topping with finely-ground nuts, sugar and enough cream to hold together. Remove chocolate base from pan and spread with topping. Cover with chocolate icing and decorate with large chunks of almonds (and/or each or all of the following: candied orange peel and lemon peel.)
