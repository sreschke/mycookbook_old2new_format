Pastry for 2 crust pie

1 1/4 c. sugar
1/8 tsp. salt
1/3 c. flour
2 c.fresh strawberries
2 c. (1 inch pieces) fresh rhubarb

Combine all ingredients. Place in bottom layer of pie shell which has been dusted with flour. Sprinkle on cinnamon. Dot with several pats of butter. Sprinkle with 1 capful of Real Lemon juice. Place on top pie crust.

Bake at 400 degrees for 15 minutes.
Turn oven down to 350 degrees & bake for 45 more minutes.
