1 lb. ground beef
1 small onion, chopped
2 C. cooked rice
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 T. soy sauce
1 can Chinese noodles

Brown ground beef and onions until meat is well browned and onions are tender.  In a large bowl, combine rice, cream of chicken soup, cream of mushroom soup, and soy sauce.  In bottom of casserole dish, sprinkle half the chinese noodles.  Spe\read meat-onion mixture over noodles.  Top with soup mixture, and sprinkle remaining noodles over the top.  Bake at 350 for 10-15 minutes, or until bubbly hot.
