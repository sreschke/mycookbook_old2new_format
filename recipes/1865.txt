Home made dressing if you prefer:
1 egg
1/3 cup oil
2 tsp white wine vinegar
3 tsp Dijon mustard

Lower egg into a pan of boiling water and cook for 1 min, drain.  Place the remaining dressing ingredients in a food processor or blender.  Add the egg and blend for 1 min or until the dressing is smooth.
