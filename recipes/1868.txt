750g chicken breast/tenderloin/thigh fillet
1/4 chopped onion
2 cloves crushed garlic
1 tbls grated ginger
2 tbsl lemon juice
3 tsp ground coriander
3 tsp ground cumin
3 tsp garam masala
1/3 cup plain yoghurt
1 tsp salt
Skewers

Soak 12 skewers.  Cube chicken.  Process onion, garlic, ginger, juice and spices very quickly.
Thread chicken onto skewers and place in a baking dish.  Coat with spice mixture and marinate for several hours or overnight, covered, in the fridge.
BBQ, grill or fry in a well greased pan until cooked through.
