Merry Muffins

1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 egg yolks, well beaten
1 tbs. maraschino cherry juice
1 cup flour
1/4 tsp baking powder
2 egg whites, stiffly beaten
maraschino cherries
pecans, finely chopped
confectioner's sugar

Cream butter gradually adding sugars until thoroughly
creamed. Mix in egg yolks, cherry juice and flour
which has been sifted with baking powder. Fold in egg
whites. Grease small size muffin tins ( 1/2 inch) and
sprinkle bottom with finely chopped pecans. Put 1
teaspoon dough in each, place cherry on top. Add
another teaspoon full of dough and sprinkle top with
pecans. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes. Remove
from muffin tins and roll in confectioner's sugar
while still warm.

Yield: Approx. 32 small muffins

From:  White Oak Manor Bed & Breakfast
Jefferson, Texas
