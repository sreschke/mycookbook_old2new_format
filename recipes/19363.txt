 3 cups cake flour
 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
 1/2 teaspoon salt
 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, room temperature
 3 cups sugar
 6 eggs, room temperature
 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
 1 tablespoon grated lemon peel
 1 cup sour cream 

Preheat oven to 325øF. Grease 16-cup tube pan. Dust
pan with cake flour; tap out excess flour.

Sift flour, baking soda and salt into medium bowl.
Using electric mixer, beat butter in large bowl at
medium speed until fluffy. Gradually add sugar and
beat 5 minutes. Add eggs 1 at a time, beating just
until combined after each addition.

Beat in lemon juice and peel. Using rubber spatula,
mix in dry ingredients. Mix in sour cream. Transfer
batter to prepared pan.

Bake cake until tester inserted near center comes out
clean, about 1 hour 30 minutes. Let cake cool in pan
on rack 15 minutes. Cut around cake in pan. Turn
 out cake.

Carefully turn cake right side up on rack and cool
completely. (Can be prepared 2 days ahead. Wrap in
foil and let stand at room temperature.) 

Serves 12.
