1 1/2 c flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 c sugar
1 egg, beaten
1/2 c milk
3 tbs oil

Sift together flour, baking powder, salt and sugar.  Combine egg, milk and oil.  Add to flour mixture stirring until mixture is smooth.  Pour into greased pan (8 inch square or 9 inch round).  Bake in hot oven at 400 for 25 min.  Top with the following mixture before baking:
2 tbs butter
1/4 c sugar
1/4 c flour
2 tbs brown sugar
1/4 tsp cinnamon

Mix with pastry blender until crumbly.  sprinkle over top.
