INGREDIENTS:

12 cups popped popcorn
2 tablespoons of butter
1 tablespoon dried ranch style dressing
2 tablespoons cheese flavored topping

WHAT YOU NEED:

1 large plastic bag
small brown lunch bags or small plastic bags




DIRECTIONS:

1) Melt two tablespoons of butter, and combine with popcorn in large plastic bag. 

2) Sprinkle dressing mix and cheese toppign over popcorn. Close bag tightly and shke to coat evenly.

3) Package individual servings in small brown lunch bags. (or small plastic baggies)
