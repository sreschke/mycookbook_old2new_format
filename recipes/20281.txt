CUCUMBER SALAD (Sue Brenner) 

2 c cucumbers, peeled and sliced
1 small onion, sliced
3 scallions, sliced
1/3 c white vinegar
2/3 c water
1 tsp sugar 

Combine all ingredients and marinate overnight. 

Note: If you like, sprinkle cucumbers with salt and let stand before 
starting.
