
   1lb ground beef
    1 chopped green pepper
    1 half chopped onion
     1 package of brown gravy mix
    three cups sliced raw potaotoes
     1 cup shredded chedder cheese

     Brown ground beef in skillet on med-high, drain well add oinion and green pepper cook for an additional five minutes. make gravy according to directions, mix gravy in with ground beef mixture. Layer in a large cassaarole dish beef mixture and potaotes starting with beef and ending with beef. Cook in preheated oven at 350* for 1 hour or until potatoes are done, Add cheese to top and let melt.
