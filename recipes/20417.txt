2 1/2 cups cooked chicken chopped
1/3 cup celery chopped
1/2 cup finely chopped walnuts
2 tbsp grated onion
1/2 cup mayonnaise
2 tbsp sour cream
3/4 tsp curry powder
1/8 tsp cajon seasoning

Place chicken in a food processor until finely chopped.  Transfer to a large bowl.  Add walnuts, celery and onion.  Mix.  In another bowl mix mayonnaise, sour cream, curry and cajon seasoning.  Mix well.  Add to chicken mixture and combine.  Slice 4 to 6 crossants and spread.  Top with fresh lettuce.
