Serves 10 to 12

1/2C Sugar
1/3C Flour
1/2tsp Salt
2C Milk
4 Egg yolks beaten 
2tsp Vanilla

Mix:  Sugar....Flour....Salt in a sauce pan (thick pan is best)
Stir in milk and cook over Med-Hi heat, stirring constantly until it boils.
Boil 1 min and remove from heat.

Separate a little over half into the egg yolks and then blend into the remaining mixture in the pan.
bring JUST to a boil, cool and add the vanilla.

Let it set a bit and then start to fill the custard puffs....
