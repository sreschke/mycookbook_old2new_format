1 Tbl. olive oil
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
3/4 Tbl. dried Italian Seasoning
1 can S&W Italian Stewed Tomatoes
3 ozs. cappacola ham, chopped into 3/4-inch pieces
1/2 cup vodka
3/4 cup milk (non-fat,1%, whatever)
1 Tbl. cornstarch
3/4 cup Parmasean cheese, divided
8 ozs. rigatoni, cooked and drained
1/8 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper

Put olive oil in a large nonstick skillet.  Add onion, garlic and Italian Seasoning and saute 4 minutes. 
Add the tomatoes and cappacola and simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
Add vodka and simmer 5 minutes.

In a small bowl or measuring cup, combine milk and cornstarch.  Add to skillet along with 1/2 cup of the cheese.  Simmer until sauce thickens slightly; about 6 minutes.  Add rigatoni and stir until sauce coats pasta.  Season with salt and pepper.


One and a half cup serving =
435 calories
  23 mg cholesterol
  13 g. fat   (27% fat)
