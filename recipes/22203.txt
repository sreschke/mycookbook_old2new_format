1/4 c. margarine or butter
1 c. chopped celery
1 c. chopped onion
1 c. chopped carrots
1/4 c. all-purpose flour
1 can condensed chicken broth
1 c. milk
1 pkg. (7 oz.) herb seasoned cubed stuffing
2 c. cubed cooked chicken or turkey
1 c. shredded cheddar cheese

In 3-quart saucepan over medium heat, in hot margarine, cook celery, onion and carrots until tender.  Add flour; cook 1 minute more, stirring constantly.  Gradually stir in broth and milk.  Cook until mixture boils and thickens, stirring constantly.

Add stuffing and chicken; toss to coat.  Spoon into 12 x 8 inch baking dish.  Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake 5 minutes more or until cheese melts.

Makes 6 servings.


Recipe from Campbell's Simply Delicious Recipes, 1992
