1 1/2 pounds top round, 1" thick
1 Tbsp dry mustard
1/2 cup flour
salt and pepper
3 Tbsp salad oil
1 cup onions, thinly sliced
1/2 cup carrots, diced
1 1 pound can tomatoes
1 Tbsp brown sugar
1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce

Spread steak on a flat surface. Combine mustard and flour and pound into steak thoroughly. Sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste. Brown on both sides in hot oil in heavy skillet. Combine remaining ingredients and spread over meat. Cover and bake in slow oven (350)for 1 1/2 hours, or until very tender.
