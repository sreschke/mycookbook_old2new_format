From MMMMiami--Tempting Tropical Tastes for Home Cooks Everywhere by Carole Kotkin and Kathy Martin

As a variation substitute sea scallops or large shrimp for the chicken. 

2 tablespoons olive oil
1-1/4 pounds skinned and boned chicken breasts, trimmed, cut into 1-inch squares and patted dry
salt and freshly ground pepper
1 medium zucchini, trimmed and cut into thin strips
1 medium yellow squash, trimmed and cu into thin strips
1 small red onion, peeled and thinly sliced
1 clove garlic, minced
pinch of dried red pepper flakes
1 red bell pepper, seeds and ribs removed, and cut into thin strips
3 tomatoes, peeled, seeded, cored and diced or 1 can (28 ounces) canned tomatoes, drained and diced
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
1/4 teaspoon oregano
2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil

Heat a heavy large skillet or wok over high heat.  Add 1-1/2 tablespoons of oil. Season chicken with salt and pepper. Add chicken and cook until almost
opaque on all sides, tossing frequently, about 2-1/2 minutes; do not cook through.  Remove from skillet.  Heat remaining oil in same skillet over
high heat.  Add zucchini, yellow squash, onion, garlic, red pepper flakes, and red pepper.  Stir-fry, stirring constantly, for 3 minutes.  Add
tomatoes, salt, pepper, and oregano.  Stir fry for 3 minutes.  Return chicken with any exuded juices to pan and stir 3 minutes more. Stir in basil.  Serve immediately. Serves 4.
