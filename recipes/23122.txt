From Grace the Table. Makes 4 servings

2 catfish fillets ( 8 to 9 ounces each)
salt and pepper to taste
1/4 cup bacon fat (or 1/4 cup vegetavle oil)
1 large onion, chopped into medium dice
pinch nutmeg
pinch cayenne
1/4 cup chopped parsley
6 eggs, beaten

Season catfish with salt and pepper. In a nonstick skillet, heat bacon fat to hot but not smoking. Saute catfish fillets and onion on medium-high heat. Cook catfish 5 minutes on each side until nicely golden brown. Drain on paper towels and set aside. 
   Pour off all but 1 tablespoon of bacon fat. Add rest of ingredients to skillet with onions and cook, scrambling eggs. Seve on warm platter.
