1 lb    Extra Lean Ground Beef 
1 tsp   cumin 
1 tsp   dried oregano 
1 jar   thick and chunky salsa (medium or hot) 
1/4 cup   raisins (plumped in hot water)

1 can   (14 oz)  crushed pineapple, well drained

Brown ground beef with seasonings for 5 minutes on high in large skillet. Drain off fat and blot well with paper towel. (This is important to final appearance of recipe.) Add salsa and raisins and cook 5 minutes on low. Meanwhile, combine cream cheese and sour cream and spread over a serving plate that has an edge. Top with hot beef mixture, well-drained pineapple, chopped green onions and pimiento. Serve immediately (or chill and serve) with sliced pita/flatbread
