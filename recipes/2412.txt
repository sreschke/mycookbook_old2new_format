4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
1 8-ounce carton sour cream
2 tbls. Dijon mustard
2 tbls. honey
1 tbls. butter
Egg noodles

In a frying pan, brown chicken in butter over medium high heat until done (4-6 minutes, check doneness by cutting into a breast).

Remove chicken to warm platter. Reduce heat to low. Add sour cream, mustard and honey. Stir to mix well. Add chicken and coat with sauce. Serve over cooked egg noodles.
