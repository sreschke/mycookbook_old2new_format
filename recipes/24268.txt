3c. reg flour
2 3/4 c. white sugar, separated
1/2 tsp. soda
1 c. butter milk
1 1/3 c.butter
4 tsp.vanilla
4 eggs
3 tbsp. water
1 tsp.salt
1 tsp.baking powder

Preheat oven to 325*.Grease and flour 1 10 inch Bundt pan. Blend together low speed, flour, 2 c.sugar,  salt, b.powder, soda ,B.Milk, 1 c. butter,  2tsp.of vanilla and the 4 eggs.Blend well.Pour into Bundt pan and bake at 325  for 60 min.Make a Butter sauce with the remaining sugar 3/4 c., 2 tsps vanilla, 1/3 c. butter, and the water, cook over med heat till fully melted and combined, DO NOT boil!. Prick holes into the still warm cake  10 - 12 times around.Slowly pour sauce over top of cake still in pan.Let cake cool completely before movin the cake from pan.
