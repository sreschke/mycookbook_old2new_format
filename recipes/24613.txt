12 large crabs
1 cup whipping cream
2 tbs flour     
1/4 tsp mace
1 tbs butter   
1 tsp minced parsley
1 tsp Worcestershire
4 hard boiled eggs yolks, mashed
Salt & pepper   
Bread crumbs

Cover crabs with boiling salt water and boil for 30 minutes. Break off claws, clean and remove the meat. Set aside. Clean the upper shells. Melt  butter and blend in flour. Stir in cream and cook til thick, stirring constantly. Add the parsley,
mashed egg yolks, seasonings and crab. Fill the shells with this and cover with bread crumbs. Bake at 350F for 10 minutes til top is golden brown.
