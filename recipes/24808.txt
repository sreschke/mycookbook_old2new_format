3/4 cup butter or oleo
1 1/2 cups brown sugar
1 egg
2 cups flour
1/8 tsp. soda
1/2 cup pecans
1/2 tsp. vanilla

Mix all together, including pecans.  Roll into balls.  Bake at 350* for 10 minutes.

FROSTING

Heat 1/4 cup of Oleo until lightly browned.  
Add 2 cups powdered sugar, scant 1//4 cup milk, & 1 tsp. vanilla.  Beat until smooth and frost cookies.
