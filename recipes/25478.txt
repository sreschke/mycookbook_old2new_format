2 tbls butter    
1/4 cup chopped onion
1/4 cup chopped carrot 
1/4 cup chopped celery 
2 tbls flour
1/2 tsp dry mustard      
1 tsp nutmeg      
1 tsp pepper
3 cups chicken broth  
1 1/2 cups cream
1 cup milk (or beer) 
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar
1 dash Worcestershire
Salt to taste  

In a heavy pan melt butter then add onion, carrot, and celery and cook til tender (not brown).  Stir in flour, mustard, nutmeg and pepper cook a couple of minutes. Stir in chicken broth and simmer about 20 minutes.  Puree in blender or food processor. Add cream and milk (or beer) and almost boil.  Add cheese heat til melted, stir ALOT.  Add Worcestershire and salt.
