                        Chicken and Rice Casserole

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
   1      10.75 oz can  cream of celery soup
   2                    halves        chicken breasts -- skinned & boned
   1      2 oz can      sliced mushrooms -- undrained
   1      envelope      dry onion soup mix
     1/2  cup           Uncle Ben's converted rice -- uncooked

Combine the soup, mushrooms and rice in a well greased slow cooker.  Stir
well.  Lay the chicken breasts on top of the mixture.  Sprinkle the onion
soup mix over all.  Cover.  Cook on LOW for 7 to 9 hours.  Serve hot.
