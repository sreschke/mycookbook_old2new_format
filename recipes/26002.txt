3 tbsp butter
1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1 stalk celery, sliced
1/2 lb mushrooms, sliced
3 tbsp all purpose flour
3 1/2 cups milk
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
4 oz cream cheese, at room temperature
3 cups diced cooked turkey or chicken
1 cup fresh or frozen peas
1/2 lb eg noodles
1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
1/2 cup dry breadcrumbs
3 tbsp butter melted

1.  Melt 3 tbsp butter in a large saucepan.  Add onion, garlic, celery and mushrooms.  Coook a few min until vegetables are tender and any liquid has evaporated.  2.  Sprinkle mixture with flour and cook a few  min. 3.  Add milk and bring to a boil. 4.  Add salt, pepper, nutmeg and worcestershie.  Whisk in cream cheese a little at a time.  Cook gently a few min. 5. Add turkey or chicken and peas.  Combine well.  6.  Meanwhile cook noodles.  Drain well.  Combine with sauce mixture.  Place in a buttered 9" x 13" casserole.  7.  Combine parmesan cheese with breadcrumbs and butter.  Sprinkle over top of casserole.  8.  Place casserole on a baking sheet to catch any spills.  Bake in a preheated 350F oven for 30 min or until browned and bubbling.  Allow to rest 5 to 10 min before serving.
