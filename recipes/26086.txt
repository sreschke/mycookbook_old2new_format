Husband's Delight

8 oz. wide noodles
1 1/2 lbs. ground beef
1/2 cup onion; chopped
1 clove garlic; minced
3 (8oz.) cans tomato sauce
1 tbsp. sugar
1/8 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
1 cup cottage cheese
1 (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese; softened
1/4 cup sour cream
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

Cook noodles according to package directions.  Cook groun beef, onion, and garlic until the beef is browned; drain.  Add tomato sauce, sugar, salt, and pepper.  Stir in the noodles.  Combine cottage cheese and cream cheese.  Add sour cream.  Layer half of the noodle mixture into a greased, 
13 X 9 X 2-inch baking dish.  Top the layer with the cottage cheese mixture and the remaining noodle mixture.  Sprinkle with cheddar cheese.  Bake, uncovered, in a 350* oven for 30 minutes or until heated through.  Serves 8.
