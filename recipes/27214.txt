6 chocolate bars w/almonds (hershey's)
8oz. frozen whipped topping-thawed
1 T. vanilla
1-9" graham cracker crust

Melt chocolate bars, fold whipped topping into melted chocolate. Stir in vanilla. Spoon onto pie crust. Garnish with grated chocolate if desired. Chill for 2-3 hrs.  6-8 servings
