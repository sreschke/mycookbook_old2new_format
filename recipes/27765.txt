Take 1 pound of sausage and mix with 3 cups bisquick.  Then add a 12 oz. package of finely shredded shard Cheddar cheese and thoroughly mix together with the Bisquick and Sausage.  Then add 10-12 tablespoons of very cold water and mix thoroughly.  Put in refrigerator for 30-40 minutes and chill well.

Then,  roll into bite size pieces; put on a cookie sheet and freeze.  After frozen, put them in a plastic bag and cook as needed.  Bake at 350 degrees for about 20-25 minutes.  Watch because they will brown on the bottom first. You will need to turn on the broiler to brown the tops.
