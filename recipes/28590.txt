1 box (12 oz) fettuccine 
6 cups cut-up assorted fresh vegetable (broccoli florets, red and/or green bell peppers, zucchini and/or yellow squash
1 jar (27.5 oz) light pasta sauce
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese


Cook fettuccine according to package directions, stirring in assorted fresh vegetables during the last 5 minutes of boiling, drain.

In medium saucepan, heat pasta sauce.

Toss with hot pasta sauce, vegetables and cheeses.

Garnish, if desired, with fresh basil leaves.
