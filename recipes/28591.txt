Makes 8 servings

2 pounds ground beef
1 jar Pasta Sauce.
1 container (15oz.) ricotta cheese.
2 cups. mozzarella Cheese, divided
1/2 cup. parmesan cheese
2 eggs.
1 tsp. italian seasoning; lemon pepper.
16 lasagna nokkles, cooked and drained

Preheat oven to 375'F.  In skillet, brown meat;  Stir in Pasta Sauce heat through.  Meanwhile, in medium bowl, combine ricotta cheesse, 11/2 cups mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, and seasonings;  set aside.

In 13x9-inch baking dish, evenly spread 1/2 cup sauce mixture.  Arrange 4 lasagna noodles, lengthwise over sauce, overlapping edges slightly.  Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over noodles; repeat layers, ending with noodles.  Top with remaining sauce and 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese. Cover with foil and bake 40 min.  Remove foil and continue baking 10 min. or until bubbling.

This Recipe is great to freeze! Make some for now and for later!
