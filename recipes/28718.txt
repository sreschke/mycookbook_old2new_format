3 ea Split chicken breasts
1 cn Cream of chicken soup, Condensed
1 cn Cream of celery soup, Condensed
1    Soup can Water
1 c  Rice
1 pk Onion soup mix
4 oz Mushrooms, canned
 
Mix all ingredients; cover chicken with mixture.  
Bake at 350 F, covered, for 1 hour and remove cover and bake 1/2 hour.
