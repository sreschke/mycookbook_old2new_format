1. Toast nuts in slow oven (325 degrees) until light brown.
2. Beat egg whites and fold in sugar & salt.
3. Melt butter in jelly roll pan (long pan with short lip - like a cookie sheet with little edging around).
4. Spread nut mixture over butter
5. Bake 325 degrees for 30 minutes stirring every 10 minutes or until nuts are coated with brown covering and no butter remains. (If some are stuck together, break apart.)
6. Cool.
