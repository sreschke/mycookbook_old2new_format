7 cups thinly sliced cucumbers
1 cup thinly sliced onions (Vidalia preferred)
1 Tbsp. salt
2 cups sugar
1 cup white vinegar
1 Tbsp. celery seed

Sprinkle salt over onion & cucumbers; let stand 1 hr.  Drain moisture created by the salt; squeeze well.  Mix remaining ingredients & pour over cucumbers & onions.  Cover & place in refrigerator.  Better after 2 days.
