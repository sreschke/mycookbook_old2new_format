1 cup tomato juice
1 cup water
1 pkg yeast
1/4 cup oil
1/3 cup honey
1/4 cup chopped parsley
1/4 cup chopped green onions
2 lg garlic cloves, pressed
1 med carrot, shaved in small pieces
1 tsp salt
5 to 7 cups bread flour
 
Heat tomato juice and water over low til warm to the touch. Pour in a large bowl, add yeast and honey, stir to dissolve. Allow to rest til creamy.  
Mix in oil, parsley, onion, garlic, carrot, and salt. Add 1 cup of flour, stir til smooth. Add more flour til a firm dough is formed. Knead five minutes on floured surface. Place in a greased bowl, and turn to coat completely. Allow to rise til double.  

Punch down, divide in halves. Form two loaves, and put in greased 9x5 loaf pans.  Let rise til double.  

Bake at 400F about 30 minutes, til golden brown. Remove from pans, let cool.
