Crust: 
1 1/2 cups crushed oreos 
2 tbls softened butter 

Filling:
24 oz cream cheese, softened 
1 cup sugar 
3 eggs 
1/2 cup mashed bananas 
1/2 cup heavy cream 
1 tbls lemon juice 
2 tsps vanilla 

Topping:
1 cup heavy cream, whipped 
1/3 cup peanuts, chopped 
16 maraschino cherries 
1/2 cup hot fudge sauce, warm 

Heat oven to 350F. In med bowl, mix crust ingredients. Press in bottom and up sides of ungreased 9" springform pan. Chill. In large bowl, beat cream cheese til smooth. Gradually add sugar, beating smooth. Add eggs 1 at a time, beating well after each addition. Add remaining filling ingredients, beat smooth. Pour into crust. Bake, with shallow pan half full of hot water on lower rack, at 350F for 55 to 65 minutes or til center is set. Turn oven off, let cake sit in oven for 30 minutes with door open. Remove from oven, cool on wire rack. Remove sides of pan. Refrigerate overnight. To serve, top with whipped cream, peanuts, cherries and hot fudge sauce.
