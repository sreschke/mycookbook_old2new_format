1 Pkg 12 oz (2 C choc. chips)
1 C walnuts
1 can condensed milk
1 10 1/2 oz marshmellows
1 tsp vanilla

Melt choc chips and  condensed milk in large bowl on high in microwave for about 3 min.  
Stir well.
Fold in nuts and marshmellows.
Spread in 9 x 13 (or bigger is better) pan
Refrigerate overnight and cut
