2 tbsp olive oil
2 cloves garlic
2 medium onions
1 small fresh red chilli, chopped finely
4 medium ripe tomatoes
1 large stick celery, chopped
1 large green apple, chopped
1/2 cup dry red wine
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 tbsp seeded mustard
1/2 tsp coarse cooking salt
1/4 tsp ground black pepper
1 tsp brown vinegar, a little more if you like

Heat oil in saucepan; add garlic, onions and chilli.  Cook until onions are
soft.  Stir in remaining ingredients; bring to boil, simmer uncovered
stirring occasionally for about 30 minutes or until mixture is thick. 
Blend or process mixture until smooth.
