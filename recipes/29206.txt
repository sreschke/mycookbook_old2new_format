6 slices bacon
1 1/2 tsp olive oil
1/2 cup onions
1/2 cup carrots
1 stalk celery 
4 cups chicken broth
4 cups potatoes, cubed
cayenne pepper to taste
1/2 cup grated cheddar 
salt and pepper to taste 
   
Cook bacon crisp, remove and drain on paper towels.  Chop veggies. Put olive oil in pan and add onion, carrot, and celery as they are cut up.  Cook til onion is soft.  Stir in broth, potatoes, and cayenne, bring to a boil, turn down heat, and simmer, covered, til potatoes are tender, 15 minutes.  Stir in cheese, heating just til melted. Crunch up bacon, add to soup. Adjust seasoning.  
5+ cups.
