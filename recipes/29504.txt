4  cups uncooked macaroni
1 lb ea sharp cheddar, swiss, mozzarella
1/4-1/2 Cup Parmesan cheese
1/2 Cup milk
1 can chopped tomatoes

Preheat oven to 350F
Grease large baking pan. Cook macaroni al dente.
Layer macaroni and cheeses beginning with macaroni and ending with cheese.  Pour milk over all. Bake approx 1 hr or until top is well browned.
This freezes wonderfully and warms up in the microwave like magic.  Serve with cooked chopped tomatoes as a topping or plain.
