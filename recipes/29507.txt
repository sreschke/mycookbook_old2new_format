BEEF BAKE CASSEROLE


1 lb. lean ground beef
1 and 1/2 cups sharp shredded cheddar cheese
1, 14 ounce can Cream of mushroom soup
1 large can French Fried Onions
1/2 Bell pepper, diced or chopped
1, 16 ounce can of tomatoes
1, 8 ounce box of elbow macaroni

Brown ground beef, drain.  Cook macaroni according to package directions,
drain.  While ground beef and macaroni are cooking, mix together in large
bowl the
cheddar cheese, mushroom soup, canned tomatoes and bell pepper.  Add
macaroni
and ground beef.  Stir well.

Pour half of the mixture in a casserole dish, add half the French fried
onions, pour remaining beef mixture over the onions.  Bake covered in 350
degree oven for 25 minutes.  Remove from oven, remove cover and top with the
rest of the French fried onions and bake for 5 more minutes or until the
onions are golden brown.
