9 large russet potatoes, peeled and cut in chunks
1 cup sour cream 
6 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
1/4 cup butter or margarine, room temperature
1/2 cup milk
2 teaspoons onion salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Place potatoes in large saucepan and cover with cold water. Boil until tender, about 30 minutes. Drain. In a large mixing bowl combine all ingredients and beat until smooth. Pour mashed potatoes into greased 9 x13-inch casserole dish. (Can be cooked now or cover and refrigerate up to 1 day.) Bake at 400 degrees in roaster oven until heated through, about 30 minutes. 

Yield: 12 - 14 servings.
