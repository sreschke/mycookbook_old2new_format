8 ounces dried chinese egg noodles
3 Tbsps. peanut oil
2 inches ginger root, cut in thin strips
8 green onions, cut in thin strips
1/2 tsp. salt
1 Tbsps. oyster sauce.

Heat a wok over high heat. Add oil and swirl it around. Add the ginger, stir for a few seconds, then add the green onions and stir until hot. Season with salt. Remove from heat.

Meanwhile plunge the noodles in boiling water for about 1 minute or until al-dente. Seperate the noodles with a fork during cooking. Drain in a colander.

Add the noodles to the wok and toss with a fork. Add the oyster sauce, toss again and serve.
