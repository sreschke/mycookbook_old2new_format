3 egg whites
1/4 tsp. cream of tarter
1 tsp. coffee granules
1 cup sugar.

Combine first three ingredients, beat at high speed until foamy. gradually add sugar until stiff peaks form. Bake at 225F for 1 hour 15 minutes and let cool in oven 2 hours.

Butterscotch Mousse
1 cup butterscotch morsels
1 Tbsp. coffee granules
1 large lightly beaten egg
3 Tbsps. butter
3 Tbsps. water
1 cup whipped cream.

Combine first 4 ingredients, cook over low heat stirring constantly, until morsels and butter
melt. Gradually add egg stirring. Cook over medium heat for 1 minute. Remove from heat and cool to rom temperature. Fold in whipped cream.
