1 pound crab meat
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp white pepper
1 tsp dry mustard
2 tsp worcestershire sauce
1 egg yolk
1 Tbsp mayonnaise
1 tsp chopped parsley
2 eggs, beaten
fine dry bread crumbs
butter or fat for frying

Mix all but beaten eggs and crumbs. Shape into 4 large or 8 small patties. dip in beaten egg, then in crumbs to coat. Fry quickly in butter or oil until browned.
