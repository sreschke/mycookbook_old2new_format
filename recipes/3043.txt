4 whole chicken breasts, boned and skinned
1 (10 oz.) can cream of chicken soup
1/2 can milk (approximately 1/2 cup)
1 can crescent rolls

Wash chicken, cut each in half and pat dry.  Wrap crescent roll around each half and place in a casserole dish. Do not crowd in dish.  Mix soup and milk together and pour over chicken rolls.  Bake at 350Ã¸ until bubbly and browned; approximately 45 minutes.
     NOTE: Can use cream of celery and/or cream of
mushroom soup.  Also can line rolls with deli-style
Williamsburg (country) ham.
	Use same amount of soup and milk if you halve recipe.
