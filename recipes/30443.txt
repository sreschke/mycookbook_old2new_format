TANGERINE SESAME COUSCOUS
(Serves 3)

1 c. orange juice
1/2 c. chopped bell pepper
1 tsp sesame oil
1/4 tsp salt
2/3 c. couscous
2 tangerines
2 green onions, thin sliced with part of green tops

In med. saucepan, combine orange juice, bell pepper, sesame oil and salt.
Bring just to boil and stir in couscous.  Cover and remove from heat.  Let
stand for full 5 min. without lifting lid.

Meanwhile, peel tangerines and cut each segment into 3 parts, discarding
seeds and any tough membrane.  Save any juices over a small bowl and add to
the couscous, along with the cut segments and sliced green onions.  Stir and
fluff with a fork and serve.
