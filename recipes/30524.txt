
1 pkg. yellow cake mix
1/2 cup (1 stick) butter or margarine
1 cup brown sugar 
1 20-oz. can pineapple slices or crushed pineapple (drained) drained maraschino cherry halves 
whipped cream 
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Melt butter in a 13 x 9 x 2" pan. Sprinkle brown sugar evenly in pan. Arrange drained pineapple slices and maraschino cherry halves on the sugar mixture. Mix cake as directed on the label. Beat as directed. Pour batter over fruit. Bake at 350 degrees for about 30 minutes, or until tests done with a toothpick. Let stand 5 minutes for topping to begin to set. Then turn upside down onto a large platter or a cookie sheet. Serve warm with whipped cream.
