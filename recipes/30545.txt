Serves 8

« cup butter
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
2 cups flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 cup sour cream
« cup brown sugar
« cup chopped nuts
2 teaspoons cinnamon

Heat oven to 350ø. Grease a 9" round cake pan. Cream butter and sugar; add eggs. Add flour and baking powder, mixing well to blend thoroughly. Add sour cream. 

In a small bowl, mix brown sugar, nuts and cinnamon thoroughly. 

Spoon half of batter into prepared pan. Top with half of brown sugar mixture. Carefully spread remaining batter over filling and sprinkle with remaining brown sugar mixture. Bake about 45 minutes or until set. Cool slightly before slicing.
