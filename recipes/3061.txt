Small head of lettuce
Rice sticks
1/4 cup Mazola Oil
Cubes of cheese
1/4 black olives
Cooked chicken or turkey strips

Fry rice sticks in hot oil, a few at a time until it    puffs up.
Place on paper towel to drain oil. Place in bowl.
Add remaining ingredients  to rice sticks.
Pour following dressing over all.  Toss well.

Dressing
1/4 cup white vinegar
1/4 cup cooking oil
2 Tbsp. sugar (or Equal)
1/2 tspp. black pepper
