1/2 cup Margarine - melted
1 Tbl Worcestershire Sauce
1/4 tsp celery salt
1/4 tsp seasoned salt
1/4 tsp onion salt
1/4 tsp garlic salt
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper powder - to taste
1 cup Cherrios
2-1/2 cup Rice Chex
2-1/2 cup Corn Chex
2-1/2 cup Crispx or Wheat Chex
1 cup pretzel sticks
1 cup salted peanuts

Preheat oven to 250 degrees (Microwave alternative below). Combine cereals, pretzels, and peanuts in baking dish. Combine melted margarine, Worcestshire sauce, and seasonings. Pour liquid mixture over cereals and mix well.

Place baking dish in 250 deg oven for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.

[Microwave] Cook on highest power for 8 minutes, stirring every 2 minutes.

Pour mixture on to newspapers to cool and place in covered container.
