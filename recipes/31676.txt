from LHJ Kitchens
English Muffin Loaf
Makes 2 loaves 

Ingredients: 
        2 cups Whole Milk
        1/2 cup Water
        6 cups All-purpose Flour, divided
        2 .25-oz. packages Active Dry Yeast
        1 Tbs. Granulated Sugar
        1 tsp. Salt
        1/4 tsp. Baking Soda
        Cornmeal

In a small saucepan, heat the milk and water over medium heat until very
warm. 
                      
Meanwhile, combine 3 cups flour, yeast, sugar, salt, and soda in a large bowl.
Add the warm milk and water, and beat well. Stir in enough of the remaining
flour 
to make a stiff batter. Spoon into two 9 by 5-inch loaf pans that have
been greased 
and dusted with the cornmeal. Cover, and let rise in a warm place for 45
minutes 
to an hour. 
                      
Bake in a pre-heated 400-F degree oven for 25 minutes. Remove from
pans immediately,
and cool on a wire rack. Store in an airtight container; may freeze for up to
90 days.
