Creamy Feta Dip
Makes 2-1/2 cups (20 servings)

1 pound feta cheese, mashed
2 cloves garlic, peeled and crushed
2 tablespoons olive oil
Juice of half a lemon 
2 tablespoons dried oregano
Freshly ground black pepper to taste (optional) 

1. Place ingredients in a food processor fitted with the steel blade. Process until smooth, about 1 minute. Chill for several hours.
2. Serve with bread, crackers or cut-up raw vegetables.
