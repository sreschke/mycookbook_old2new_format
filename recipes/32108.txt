1 (4 serving size) pkg. orange flavored gelatin
1 1/2 cups sugar
2 cups boiling water
1 (46oz) can pinapple juice, chilled
2 cups ReaLemon brand Lemon juice
1 quart orange sherbet
1 (32oz.) bottle club soda, chilled

In bowl, dissolve gelatin and sugar in water; set aside.  In large bowl, combine pineapple juice, ReaLemon juice and gelatin mixutre.  Chill.

 Just before serving, pour juice mixture into punch bowl; add sherbet and club soda.
