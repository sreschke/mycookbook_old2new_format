1 lb. lean hamburgar meat
2 carrots,sliced
1 sm. onion,diced
2 summer squash,sliced
1 can corn,drained
1 can green beans,drained 
2 zucchini,sliced
1 lg.(30 oz) can stewed tomatoes
1 1/2 tablespoons oregano leaves
salt and pepper to taste

In large pot, add all prepared vegtables. Add salt,pepper and oregano. Mix in stewed tomatoes. Add hamburgar, broken in pieces, on top of vegtables. Cover and boil over med/high heat for 45 minutes.
You can add just about any fresh/can vegtables you like in this dish.  Wonderful on cold winter nights along with fresh baked bread or just pain crackers.
