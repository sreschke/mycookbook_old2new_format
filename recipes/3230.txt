6   Chicken Breast Halves, boneless/skinless (2lbs.)
2   Tablespoons Margarine - melted
1/2 Cup Parmesan Cheese (in can)
1/4 Cup Dry Bread Crumbs
1   teaspoon EACH: dried oregano leaves & parsley  flakes.
1/2 teaspoon EACH:  paprika, salt & black pepper

Heat oven to 400 degrees.  Spray 9 X 13 baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.  
Dip chicken in margarine. Then coat with DRY ingredients.  Place into baking dish.
Bake 20 - 25 minutes or until tender.
