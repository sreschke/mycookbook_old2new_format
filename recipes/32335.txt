From MMMMiami--Tempting Tropical Tastes for Home Cooks Everywhere by Carole Kotkin and Kathy Martin

This luscious dessert can be made in three minutes!

1/2 cup sugar
3/4 cup ginger ale
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1 package (20 ounces) frozen whole unsweetened strawberries
4 fresh strawberries 

Process sugar in food processor or blender for 30 seconds.  Add ginger ale and lemon juice; process 30 seconds more.  Add 2 cups strawberries and
pulse several times to break up berries. With machine running, add remaining berries and process until smooth.  Serve at once garnished with
whole fresh berries. Makes 4 servings.
