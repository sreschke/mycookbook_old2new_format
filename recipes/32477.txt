Ingredients:   
        6 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup white sugar
1/8 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 (4 ounce) jar prune baby food with apples
2 eggs
  
 
   
   Directions:  
  1 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease an 8x8 inch square pan.  
  2 In a medium bowl, stir together the flour, cocoa powder, sugar and salt. Add the oil, vanilla, baby food and eggs. Stir until everything is well blended. Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven. Top will be shiny, and a toothpick will come out clean when inserted.
