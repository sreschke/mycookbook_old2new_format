3 cups peeled potato chunks 
2 cups carrot chunks 
1 cup celery chunks 
1 cup water 
10oz  can chicken broth 
1 lb Velveeta 
1/4 cup parsley, chopped 
cracked black pepper 
  
Cook potatoes, carrots and celery in chicken broth and water til veggies are tender. Combine cheese and 1/2 cup of broth from vegetables in microwave safe bowl. Nuke 6 minutes or til cheese melts, stirring every 2 minutes. Add melted cheese to vegetables and broth, stir in parsley. Add more water for thinner chowder. Season to taste with pepper.
