  1 jar marrachino cherries; drained
  2 8oz. cns pineapple tidbits; drained
  1 cn manarin orange segments
  bunch seedless grapes (red or green)
  1 large apple; chunked
  1 16oz. bag walnut pieces
  sour cream and  honey dressing (recipe follows)  

    have all fruit chilled; just before serving, drain canned fruit thoroughly; place all ingredients
in large bowl.  pour dressing over mixture and mix until well coated.  keep  refridgerated until ready to serve.

  sour cream and honey dressing
  
  blend 1 cup sour cream, 2 tbs honey, 2 tbs orange juice.  (if not sweet enough, just add honey and oj until u like it)
