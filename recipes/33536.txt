1 lb ground beef          1 cup sour cream
15 oz chili beans         1/2 cup sliced scallions
8 oz can tomato sauce     1 chopped tomato
2-4 tsp chili powder      1 cup shredded cheese
1 tsp garlic salt
2 cups coarsely broken tortilla chips

Cook and stir ground beef in 10" skillet; drain.  Stir in beans, tomato sauce, taco sauce, chili powder, and garlic salt.  Place chips in ungreased 2 quart casserole.  To with beef mixture.  Spread with sour cream.  Sprinkle with scallions, tomato, and shredded cheese.  Bake uncovered at 350F until hot and bubbly, about 20-30 minutes.
