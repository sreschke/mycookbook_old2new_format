1 1/2 sticks margarine or butter
1 1/2 cup peanut butter
1/2 cup crushed graham crackers (4 whole)
1/2 cup powdered sugar

Mix together and spread in an 8x10 pan lined with wax paper.

Melt 6 oz chocolate chips and 1/2 stick margarine or butter.  Spread on top of peanut butter mixture.

Keep refrigerated.
