2 jars marinated artichoke hearts chopped
1/2 c. mayonnaise
1 large container cream cheese
1/2 c. parmesean cheese
dill weed and/or garlic powder to taste

In food processor, blend all ingredients until smooth.  Pour into 8 x 8 pan.  Bake at 375 for 20 - 30 minutes.  Serve with cubed french bread or crackers.
