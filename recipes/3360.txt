GINGER GLAZE:
2 tbs chopped fresh mint
2 tbs low sodium soy sauce
1 tsp grated lime peel
2 tbs fresh lime juice
1 tbs firmly packed brown sugar
1 tsp grated fresh ginger
1 tsp olive oil
1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper
_________________________________________________
8 pieces of salmon fillet (skinned) 6-8oz each
________________________________________________
GREEN HERB SAUCE (recipe follows)

1. Make GINGER GLAZE:combine all ingredients in small bowl.

2. Place salmon in shallow glass dish and spoon on glaze, turning to coat.  May be made ahead and refrigerated up to 24 hours.

3. Heat broiler.  Line jelly-roll pan with foil.  Arrange salmon on prepared pan and spoon glaze on top.  Broil 4 inches from heat without turning until fish is opaque throughout, 8-10 minutes.  Let stand 5 minutes.  Serve with GREEN HERB SAUCE.  (May also be served without the Green Herb Sauce).
___________________________________________________
GREEN HERB SAUCE:
1/2 c sour cream (use fat-free or low-fat)
2 c loosely packed fresh cilantro leaves
2 c loosely packed fresh mint leaves
1/2 c loosely packed freah parsley leaves
2 jalapeno chiles, micned
1 tbs fresh lime juice
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp freshly ground black pepper

Combine all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth, scraping down sides.  (May be made ahead. Cover and refrigerate up to 8 hours).  Makes 
1 1/4 cups.
