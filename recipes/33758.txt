Trim beans, steam 3 minutes, plunge in ice water to stop cooking, drain.
Combine ingred. for sauce, set aside
Heat peanut oil til very hot, stirfry ginger etc 1 minute or so. Add beans, stirfry 1-2 minutes, stir in seasoning sauce, cook and stir till sauce is absorbed.

Serves 6
