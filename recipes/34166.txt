1 1/2 lb. salmon fillets or steak

salt & pepper

1/4 cup dry white wine

1/3 cup mayonnaise

1/3 cup plain yoghurt

1 Tb. lemon juice

2 Tb. chopped fresh dill

Remove skin from salmon if using fillets.  Cut fillets into serving size pieces; season lightly with salt and pepper and arrange in a single layer in a greased (PAM) shallow baking dish; pour wine over.  Cover and bake in 450 oven for 10 minutes per inch of thickness or until salmon is opague.  Carefully drain cooking liquid into a small saucepan.  Keep salmon warm while preparing sauce.  Stir mayonnaise, yoghurt and lemon juice into liquid in saucepan; heat over low heat until just warmed through - DO NOT BOIL. Stir in dill and season with salt and pepper to taste.  Spoon over salmon and serve.  Makes 4 to 6 servings.
