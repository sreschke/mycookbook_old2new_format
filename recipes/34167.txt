1 lb. salmon fillet
1 cup soy sauce
1 cup teriyaki sauce
juice of one orange
juice of one lemon
juce of one lime
1/2 jalepeno, sliced
1/4 c. shallots, sliced
1/8 c. garlic, diced
1 T fresh ginger
1/4 cup cilantro 

Mix all ingredients, except salmon. 

Place salmon in baking dish.  Pour sauce ingredients on top.  Cover and bake in 375 oven for 20 minutes.  DO NOT PREHEAT OVEN. 

Serve over rice.
