
ORANGE SUNSHINE CAKE:

1    box golden (yellow) cake mix
1    cup water
3    eggs
1/3  cup oil
1    cup orange marmalade
2    tsp grated orange rind

GLAZE:
3/4  cup icing sugar
1    tsp grated orange rind
2    tsp orange juice


Preheat oven to 350`
Mix all the ingredients.
Grease (spray) tube pan & pour in mix.
Bake 45-50 minutes.
Cool 10 minutes then remove from pan & cool completely.
Blend glaze ingredients. 
(you can fix the glaze the way you like by adding more orange juice)
Drip glaze over cooled cake & serve.
