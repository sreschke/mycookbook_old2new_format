2 tablespoons olive oil
1 large onion, chopped
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 tablespoon peeled and minced fresh ginger
1-1/2 tablespoons curry powder
6 medium carrots, peeled & cut into 1-inch lenghts
3 russet potatoes, peeled & cut into 1/2 inch dice
1 medium-sized cauliflower, cut into small florets
4 cups vegetable broth
2 tablespoons honey
1 small cinnamon stick
1 can (19 ounces) chickpeas, drained
1 cup frozen peas, thawed
2 cups seeded and diced plum tomatores
1/2 cup plus 3 tablespoons coarsely chopped parsley
4 cups cooked couscous

Heat oil in large pot over medium heat.  Add onion and cook 10 minutes.  Add garlic and ginger; cook, stirring, 2 to 3 minutes more.  Sprinkle with curry powder; cook, stirring to mellow flavors, 1 to 2 minutes.

Add the next 6 ingredients; simmer, uncovered, until the vegetables are tender, about 20 minutes.  Add the chickpeas and peas; simmer 15 minutes, stirring occassionally.

Before serving, stir in the tomatoes and 1/2 cup parsley; cook curry to heat through.  Divide the couscous among 8 shallow bows and top with curry.  Sprinkle with the remaining parsley and serve.

Serves 8.  Per serving:  445 calories, 87 g carbohydraes, 14 g protein, 5 g fat, no cholesterol.
