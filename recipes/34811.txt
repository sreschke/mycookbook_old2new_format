2 LARGE PKGS. PILLSBURY CHOCHOLATE CHIP COOKIE DOUGH 
3 8OZ PKGS CREAM CHEESE 
3 EGGS 
1 1/2 C SUGAR 
2 TB VANILLA
9X13 PAN

SLICE ONE PKG OF COOKIE DOUGH AND PRESS INTO BOTTOM OF 9X13 PAN.  MIX OTHER INGREDIENTS, BLEND WELL AND SPREAD INTO PAN. SLICE REMAINING PKG. COOKIE DOUGH AND PRESS INTO 9X13 SHAPE BETWEEN TWO PIECES OF PLASTIC WRAP.  GENTLY LAY THE 9X13 TOPPING ON THE BATTER. BAKE AT 350 FOR 45-60 MINUTES.
