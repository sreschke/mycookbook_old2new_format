

2 1/2   to 3lbs Chicken, cut up
1/4     Cup Olive Oil
2       Medium Onions, 1/4" slices
2       Cloves Garlic
1       lb Can Tomatoes
1       Can Tomato Sauce
1       Teaspoon Salt
1/4     Teaspoon Pepper
1/2     Teaspoon Celery Seed
1       Teaspoon Oregano

Mix all together and cook.  Simmer 1 1/2  Hours
