Base:
1/2 c. icing sugar
1 1/2 c. flour
3/4 c. margarine

Topping:
8 oz cream cheese
1/3 c. sugar
1 tsp. vanilla

Glaze:
1 c. pineapple juice
2 Tbsp. cornstarch
1/3 c. sugar
1 tsp. lemon juice

Assorted fresh and canned fruit:  mandarin oranges, bananas, fruit cocktail, pineapple chunks, grapes, strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, kiwi, peaches, etc.

Mix together base ingredients; press into pan (baking sheet).  Bake 15 minutes at 350 F or until light brown.

Combine topping ingredients.  Spread over crust.  Top with fruit.

Cook glaze ingredients over medium heat until clear and thick.  Cool until lukewarm and spread over fruit.  Refrigerate.  Serve chilled.
