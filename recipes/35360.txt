4 large baking potatoes
4 oz. goate cheese
2 tablespoons unsalted butter
1/2 cup milk
1 1/2 tablespoons finely chopped tarragon
Salt/White Pepper
2 table finely grataed Parmesan cheese

Preheat oven to 400*. Put potatoes on oven rack and bake until very tender, about 1 hour. Slice third top off each potatoes and discard or save for another use. Being carefull to leave skin intact, use a spoon to gently scoop out the flesh from the potatoes and place in a mixing bowl. Transfer skins to baking sheet.
while flesh is still hot add the goat cheese and butter and use a potaote masher or fork to mash them in. Mash till smooth. Stir in milk, tarragon, season with salt and pepper. 
Place flesh back in skins. Raise temp in oven to 450*. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese over the tops. Bake till tops are golden about 20-30 minutes
4 servings.
