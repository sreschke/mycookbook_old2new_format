4 CHICKEN BREASTS
1 CAN CREAM OF CELERY SOUP
1 CAN CREAM OF CHICKEN SOUP
1 BAG PEPPERIDGE FARM HERB STUFFING

BOIL CHICKEN AND DEBONE SAVING BROTH.  ADD BOTH CANS OF SOUP TO THE BROTH AND MIX  IN CHICKEN.  LAYER 1/4 BAG OF STUFFING IN THE BOTTOM OF GREASED CASSEROLE DISH.  SLOLWY POUR CHICKEN MIXTURE ON TOP.  SPRINKLE REMAINING STUFFING ON TOP OF CHICKEN.  BAKE AT 350 UNTIL GOLDEN BROWN (ABOUT 30 MINUTES).
