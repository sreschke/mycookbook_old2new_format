1 pnd of beef strips
2 packages of beef ramen soup
2 cups of water
1 1/2 tsp. cornstarch
1 bunch of broccoli (cut up)
1 carrot (sliced thin)

In bowl, combine seasoning packets from soup with 1/2 cup of water and cornstarch.  Add beef to bowl and set aside.  Stir fry broccoli and carrots in a little bit of oil for one minute.  Break up noodles and add to skillet with 1 1/2 cups of water.  Cover and simmer till broccoli is done, set aside.  Add beef mixture to pan and cook till done. Next add veggies.
