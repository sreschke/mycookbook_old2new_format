Lentils are the dried seeds of a pod-bearing plant. A main ingredient in recipes around the world,     lentils are often used as a meat substitute.

Serves 6-8

4 cups Water

2 cups Beef broth

2 cups Chicken broth

1 pound Lentils, dried

2 stalks of Celery, diced

2 medium Carrots, diced

1 medium Onion, diced

1 large Ham hock

1/2 teaspoon Thyme

1 teaspoon Garlic, minced

Salt and pepper to taste

Place all ingredients in a large soup pot. Bring to a boil.
Reduce the heat to a simmer. Cook for one hour. Remove the
ham hock. Allow to cool. Remove the meat from the hock and
return it to the soup. Serve warm.
