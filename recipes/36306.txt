Makes: About 1/2 Cup
1    Hot Red Pepper, Seeds Removed (wear plastic 
         gloves when handling)
3    Cloves Garlic, Chopped
2    Slices Firm Bread, Crusts Removed
1/3  Cup Olive Oil
     Ground Black Pepper to Taste

Chop the red pepper.  Using a mini food processor or a mortar and pestle, thoroughly crush the peppers and garlic into a paste.

Hold the bread under cold running water.  When soaked, squeeze out the moisture.  Add the bread to the food processor or mortar and blend well.

Slowly blend in the oil to fform a sauce of medium consistency.  Add black pepper to taste.

This fiery dipping sauce is great with vegetables, or stir spoonfuls of it into soups and fish stews.
