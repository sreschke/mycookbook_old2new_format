500g (1lb) cream cheese
500g (1lb) smoked roe, skinned and chopped
600ml (2 1/4 cups) sour cream
1/3 cup chopped parsley
4 pickled baby cucumbers
2 tbls lime juice
2 tsp Dijon mustard
S & P

Beat cream cheese until light and fluffy; add smoked roe and mix well.  Stir in sour cream, parsley, cucumbers, lime juice and mustard and S & P to taste.  Place in serving bowl, cover and chill.
