1/3 c. bourbon
1/3 c. water
2 T. sherry
2 T. minced fresh ginger
2 or 3 T. minced garlic
2 pork tenderloins 
1/4 c. molasses
1/4 c. ketchup
1 T. oil for brushing
salt and freshly ground pepper

1. Combine the first 6 ingredients and pour over pork. Marinate at room temperature for 1 hour. Turn a few times.
2. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees.Remove the pork from the marinade, pat dry. 
3. In a small saucepan, boil the marinade until reduced by 1/3. Reduce heat and add molasses and ketchup. Cook until sauce thickenes--about 5 minutes.
4. Heat oil in an ovenproof skillet and sear the pork on all sides--3 to 4 minutes.
5. Pour the BBQ sauce over the pork. Roast for 12 minutes or until cooked through.
6. Transfer the meat to a platter and cover with foil. Rest for 5 minutes. Slice and serve with the remaining sauce poured over the meat.
