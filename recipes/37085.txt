1 med onion
3 tbsp butter
2 cloves garlic minced
1 small head of cabbage
1 apple cored but not peeled
1 medium piece of cooked ham

liquid:
1/2 tsp salt 
pepper to taste
2 tbsp red wine vinegar
dash of sugar
1/2 cup water

Slice onion, saute the onion and garlic in butter. Finely shred cabbage. Cut up the apple into chunks. Add to the onion mixture. Combine salt, pepper, vinegar, sugar and water; Pour over the cabbage and apples. Lay ham on top of all. Cover and steam until cabbage is done (approx 20 min)
