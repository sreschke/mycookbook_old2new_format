2 c. diced rhubarb
1/2 c. sugar
1/4 c. all-purpose flour
Mix & reserve.

Make a double crust pastry. Press 1/2 into bottom of pie pan. Mix other 1/2 of recipe w. 1/2 c. confectioner's sugar & 1/2 t. almond flavor. Reserve for crumb topping.

oven 400

Custard;1 1/2 t. flour
1/2 c. sugar
1/8 c. oil
1 t. butter flavoring 
(or 1/4 c. melted butter for last 2 ingredients)
1 large egg
1/2 c. buttermilk
Whisk all together. Pour into crust. Drop rhubarb mix on top. Top w. crumbs.
Bake 1 hr. until set & lightly browned.
