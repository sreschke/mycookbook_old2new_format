24 slices wholemeat bread, crusts removed
2oz butter or marg, melted
7oz frozen spinach, thawed
4oz feta cheese, crumbled
6 eggs, beaten
1/4 cup milk
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
pepper
Line greased muffin tins with bread slices.  Brush with butter.  Bake in a mod oven for about 15 mins or until crisp and golden.
Drain spinach well. Combine with cheese.  Place about 1 tbls into each bread case.
Whisk eggs, milk, nutmeg and pepper together.  Pour equal amounts into each case.  Bake for a further 15 mins or until set.  Serve hot.
Makes 24.
