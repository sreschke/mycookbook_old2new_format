1 can (14 oz.) artichoke hearts drained & chopped
1 c. (4 oz.) DiGiorno grated parmesan cheese
1 c. Kraft real mayonnaise
1 clove garlic (minced)

heat oven to 350 degrees

Mix all ingredients.Spoon into 9 in. pie plate. 

Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until lightly browned.Serve with crakers or pita bread triangles.
