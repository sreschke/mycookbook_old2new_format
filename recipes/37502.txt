4 baking potatoes
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
1/4 cup butter or margarine softened
Salt & Pepper

1. Preheat oven to 400.
2. Cut potatoes lengthwise into 1/2-inch slices using sharp knife.
3.Drizzle oil over bottom of pan.
4.Spread 1 teaspoon of butter with table knife on one side of potato slice as if buttering bread.
5. Place potato slices, buttered-side-up, in pan.
6. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until light brown and crisp.
7. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Remove from pan using spatula. Serve immediately.
