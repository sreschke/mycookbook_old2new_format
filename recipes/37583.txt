    2 envelopes unflavored gelatin
    1 cup orange juice 
    1/4 cup sugar
    2 cups (two 8-ounce containers) Swiss style    strawberry yogart
    1 cup orange juice
    1/4 cup maraschino cherry syrup 
    1/2 cup finely chopped marschino cherries
    1/2 cup cut orange sections
    1/2 cup flaked cocanut
    
    Sprinkle gelatin over 1 cup orange juice in saucepan. Dissolve over low heat. Remove from heat; add sugar and stir until dissolved. Stir in    yogart, 1 cup orange juice and remaining ingredients. Chill until slightly thickened. Turn into 5-cup mold, bowl or individual serving dishes. Chill until firm. Makes 8 servings.
