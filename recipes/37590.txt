1 MEDIUM HEAD CAULIFLOWER
2 BUNCHES BROCCOLI
1 CAN NACHO CHEESE SOUP

Wash cauliflower and broccoli, cut into bite size pieces.  Drain off all but 1 tablespoon of water.  Stir in the soup.  Microwave on medium for about 8 minutes.  Stir,check if tender.  If needing more time, cook for 1 to 2 min at a time.  Serves 6

This is especially good for large get togethers.
