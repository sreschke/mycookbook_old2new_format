1-1/2 lbs lean ground beef
3 slices bacon, diced
1 small onion, chopped
2 tbs flour
1/4 tsp paprika
1 tsp salt
1 can (10-3/4 oz) condensed cream of mushroom soup
2 tbs dry red wine
1 cup dairy sour cream
6 to 8 hamburger buns, toasted and buttered

In a large skillet, brown beef and bacon until red color disappears; drain.

In crockpot, mix together drained beef, bacon, onion, flour, paprika and salt.

Stir in undiluted soup and wine.  Cover pot and cook on low 4 to 5 hours.

Stir in sour cream.

Spoon mixture over toasted buns.

Serves 6 to 8.
