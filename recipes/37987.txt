Brown 1 lb. ground beef, adding onion flakes, 1 envelope taco seasoning, and garlic powder.  Drain grease and stir in 1/2 can enchilada sauce and 3/4 cup shredded cheese (choose your favorite).

Spoon 2 tablespoons meat mixture into a flour tortilla, roll tortilla and place seam side down in a baking dish.  When all tortillas are in dish, pour remaining 1/2 can enchilada sauce and 1 1/4 cups cheese over enchiladas.  Bake at 350% until cheese melts.

Serve with lipton's mexican rice, refried beans, sour cream and tortilla chips.  1 lb. ground beef makes 12 enchiladas.
