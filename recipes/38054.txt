HONEY  GARLIC  CHICKEN  WINGS

1 large onion, chopped
1/2 cup honey
1/4 cup soy sauce
Garlic as desired
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 tsp dry mustard
5 lbs chicken wings, thawed

     Combine above ingredients, except for chicken wings and cook for 5 minutes.  Spread chicken wings in a large shallow pan, 15x10x1.  Pour sauce over the wings.  Bake at 350 degrees uncovered for 1 1/2 hours, turning a couple of times during the cooking.
