2 tablespoons olive oil or vegetable oil
1/2 cup chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
3-1/2 cups (28-ounce can) tomatoes, undrained
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
2 teaspoons basil
1 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon oregano
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper
3 cups (8 ounces) San Giorgio Trio Italiano noodles
1 cup (4 ounces) shredded mozzarella cheese


In 2-quart casserole, heat oil and cook onion and garlic until tender but not brown.  Stir in tomatoes with liquid, parsely, sugar, basil, oregano, salt and pepper; bring to a boil then reduce heat and simmer, stirring occasionally to break up tomatoes, 15 to 20 minutes.  Meanwhile, cook noodles according to package; drain.  Toss pasta wit sauce and place in 1-quart casserole.  Top with mozzarella cheese and broil for 3 minutes or until cheese is melted. 

Makes about 4 servings.
