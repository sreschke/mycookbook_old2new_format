1 tbsp margarine
1 boneless, skinless Chicken breast (about 1 lb), cut into strips
1-2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 c. milk
1 (16 oz)pkg Green Giant Primavera Pasta Accents Frozen Vegetables with Pasta

Melt margarine in large skillet over med-high heat.  Add chicken and garlic; cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink, about 3-4 min.  Add milk and frozen vegetables with pasta.  Cover; simmer 4-8 min or until vegetables are crisp-tender.
