1/2 mashed banana
1 Tablespoon peanut butter 
1 package cocoa or milkshake mix 
1/4 cup grape nuts 

mix together mashed banana and peanut butter.  Then 
stir in cocoa mix and add grape nuts last.  Pat out 
into bar shape onto a 7" by 9" piece of aluminum foil.  Place into freezer and let freeze until solid.  May be eaten as an on the run breakfast bar 
or afternoon snacks.
