3 lbs	Potatoes, skinned and diced
4 T	Vinegar
1 C	Mayonaise
2 T	Milk
1 T	Parsley
Salt & Pepper to taste

Boil potatoes until just tender, not mushy. Allow to cool. Add vinegar. Mix mayonaise, milk  and parsley in another bowl until well blended. Mix lightly into potato mixture. Serve cool or at room temperature.
