4 slices bacon (cut into 1/2-in. pieces)
1 small onion, thinly sliced, optional
2 (28-0z.) can green beans with liquid
1 (14 1/2-oz.) can hunts diced tomatoes with liquid
1 envelope(lipton's)dry onion soup mix
1/2 tsp. dried tarragon
1/2 tsp. fresh garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. sugar
salt and pepper to taste

  Fry bacon pieces in a deep skillet until crisp, add onion slices, if using, and cook until tender and translucent.
  Add diced tomatoes with liquid, dry onion soup mix, tarragon, garlic and sugar. Cook over low heat for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
  Add green beans and salt and pepper. simmer for approximatly 15-20 minutes.
  Note: This dish taste even better the second time around.
  Makes 10 servings.
