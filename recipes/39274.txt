Red Chili Sauce

20 Dried Red Chilies
3 Cups Water
3 Cloves of Garlic
1 Tsp Cumin
1 Med Yellow Onion
 Salt
1 Tsp Sugar (Optional)

	Devein and seed chilies
	Wash the chilies and put into pot of boiling water, cover.
	Let stand for 1 hour.
	Take chilies, onions and garlic and puree w/ 2 cups of cooking water. 
		(add more water, if necessary, to reach proper consistency)
	Transfer to sauce pan and add cumin and salt (and sugar if bitter).
