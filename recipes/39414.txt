1 cup graham cracker crumbs
3 Tbs. butter or margarine, melted
3 8-oz. pkgs. Philadelphia Cream Cheese, softened
3/4 cup sugar
5 tsp. lemon juice
1 1/2 tsp. grated lemod peeel
1 tsp. vanilla
3 eggs, separated

Combine crumbs and margarine; press into bottom of 9-in. springform pan.  Bake at 325 degrees for 10 min.

Combine cream cheese, sugar,juice, peel and vanilla mixing at medium speeed on electric mixer until well blended.  Add egg yolks, one at a time, mixing well after each addition.  Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form; fold into cream cheese mixture. Pour over crust.  Bake at 300 degrees for 45 min.    Loosen cake from rim of pan; cool before removing rim of pan.  Chill.  Top with cherry pie filling or fresh fruit, if desired.   
Serves: 10 - 12

Variation:  Top with 6 oz. almond brickle chips before baking.  Omit chocolate pieces.
