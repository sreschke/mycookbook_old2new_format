Nut Crust:
2 cups ground pecans
2 Tbs. brown sugar
1 egg white, beaten until frothy
1 tsp. powdered ginger
1 tsp. finely grated lemon rind

In bowl, mix nuts with sugar, egg white, ginger and rind just until mixture is bound together.  Press onto bottom and a little up the sides of 10 inl. springform pan.

4 eggs
3 egg yolks
5 8z. pkgs, cream cheese, softened
3/4 cup firmly packed dark brown sugar
2 tsp. ground cinnamon
1 tsp. ground nutmeg
1 tsp. ground ginger
1/4 tsp. ground allspice
2 1/2 tsp. freshly grated lemon rind
3 Tbs. flour
1 cup whipping cream
1 Tbs. vanilla
1 lb. pumpkin puree, fresh or canned
Coarsely grated lemon rind for garnish

Prepare nut crust.  
In large mixing bowl, lightly beat eggs and yolks.  Add softened cream cheese and sugar and beat until thoroughly mixed.  LBeat in cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger, allspice, lemon rind and flour.  Beat in cream, vanilla and pumpkin.  Pour into nut crust.  Place pan of hot water in bottom of oven to keep cake from cracking.  LBake at 400 degrees for 20 min.  Reduce heat to 250 degrees and bake 50 to 60 min. longer.  
Turn off heat and allow cake to cool in oven 2 hrs. Then refrigerate to chill.  Remove springform before serving and decorate cake with coarsely grated rind.

Makes 12 - 16 servings
