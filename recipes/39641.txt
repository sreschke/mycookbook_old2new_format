Recipe By     : Nestle
Serving Size  : 40   Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : Cookies

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
          20-22         chocolate sandwich cookies
   1 1/2  cups          chopped pecans
   1 1/2  cups          mint flavored semisweet chocolate chips
   1      cup           flaked coconut
  14      ounces        sweetened condensed milk

Crush cookies with filling until finely ground (should measure 2 cups); press onto bottom of greased 13x9" baking pan. Layer pecans, 1 1/4 cups
chips and coconut over crumbs; drizzle with sweetened condensed milk. Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 20-25 min. or until coconut is light
golden brown around edges. Cool in pan. Microwave remaining 1/4 cup chips in heavy-duty plastic bag on HIGH (100 percent) power for 30 seconds. Knead
until smooth. Cut tiny corner from bag; squeeze to drizzle chocolate over bars. Chill for 5 min. to set chocolate. Cut into 2 1/2x1" bars.
