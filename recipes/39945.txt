1/3 C red lentils
1 garlic clove, minced
2/3 C water
1 lb. Capellini
15 oz. can small red beans, rinsed and drained
1 jar (3 1/2 C) bottled tomato sauce
1/2 t basil
2 T fresh parsley

Bring  lentils, garlic and water to a boil in a small saucepn over medium-high heat.  Reduce heat and simmer 8-10 minutes, uncovered, or until lentils are firm tender.

Bring a large pot of water to a boil.  Add pasta. Cook until tender.

Place beans, pasta sauce, basil and lentils with any remaining liquid in a large saucepan.  Bring to a boil over medium-high heat.  Stir, reduce heat, cover and simmer 10 minutes.

Top pasta with sauce and garnish with fresh parsley.
