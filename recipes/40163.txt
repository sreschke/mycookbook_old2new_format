1 lb. ground beef
1 jar (16 oz.) Thick 'N Chunky Salsa, divided
1 pkg. (8 oz.) Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese, 
   divided
10 flour tortillas

BROWN meat; drain. Stir in 1/2 cup salsa and 1 cup cheese.

SPREAD 1 cup salsa in 13 x 9-inch baking dish. Place 1/4 cup meat mixture down center of each tortilla; roll up. Place tortillas, seam-side down, on salsa. Top with remaining 1/2 cup salsa and 1 cup cheese.

BAKE at 350.F for 20 to 25 minutes.

MICROWAVE  Prepare as directed except for baking. Cover dish with plastic wrap. Microwave on HIGH  6 minutes or until cheese is melted and tortillas are hot.
