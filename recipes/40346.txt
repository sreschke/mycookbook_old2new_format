2 lbs. finely diced venison or sirlion
1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce ...(rtread uses 2 cans)
1/2 cup chili powder 
2 cups diced onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 - 1 tsp cumin  
1 tsp oregano 
2 tsp paprika
Salt to taste
Red Pepper to taste
2 TBLSP Masa Flour 

Sear 2 lbs vension or sirloin.                     - Add tomato sauce and water.(rtread says he throws in a can of Ro-tel tomatoes too)
- Stir in all ingredients except the red pepper and masa flour. over skillet or saucepan and simmer for 30 minutes. Stir occasionally, taste oten            
- Stir masa flour into 1/4 cup warm water to make a thick, flowable mixture. Add masa mixture and simmer
another 20 minutes. Masa mixture will "tighten" the flavor.
