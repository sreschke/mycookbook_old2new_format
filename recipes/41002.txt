1 tsp vegetable oil
4 TBSP butter
2 tenderloin steaks
1/2 c. chopped mushrooms
1 TBSP chopped green onions
1 TBSP flour
1/8 tsp salt
Dash pepper
2/3 c. beef broth

In a skillet, heat oil and 2 TBSP butter over med-high heat. Cook steaks 6-7 mins on each side, or until desired doneness. Remove from heat and keep warm. To pan juices add mushrooms, onions and remaining butter; saute until tender. Add flour, salt and pepper; stir in broth until smooth. Bring to a boil; boil and stir for 2 mins. Spoon over steaks; serve immediately. Serves 2.
