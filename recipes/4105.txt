1/2 cup shortening 
1/2 cup sugar, brown or granulated
2 eggs, unbeaten
1 cup molasses
1 cup water
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
2-1/2 cups sifted bread flour
1 teaspoon ginger
1 teaspoon cloves

Crem shortening; add sugar gradually, creaming well.  Add one egg at a time, beating well.  Combine water and molasses; sift together dry ingredients.  Alternately add dry ingredients and liquid, beating only till smooth.  Bake in well-greased, floured pan (13x9x2 inches) at 350 degrees for 40 minutes.  Cool in pan for 5 minutes; turn out on wire cake rack.  Serve warm or cold. Top with tart apple sauce or whipped cream.
