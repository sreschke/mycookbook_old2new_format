1/2 Package Nasbisco chocolate wafers
1/2 cube Butter,melted
1qt.Coffee ice cream
1 1/2 cup Fudge sauce
Crush wafers and add butter,mix well.Press into 9" pie plate. Cover with soft ice cream. Put into freezer until ice cream is firm. Top with cold fudge sauce(it helps to place in freezer for a time to make spreading easier). Store in freezer for 10 hours.
Slice Mud Pie into eight portions and serve on a chilled dessert plate with a chilled fork.  Top with whipped cream and slivered almonds.
