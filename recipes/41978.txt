 32 oz jar Ragu
1 lb fettuccine
2 Tbls butter
8 oz cream cheese
small carton sour cream
1 cup chopped black olives
1 cup chopped green onions
2 cups shredded cheddar
2 lbs ground beef
5 tsps  salt  

Sauce: Brown beef with 2 tsps of salt added til cooked. Drain. Mix in Ragu. 

Boil fettuccini with 1 Tbls  salt til cooked. Drain. Mix with butter. 

Mix  sour cream, cream cheese, olives, and onions. 

Place 1 cup sauce in greased 9x13 baking dish. Top with noodles. Top with remaining sauce. Spread cream cheese mix over sauce. Top with cheddar cheese. Bake at 350F til bubbly  (about 25 minutes).  

Original recipe by William Conrad (Jake and the Fat Man)
