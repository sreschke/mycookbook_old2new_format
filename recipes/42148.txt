Serves 4 to 6

Ingredients:

2 Tbs. Olive Oil
1 Onion, chopped finely
1 clove Garlic, chopped finely
500g (or 1 lb.) Beef, cubed
3 medium Potatoes, cubed
1 medium Eggplant, cubed
1 cup crushed fresh Tomatoes OR
1 tin of peeled Tomatoes, crushed
Salt and Black Pepper to taste
5-6 cups Water
Croutons
 
Warm the olive oil over medium heat in a 3-quart pot. 

Add the onion and garlic, and sautâ€š until onion is soft. 

Add the beef, stirring until browned. Add the potatoes and eggplant, and stir for a few minutes more.
 
Add the tomatoes, salt, pepper, and the water, and stir to mix through.
 
After bringing to a boil, reduce the heat to simmer for about 20 to 30 minutes or until everything is cooked through.
 
Serve warm with croutons.
