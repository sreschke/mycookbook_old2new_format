1 large onion, diced
1 large green bell pepper, diced
1 lb. smoked sausage, cut into 1/2 inc slices
1 T. olive oil
4 cups chopped cooked chicken
3 cups uncooked long grain rice
2 10 1/2 oz cans French onion soup, undiluted
1 14 1/2 oz. can chicken broth
1 14 1/2 oz. can beef broth
2 to 3 t. Creole seasoning
2 to 3 t. hot sauce
Garnish: fresh cilantro sprigs
1. Saute first 3 ingredients in hot oil in a Dutch oven 4 to 5 minutes or until sausage is browned. Stir in chicken and next 6 ingredients.
2. Bake, covered, at 350 degrees for 40 minutes, sirring after 30 minutes. Garnish, if desired.
Yeild: 8 to 10 servings
