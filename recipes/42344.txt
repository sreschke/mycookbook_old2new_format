2 squares unsweetened chocolate, 1 oz.
1 can condensed milk, 14 oz
3 cp sweetened flaked coconut
1 tsp vanilla
1 pinch salt
   whole almonds

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Line baking sheet with parchment paper.

Melt the chocolate and condensed milk, stir frequently.

Pour the melted chocolate mixture over the coconut.  Stir in the vanilla and salt.  Mix well until the coconut is completely coated.  Drop dough by teaspoonfuls onto the prepared baking sheet.  Press one whole almond into the top of each cookie.

Bake at 350 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes.  Check at 8 minutes as the bottoms tend to burn easily.  Remove from oven & cool.

makes 3-4 dz & tastes a lot like almond joys
