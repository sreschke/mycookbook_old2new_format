1 lb. jumbo shrimp, peeled and deveined
2 TBSP butter
1 clove garlic, finely chopped
1 tsp chopped fresh parsley
1/2 tsp basil leaf, crumbled
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1/3 c. fresh bread crumbs
1/4 c. dry vermouth
1 TBSP dry vermouth

Finely chop 2 shrimp. Combine chopped shrimp, butter, garlic, parsley, basil, salt and pepper in a bowl. Stir in bread crumbs and 1 TBSP vermouth. Cut lengthwise down the bellies of remaining shrimp without cutting through. Divide fillingamong shrimp. Fold tails over. Arrange in a 8x8" baking dish. Add remaining vermouth. Bake @ 450F for 10-12 mins or until just cooked through. Serves 2.
