
2 cups shredded Swiss Cheese
1 tbs. Dill Weed
2 tbs. Flour
2 cups Milk
3 cups cubed, cooked Ham
1/4 cup Bread Crumbs
  1/2 cup sliced Green Onions
2 tbs. Butter
1 tsp. Salt
5-6 lg. Potatoes, cut into thin slices
Boiling water
 

Put potato slice in boiling water for 10 minutes, or until soft. Drain. In small bowl, toss together 1 1/2 cups cheese, onion, and dill weed. Set aside. Melt butter in 1 quart saucepan, blend in flour and salt. Cook over low heat until mixture is smooth. Stir in milk, then heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil one minute. Layer 1/3 potatoes, 1/2 ham, 1/2 cheese mixture, 1/2 sauce in buttered, shallow 3 quart baking dish. Repeat - making last layer with 1/3 potatoes. Tor with remaining cheese and bread crumbs. Bake 350 for 30 minutes.
