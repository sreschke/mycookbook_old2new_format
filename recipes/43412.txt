Carrot Patties

1 pound carrots, peeled and grated
1 clove garlic, minced
4 eggs
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/4 cup matzo meal
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 pinch ground black pepper
2 tablespoons vegetable oil 
 
In a medium size mixing bowl, combine the grated每carrots, garlic, eggs, flour, bread crumbs, salt and black每pepper; mix well. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat. Make每the mixture into patties, and fry until golden brown on每each side.
