1/2 cup minced onion
1 clove garlic, minced
2 Tbs. vegetable oil
1 (16 oz.) can tomatoes, drained and coarsely chopped
1 cup Pace Picante sauce
1 Tbs. chili powder
1 Tbs. ground coriander
1/2 tsp. ground cumin
12 oz. mushrooms, sliced
1 (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese softened and cubed
1/4 cup sliced green onions with tops
8 (6 to 7 in.) flour tortillas
3/4 cup shredded Montertey Jack cheese
Dairy sour cream
Shredded lettuce

Cook onion and garlic in 1 Tbs. oil until tender.  Stir in tomatoes, picante sauce, 1 tsp. chili powder, 1 tsp. coriander and the cumin.   Bring toa boil, simmer uncovered 15 min.,
stirring occasionally.  In 10 in. skillet, cook mushrooms with remaining chii powder and coriander in remaining oil over medium-high heat until mushrooms are tender and liquid evaporates.
Remove from heat; stir in cream cheese until melted.  Stir in 1/4 cup of the picvante sauce mixture and green onions.  Spoon 1/3 cup mushroom mixture down center of each tortilla; roll up and place seam side down in greaed 12x7 in. baking dish.  Spoon remaining mixture over enchiladas.  Cover tightly with foil; bake at 350 degrees for 20 min.  Top with cheese, sour cream lettuce and additional picante sauce.
Makes 4 servings.
