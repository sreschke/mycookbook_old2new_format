1 Tbs. oil
2 boneless skinless chicken breast halves, cut into thin bite-size pieces
1 garlic clove, minced
1/4 tsp. ginger
1/2 cup water
2 Tbs. soy sauce
1 (10 1/2 oz.) can condensed chicken broth
4 oz. uncooked angel hair pasta (capellini) broken into thirds
1 (1 lb.)) pkg. frozen broccoli, carrots and water Chestnuts

Heat oil in large skillet or wok over medium-high heat until hot.  Add chicken and garlic;  cook and stir 4 to 5 min. or until chicken si no longer pink.
Add ginger, water, soy sauce and broth.  Bring to a boil.  LStir in pasta.  Add frozen vegetables; stir gently.  Return to a boil.  Reduce heat to medium-low; cover and simmer 5 to 8 min. or until pasta and vefetables are tender, stirring occasionally.
