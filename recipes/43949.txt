2 T oil
2 3/4 cups celery, chopped
1/3 cup shallots, chopped
1 T garlic minced
2 lbs. ground pork
1 cup breadcrumbs
4 oz. dried apples
3 large eggs, beaten
1 cup parsley, chopped
2 tsp sage
2 tsp salt
3/4 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp allspice 
1 cup beef broth
Heat oil in medium skillet. Add celery, shallots, garlic and saute till tender. Transfer to bowl and mix in remaining ingredients except broth. Just add enough broth to moisten, this may be added to pork roast or cooked seperately in pan till temp measures 155 degrees . About 1 hour
10 servings  I use this to stuff a crown roast of pork for the holidays.
