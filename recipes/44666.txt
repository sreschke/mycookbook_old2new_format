1/2 C flour
4 TB margarine
2 TB sugar
dash of lemon
1/4 tsp vanilla
1 egg yolk

Mix in food processor.  Put 1/3 on bottom of springform pan.  Bake 7 min. at 400 d.  Remove from oven. Put remainder of uncooked dough on the sides of the pan.  Pour Real Cheese Cake batter in. Bake as per Cheese Cake instructions.
