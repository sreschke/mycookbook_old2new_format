
1 lb pasta (spaghetti,linguine or taliatelle)
4 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
2 cloves garlic, thinly spliced, or more to taste
1 to 2 cups corasley chopped herbs, such as (basil, parsely,thyme,oregano, summer savory,rosemary, or 
chervil
salt and pepper to taste
freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Cook pasta according to package directions.
Heat oil in a skillet over low heat. Add garlic and cook, stirring oaccionally, until the garlic begins to brown (about 8 minutes). Add the herbs and salt and pepper. Cook, stirring occassionally, for 1 minute.The herbs should remain green. Do not overcook. Remove pan from heat. 

Drain pasta and transfer to a large bowl. Add the herb mixture.Divide among the plates. Sprinkle w/cheese. Serve immediately.
