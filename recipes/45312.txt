8 ounces extra-lean ground beef 
1 large onion, chopped 
1 small stalk celery, chopped 
1 small apple, chopped 
1 2/3 cups sliced mushrooms 
2 cups defatted reduced-sodium beef broth 
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme leaves 
1/4 teaspoon caraway seeds 
2 cups uncooked medium yolk-free egg noodles 
1 tablespoon paprika 
3 tablespoons tomato paste 
2 tablespoons plain nonfat yogurt 

Coat a large frying pan with nonstick spray. Crumble the beef into the pan. Add the onions, celery, apples and mushrooms. Cook over medium-high heat, breaking up the mixture with a wooden spoon, for 4 to 5 minutes, or until the meat is lightly browned. 

Stir in the broth, thyme, caraway seeds and noodles. Bring to a boil. Reduce the heat, cover and simmer for 5 to 8 minutes, or until the noodles are just tender. 

Stir in the paprika and tomato paste. Cover and simmer for 5 minutes. 

Remove from the heat, stir in the yogurt and serve.
