               

   1 lb. hamburger             1 (8 oz.) pkg. shredded Cheddar Cheese
   1/2 onion                         6 or 8 servings instant mashed potatoes
   1 jar Homestyle brown gravy
   1 can drained corn

   Brown hamburger and onion; drain. In large casserole or Dutch oven, mix hamburger and gravy to make the bottom layer. Next layer the corn.. Follow directions for mashed potatoes for top layer. Sprinkle top with Cheddar cheese. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes or until cheese is melted and potatoes are golden brown.
