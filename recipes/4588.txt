Serves 6

2tsp Cornstarch
1/4 C Soy Sauce
1/4C Apricot preserves
2Tbsp Sugar
2Tbsp White vinegar
1 Mediun clove garlic (minced)
1/4tsp Ground ginger
Dash of pepper
1Tbsp Orange juice
1Tbsp Brandy (unflovored)
1 Chicken fryer 3 1/2 to 4 lbs (cut into serving size)

Pre-Heat Oven to 350deg
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
STIR:/......Cornstarch & soy sauce in a sauce pan,
Add....apricot, sugar, vinegar, garlic, ginger, & pepper  (mix well)
Cook over medium heat until thick & bubbling, about 2-4 min.

Remove from heat and stir in the Orange Juice & Brandy
Now place the chicken SKIN DOWN, in a shallow glass (13x9x2) dish
Pour sauce over the top
Bake 30 minutes, turn chicken & baste
Bake another 30 minutes (or more if necessary) until tender

Enjoy!
