1 box corkscrew noodles, cooked
1 c tomatoes, chopped
1/4 lb provolone cheese, chopped
1/4 lb hard salami, chopped
3/4 cup(?) red bell pepper, chopped
1/2 cup pepperoncini, chopped
green olives

Mix cooked noodles with other ingredients.  Toss with Italian dressing.  Refrigerate.  Will last several days if tomatoes are removed.

Serves 6?
