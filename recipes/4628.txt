6 slices bacon
2 Tbsp brown sugar
1 med head of cabbage, cut in thin strips
1 clove garlic, minced
1 teasp seasoned salt
1 med onion, chopped
1 lb kielbasa or polish sausage, cut in 1" cubes
1/2 cup water

Cook bacon in large skillet or dutch oven until crisp; remove and crumble; set aside. To the bacon drippings add cabbage, onion, water, sugar, garlic and salt; cover and simmer 6 minutes over medium heat, turning cabbage once. Add sausage; cover and cook 5 minutes ot until sausage is heated. Transfer to serving dish with slotted spoon; sprinkle with bacon.
