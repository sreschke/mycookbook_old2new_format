1 can of small shrimp - reserve liquid
1 8 oz pkg of Philly Cream Cheese - softened
1/2 small onion, finely chopped
1/4 C mayonaise
canned evaporated milk - to taste
w/8 tsp worchestershire sauce

Blend shrimps onion and cream cheese - reserving the 2 T liquid from the shrimp.   Add in worchestersire sauce, maynaise until well blended.    Add in milk unitl creaming and dipable.(Time - 15 minutes)
