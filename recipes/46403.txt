1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup wheat germ
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup butter, melted
1 egg
2 ripe bananas, mashed
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/3 cup cocolate chips

Heat the oven to 350 degrees F.  In a bowl, combine the first five ingredients.  In another bowl, mix the sugar, butter, egg, bananas and vanilla extract.  Combine the two mixtures and spread the batter into a lightly greased 13x9 in. pan.  Top with the nuts and chocolate chips.  Bake for 20 to 25 min.  Let cool and cut into small squares.
Yield:  20 to 24 bars
