4 Cups    Sliced peeled fresh or frozen Peaches thawed.
1 1/2 cups sugar, divided.
1/2 tsp almond extract.
1/2 cup of butter or margarine melted.
3/4 cup of all purpose flour.
2 tsp baking powder.
pinch of salt.
3/4 cup of milk.
Vanilla ice Cream optional.

In a bowl gently toss peaches, 1 cup of sugar and extract.   Set aside.   Pour butter into a 2 quart baking dish.   In a bowl, combine flour, baking powder, salt and remaining sugar;  stir in milk.   Mix well.   Pour evenly over butter, (do not stir).   Top with Peach mixture.   Bake at 350 degrees for 50 - 55 minutes or until golden brown.   Serve with ice cream if desired.  Yield 8 servings.
