Cherry filling:
1 1/2  C sugar
1/3 C flour
4 cups fresh or frozen red tart cherries
1/2 teas almond extract.
Crunch Topping:
4 tab. sugar
4 tab. brown sugar, firmly packed
2/3 c quick cooking oats
6 Tab butter, melted

Mix sugar and flour together.  Stire in cherried and extract.  Pour into pastry lined pan.
For topping, combine sugars, flour and oats, mix in melted butter.  Spoon Mixturer over pie filling
Bake 425 for 35 to 45 min.
