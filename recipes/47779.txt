2 1/2 lb.tied veal roast salt and peppered
2 Tbs butter or olive oil
3/4 cup finely cubed carrots
3/4 cup finely diced oninon
1 garlic clove
2 sprigs of fresh rosemary or 2 tsp dried
1/2 cup white wine
1 cup diced tomatoes
2 sprigs parsley

   Heat oil in heavy casserole so veal fits snugly.  Brown veal on all sides over moderate heat (10-15 mins.)
   Add carrots, onion, garlic and rosemary. Stir and cook 2 mins. 
   Add wine tomatoes and parsley. 
   Cover abd cook over low heat for 1 - 1 1/4 hours.  
   Uncover.  Turn meat.  Cook another 15 mins.
