TODAY'S RECIPE: SPINACH SPAGHETTI 


We all know that spinach is good for us--and, given that not everyone 
enjoys a steaming heap of cooked spinach, we're also finding various 
ways to blend this vitamin-rich ingredient into recipes we enjoy. In 
this case, ordinary cheese-and-noodles becomes special with the 
addition of spinach and Monterey Jack cheese. Serves 2-4. Per serving: 
448 cal, 27.1 g fat, 122 mg cholesterol, 27.2 g carbohydrates, 2.9 g 
fiber, 24.7 g protein, 757 mg sodium. 

1 egg, beaten 
2 tsp minced dried onion 
1/2 cup sour cream 
1/4 cup milk 
1/2 tsp salt 
Dash pepper 
8 oz shredded Monterey Jack cheese 
10-oz pkg spinach, cooked and drained 
4 oz spaghetti, cooked and drained 
4 tbsp Parmesan cheese 

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Combine egg, onion, sour cream, milk, 
salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese. Add Monterey Jack 
cheese; mix well. 

Mix in spinach and spaghetti. Place in 10-inch by 6-inch baking dish; 
sprinkle with remaining Parmesan. Bake 15 minutes covered, then 15 
minutes uncovered.
