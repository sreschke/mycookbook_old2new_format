1 pint fresh strawberries, hulled and chopped
1/2 orange, zested and juiced
2 to 4 teaspoons sugar, (depending on the sweetness of the berries)
1 tablespoon instant espresso powder, dissolved in 2 tablespoons warm water
2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
1 8 oz. container mascarpone cheese
1/2 cup powdered sugar, divided
1 cup heavy cream
1 package cannoli shells, (6 shells)
 chopped pistachio nuts, (optional)

  Place the chopped strawberries in a medium bowl.  Add orange zest and juice. Sprinkle sugar over. Toss to combine. Set aside to blend flavors.
  Combine the dissolved espresso and vanilla. Set aside. Place softened mascarpone cheese in the bowl of an electric mixer. Beat lightly to soften. Slowly blend in 1/4 cup of the powdered sugar.Slowly beat in espresso mixture. Set aside. Place the heavy cream in another mixing bowl. While beating on high speed, gradually add remaining powdered sugar. Beat just until soft peaks form.
   Fold 1/2 of whipped cream into cheese mixture to lighten the cheese mixture. Fold in remaining whipped cream. Place mixture in gallon-size zipper bag. Snip off 1 corner of bag and pipe mixture into cannoli shells. Spoon strawberry mixture over. Sprinkle with chopped pistachio nuts if desired. Serve immediately.
