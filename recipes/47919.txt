1 tablespoon olive oil
1/2 onion, chopped
1/4 pound smoked ham, chopped
1 cup shredded potatoes, (refrigerated Simply Potatoes)
6 large eggs, beaten to blend
 salt & freshly ground black pepper, to taste

   Preheat broiler. Heat oil in a 10-inch ovenproof skillet over medium heat. Add onion and saute until starting to brown, about 5 minutes. Stir in ham and potatoes. Add eggs and stir to distribute. Using a spatula, lift edges and allow uncooked eggs  to run underneath. When eggs are set but still moist on top, place skillet in oven and broil until top is set and lightly browned. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Serve in wedges, hot or at room temperature.
