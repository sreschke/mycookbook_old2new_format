 vegetable cooking spray
1 9 oz. package fresh Chinese noodles
1 teaspoon sesame oil
1/2 teaspoon soy sauce

Preheat broiler. Spray a small frying pan with vegetable cooking spray. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add noodles. As soon as the water returns to a boil, drain noodles and rinse under cold water. Shake dry and place in a large bowl. Toss noodles with sesame oil and soy sauce. Spread noodles in evenly in frying pan. Place pan on the middle rack of the oven. Broil for about 10 minutes, until noodles are crisp. (Watch carefully.) When noodles are crisp, remove to plate and flip carefully back into skillet. Sprinkle lightly with more sesame oil and return to broiler for another 5 minutes. Cut noodles into 4 portions with scissors or a pizza cutter and serve, spooning stir-fry and sauce on top.
