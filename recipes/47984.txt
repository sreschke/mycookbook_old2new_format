2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into wedges
3 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon chili powder

 Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Toss sweet potatoes with oil and spices. Place sweet potato wedges in a single layer on a baking sheet. Roast until potatoes are tender, about 30 minutes, turning wedges every 10 minutes. Serve immediately.
