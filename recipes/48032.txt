2 tablespoons unsalted butter
1/2 large onion, minced
1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
1/4 teaspoon chili powder
1 cup long grain rice
1 1/2 cups chicken broth
1 can (2.25 ounce) sliced black olives, drained
1/4 cup chopped roasted red peppers
1/4 cup chopped fresh Italian parsley

  Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and saute until translucent, about 5 minutes.Stir in cumin and chili powder. Add rice and stir to coat. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil. Cover and reduce heat to a simmer. Cook about 15 minutes until liquid is absorbed and rice is tender. Remove from heat. Stir in olives, red pepper and parsley. Recover and let stand until all ingredients are heated through, about 5 minutes.
