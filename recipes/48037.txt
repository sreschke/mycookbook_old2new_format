1 tablespoon unsalted butter
1/2 onion, chopped
1 cup rice
1 3/4 cups chicken broth
 zest of 2 lemons
 juice of 1 lemon
2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Melt butter in large saucepan over medium heat. Stir in onion and saute until translucent. Stir in rice until coated with butter. Stir in chicken broth and zest from 1 lemon and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer until rice is tender, about 15 minutes. Stir in remaining lemon zest, juice and chopped parsley. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
