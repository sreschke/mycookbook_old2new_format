1 - 14 oz. pkg. carmels
1 - 18 oz. pkg. German Chocolate Cake Mix
3/4 cup melted butter
1 cup chopped nuts
1 - 6 oz. package (1 cup) chocolate chips
2/3 cup evaporated milk, separated

Preheat oven to 350.  Combine carmels and 1/3 cup milk in double boiler and melt.  Combine cake mix and 1/3 cup milk, butter and nuts in a large bowl.  Mix.  Press 1/2 mixture in 9 x 13 pan, greased and floured.  Bake 6 minutes at 350 degrees.  Remove from oven and sprinkle with chocolate chips.  spread carmel mixture over chips.  Crumble (dough is sticky) remaining dough over the carmel mixture.  Return to oven.  Bake 15 - 18 minutes at 350 degrees.  Cool.  Makes 2-1/2 dozen.
