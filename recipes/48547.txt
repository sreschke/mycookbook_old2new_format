Ingredients for 4 Servings 

2/3 lb Elbow Macaroni, Rotini, or other medium pasta shape 
1 1/3 tbsp Margarine 
4 tbsp All-purpose flour 
1 1/3 cups Milk 
1 cups Grated Cheddar cheese 
1/5 tsp Cayenne pepper 
2/3 tsp Salt 
1/5 cup Grated Parmesan cheese 

Procedure: 

1. Prepare pasta according to package directions; drain and rinse under cold water and set aside. 
2. Preheat oven to 375Ã¸. 
3. In a medium saucepan, melt margarine over low heat. 
4. Stir in flour with a whisk and cook, stirring, for 1 minute. 
5. Gradually whisk in the milk and bring the sauce to a boil, stirring constantly. 
6. Remove from heat and add Cheddar cheese, cayenne pepper and salt. 
7. Stir until the cheese has melted. 
8. In a large mixing bowl, stir the cheese sauce and pasta together. 
9. Spoon into a 1 1/2-quart oven proof casserole dish. 
10. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top. 
11. Bake until browned on top and hot all the way through, about 30-35 minutes.
