6 lg. or 10 med. potatoes, peeled & grated
3 eggs, beaten
1/4 lb. bacon, fried & dried and crumbled to add to 
        bacon drippings (1/2 to 2/3 c), otherwise 
        1/2 lb bacon
1 med. onion (grated)
1 c. evaporated milk
1/4 c. cream of wheat
1/2 to 1 tsp. salt

Preheat oven to 350.

Mix all incredients large mixing bowl.  Pour in to 9x13 baking pan.  Bake 1 to 1-1/2 hrs at 350.

Serve with applesauce or sour cream
