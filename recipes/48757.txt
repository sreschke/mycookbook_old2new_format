1 C peanut butter
1 C sugar
1 egg
1 t baking soda
   NO, I did NOT forget the flour.  There is none!

Beat egg lightly.  In another bowl beat peanut butter & sugar.  Beat in egg & baking soda.

Roll level teaspoons full into balls.  Place on cookie sheet & flatten with fork tines.  Bake 10 minutes until puffed and pale golden.  Cool on cookie sheet for 2 minutes, then cool on rack.
