2 cups chocolate chips (12 oz.)
1 can sweetened condensed milk
2 Tbsp. butter or margarine
3 Cup salted dry roasted peanuts
1 pkg. (10-1/2 oz.) miniature marshmallows

In saucepan, combine chocolate chips, milk and butter. Cook and stir on medium heat until chips are melted and smooth. Remove from heat, stir in peanuts and marshmallows. Spread into greased 13 x 9" pan. Refrigerate until firm. Cut into squares. 
Yield: 3-1/4 lb.
