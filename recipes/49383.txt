4 1lb lobsters
4 tbs butter
8oz white mushrooms, sliced
1 shallot, peeled and minced
salt and fresh ground white pepper
3 egg yokes
1 cup cream
2 tbs fino sherry
1 tbs minced fresh parsley
1 tsp fresh minced thyme leaves
1 box Uncle Ben's Long Grain Wild Rice
 
1. Cook lobsters in a large pot of boiling water over high heat for 15 minutes. Drain. When cool, remove meat from shells. Cut meat into chunks and set aside. Prepare rice according to directions.

2. Melt butter in a a heavy saucepan over medium low heat. Add mushrooms and shallots, season to taste with salt and pepper, and cook until soft, about 10 minutes. Set saucepan over a large pot of simmering water over medium heat.
3. Lightly whisk egg yolks in a mixing bowl, then whisk in cream. Add sherry, parsley, thyme, and lobster, then add mushrooms, stirring constantly with a wooden spoon until thick, 10-15 minutes. Adjust seasonings and serve over rice.
