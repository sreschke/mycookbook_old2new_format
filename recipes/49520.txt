10 ounces large elbow macaroni, cooked
1 (8 oz) package shredded cheddar cheese
1 (8 oz) package shredded mozzarella cheese
1 (10 3/4 oz) can cream of mushroom soup, undiluted
1/2 cup mayonnaise
3/4 cup milk
1/2 cup seasoned bread crumbs

STIR together all ingredients in a lightly greased 3 quart baking dish.
BAKE at 375 degrees for 35 minutes, sprinkle Additional 1/2 cup of seasoned bread crumbs on the top and place under broiler until lightly browned.  
YIELD:  approximately 6 to 8 servings
