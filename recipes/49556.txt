1/2 cup fat-free Italian dressing 
1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley (or 1 teaspoon dried parsley) 
1 teaspoon dried whole basil 
2 medium yellow squash, cut in 1-inch slices 
8 small boiling onions 
8 cherry tomatoes 
8 medium fresh mushrooms 

Mix dressing, parsley, and basil in a small bowl; refrigerate.  

Place vegetables in an alternating sequence on eight skewers. Apply nonstick cooking spray to grill rack. Position kabobs on rack; grill over medium heat until vegetables are tender, about 15 minutes. Baste with dressing mixture and turn frequently.
