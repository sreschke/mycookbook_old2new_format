One recipe pizza dough 
1 tbls oil 
1 tbls butter 
2 lg sweet onions,sliced thin 
Salt and pepper
2 tsp oregano 
1 tbls chopped parsley 
3/4 cup grated mozzarella  
2 tbls Parmesan  
 
Heat the butter and oil in a large skillet over medium-high. Add onion slices and turn heat to low. Saute til softened, add the salt and pepper.  Continue to saute slowly, stirring occasionally, until brown and caramelized, about 20 minutes.  Remove from heat and cool. 

Preheat oven to 450F. Lightly grease a 14-inch pizza pan with oil. Sprinkle with cornmeal. Press dough into pan, shaping as you press til you like it. Top crust with the cooled onions. Sprinkle with oregano, parsley, extra pepper and cheeses. Drizzle with just a little olive oil. Bake for 10-15 mins or til dough is done and toppings are hot. 

This is good with anchovies added, if you like them.
