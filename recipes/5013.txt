3/4 lb. thinly sliced beef ot top round steak
1 Tbsp. oil
1-1/2 cups sliced mushrooms
1 pkg. (7/8 oz.) mushroom sauce or gravy mix
milk
1 can (14-1/2 oz.) green beans, drained
1/2 cup sour cream
noodles (optional)


In large skillet, brown meat in hot oil.  Add mushroom; cook until tender.  Prepare sauce according to package directions, using milk.  Stir into skillet; cook until thickened; about 5 minutes.  Add beans; heat through.  Just before serving, stir in sour cream.  Serve over noodles, if desired.

4-6 servings
