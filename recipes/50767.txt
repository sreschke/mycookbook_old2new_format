4 - 6 chicken pieces
1/4 c. olive oil
1/4 c. butter
1 onion, diced
1 #2 1/2 can tomatoes
1 lb. button mushrooms
salt and pepper
1/2 t. oregano
1/2 c. dry white wine
black olives

Wash and dry chicken.  Brown chicken in oil and butter in heavy frying pan.  Season with salt and pepper.  Remove from the pan.

Saute onion in the same frying pan (with oil/butter still in it).  Add tomatoes and mash into smaller pieces.  Simmer for 5 minutes.

Wipe mushrooms clean with damp cloth and quarter or thickly slice.  Add to sauce with oregano and salt/pepper to taste.

Add browned chicken to sauce, cover pan and simmer until chicken is tender, 25 - 30 minutes.  Turn chicken at least once.

Add wine and olives and simmer additional 5 minutes.  Serve chicken with sauce over pasta.
*May be kept warm for awhile before serving, it improves the flavor.

Serves 4
