1/2 lb. (8-oz) macaroni
1 T.  butter
1 T. flour
1/2 t. salt
1/8 t. freshly ground black pepper
1 c. milk
1 - 2 c. (1/4 - 1/2 lb.) shredded cheddar cheese

Cook macaroni in boiling salted water.  When tender, drain well.  Melt butter, stir in flour and cook until it bubbles.  Add salt and pepper; then slowly stir in milk and cook until smooth.  Add 3/4 of the cheese; stir until partially melted.  Place macaroni in layers with the cheese sauce; sprinkle remaining cheese and dots of butter over the top.  Bake uncovered at 400 for 20-25 minutes or until top is golden and bubbly.

Makes 4 servings

VARIATIONS:
Add slices of tomatoes between the layers.  Use swiss, cheddar or munster type cheeses.

For a spicier version, add 1/2 can drained Rotel instead of sliced tomatoes between layers.
