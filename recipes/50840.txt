3 T. butter
3 T. flour
1 c. milk
1/2 t. salt
pinch pepper
1 to 1 1/2 c. shredded cheese (swiss, mild or sharp cheddar)
1/2 t. cornstarch
4 eggs, separated

First make a cream sauce:  Melt butter, stir in flour and cook until mixture bubbles.  Slowly stir in milk, add seasonings, cook until smooth and thickened.  Toss cheese with cornstarch and add to cream sauce, stirring until smooth. (Can be done in advance.)

An hour before the souffle is to be served, beat egg yolks until thick and stir into cooled sauce.  Beat egg whites until stiff, fold in gently.  Preheat oven to 350.  Pour mixture into buttered 1-1/2 quart souffle dish or straight sided casserole dish.  Bake 30 to 35 minutes until souffle is high, puffed and golden.  Serve immediately.

Serves 4
