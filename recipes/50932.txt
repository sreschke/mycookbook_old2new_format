Dough:
1/4 lb. butter
3 oz.  pkg. cream cheese
1 c. flour

Set out butter and cheese for 1 hour before preparing.  Mix all three ingredients, forming a pastry dough; shape into walnut sized balls and press into mini-sized muffin tins.

Filling:
6 oz. cream cheese, softened
1/2 c. sugar
1 egg
1/2 c. crushed pineapple, drained
1 t. vanilla

Cream sugar and cheese; add egg; fold in pineapple; add vanilla.  Mix well.  Pour filling into pastry shells (3/4 full).  Bake at 400 for 20 minutes.

Note:  1-8 oz. pkg. cream cheese can be used for entire recipe.

Makes 24
