4 c. cooked chicken, cut into small pieces
3/4 c. mayo
3/4 c. cream of chicken soup
2 c. chopped celery
4 hard boiled eggs, diced
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. minced nion
2 tbsp. lemon juice
1 c. crushed potato chips
2/3 c. cheese

Mix first 8 ingredients; put in shallow baking dish. Combine potato chips with cheese and sprinkle on top. Chill several hours or overnight. Bake at 400 for 25 minutes. Serves 8.
