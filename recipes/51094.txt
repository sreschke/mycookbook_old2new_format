1 3 oz pkg. instant vanilla pudding
4 snicker bars cut into pieces
1 c milk
4 or 5 apples (diced and peeled)
1 8 oz cool whip

Mix pudding and milk until thick. Add snicker pieces and apples to pudding mixure. Fold in cool whip, using only about 3/4 of the container. Chill until set. Freeze snikcer bars, making them easier to cut into pieces.
