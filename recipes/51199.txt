1 c  Ketchup
2 tb Brown sugar
1 ts Dry mustard
1/2 ts Salt
1 sm Onion; chopped
3 c  Fresh corn
3 sl Bacon; diced
                   
Preheat oven to 350F. Grease a 1 1/2 quart casserole.
                    
Mix ketchup, brown sugar, dry mustard and salt;  stir in remaining ingredients, except bacon, til well blended. Top with bacon; transfer to casserole and bake until bacon is cooked and heated through, about 40 mintues.
                    
Makes 6 to 8 servings.
