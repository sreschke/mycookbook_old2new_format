12 ounces thinly sliced cooked potatoes 
4 ounces low-fat shredded cheddar cheese 
1 cup low-fat cottage cheese 
2 eggs 
2 tablespoons dried minced onion 
1 teaspoon dried parsley 
Salt and pepper to taste 

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a 1-1/2 quart baking dish with nonstick cooking spray. Place half of potatoes in baking dish; sprinkle with half of the cheddar cheese. Top with remaining potatoes 
and remaining cheese. 

In a blender, combine remaining ingredients; blend until smooth. Pour mixture over potatoes. Bake uncovered 30 minutes, until lightly browned.
