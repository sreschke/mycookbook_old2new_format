3/4 cup shedded sharp chedder cheese
1/2 cup shedded swiss cheese
1/4 cup Parmeasan cheese
3 Large green onions chopped

3 large eggs separated 
Preheat oven to 375
Add milk, mustard,cheese,green onions,chopped onions,salt , pepper, and egg yolks. Mix together until well blended.
In a smal bowl whip eggs whites until peaks form.Gently fod egg whites into mixture Spoon into a greased souffle' dish
