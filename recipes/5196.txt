1 c. celery
1 c. green onions
2 pkgs. cornbread (cooked accord. to dir.)
1 pkg. pepp. farm herb stuffing mix
3 eggs
3 cans chicken broth
1/2 t. pepper
1 t. salt
1 1/2 t. poultry seasoning
1 1/2 t. sage
2 T. parsley flakes
1 sm. can mushrooms
1 stick butter

saute mushrooms, green onions, and celery in butter.  put 3 eggs in with bread and seasonings.  add 2 cans chicken broth and add vegetables.  bake at 350 for 35 min adding broth on top
