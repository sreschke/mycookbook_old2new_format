4 C water, divided
1/4 C chopped fresh lavender leaves
2/3 C sugar
1 C fresh lemon juice (about 6 lemons)
Lavender stems

Bring 1 C water to a boil in a medium saucepan.  Combine the boiling water and lavender in a medium bowl; cover and steep 30 minutes.  Strain the lavender mixture through a fine sieve into a bowl; discard lavender leaves.

Combine 3 C water and sugar in saucepan.  Bring the mixture to a boil, and cook 1 min or until sugar is dissolved.  Combine lavender water, sugar syrup, and lemon juice in a pitcher.  Cover and chill.  Serve over ice.  Garmish with lavender stems, if desired.
