This recipe has been a favorite of Mary Ellen Fitzgerald, a mother of eight children. She says it lasts several days in the refrigerator.

1 pound lump or backfin crabmeat
1 pint sour cream
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
3 tablespoons Old Bay seasoning
4 tablespoons mayonnaise
2 pounds cream cheese
1 pound package shredded Cheddar cheese

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.  Combine all ingredients in bowl of electric mixer and beat on low speed until blended.  Scrape into a 2-1/2 quart round casserole.  Place in oven and bake for 20 to 25 minutes. Leave out, stirring occasionally.  Serve with crackers or French bread. Can be reheated. Serves up to 30.
