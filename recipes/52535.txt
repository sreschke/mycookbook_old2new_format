3 1/2 lbs sweet potatoes
1 cup brown sugar
juice and zest of one orange
1/3 cup bourbon
1/4 cup butter 
1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
marshmallows 

Boil sweet potatoes til tender.  Peel and mash. Add brown sugar, orange juice and zest, bourbon, butter, and pumpkin pie spice. Mix well. Spread in shallow casserole. Bake 30 minutes at 350F. Top with marshmallows and broil briefly.  
8 servings
