1 1/2 lbs. boneless round steak (cut in 1-inch cubes)
5 medium potatoes (cut in eighths)
2 c. carrots (1-inch chunks)
1 c. very coarsely chopped onions
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1 c. sliced celery (1/2-inch)
2 (14 1/2 oz. ea.) cans stewed tomatoes
2 T. quick cooking tapioca
1 T. sugar
1/8 tsp. pepper

Combine all ingredients in large bowl; mix gently, but well.  Turn into 3-qt. casserole.  Cover with lid.  Bake in 275 degree oven 5 hours.  Let stand 5 minutes before serving.  Makes 6 servings.
