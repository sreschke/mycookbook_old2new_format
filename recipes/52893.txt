12 slices day-old bread, crusts removed
1 1/2 c. shredded Cheddar cheese
4 eggs
2 1/2 c. milk
1 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. crushed dried basil
1 tsp. prepared mustard
2 tbs. minced onion
2 tbs. chopped pimiento
Dash pepper
6 thick slices tomato
Salt and pepper

Arrange 6 bread slices in buttered 12 x 7 1/2 x 2 in. baking dish.  Cover with cheese, then with remaining slices of bread.  Beat eggs.  Blend in milk, salt, basil, mustard, onion, pimiento and pepper.  Pour over bread.  Top each sandwich with a tomato slice.  Season with salt and pepper.  Bake at 325 degrees for 50 minutes or until puffy and brown.  Serve immediately.  6 servings.
