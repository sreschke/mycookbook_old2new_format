1 pkg. cake mix
2 1/2 cups quick-cooking oatmeal
3/4 cup margarine, melted
1 (12oz) jar raspberry jam or apricot
1 T. Water.
     Combine cake mix and oat meal.  Stir in melted margarine until mixture 
is crumbly.  Put about 3 cups of mixtgure in a 9" x 13" pan and   press
firmly.  Combine jam and water and spoon over crumb mixture.  Spread 
evenly.  Cover with remaining crumb mixture; make top even by patting
firmly.    Bake at 375 degrees for 20 minutes.  Cool before cutting into Bars.
Time: 20 minutes      Temperature 375 Degrees.   Serves 10 to 12
