2 PKGS FROZEN CHOPPED BROCCOLI
2 C CHICKEN-COOKED & BONED
2 CANS CREAM OF CHICKEN SOUP
1/2 C REAL MAYO
1/2 CHICKEN STOCK
1/4 TEA TABASCO SAUCE
2 C SHREDDED CHEDDAR CHEESE
1 1/2 C BREAD CRUMBS
6 TBS MELTED BUTTER

Cook chicken & cook broccoli & mix next 5 ingredients well. Top w/cheese then buttered crumbs.

Bake 350 for 35-45 mins.  Sprinkle w/Parmesan cheese.
