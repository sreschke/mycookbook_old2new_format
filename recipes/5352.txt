1 PKG GERMAN CHOCOLATE CAKE MIX
1 PKG KRAFT CARAMELS
1/2 C EVAPORATED MILK
6 TBS BUTTER
1 C CHOPPED PECANS
1 C CHOCOLATE CHIPS OR PEANUT BUTTER CHIPS

Mix cake mix as directed & pour 1/2 batter in 9x13.
Bake 18 mins at 350.

Melt caramels, milk & butter.  Remove cake & mix in nuts & pour over hot cake.  Sprinkle on chocolate chips & pour on remaining cake batter.
Continue baking till cake is done.
