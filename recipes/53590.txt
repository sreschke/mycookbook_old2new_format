Winter Bean Stew with Barley- slow cooker
serves 4
ÿ
1 cup dried baby lima beans, rinsed
2/3 cup dried navy beans, rinsed
1 pound potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 inch chunks
2 carrots, halved lengthwise and cut into 1 inch lengths
1 large onion, diced coarsely
1 parsnip, peeled and cut in half lengthwise and cut into 1 inch lengths
1/2 pound rutabagas, peeled and cut into 1 inch chunks
6 cloves of garlic, slivered
2 tablespoons pearl barley
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 1/2 teaspoons paprika
1 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup fresh chopped parsley
ÿ
In a medium pot, combine the beans with enough water to cover them by 2 inches. Bring to boil and boil for 2 minutes, cover and let stand for 1 hour. Drain well.
In a 4 quart slow cooker, combine the beans and all remaining ingredients up to the pepper, with 4 cups of water. Cover and cook on Low 6 to 8 hours or until beans and vegetables are tender. Stir in the salt and parsley and serve.
ÿ
You can soak the beans overnight in a bowl with water to cover by 2 inches in the refrigerator. Saves time.
