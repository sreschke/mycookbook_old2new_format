2 lbs. large uncooked shrimp
1 stick butter
1/2 cup olive oil
2 bay leaves crumbled
4 Tbsps. lemon juice
2 Tbsps. Worcestershire sauce
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 Tbsp. parsley, chopped
2 tsp. paprika
2 tsp. oregano
2 tsp. cayenne pepper
1 tsp. hot sauce
2 tsp. black pepper
1/2 cup chile sauce
2 lemons sliced thin.

Wash the shrimp and spread out in shallow baking dish.

Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and simmer over low heat 10 minutes.

Pour the mixture over the shrimp and mix thoroughly.

Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, stirring occasionally.

Preheat oven to 325F and bake shrimp for 30 minutes.
