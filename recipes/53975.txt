2 (3/4 lb) pork tenderloins
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/2 tsp. ground ginger
5 cloves garlic, halved
2 Tbsps. brown sugar
3 Tbsps. honey
2 tsps. dark sesame oil
Vegetable cooking spray.

Combine soy sauce, ginger and garlic in a zip top plastic bag. Add pork. Refrigerate at least 3 hours.

Remove tenderloins from marinade. 

Cmbine brown sugar, honey and oil. Cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until sugar dissolves.

Place tenderloins on grill and brush with honey mixture.

Basting mixture becomes very thick when cool.
