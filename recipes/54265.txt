2 lbs. hamburger
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can nacho cheese soup
1 can Rotel tomatoes-do not drain
1 Bag Doritos, crushed divided (spicy kind is best)
1 bag Mexican shredded cheese

Brown and drain meat. Mix all soups and Rotel, then add meat. In 9x13 inch dish, crush Doritos and cover bottom of dish. Pour hamburger mixture on top. 
Bake at 350* for 15 minutes. Take out and add cheese and remaining Doritos. Put in oven 5-10 minutes til cheese melts.
