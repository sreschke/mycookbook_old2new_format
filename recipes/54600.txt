FILLING
ready made 10 inch pie crust
1 c sugar
1/3 c all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1 tsp cinnamon
dash of salt
8 c thinly sliced pared tart apples

*CRUMB TOPPING
1 c all-purpose flour
1/2 c butter or margarine, softened (firm)
1/2 c packed brown sugar

*Mix all ingredients together until crumbly

Heat oven to 425, prepare pastry.  Stir together sugar, flour, nutmeg, cinnamon, and salt.  Mix with apples. Turn into pie shell and sprinkle on crumb topping.  Bake 50 min.  Cover topping with aluminum foil last 10 min of baking if topping browns too quickly.  Best served warm.
