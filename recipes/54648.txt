INGREDIENTS:
1/4 tsp. salt & pepper
1/4 tsp. paprika
1/4 tsp. garlic powder
1/4 tsp. cinnamon
1 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. apple sauce
1/4 c. ketchup
3 Tbsp. lemon juice
3 lbs. pork ribs

METHOD:
1.  Mix all ingredients together on stove.
2.  Marinate ribs in sauce the night before.
3.  Bake at 350F for 1 1/2 hrs.  (basting regularly).
