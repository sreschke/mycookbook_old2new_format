125g white chocolate melts
4 eggs, separated
4 tsp Tia maria
1 cup cream, whipped
Melt chocolate.  Remove from heat and cool slightly.  Whisk in egg yolks and Tia Maria.  Beat until smooth and thick.
Fold cream into chocolate mixture.  Beat egg whites until solft peaks form.  Fold gently into chocolate mixture.  Spoon into serving glasses.  Refrigerate until firm.
Serves 4.
