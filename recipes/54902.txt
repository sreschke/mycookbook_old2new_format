REAL ITALIAN MEATBALLS

2 lb. lean ground beef
1 lb. Italian Sausage
3 slices of bread  broken in small pieces(old)soaked with milk just to moisten
2 eggs
1/2C grated Italian cheese
4-6 cloves of garlic minced
1 small onion minced
3T parsley
salt 
pepper
3T Olive oil


Mix everything together but don't over mix because the meatballs will be tough.  Form into balls. On cookie sheet with lip on it put Olive Oil and coat the pan put meatballs on top turn them to coat with the oil Bake for about 45 min at 400 degrees till they are brown
Add to sauce but don't leave them in there for more than 15 min because they will absorb the sauce.
