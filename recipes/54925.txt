1 cup all purpose flour
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 tea. salt
1/2 cup margarine
FILLING
2 cans (16 oz. each) sliced peaches
1 cup sugar
1/4 cup cornstarch
TOPPING
1-1/2 cups oatmeal
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup all purpose flour
5 Tbs. margarine

In a bowl combine flour,sugar and salt. Cut in margarine until crumbly.  Press into a grease 9" square pan.  BAKE 350 for 15 min.

Meanwhile:  Drain peaches and reserve juice in a saucepan.  Add sugar and cornstarch.  Bring to a boil, stirring constantly.  Boil for 2 min. or until thickened.  Remove from heat, stir in peaches.  Pour into crust.

Combine oats, sugar and flour in a bowl.  Cut in margarine.  Sprinkle over filling.  
BAKE 350   25-30 min. or until golden brown
