
Classic Green Bean Casserole

Prep Time:5 mins.
Skill: No Experience Required
Ready In: 40 mins.
Serves: 8

Ingredients:
------------
3 pkg. (9 oz. each) frozen French cut green beans , thawed, drained
1 can (10-3/4 oz.) condensed cream of mushroom soup
1 cup CHEEZ WHIZ Cheese Dip *
1/8 tsp. ground black pepper
1 can (2.8 oz.) French fried onions , divided

Preparation:
------------
MIX all ingredients except 1/2 can onions in 1-1/2-quart casserole.

BAKE at 350 degrees F for 30 minutes. Top with remaining 1/2 can
onions. Bake 5 
     minutes.  Garnish with slivered red onion, if desired.

To Make Ahead:  Prepare as directed except for baking; cover.
Refrigerate 
 overnight.  When ready to serve, bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F
for 45 to 50 
 minutes or until thoroughly heated, topping with remaining onions
during last 5 
 minutes of cooking time.

Ingredient Note:
-----------------
*Or use CHEEZ WHIZ LIGHT Cheese Dip.
