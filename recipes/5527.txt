1/4 c butter or margarine
2 tsp seasoned salt
5 tbs Worcestershire Sauce
1 box of Chex or Crispix
1 c peanuts and/or cashews
1 c pretzels (small twists or sticks)


Melt butter in open roasting pan in pre-heated 250 degree oven.  Stir in seasonings.  Gradually add cereals, nuts and pretzels and coat evenly.  Bake 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes.  Spread on absorbent paper towels to cool.  Store in air-tight container.
