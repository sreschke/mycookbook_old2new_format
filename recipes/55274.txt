
Cherry Bars

Base
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup melted butter
1 cup flour

Mix together and pat into an 8" square pan.  Bake at 350 degrees F.
for 10 minutes.

Topping
1 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs beaten
1 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
2 Tbs. flour
1 tsp. baking powder

Beat eggs with brown sugar, add vanilla and mix well.  Add flour salt
and baking powder and mix well.  To this mixture add the following
mixing well,

1 cup shredded coconut
1/2 cup candied cherries chopped
1 cup chopped walnuts

Pour this onto the top of cooled base and bake for 25 minutes at 350
degrees F.
