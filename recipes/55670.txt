1 pkg. yellow cake mix
1/2 cup water
2 or 3 bananas, mashed
2 eggs
1 tsp. baking powder
1 cup nuts, chopped

Beat first 5 ingredients together 'til smooth. Mix in nuts. Pour into 9 x 13 pan or two 8 in. round pans. Bake at 350 degrees 35-40 minutes. Cool. Frost with homemade cream cheese frosting.

When we were kids, our neighbor across the street,  (Phyllis Lombardi), used to make this. I never forgot it!
