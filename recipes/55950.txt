2 qts chicken broth
1/2 C uncooked pearl barley, rinsed and drained
2 carrots, cut into 1/4 inch dice
1 T chopped fresh sage leaves or 12 tsp dried (opt
1 T unsalted butter
1 T olive oil
1 onion, chopped
10 oz white mushrooms, cleaned, trimmed, and quartered
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 C chopped fresh parsley
Combine the broth, barley, carrots and sage in a pot
Simmer 40 minutes
Meanwhile heat butter and oil in a skillet over med-low heat
Add chopped onion; cook 10 minutes
Add mushrooms and cook 5 minutes
Add garlic and cook 5 minutes more
Stir mushroom mixture into the broth mix
Season with salt and pepper
Simmer partially covered for 20 minutes
Stir in parsley and serve
Serves 4
229 calories, 7g fat, 7g protein, 31g carbs
