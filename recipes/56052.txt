

Classic Peanut Butter Cookies

1 c Unsalted butter
1 c Crunchy peanut butter 
1 c Granulated sugar 
1 c Light brown sugar, firmly -packed
2 Eggs 
2 1/2 c All-purpose flour 
1 ts Baking powder 
1 1/2 ts Baking soda
1/2 ts Salt
3/4 c salted peanuts
Cream together butter, peanut butter and sugars. Beat in eggs. In a
separate bowl, sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and
salt. Stir into batter.Add peanuts and stir into batter. Put batter in refrigerator for 1 hour. Roll
into 1" balls and put on baking sheets. Flatten each ball with a fork,
making a criss-cross pattern. Bake in a preheated 375 deg F oven for
about 10 minutes or until cookies begin to brown. Do not over-bake.
Truly enjoyable!
