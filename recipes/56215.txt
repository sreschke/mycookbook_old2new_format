!lb Ground Pork 
1 small onion 
1 clove garlic
2 peaches ,sliced
1medium pear pealed and cored 
8 oz tomatoe sauce
1/4 cup raisins
3/4 tsp salt 
1/4 tsp ground cinnamon 
6 large  poblano chiles
Nut sauce 
1/4 cup each walnuts and blanched almonds 
1cup sour cream 
1/4 cup milk 
Cinnamon ,salt ,pepper to taste 
In a large skillet ,cook pork ,onions, garlic until meat browned and onions tender.Drain Chop peaches and pears and add to meat along with tomatoe sauce , salt, and cinnamon. Simmer uncovered for 10 min . Cut tops of chiles , remove seeds, veins . Cook in boiling water for 10b min ,Drain salt inside of chiles and fill each one with mixture 
Place filled chiles in shallow baking pan and bake @ 350 for 20 minutes or until heated thru . While peppers are cooking gring walnuts and almonds in a blender . In a bowl combine sour cream and milk .Stir in nut mixture and add cinnomon ,salt ,pepper to taste After done top with nut sauce
