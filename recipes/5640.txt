2 LBS STEW MEAT
1 C WATER
2 C TOMATOE SAUCE
1 1/2 TEA SALT
1/2 TEA PEPPER
7 LG POTATOES-CUBED
6 CARROTS-CHUNKS
1 ONION
1 SLICE WHITE BREAD-PIECES
1 C TOMOTOE WEDGES

Combine all and put in roaster.  Cover and bake at 
350 for 5 hours.  Stir a few times during baking.

I have also done this in a crock-pot.

From Joyce Trumbull.
