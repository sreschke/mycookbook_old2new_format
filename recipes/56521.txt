baked pie crust
filling
1 cup maple syrup
l cup heavy cream
2 tbsp butter
warm on stove slowly
two slightly beaten egg yolks
1 tsp vanilla
2 tbsp cornstarch
mix with 1/4 c water
add to first ingredients
heat until thick stirring constantly
pour into pie shell

Meringue

3 egg whites  1/2 tsp vanilla  1/4 tsp cream of tarter, combine three ingredients
6 tsp sugar one at a time
cover pie 350 degrees for 10 to 15 minutes
