1 fresh brisket (4-7 pounds)
1 package dry onion soup
1 medium onion, sliced into rings
2 medium stalks celery chopped
1 cup bottled chili sauce, 1/2 cup water
1 can beer

Brown brisket, drain any grease, sprinkle soup over top, arrange onions and celery on top.  Combine chilli sauce and water and pour around meat.  Bake covered.  Baste occasionally with liquid.  About 45 minutes before done, pour beer over top.  Continued baking until done.

Refrigerate overnite.  Remove all grease and slice meat across grain.  Heat about 30-45 minutes per pound in a 350 degree oven.
