Of all the Moroccan cookies and pastries, this is probably the easiest - and a favorite among my children.
 
Makes 30 servings
 
1 cup oil 
1 cup sugar 
3 1/2 cups flour (all-purpose) , to 4 cups 
1/2 teaspoon orange blossom water 
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda 
drop cinnamon 
 
Mix oil and sugar, stirring, in a large bowl. Add flour, cup by cup, and mix by hand. The consistency should be such that small cookies shaped like space capsules can be formed and hold together. 

Form the cookies, flatten just a little, and dip the tip into cinnamon, or sprinkle the cinnamon on the top. Place 1 inch apart on a cookie sheet. 

Bake at 375 degrees F. for 20 to 30 minutes. Ribats should brown on the bottom and remain whitish on top.
