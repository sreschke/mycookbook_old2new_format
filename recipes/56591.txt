3 cups granulated or brown sugar
1/2 cup milk
2 tbs margarine
3 tbs marshmallow fluff
3 tbs peanut butter
1 tsp vanilla

Bring sugar, milk and margarine to a boil.

Let boil for 3 minutes.

Take off stove; add marshmallow fluff, peanut butter and vanilla.

Beat for a few minutes.

Pour into greased pan.

Let set, cut into squares when cool.
