Gingersnaps

Sift together:
2c sifted flour
1T ginger
2t baking soda
1t cinnamon
1/2 t salt

Cream and add dry ing:
3/4 c softened butter
1 c sugar
1 egg
1/4 cup molasses

To make gingerbread men:
Chill.  Roll chilled dough thin, using sugar instead of flour to keep from sticking.  Cut out with small gingerbread cookie cutter.  Bake only a few minutes at 350, watching carefully to prevent burning.

For crispy cookies,
Med size:  approx. 11 min.
Small: approx. 7 min.

Bake less time for chewy cookie.
