Ingredients for 4 Servings

1/2 pkg (18 ounces) NESTL�? TOLL HOUSE? Refrigerated Chocolate Chip & White
Fudge Cookie Dough, softened
4/5 cups Milk
1/2 pkg (3 1/8 ounces) banana cream or vanilla instant pudding and pie filling
mix
1 med Bananas, sliced
1/2 cup Frozen non-dairy whipped topping, thawed
Chocolate Flavor NESTL�? NESQUIK? Chocolate Syrup (optional)
NESTL�? TOLL HOUSE? Semi Sweet Chocolate Mini Morsels (optional)

Procedure: 

1. PREHEAT oven to 350ø F.
2. Grease 9-inch pie plate.
3. PRESS 3/4 package cookie dough* onto bottom and up side of pie plate.
4. BAKE for 18 to 24 minutes or until golden brown; flatten down with back of
spoon to form pie shell.
5. Cool completely on wire rack.
6. BEAT milk and pudding mix according to package directions in small bowl;
refrigerate for 5 minutes.
7. Spread 1 cup pudding over cookie crust (save remaining pudding for later
use).
8. Top pudding with bananas; spread with whipped topping.
9. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour or until set.
10. Drizzle with NesQuik; top with morsels.
11. Cut into wedges.
12. * Bake up extra for an additional treat the kids (and grownups) will love!
