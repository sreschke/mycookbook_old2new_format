CRUST:
1 1/2 C graham cracker crumbs
1/2 C toasted almonds, finely chopped
1/2 C chopped Skor toffee bits
2 T packed brown sugar
1/4 t salt
6 T unsalted butter, melted
FILLING:
4 8-oz. pkgs cream cheese, room temp
1 C packed brown sugar
4 large eggs
1 T vanilla
1/4 t almond extract
8 oz. Skor toffee bars cut into 1/2 inch pieces
TOPPING:
1 16 oz. sour cream
1/2 C sugar
1 t vanilla
Assorted candies such as red and green gumdrops and holiday M&Ms with red/green sugar and sprinkles

Preheat 350.  Mix first 5 ing.  Add butter, stir until moist clumps form.  Press mixture over bottom and 1 inch up sides of 10" springform pan.  Bake crust until just set about 5 min.  Set aside.  Reduce oven to 325.
Beat cream cheese and sugar until blended.  Beat in eggs 1 at a time.  Beat in both extracts.  Pour half of mixture into prepared crust; sprinkle with toffee pieces.  Pour remaining mixture over.  Bake until edges are puffed but center is barely set, about 55 minutes.
Mix sour cream, sugar and vanilla until smooth.  Pour topping over hot cheesecake.  Bake cake until topping is just set, about 5 minutes.  Transfer to rack; cool 10 minutes.  Run knife between cake and pan sides.  Chill cake uncovered overnight.  
Remove pan sides and place on platter.  Garnish top with candies.
