12-14 stuffing mushrooms
1 tbsp butter
1 clove garlic, minced
4 green onions, chopped
1 can flaked crab meat (6 oz drained)
1 can water chestnuts (5 oz chopped up)
1/2 cup mayonaise
2 tbsp chopped parsley
6 slices of bread made into crumbs OR about 2 cups dry bread crumbs

Melt butter in frying pan and saute the garlic and onions.  Add the crab meat and bread crumbs.  Remove from heat and add water chestnuts, mayo, and salt and pepper to taste.  
Quickly wash and pat dry the mushrooms.  Remove stems.  Fill the caps with bread crumb mixture and pat into a ball.
Put mushrooms on a baking tray and bake for 25 mins at 350 degrees Fahrenheit or until cap start to brown.
