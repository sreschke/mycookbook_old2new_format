
  1 bag frozen hash browns thawed
  1 onion chopped up
  1 stick margerine melted   add to above and mix well.
  1 can cream chicken soup
  16Oz sour cream
  mix well
  add 10 oz.  grated cheddar cheese   mix well and put in greased cake pan
  bake 350  for 50 to 55 min till little brown on top.    mmm good goes with any meat
