FOR SPINACH: Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F. Butter an 8x5-inch ceramic baking dish. Place spinach in heavy large pot. Cover and cook over medium heat until spinach wilts, stirring once or twice, about 5 minutes. Cool slightly. Squeeze out any excess moisture. If using frozen spinach, simply squeeze out any excess liquid.

Pour in cream; bring mixture to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until mixture is lightly thickened. Remove from heat and immediately stir in cheese. When melted, stir in pur‚ed spinach and chopped spinach. Add chopped jalape¤o. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Spoon into prepared dish.

FOR TOPPING: Melt butter in small skillet over medium heat. Stir in breadcrumbs and saut‚ until golden, about 8 minutes. Scatter crumbs over spinach mixture. Bake until topping is brown and spinach mixture bubbles, about 20 minutes. Serve warm.
