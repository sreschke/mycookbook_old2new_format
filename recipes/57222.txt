1 lb. gound beef           1/4 C Parmesan cheese
1 clove garlic (crushed)   1/2 C chopped onion
1-4oz can mushrooms        1-16oz can tomato sauce
   (drained)               1 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp oregano leaves     1/2 tsp. basil leaves
1/4 tsp. salt              1/4 tsp pepper
2 C Bisquick               1/2 C milk
1 egg                      8 slices American cheese

Preheat oven to 400.  Cook and stir beef, onion and garlic until brown; drain.  Stir in sauce, mushrooms, sugar, oregano, basil, salt and pepper.  Heat to boiling; reduce heat, simmer 10 minutes.  Mix baking mix, milk and egg; spread half in a 8x8x2 greased pan.  Top with 4 cheese slices.  Spoon beef over cheese.  Top with remaining cheese slices; sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.  Drop remaining dough by spoonfuls onto cheese.  Sprinkle with paprika.  Bake 20 minutes.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
