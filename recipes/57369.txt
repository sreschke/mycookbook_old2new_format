Crispy Oven-Fried Chicken 
========================= 
2 1/2 to 3 pounds chicken pieces, skin removed -OR- 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves 
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
2 egg whites, slightly beaten
1 cup cornflake crumbs 
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Lightly spray cookie sheet with no-stick spray. To coat chicken, first roll pieces or breast halves in flour to evenly cover all sides. Then dip chicken in egg whites and roll in cornflake crumbs. Place coated chicken on prepared cookie sheet. Bake 45 to 55 minutes for chicken pieces -OR- 20 to 30 minutes for the boneless breast halves, or until chicken is tender and no longer pink. (Do not turn chicken over during baking.) Makes 6 servings. 
Calories per serving: 226, Fat 2g., 6% Calories from Fat, Cholesterol 68mg.
