Hot Spinach and Artichoke Dip 

1. Bring to room temperature: 

1- 8 oz. pkg. cream cheese 

2. Cream together: 

1/4 c. mayonnaise (not Miracle Whip)
1/4 c. parmesan cheese
1/4 c. romano cheese (or use all parmesan)
1 clove garlic, finely minced
1/2 t. basil
1/4 t. garlic salt and pepper, to taste 

3. Add: 

1 can (14 oz.) artichoke hearts, drained and coarsely chopped
1/2 c. chopped spinach, steamed 

4. Mix till well blended. 

5. Spray a pie pan with: 

nonstick cooking spray 

6. Pour mixture into prepared pan. 

7. Top with: 

1/4 c. grated Mozzarella cheese 

8. Bake at 350 degrees (F) for 25 minutes, or until top is browned. 

9. Serve with: 

toasted bread
