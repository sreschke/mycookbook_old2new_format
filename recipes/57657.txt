Ingredients:Eggplant,3/4 cup sugar,1/2 block margarine-chipped,1 egg,1tsp.baking powder,flour.
    Boil eggplant until tender,drain in collinder-press water out.Place in bowl and mash.Mix margarine,sugar,cooled eggplant,beat in 1 egg(not scrambled).Wisk flour(3/4 to 1 cup)depending on consistancy,add baking powder added on top of flour.Drop by tsp. in hot grease.Fry until golden to dark brown.
