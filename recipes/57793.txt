1 18 ounce yellow cake mix (large)
1 3 1/2 ounce package instant pudding (vanilla or lemon)
4 eggs
3/4 c. oil or applesauce
3/4 c. sherry or orange juice
1/2 - 1 t. nutmeg
powdered sugar

PREHEAT OVEN --350. 
Put all ingredients except powdered sugar in large bowl. Beat at medium speed about five minutes.

Pour into greased (and sugared) 10" bundt pan. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.

Cool about 10 minutes. Turn out on platter. Sprinkle with powdered sugar. A really smooth but simple pound-type cake.
