1 pkg (8 oz) cream cheese
1 C butter or margarine
1 1/2 C sugar
1 1/2 t. vanilla extract
4 eggs
2 1/4 C all purpose flour, divided
1 1/2 t. baking power
1/2 t. salt
2 C. fresh or frozen cranberries, patted dry
1/2 C. chopped peans or walnuts
Confectioners sugar

In a mixing bowl, beat cream cheese, butter, sugar and vanilla until smooth. 

Add aggs, one at time, mixing well after each addition.

Combine 2 C. flour, baking powder and salt; gradually add to butter mixture.  

Mix remaining flour with cranberries and nuts and fold into batter.  Batter will be very thick.

Spoon into a greased 10 inch fluted tube pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 60-70 minutes or until cake tests done.

Let stand 5 minutes before removing from the pan.  Cool on a wire rack.  Befgore serving, dust with confectioners sugar.

Yield:  about 16 servings.
