2 pounds Jimmy Dean Sausage
1 1/2 cups all-purpose baking/biscuit mix
4 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1/2 cup finely chopped onion
1/2 cup finely chopped celery
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

-Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
-Mix all ingredients.  Form into balls.
Bake 15 minutes on and ungreased cookie sheet until golden brown.  Makes about 6 dozen.  Sausage cheese balls may be frozen uncooked.
