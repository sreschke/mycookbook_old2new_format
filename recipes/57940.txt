Chicken-Fried Steak & Cream Gravy 
This is great with sides: French-fried or mashed potatoes, crowder peas, squash casserole, Texas toast, beer. 
2 large eggs 
4 cups milk, divided 
1-1/2 cups all-purpose flour 
1 Tbsp. Meat Seasoning (see below) 
tenderized round steak 
vegetable oil 
2 Tbsp. all-purpose flour 
1/2 tsp. salt 
1/2 tsp. pepper 
2 tsp. Worcestershire sauce 
1 tsp. hot sauce 

Whisk together eggs and 2 cups milk in a medium bowl; set aside. Combine 1-1/2 cups flour and Meat Seasoning (see below) in a medium bowl. Dip steaks in egg mixture, and dredge in flour mixture. Dip again in egg mixture. Pour oil to a depth of 3/4 inch into a large heavy skillet. Fry steak in batches in hot oil over high heat 1 1/2 to 2-1/2 minutes on each side or until golden. Remove steak, reserving 2 Tbsp. drippings in skillet, and drain on paper towels. Keep warm. 
Heat reserved drippings in skillet over medium heat; whisk in 2 Tbsp. flour, and cook, whisking constantly, until mixture is thickened and bubbly 
Stir in salt and pepper, worchestershire sauce, and hot sauce. 
Serve gravy with steak. 

MEAT SEASONING:
 
1/4 cup salt 
2 Tbsp.garlic powder 
2 Tbsp. ground black pepper 
1 Tbsp. ground white pepper 
1 Tbsp. onion powder 
1 Tbsp. paprika 
3/4 tsp. ground red pepper 
3/4 tsp. ground cumin 

Combine all ingredients. Store in an air-tight container.  
Yields: 2/3 cup
