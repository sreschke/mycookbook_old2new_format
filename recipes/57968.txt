1 3-1/2 lb. chicken cut into serving  pieces
Salt and Pepper to taste
1/2 tsp. oregano
1 tbsp. finely minced garlic
3 tbsps. red wine vinegar
1/4 cup olive oil
1 cup finely chopped onion
1 cup sweet red or green peppers or combination of both cut  into 1/2" cubes.
1-1/2 cups red ripe tomato, peeled, or use canned, drained
1 bay leaf
24 stuffed green olives
1/4 cup drained capers
1 tbsp. chopped cilentro
1 cup cooked ham in 1/2" dice
2 cups chicken broith
 1 tsp. saffron, (optional)
1-1/2 cups Uncle Ben's converted rice
garnish: 1 cup fresh or frozen tiny tender peas
              an Italian sweet roasted pepper cut into strips

Put chicken on a flat dish and add salt and pepper to taste, oregano, garlic, and vinegar. 
Blend in well with hands.  Heat 1/4 cup of oil in heavy casserole and add chicken pieces, skin side down.  Cook, turning often until pieces are golden brown all over.  Add onions and pepper cubes and cook, stirring, about 5 minutes.  Add tomatoes, capers, bay leaf olives, cilentro, ham and broth.  Add optional saffron and rice and stir to blend.  Cover closely and simmer about 20 minutes or until rice is tender and liquid is absorbed.  Uncover casserole, scatter in peas and pepper strips and cover again until garnish is hot.
