1/2 C sugar
1/4 C orange juice
Heat orange juice with sugar to dissolve
Pour over:
2 C chopped cranberries
Let cool to room temperature
1 pablano chili
1 red bell pepper
2 jalapenos
1/4 C chopped cilantro
orange peel (Opt)
Roast peppers then cool in plastic bag
Remove skins
Chop and then add cilantro
Combine all ingredients and cool completely
Can be served warm with a meat dish
Delicious with tortilla chips also
