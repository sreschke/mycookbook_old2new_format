6 Large Onions, chopped
1/2 c. Butter
6 cans Condensed Beef Broth, undiluted
1 1/2 tsp Worchestershire Sauce
3 Bay Leaves
10 slices French Bread
Shredded Parmesan Cheese
Shredded Mozzerella Cheese

In a large skillet, saute onion in butter until crisp-tender. Transfer to an ungreased 5 qt. slowcooker. Add broth, worchestershire and bay leaves. Cover and cook on low for 5-7 hrs, or until onions are tender. Discard bay leaves. Top each serving with bread slices and cheeses. Serves 10.
