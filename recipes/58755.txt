1 lb. ground beef
3/4 cup Pour N Simmer Taco Seasoning
10 Soft Tortillas
1 can Refried Beans
10 Taco Shells

Brown meat & drain. Add seasoning and simmer 5 min.

Spread warm tortillas each with 2 tablespoons of refried beans. Fold soft tortilla over taco shell.

Fill each taco shell with seasoned meat. Top with any favorite taco fixings: shredded cheddar cheese, sour cream, tomato, lettuce and taco sauce.
