10-15 saltine crackers
1 12-13 oz. can tuna, flaked
1 C (4 oz.) shredded swiss cheese or American cheese
1/2 C chopped onion
3 eggs
1 C mayonnaise
1/2 C milk

Crumble crackers in a 9" pie plate.
In a large bowl toss tuna, cheese & onion:spoon into pie plate. In same bowl stir tog. eggs,mayonnaise&milk. Slowly pour over tuna pie.
Bake at 375 F, for 50 minutes or until knife inserted in the centre comes out clean. serves 8
       (Jean Hoglund)
