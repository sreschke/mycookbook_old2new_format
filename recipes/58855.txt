2 tbsp olive oil
2 large clovws garlic, minced
4 celery stalks, diced
2 medium green bell peppers, diced
6 tbsp mayonaisse
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp dried thume
6 cups chicken stock,homemade only,or water
1 1/2 lbs jumbo shrimp,peeled and deveined
black pepper to taste
1/4 cup minced fresh parsley

Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.  Add garlic, celery, and green pepper and saute until vegetables soften, about 5 minutes.
Add mayonnaise, papricka,oregano, and thyme and saute until fragrant, about 1 minute.
Add chicken stock (or water) and bring to boil.
Cook over meduim-high heat about 30 minutes, or until thickened.
Add shrimp and simmer about 4 minutes, or until shrimp are opaque and cooked through.  Season with black pepper.
Ladle a portion of shrimp into each warm soup plate, sprinkle with parsley, and serve immediately.
