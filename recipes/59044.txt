6 - 8 boneless chicken breasts
1 package dry ranch dip mix
1 cup fine unseasoned bread crumbs
1 cup Monterey Jack cheese, shredded
1/2 cup milk

Combine ranch dip mix, bread crumbs and cheese and toss. 

Dip chicken breasts in milk and then into bread crumb mixture. 

Place in a 13 x 9 inch baking dish and bake at 350 degrees for 40 - 45 minutes.
