1/2 cup Kikkoman Teriyaki Baste and Glaze
1/4 tsp anise seed, crushed
1/4 tsp black pepper
1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
2 pork tenderloins, 3/4 pound each

Combine teriyaki, anise, pepper and cloves.  Brush both ides of each tenderloin thoroughly with glaze mixture; place on shallow foil-lined roasting pan, tucking thin ends of each tenderloin under.  Bake  at 400 for 35 mins until inside temp reaches at least 150, brushing every 10 mins with remaining glaze mixture.  Let stand 10 mins. Cut across grain.

Good with mustard soy dipping sauce:
2 Tbsp dry mustard 
1 tsp distilled white vinegar
1 tsp water.
Mix into paste, cover and let stand 10 mins. Thin with Soy Sauce to dipping consistency.
