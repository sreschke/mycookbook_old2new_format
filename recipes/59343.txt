Coffee Cake

3/4 cup sugar
3 TBSP butter, softened
1 egg
1/2 cup buttermilk
1 cup flour
1/4 cup whole wheat flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp allspice

Topping

1/4 cup cream cheese
1/4 cup sugar
1 egg white
1 pkg (10oz) frozen raspberries, thawed, well drained

Heat oven to 375 degrees.  Spray 9" round cake pan with nonstick cooking spray.

In large bowl, combine 3/4 cup sugar and butter; beat at medium speed with an electric mixer until well mixed.  Add egg; beat at medium speed 1 minute.  Add buttermilk; beat until combined.

Lightly spoon flour into measuring cup; level off.  In medium bowl, combine all purpose flour, whole wheat flour, baking powder, baking soda and allspice; mix well.  Add flour mixture to buttermilk mixture; beat until combined.  Pour batter evenly into spray-coated pan.

In small bowl, combine cream cheese and 1/4 cup sugar; mix until smooth.  Beat in egg white until combined.  Arrange raspberries over batter; pour cream cheese mixture over top.

Bake at 375 degrees for 25 to 35 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.  Serve warm.  12 servings

TIP:  To substitute for buttermilk, use 1 1/2 teaspoons vinegar or lemon juice plus milk to make 1/2 cup.
