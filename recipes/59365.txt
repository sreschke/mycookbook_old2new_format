1 lb lean beef chuck, cut into 1" cubes
2 TBSP flour
2 tsp oil
2 large yellow onions, thinly sliced
2 cups sliced mushrooms
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 tsp tomato paste
2 cups beef broth
4 cups sliced carrots
2 medium russet potatoes, thinly sliced
1 cup green bean pieces
1 TBSP cornstarch
1 TBSP cold water
1/4 cup chopped parsley

Coat beef with flour, shaking off excess.  In a large nonstick pot, heat oil over medium-high heat.  Add beef; saute until browned, about 6 minutes.  Place on a plate.

Add onions and mushrooms to pot; saute for 6 minutes.  Add garlic; saute, stirring or 1 minute.  Pour off fat.  Return beef to pot; stir in tomato paste, then broth.  Add enough water to just cover; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat to low; simmer until beef is tender, about 1 1/4 hours.  Skim off any foam.

Add carrots, potatoes adn green beans.  Cover partially; simmer for 15 minutes.

In a small bowl, mix cornstarch and cold water; stir into stew.  Increase heat and boil uncovered for 1 minute.  Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
