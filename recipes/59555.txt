1 1/2 1bs. chicken strips
4 cups frozen vegetables, thawed
3/4 cup teriyaki stir-fry sauce
1/12 cups French's French Fried Onions

In skillet, stir-fry chicken 5 min.
In 1 tbsp. hot oil until browned.
Add vegetables; stir-fry 3 min.
Add Sauce, cook and stir unti lhot.
Top with French's French Fried Onions.
Serve with rice, if desired.
