1 1/2cups  flour
2/3 cup powdered sugar
3/4 cup margarine
Combine and pat into a greased 13x9x2 inch pan.  Bake at 350 for 20 minutes.

Mix  the following and pour over the hot crust:
3 eggs well beaten
1 1/2 cups sugar
3 Tablespoons flour
1/4 cup lemon juice

Bake at 350 for 20 to 25 minutes until light golden brown.  Dust with powdered sugar and cool on a rack.  Makes 3 to 4 dozen bars.
