
Sweet and Sour Chicken Wings
makes 24

24 chicken wings 
1 Cup sugar 
1 Cup water 
1 Cup Soy Sauce 
1/4 cup oil 
1/4 cup unsweetened pineapple juice 
1 tsp. garlic powder 
1 tsp. ginger 

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
 
Mix all above ingredients.  Place wings in a pan and pour mixture over
wings. 
Let Marinate overnight. Bake in pan at 350Ã¸F for 50-60 minutes.  Serve
warm.
