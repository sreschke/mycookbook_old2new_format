4 cups black beans, soaked in water overnight (or 4 cups of canned) 
1/2 cup onion, diced 
1/4 cup carrots, diced 
1/4 cup celery, diced 
2 tablespoons garlic, minced 
8 ounces smoked tofu, cubed (or you may use regular tofu) 
2 cups vegetable stock 
1 tablespoon jalepe¤o chilies, seeded and minced 
1 tablespoon sugar 
2 tablespoon tomato paste 
1 cup fresh tomatoes, diced (or 1 cup canned) 
4 tablespoons cilantro, chopped 
Salt and pepper to taste 


If you are using dried beans, cover with an inch of water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and allow to simmer for approximately 45 minutes, adding more water if necessary. Beans should be soft but whole (not mushy) when done. Drain the beans. 

Puree or mash one cup of beans and add them back to the whole beans. Saut‚ onion, garlic, carrot and celery with vegetable spray in a medium size sauce pan. Add black beans and vegetable stock, simmer for 20 minutes. 

Add tofu, tomato paste, chilies and sugar. Season with salt and pepper and simmer 5 more minutes. Add chopped tomato and cilantro just before serving. 

Garnish with sour cream, chopped green onions, cilantro sprigs and/or salsa.
