Serves: 4

1 lb. salmon filets
Fresh pepper

Crust:

1 1/2 tablespoons horseradish
1 tables lemon juice
1 tables dijon mustard
1-2 tables olive oil
3 tables finely chopped parseley (Italian parseley,
   which is the wide-leafed parseley, not the curley
5-6 tables dried breadcrumbs

Preheat oven to 350

1.  Wipe salmon filet with paper towels & sprinkle
    with fresh pepper.  Place on shallow, greased,
    baking dish.

2.  In mixing bowl combine: crust ingredients.

3.  Coat the top fo the salmon with an even layer of
    the crust mixture.

4.  Bake for 15-17 minutes.
