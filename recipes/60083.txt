4 Tablespoons chili powder
2 Cups of flour
2 Cups plus 2 Tablespoons water
2 Tablespoons vegetable oil
2 pounds ground beef
1 package taco seasoning (optional)
3 Tablespoons vegetable oil
1 8-oz cream cheese, softened
1 package flour tortillas
1 can 10 1/2 oz tomato soup
1 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese

Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Spread 3 Tablespoons of vegetable oil in 13"x9"x2" pan. Set aside.

In a pie plate mix chili powder and flour with a fork. Gradually add water until well mixed.  Add 2 Tablespoons vegetable oil. Set aside.

Brown gound beef in a skillet. Drain grease. Add taco seasoning and prepare as instructed.  Sprinkle 2 Tablespoons flour on taco meat and stir.

Spread 1 heaping teaspoon of cream cheese on 1/3 of tortilla. Dip one side of tortilla in chili powder mixture. Place about 1/2 cup of ground beef onto tortilla. Roll tortilla and put in pan. Repeat with remaining tortillas.

Top rolled tortillas with tomato soup and spread. Sprinkle with grated cheese.

Bake at 375 degrees for 25-30 minutes or until cheese is browned.
