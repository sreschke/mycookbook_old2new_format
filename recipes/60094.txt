3/4 cup fresh lemon juice
1/2 cup soy sauce
6 T honey
2 small shallots, coarsely chopped
2 garlic cloves, halved
2 bay leaves
2 tsp, salt
2 tsp. pepper
1 tsp. dry mustard
1/2 ground ginger
4 12oz. pork tenderloins
Puree first 10 ingredients in blender. divide tenderloins between two large plastic reseable bags. Add half of marinade to each and seal tightly. Turn to coat. Refrigerate overnite. Prepare barbecue med. high heat. Transfer marinade to heavy saucepan and put pork on grill. grill to desired doness. About 20 minutes. Meanwhile, boil marinade until reduced to sauce consistency, 5 minutes. Serve with pork.
