1 lb. ground beef  
1(5-6oz.)box of scalloped potatoes
16oz. can of tomatoes 
10oz. can of pizza sauce 
1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup Parmesan cheese

Brown ground beef and drain well; mix with the
dry ingredients from potatoes (in crock pot), then add the tomatoes(chopped & drained); then the 10z. can of pizza sauce; then potatoes - cook 4-5 hours. Remove lid and add the cheese; cover and let cook another 15 mins,
