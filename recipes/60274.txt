2 tbsp. flour
1/2 tsps. Garlic powder
1/2 tsps. pepper
1/4 tsp paprika
1 3/4 lbs. beef round steak
1 can cream mushroom soup
1/2 cup water
1 pkg. onion soup mix
1 jar (9oz) sliced mushrooms
1/2 cup dairy sour cream
1 tbsp. minced fresh parley
 
Combine flour, garlic powder, pepper and paprika. Cut meat into 1 1/2 X 1/2 inch strips. Place in flour mixture and toss until blended. Cover and cook on low 6 to 7 hours or until very tender. Stir in mushrooms, sour cream and parsley. Cook on high for 10 to 15 mins.
 
Note: This is the original recipe. When I made it at the beach, I made a couple of substitutions, like fresh mushrooms, garlic, etc.
