3- to 3 1/2 pound boneless beef pot roast (rump, chuck, or round)
1 envelope Lipton Recipe Secrets Onion Soup Mix
2- 1/2 cups water

*In Dutch oven or 6-quart saucepot, brown roast over medium-high heat.
*Add onion soup mix blended with water.  Bring to a boil over high heat.
*Reduce heat to low and simmer covered, turning roast occasionally, 2- 1/2 hours or until tender.
*If desired, thicken gravy.
*Makes about 8 servings.
