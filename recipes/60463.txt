This makes 4 servings.

4 T balsamic vinegar
¬ C lemon juice
1« T olive oil
1 t dried oregano
4 (4 oz.) boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
Cooking spray
4 curly lettuce leaves
¬ C salsa
4 (6 in.) flour tortillas
Red onion rings (optional)
Fresh oregano (optional)

Combine vinegar, lemon juice, olive oil and chicken in a zip-top plastic bag; seal and marinate in refrigerator 8 hours.

Remove chicken from the bag; discard marinade.

Place chicken on broiler pan coated with cooking spray; broil 8 minutes on each side or until done.

Divide chicken, lettuce and salsa evenly among tortillas and roll up.

Garnish with onion and oregano, if desired.


This is a Weight Watchers recipe.

Each serving provides 6 points.
