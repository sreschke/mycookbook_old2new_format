2 C thinly sliced onions
1 C chopped celery
2 T butter or margarine
2 C water
2 cups diced potatoes
1 C sliced carrots
1 t salt
¬ t pepper
1 lb. fresh or frozen whitefish fllets, cut into 3/4 -inch pieces
2 C milk
8 oz. processed American chese, cubed
Chives or parsley, optional

In a 3-qt. saucepan, saute onions and celery in butter until tender.

Add the water, potatoes, carrots, salt and pepper; bring to a boil.

Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 10 minutes.

Add fish and simmer 10 minutes longer.

Stir in milk and cheese until cheese is melted (do not boil).

Garnish individual servings with chives if desired.

Yield:  8 servings
