1/2 package fast rising yeast
1/2 cup sugar
3 tablespoon of butter
3 cups flour
1 cup warm water


mix all ingeridents well. pour out unto counter to and knead for 8 - 10 minutes. put back into a dark bowl, rub oil all over the dough. cover with plastic wrap. let it set for 45 - 55 minutes
roll and knead into two balls, cut to your style. 
cook at 350 degree untill light brown on the bottom of the rolls
makes about 16 rolls
