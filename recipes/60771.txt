Good with Meatloaf and baked potatoe.

2 medium acorn squash, halved and seeded
1/4 cup butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 tsp. ground cinnamon

1. Place squash in 11*7" baking dish. dot 1 Tbsp. butterin each squash half. evenly sprinkle brown sugar and cinnamon in squash halves.

2. Add enough water to baking dish to just cover the bottom of the pan; cover with foil.

3. Bake at 350* for 1 hour, until squash is tender. serve with additional brown sugar if desired.

Yields: 4 servings
