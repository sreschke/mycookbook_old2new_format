RED LOBSTER CHEDDAR BAY BISCUITS
(Makes 20)

Dough:
1-1/4 lbs Bisquick
3 oz freshly shredded cheddar cheese
11  oz cold water

Garlic Spread:
1/2 cup melted butter
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon onion powder
1/8 teaspoon dried parsley
