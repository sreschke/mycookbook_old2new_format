Prep time: 5 minutes
Cook time: 20 minutes

1 can kidney beans, rinsed and drained
1 can diced tomatoes with basil, garlic & oregano
1C broken uncooked egg noodles
1 stalk celery, diced
2 cans chicken or beef broth
1 can sliced carrots, drained

Combine all but carrots in large pot. Cover and simmer 10-12 minutes.

Add carrots and simmer 5 more minutes. Serve with warm bread, if desired.
