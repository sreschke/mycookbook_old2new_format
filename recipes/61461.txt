8 oz. diced frozen potatoes, thawed
1t dried onion
5 eggs
1/2C low-fat cottage cheese
4 oz. grated Cheddar cheese (1C)
1t salt
1/2t pepper
1c chopped ham
Paprika

START THIS RECIPE THE NIGHT BEFORE!

Spray pie pan with non-stick spray. 

Beat eggs; stir in rest of ingredients. Pour into pan; sprinkle with paprika. Cover and refrigerate overnight.

The next day:

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Cook, uncovered, 40-45 minutes.

Serves 8
4 WW points per serving
