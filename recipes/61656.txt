Rice Pudding

1 qt. whole milk
1/2 Cup RIVER BRAND RICE
1/2 cup of white sugar
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
Cinnamon
Dash of Salt

In heavy sauce pan, pour milk, add rice & salt.  Simmer until he pudding is the desired thickness (Can take 1 to 1 1/2 hours) Remove from heat if too thick, add some milk. Add sugar, vanilla & cinnamon.

Mix well, cover & refrigerate.  Can add raisins, when adding the sugar or serve with sliced strawberries.
