1   quart cherry tomatoes, halved
1/4 cup olive oil
3   tbs balsmic vinegar
1/4 cup minced fresh parsley
2   tsp minced fresh basil
2   tsp minced fresh oregano
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp sugar
lettuce leaves
Place tomatoes in shallow bowl. In a jar with tight lid, combine oil, vinegar, parsley, basil, oregano, salt and sugar; shake well. Pour over tomatoes. Cover and refrigerate overnight. Serve on a bed of lettuce. Serves 6-8
