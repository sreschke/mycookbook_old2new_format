2-1/4 c.flour 3/4 c.sugar 3/4 c. butter 1/2 tsp. baking powder 1/2 tsp. baking soda 1/4 tsp. salt 3/4 c. sour cream 1 tsp. of almond extract 1 egg 8 oz. cream cheese (softened) 1/4 c. sugar 1 egg 1 c. raspberry preserves 1/2 c. slivered almonds, Grease and flour springform pan,combine flour 3/4 c. sugar mix well cut in butter, till coarse, ( reserve 1 cup) to remaining mix add baking powder and soda, salt,and sour cream, almond extract and 1 egg, blend well spread over bottom and up sides 2", mix cream cheese 1/4 c. sugar and 1 egg pour onto batter, spoon preserves over mix combine crumb mix and almonds over preserves. Bake 350* 1 hr. and 15 mins. cool completely and serve.
