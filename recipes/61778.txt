1/2 pound ground beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/3 cup taco sauce
1/4 cup chopped green chilies
1/4 tsp salt
1 cup broken tortilla chips
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese

In a skillet, cook beef and onion over medium heat until no longer pink. Drain.  Stir in taco sauce, chilies and salt.  Cover and cook over med heat for 6-8 minutes until heated through. Spoon over chips and sprinkle with cheese.
