1 lb. spiral macaroni, cooked/drained
1 lb. cheddar cheese, cubed
1 bunch green onion, sliced
3 oz. sliced pepperoni
3/4 c. vegetable oil*
2/3 c. grated parmesan*
1/2 c. red wine vinegar*
2 tsp dried oregano*
1 tsp garlic powder*
1 tsp salt*
1/4 tsp pepper*
Garlic croutons 

(*for a even easier recipe, all * ingredients could be substituted for bottled italian dressing and parmesan cheese.) 

In a large bowl, combine macaroni, cheddar cheese, green onion and pepperoni. In a smaller bowl, combine oil, vinegar, parmesan and seasonings; pour over macaroni mixture. Cover and refrigerate for several hours. Top with croutons just before serving. Serves 16.
