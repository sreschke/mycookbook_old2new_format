2 lbs		Medium sized fresh shrimp
1 C		All purpose flour
1 1/2 t	Baking powder
1/2 t	Salt
2/3 C	Water
2 T		Lemon juice
1 T		Vegetable oil
1		large egg, lightly beaten
		Vegetable oil

Peel shrimp, leaving tails intact, devein is desired. Combine flour, baking powder and salt in med. bowl. Add water and next 3 ingredients, stir until smooth.

Pour oil to depth of 2 inches into a Dutch oven. Heat to 375 degrees. Dip shrimp into batter; fry, a few at a time, until golden. Drain. 
Yields 2 to 4 servings.
