10-12 hard boiled eggs, sliced
2 cups chopped (or slivered) baked ham
1 1/2 cups crushed potato chips

Cheese Sauce:
6 Tbsp. oleo
1/2 tsp. salt
6 Tbsp. flour
1 tsp. dry mustard
3 cups milk
1 1/2 cups grated cheddar cheese
1/2 cup onion, chopped

Melt oleo and add flour, salt, mustard, add milk and cook until smooth and thickened.  Add cheese and stir over low heat until melted.  ADD 1/2 cup chopped onion, which has been sauted in 1 tbsp. oleo.

In large pyrex, layer eggs, ham, chips & sauce... ending with sauce.  Sprinkle with cheese.  Bake at 350 about 20 minutes or until bubbly hot.

Serves 12-15

(Melba Neeley)
