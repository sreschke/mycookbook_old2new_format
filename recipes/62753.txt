5 med. yellow squash
1 egg, beaten
1/2 c. buttermilk
1 onion, chopped
3/4 c. self rising cornmeal
1/4 c. flour
oil for frying

Cook squash til tender. Drain, mash and drain again. Combine squash, egg, buttermilk, onion, cornmeal, and flour. Drop mixture by scant tablespoons into hot oil and fry like cornbread.  

 2 1/2 dozen.
