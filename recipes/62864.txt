Creole Sauce

         1 can tomato soup
         1/2 cup water
         4 tablespoons green bell pepper, chopped
         2 tablespoons white onion, chopped
         6 stuffed olives, chipped
         1/2 teaspoon salt
         2 tablespoons margerine or clarified butter

         Cook all together about 15 minutes.  Serve over fillet of steamed or
         poached fish.
