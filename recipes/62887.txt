3/4 pound bulk Italian Sausage
1/2 cup chopped onion
1 garlic clove, minced
1 can (15.5 ounces) butter beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (15 ounces) black beans, rinsed and drained
1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
1 can (14.5 ounces) beef broth
1 Tbs minced fresh basil OR 1 tsp dried basil
2 Tbs shredded Parmesan cheese

In a large saucepan, cook sausage, onion and garlic until the sausage is browned; drain. Add beans, tomatoes, broth and basil.  Cover and simmer for 10 minutes.  Sprinkle each serving with Parmesan cheese.  Serves 6.
