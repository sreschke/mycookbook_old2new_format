1-1/2 cups dry great northern beans
12 cups water
1 tablespoon beef bouillon
4 slices bacon
1 large onion, finely chopped (1 cup)
2 medium carrots, coarsely chopped
1 28-oz. can tomatoes, cut-up
1 cup frozen loose-pack diced hash browns
1/4 teaspoon pepper
1/2 cup snipped fresh parsley

Rinse beans.  Combine beans and 6 cups of water, bring to boil, reduce heat and simmer for 2 minutes.  Remove from heat.  Cover and let stand for 1 hour.  

Combine beans, remaining water, bouillon and bring to boil; reduce heat.  Cover and simmer the mixture for 45 minutes.  Meanwhile in a skillet, cook bacon until crisp; drain, reserving 2 T. of the drippings.  Crumble the cooked bacon, set aside.  Cook and stir onion and carrots in the reserved bacon drippings for 10 minutes.  Stir cooked vegetable mixture, undrained tomatoes, potatoes and pepper into bean mixture.  Cover and simmer for 20 minutes or until vegetables are tender.  Just before serving, stir parsley into soup.  Top with crumbed bacon.
