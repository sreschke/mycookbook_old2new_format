4 Lrg. Baking Potatoes
2/3 cup margarine
2/3 cup all-purpose flour
6 cups milk
3/4 tea. salt
1/2 tea. pepper
4 green onions
1/2 cup bacon bits
5 oz. shredded cheddar cheese(divided into 2 parts)
1  16 oz. sour cream

Bake and peel potatoes.  Mash part of them and cube the other part.  Set aside.
Melt butter over low heat; add flour until smooth.  Gradually add milk and cook until thick and bubbly.
Add potatoes, salt, pepper, onions, and bacon bits. Cook until throughly heated; add sour cream and 1/2 of the cheese. If too thick, add a little more milk. When ready to serve, add cheese and bacon bits on top of bowl.
