4to6 chicken breast
1 cup margarine
1 onion, diced
1 clove garlic, chopped
1 1/2 cups flour
1 tsp. seasoned salt
2 tsp. paprika
2 pints sour cream
1/4 tsp. cumin
1 can green chilies

 Cook chicken in 12-16 cups water until done. Debone chicken and reserve broth. In a large saute pan, combine butter, onion and garlic. gradually add flour, seasoned salt, and paprika. This will make a White sauce. if too thick, add alittle broth and stir. Add remaining broth and sour cream. Stir well. Add remaining ingredients and mix well. cook until heated through and serve. 
Serve with tortilla chips, grated cheese, green onions and picante sauce.
