4-6 fresh peaches (or well-drained can peaches

        Batter Mixture
1 stick margarine
1/2c. sugar
1/2c. milk
1c. self-rising flour

          Syrup Mixture 
1c. sugar
1/2c. hot water

Peel and slice peaches. Line 8x8x2 pan with peaches.  Pour batter mixture over fruit. Spread. Mix syrup mixture and pour over batter. Bake 350 for 1 hour.

(If using frozen fruit, cut the sugar in the syrup  
  mixture to 1/2c.)

APPLE COBBLER; Use fresh apples instead of peaches.
