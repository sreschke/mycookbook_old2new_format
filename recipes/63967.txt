10 ounces frozen mixed vegetables
1/2 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup chopped onion
1/2 cup chopped green pepper
1 can kidney beans
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup white vinegar
1 tablespoon cornstarch

Cook veggies in microwave for 3 minutes and drain.
Drain and wash kidney beans.  Put ALL veggies in a large bowl, sprinkle with garlic salt.
In a saucepan, mix sugar and cornstarch, add vinegar-cook over medium heat until thick-cool slightly-add to veggies- toss and chill.
