Ingredients for 4 Servings

2/3 lb California shrimp or spiny lobster
2/3 tsp Butter
1 1/3 tsp Olive oil
2 cloves Garlic, minced or pressed
1/5 cup Italian parsley, finely chopped
1/5 cup White wine
1/5 tsp Salt and pepper
Juice of 1 lemon
Garnish:
Lemon wedges

Procedure: 

1. Melt butter and oil together in saut‚ pan.
2. Add garlic, saut‚ for one minute, and add shrimp.
3. Saut‚ for one minute, add wine, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
4. Saut‚ quickly while sauce reduces and shrimp turns pink.
5. Do not overcook.
6. Sprinkle with parsley before serving.
7. Serve with sauce over noodles or rice.
8. Garnish with lemon wedges.
9. Courtesy of the California Seafood Council
