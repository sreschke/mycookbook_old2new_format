1 cup bottled barbecue sauce
1 cup milk
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tbsp mustard
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 onion, finely chopped
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
6 pork butt chops (3/4" thick)

In a medium sized saucepan, combine the barbecue sauce, milk, brown sugar, mustard, soy sauce, onions and garlic.
Bring mixture to a boil.  Simmer gently uncovered, stirring often, 10 to 15 minutes or until thickened.  (Sauce can be used immediately or refrigerated for up to 5 days).
Pat pork chops dry.  Place them in a 9" x 13" baking dish in a single layer.
Spoon sauce on top.
Bake in preheated 375F oven for 40 minutes.

Prepration time: 10 min
Cooking time: 40 min
