1/2 lb ground turkey
1 tsp minced ginger
2 tsp minced green onion
2 tsp minced water chestnuts
1 tsp soy sauce
1 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes 
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp garlic powder
2 eggs, beaten
oil for frying
24 wonton wrappers  
 
Mix all the ingredients except egg, wrappers and oil in a bowl. Add 2 tablespoons of egg. Save the rest of the egg. Preheat oil in a deep fryer to 375F. Use enough to cover the pot stickers, about 2 inches. Invert a glass on the center of a wonton wrappers and cut to make a circle (one at a time. Spoon 1/2 tablespoon of filling into the center of one wrapper. Brush beaten egg around half of the edge of the wrapper and fold over filling. Gather  as you seal so it is crinkled. Repeat with remaining. Deep-fry, six at a time in hot oil til they are brown. Drain on paper towels. Serve with  oriental sauce of your choice.
