Crust:
1/2 C SHORTENING
1/4 C SUGAR
1 C FLOUR

Mix well.  Pat into 9x9 pan & bake at 350 for 25 mins.

Topping:
2/3 C CHOPPED DRIED APRICOTS
1/3 C FLOUR
1/2 TEA BAKING POWDER
1/4 TEA SALT
1 C BROWN SUGAR-PACKED
2 EGGS
1/2 TEA VANILLA
1/2 C CHOPPED PECANS

Mix well & pour over crust.  Bake at 350 for 30 mins.  Cool and cut into small squares & roll in powered sugar.  

Freezes well.

Grandma Gladys always made these at the Holidays.
