1 box cornbread mix 
10oz. package of frozen chopped broccoli, thawed and drained 
1 large onion, chopped 
10+ oz cottage cheese 
1/2 cup of melted butter 
1 tsp. salt 
4 eggs beaten well 

Mix all ingredients, except corn bread mix. Add corn bread mix. Pour in a greased 9 x 13 casserole. Bake 15-20 minutes at 400 degrees. serve hot. Do not overbake. 

12 Servings
