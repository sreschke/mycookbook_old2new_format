1/2 cup shortening
1/2 cup molasses
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 cup peanut butter
1/2 tsp salt
1 unbeaten egg
1 cup sifted flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup chopped pecans

Combine shortening, molasses, sugar, peanut butter, salt and egg. Beat well. Sift flour and baking powder together. Add flour and nuts to beaten mixture and mix well. Drop by tablespoons into greased baking sheet. Flatten cookies with a glass covered by a damp cloth. Bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes. Makes about 3 dozen.
