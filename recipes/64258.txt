Pam Olive Oil Spray
1 large onion, quartered and sliced
1 c fresh mushrooms, sliced

2 c chicken broth
2 Tbsp all purpose flour
1/2 tsp Italian Seasoning
1 tsp salt (or more to taste)
1/4 tsp garlic powder
Freshly ground black pepper

1 (28 oz) can petite diced tomatoes

1 c white wine (Chardonnay or other white wine)

1 pound chicken breasts, cut into strips

cooked rice
................................................
1. Spray a large pot and saut‚ the onions for 3 minutes.  Add the mushrooms and saut‚ 3 minutes more.

2. Stir together chicken broth, flour, and seasonings

3. Add the chicken broth mixture, tomatoes, and wine to the pot

4. Simmer for 30 min.  Add chicken.  Turn off the heat and allow the mixture to rest, covered, for about 30 min.  Check the chicken for doneness and adjust seasonings as needed.

5. Serve over rice
