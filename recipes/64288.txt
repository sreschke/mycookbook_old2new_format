Burger Bundles
1 lb. ground beef
1 box Stove Top stuffing
1 can cream of mushroom soup
2 Tbs. catsup
2 Tbs. Worcestershire sauce 
Make stuffing according to pkg. directions. Divide ground beef into 6 pieces. Flatten into thin circles. Place stuffing on each circle and mold the ground beef around the stuffing, forming a ball. Place in baking dish. Combine soup, catsup and Worcestershire sauce. Bake at 350 degrees for one hour.
