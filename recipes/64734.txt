6 med potatoes, cut into chunks
4 med carrots, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
can crean of chicken soup
can cream of mushroom soup
1/8 tsp garlic salt

Place potatoes and carrots in slow cooker. Top with chicken. Combine soups and garlic salt.  Pour over chicken.  Cover and coiok on low for 8 hours.  To thicken if desired, stir 2-4 Tablespoons of potato flakes into gravy and cook 30 mins longer.
