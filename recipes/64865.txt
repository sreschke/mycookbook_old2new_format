1 large bunch broccoli (about 1 1/2lbs.)
1 lb. beef tenderloin steaks, trimmed, thinly cut into 1/8 in. strips
3 garlic cloves, crushed
1 Tbs. grated,. fresh ginger
1/4 tsp. crushed red pepper
1 tsp. olive oil
3/4 cup chicken broth
3 Tbs. soy sauce
1 Tbs. cornstarch
1/2 tsp. Asian sesame oil

Cut broccoli flowerets into 1 1/2 in. pieces.  Peel broccoli stems and cut into 1/4 in. diagonal slices.
In nonstick 12 in. skillet, heat 1/2 in. water to boiling over medium-high heat.  Add broccoli and cook 3 min., uncovered, or until tender-crisp.  Drain broccoli and set aside.  Wipe skillet dry.
In medium bowl, toss beef with garlic, ginger and crushed red pepper.  Add 1/2 tsp. olive oil to skillet and heat over medium-high heat until hot but not smoking.  Add half of beef mixture and cook 2 mi or until beefjust loses its pink color throughout, stirring quickly and frequently.  Transfer beef to plate.  Repeat with remaining 1/2 tsp. olive oil and beef mixture.
In cup, mix broth, soy sauce, cornstarch and sesame oil until blended.  Return cooked beef to skillet.  Stir in cornstarch mixture; heat to boiling.  Cook 1 min. or until sauce thickens slightly, stirring.  Add broccoli and toss to coat.
4 servomgs
Nutrition:  245 calories, 10g carbohydrates, 11g total fat
