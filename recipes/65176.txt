4 cups mini marshmallows
2/3 cup evaporated milk
1/4 cup butter
1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 12-ounce package semisweet chocolate pieces-2 cups
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 cup chopped nuts

Grease a 9-inch square baking pan.
Combine marshmallows, milk, butter, sugar and salt in saucepan. Cook and stir until mixture comes to a full boil. Boil 5 minutes over medium heat, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat and add chocolate pieces, beating until melted. Fold in vanilla and nuts. Pour into a prepared pan. Chill until firm.
