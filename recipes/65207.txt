TODAY'S TIP: BANANA CHOCOLATE LOAF 
 
by Tiffany Taylor  
 
Serves 12. 

1/2 cup butter, at room temperature 
1-2/3 cups sugar 
2 eggs, at room temperature, slightly beaten  
1 tsp vanilla 
1 tsp salt  
1-1/2 tsp baking powder  
1/2 tsp baking soda 
1/4 cup sour cream, at room temperature 
1 cup mashed banana 
2 cups cake flour 
2 cups chocolate chips 

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 9-inch loaf pan. 

Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs, vanilla, and 
salt; mix well. 

Dissolve baking powder and baking soda in sour cream; add to creamed 
mixture. Stir in mashed banana. Gradually stir in flour, followed by 
chocolate chips. 

Pour into prepared pan. Bake 1-1/2 hours, until a tester inserted in 
the center comes out clean.
