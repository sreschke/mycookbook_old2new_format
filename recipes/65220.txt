The Cow Pie 
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~ 
2 cups (12 ounces) milk chocolate chips 
1 tablespoon shortening 
l/2 cup raisins 
1/2 cup chopped slivered almonds 
------------------------------- 
In a double boiler over simmering water, melt the chocolate chips and 
shortening, stirring until smooth. Remove from the heat; stir in raisins 
and almonds. Drop by tablespoonfuls onto waxed paper. Chill until ready 
to serve. Yield: 2 dozen. 
This comes from my friend Chet Boni whose first sexual experience was with a cow (It's rough when you lived in the middle of Montana with no neighbors for miles).
