8 cups water
6 potatoes, cubed
1 onion
1 tbls butter
1 bay leaf
2 tsps dry parsley
salt and pepper
1 cup flour
2 eggs
1/2 cup half and half 

Quarter onion and separate the layers. Boil water with potato, onion, butter, bay leaf, parsley, and salt and pepper to taste.  

When the potatoes are done, mix flour, eggs, and about 1 tsp salt in a bowl. Add water til it becomes a thick dough.  Spoon noodles in soup. 

After noodles are fluffy, pour in cream. Heat through.   

4 servings
