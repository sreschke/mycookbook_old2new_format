
4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves
2 TBS. butter or margarine
Salt and pepper
1 medium clove finely minced garlic
1 TBS. finely minced onion
1/2 lb. fresh mushrooms, sliced
1/4 Cup Grey Poupon Dijon Mustard
1 Cup heavy cream*

In a large skillet, cook chicken in butter. Remove to heated platter. Keep warm. To drippings in pan add garlic, onion and mushrooms. Cook, stirring, until onion is tender. Stir in mustard and heavy cream. Simmer, stirring, until sauce is slightly thickened. Add chicken, turning to coat in sauce. 

Serve over rice. 

Serves 4

* I use Land O Lakes FAT FREE half & half
