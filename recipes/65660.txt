Young Spinach Salad with Miso Dressing

1 bag of young spinach or young Romaine
1 small red onion, sliced thin
1  can of hearts of palm (chopped in chunks)
1 cucumber

Dressing

1 Cup canola oil
1/3 cup water
1/4 cup miso paste (Mellow White)
1/8 cup tamari (or soy sauce)
1/4 cup cider vinegar
2 TBSP honey
1 clove garlic

Blend all in blender
