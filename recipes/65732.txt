1 baked 9" pie shell
1 pint of vanilla ice cream
1 cup of milk   (Not non-fat.  Non fat makes filling runny)
1 3-oz size pkg of Chocolate Instant Pudding

Blend together lightly in a bowl the ice cream and milk. Add the chocolate instant pudding.  Beat until just mixed....about l minute. Pour into the cooled pie shell.  Refrigerate.  Serve with whipped cream or Kool Whip.
            
Can also make the pie using Lemon Instant pudding with Pineapple Ice ream, or vanilla instant pudding with pistachio ice cream.  The Chocolate is best!

A double recipe will make a full 10" pie with a little bit left over for pudding.
