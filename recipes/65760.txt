6 New York strip steaks
12-ounce bottle of dark beer
1/2 cup (packed) dark brown sugar
5 tablespoons fresh lime juice
3 tablespoons minced onion
6 garlic cloves, chopped
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
2 tablespoons grainy mustard
2 tablespoons oil
1 tablespoon minced ginger
1/2 teaspoon Tabasco sauce
1/2 cup white wine
1 green onion, finely chopped
2 tablespoons crushed peppercorns 
1 3/4 cups chicken stock 
1 3/4 cups beef stock  
1/2 cup heavy cream

Put steaks in single layer in glass baking dish. Whisk beer, sugar, lime juice, onion, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, mustard, oil, ginger and Tabasco together. Pour over steaks. Cover and refrigerate overnight.

Bring white wine, chopped green onion and crushed peppercorns to boil, simmer til reduced by half, about 5 minutes. Add chicken stock and beef stock and boil til reduced to 2 cups, about 25 minutes. Add cream and cook til sauce coats spoon, about 6 minutes.  

Prepare barbecue (medium-high). Grill steaks to desired doneness. Transfer to plates. Meanwhile, bring pepper sauce to simmer. Drizzle over steaks.

6 servings.
