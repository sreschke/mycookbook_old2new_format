3/4 c Hollandaise Sauce
5 Tbsp coarsely chopped green onion
1 Tbsp pepper
1 cup dry vermouth, or dry white wine
3/4 cup cider vinegar
1 1/2 Tbsp tarragon
1 Tbsp fresh parsley
2 Tbsp chervil, optional

Prepare Hollandaise Sauce.

In blender or food processor, place remaining ingredients. Blend at high speed 30 seconds. Pour into a heavy saucepan and boil until liquid is gone, about 30 minutes. Cool. Beat 1 tsp of this essence into the Hollandaise Sauce. Remaining essence may be frozen or refrigerated for several weeks.
