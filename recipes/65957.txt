ground beef, browned and drained and seasoned with Taco Seasoning
tortilla chips
jalapeno slices
salsa
shredded Monterey Jack cheese
refried beans
shredded lettuce
diced tomato

1. Place tortilla chips on a cookie sheet or oven safe plate.
2. Spread with refried beans
3. Top with ground beef mixture
4. Top with cheese and a pepper slice
5. Bake until cheese melts
6. Add lettuce, tomato and salsa
7. Serve hot
