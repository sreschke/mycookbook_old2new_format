Ingredients:
1fillet of salmon, grilled or broiled
mixed green lettuce (ie: Romaine, Mescalane, etc..)
crumbled feta cheese
balsamic vinegar dressing

Grill or broil salmon as preferred.  Mix lettuce with dressing in a large bowl.  Then place lettuce on a flat good sized salad plate.  Lay a good amount of crumbled feta on topof the salad, in the shape of a circle completely filled.  Then place the salmon on top of the bed of feta.  Enjoy.
