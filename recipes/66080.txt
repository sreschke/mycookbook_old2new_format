2 pounds frozen hash brown potatoes
1/2 cup melted margarine
1/4 cup shopped onion
1 can cream of chicken soup
16 oz. sour cream
10 oz. grated cheddar 
bread crumbs

Thaw potatoes completely.  Combine potatoes in a 13x9 baking dish with margarine, onion, soup, sour cream and cheese.  Sprinkle with bread crumbs.  Bake 1 hour 15 minutes at 350 degrees.
