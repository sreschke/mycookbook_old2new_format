*4 squares Baker's Semi-Sweet Baking Chocolate or 1 pkg. (4 oz.) Baker's German's Sweet Baking Chocolate 
*2-1/2 cups cold milk
*2 pkgs. (4-serving size each) Jello Chocolate Flavor Instant Pudding & Pie Filling Thawed COOL Whip Whipped Topping, for garnish Grated BAKER"S Semi-Sweet Baking Chocolate, for garnish
*¬ cup (1/2 stick) butter or margarine
*_ cup sugar
*2 eggs
*1 tsp. Vanilla
*« cup flour
*« cup chopped nuts

Microwave chocolate and butter on high 2 minutes; stir until chocolate is melted and mixture is smooth.  STIR in sugar, eggs and vanilla.  Blend in flour then nuts.  Spread butter in greased 9-inch pie plate.  Bake in preheated 350 F oven (325 F for glass pie plate) 25 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out with fudgy crumbs.  DO NOT OVERBAKE.  Cool.  POUR milk into large bowl.  Add pudding mixes.  Beat with wire whisk 1 minute.  Spread over brownie pie.
TOP with whipping topping; sprinkle with grated chocolate.  Refrigerate until ready to serve.  
Males 8 servings.
