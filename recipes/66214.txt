1 cup of walnuts (crushed)
2 sticks of butter (melted)
2 cups of flour
1 1/2 boxes of vanilla pudding
2 banana's (sliced)
1 box of cream cheese
whipped cream

In a pan, mix together walnuts, butter, and flour: until blended.  Bake at 350 for 30 min.  In a bowl; combine vanilla pudding and cream cheese. Place banana's on dry crust and pour pudding mixture into pan and top with whipped cream. Fridge for at least one hour.
