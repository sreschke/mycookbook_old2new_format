1 Keebler Chocolate pie crust
  or regular pastry crust, baked
6 oz. semi-sweet chocolate, chopped
2 T margarine
1/4 c powder powder sugar
8 oz cream cheese
21 oz can cherry pie filling
1 c whipping cream, whipped (or coolwhip)
2 oz grated semisweet chocolate

Melt chocolate and magerine over low heat. Beat sugar and cream cheese together, stir in choco mixture, fold in 1 cup of cherry pie filling, set aside remaing pie filling. Spread mixture evenly in cool pie crust. Refrigerate 1 hour.

Mix together whipped cream and grated chocolate, spread remaining pie filling on pie, top with the whipped cream, garnish with grated or shaved chocolate. Store in refrigerator.
