1 lb		Large freash shrimp
1/4 C	All-purpose flour
1/4 C	Cornstarch
1/8 t	Salt
1/4 C	Beer
2 T		Butter or marjorine, melted
1		Egg yolk
		Vegetable oil

Peel shrimp, leaving tails intact; devein is desired. combine flour, cornstarch and salt. Add beer, butter and egg yold, stir until smooth.

Pour oil to depth of 2 inches into a Dutch oven. Heat to 375 degrees. Dip shrimp into batter; fry, a few at a time, until golden. Drain. 
Yields 2 to 4 servings.
