5 lbs. spareribs
1/2 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. sugar
2 tbsp. cornstarch
2/3 c. vinegar
1 c. ketchup
1/2 c. cold water

1)   Place ribs in shallow pan.
2)   Bake uncover at 350 degrees for 1-1/2 hours.
3)   Combine sugars and cornstarch in medium saucepan.
4)   Stir in ketchup, vinegar, and water.
5)   Bring to a boil.  Cook and stir until clear.
6)   Remove ribs and discard fat.
7)   Replace ribs back in pan.
8)   Pour 1 to 1-1/2 c. of sauce over ribs.
9)   Bake 30 minutes longer.
10) Cut ribs in serving size pieces.  Pour remaining sauce over ribs.
