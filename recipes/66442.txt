8 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
2 cups buttermilk
2 Tbsp. Old Bay seasoning (you can substitute paprika)
1 tsp. Tabasco sauce
1 small onion, minced
I clove garlic, minced
salt and pepper
1 cup flour
1 cup vegetable oil

Combine buttermilk, Old Bay (or paprika), Tabasco, onion, garlic,salt and pepper.  Pour over chicken breasts and allow to marinate for 3 - 4 hours.  

Pour flour onto large plate.  Remove chicken
breasts from marinade and coat with flour.  Place floured chicken on wax paper and refrigerate for 1 hour.  

Remove chicken from refrigerator and allow to warm to room temperature (about 15 minutes).

In a large frying pan or skillet, heat oil to 370 degrees.  Cook chicken breasts one at a time for about 7 - 10 minutes each side until brown and cooked through.  

Drain chicken breasts on paper towels.
