Bake Time: 25 to 30 minutes

Ingredients:
1 tube (8oz.) crescent rolls
2 C. corn chips
1/2 - 2 lbs. ground beef, browned
1 carton (16 oz.) sour cream
1 pkg. taco seasoning mix
2 C. shredded cheese

Preparation:
PAT crescent rolls in UNGREASED cake pan. 
Put 1 cup crushed corn chips over dough. 
BROWN hamburger and ADD taco seasoning; 
MIX according to package directions. 
Place meat mixture over dough with crushed chips. 
Put sour cream over meat and cover with shredded cheese. 
ADD another cup of crushed corn chips. 
BAKE at 350 degress for 25 - 30 minutes or until cheese is MELTED. 

SERVE with lettuce, tomatoes, black olives and taco sauce on the side.
