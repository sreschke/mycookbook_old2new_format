Ingredients: 4  small zucchini 
2 tsp Cajun or creole seasoning 
2 tbsp butter
1/4 tsp salt 
1/2 tsp dried thyme leaves 
2  medium sweet potatoes or yams 
1 1/2 cup frozen small whole onions 
1 lbs pork tenderloin 

Cooking Instructions: 

Heat oven to 425F. Rub Cajun seasoning into pork. Place in ungreased jelly roll pan. Insert meat thermometer horizontally into center of thickest part of pork.
Cut sweet potatoes and zucchini lengthwise into halves. Place sweet potatoes, zucchini and onions around pork. Drizzle butter over vegetables. Sprinkle with thyme and salt.
Roast uncovered about 35 minutes or until thermometer reads 160F. Loosely cover pan with aluminum foil and let stand 10 minutes.
Cut pork into thin slices. Serve with vegetables.
