                            COCONUT CREAM PIE

Recipe By     : unknown
Serving Size  : 6    Preparation Time :0:00
Categories    : Pies & Pastries

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
   2      cups          milk
     1/2  cup           granulated sugar
   2      tablespoons   flour
   2      large         eggs,separated
   1      tablespoon    butter
     1/2  teaspoon      salt
   1 1/4  cups          shredded coconut
   1      teaspoon      vanilla

Scald 1-1/2 cups milk in top of a double boiler.Combine
sugar,flour,cornstarch & salt.Stir in remaining 1/2 cup milk & egg
yolks.Stir this flour mixture into hot milk,cook until thickened.Remove from
heat.Add butter,vanilla & 3/4 cup coconut.Allow mixture to cool,then pour
into baked 9 inch pie shell.Cover with meringue & remaining coconut.Bake at
350 degrees 10-12 minutes,or until meringue browns lightly.
