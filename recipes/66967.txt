Makes 2 servings
 
2 large fresh plump heads of garlic 
 
Preheat oven to 375 deg. F. 
Cut a 1/4-inch slice from the top of each head of garlic. Discard any loose, papery skins. Wrap each head of garlic in foil. 
Bake for 30 to 40 minutes, until very soft. Cool slightly. 
Squeeze cloves out of the skin directly onto crackers or grilled bread and spread with a knife. 
Do not freeze
