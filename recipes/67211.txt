 - Contributed by Mary Lou Bastian
Sift and set aside:
3 cups flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt

4 egg whites - beat stiff and set aside

Cream together:
1 cup shortening
2 cups sugar
Add whites to sugar/shortening

Beat and add:
4 egg yolks

add: 2/3 cup milk
2 teaspoons vanilla
Add dry ingredients and 3 cups blueberries

Mix all. Pour into greased 13"x9" pan. Sprinkle with sugar. Bake 60 min. at 350 degrees.
