6 cups chicken broth   
8 celery stalks
2 cups diced onion 
1 tsp garlic salt
1/2 tsp white pepper   
2 lbs Velveeta    
1 cup diced jalapenos
Sour cream
Flour tortillas 

Dice celery, onions and jalapenos. Cut Velveeta up.
In a large pan put chicken broth, celery, onions, garlic salt, and white pepper. Cook over high heat for 10 minutes, or til reduced and thickened slightly.  Add Velveeta and simmer for 5 minutes.  Add the diced peppers and mix well.  Serve with sour cream and warm tortillas.
