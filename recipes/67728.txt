1and1/2 pounds beef tips cut into bite size pieces
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
2 cans of water
2 packs of instant onion soup mix


Lightly brown beef tips until moisture has evaporated. Mix in bowl soup,water,onion soup mix. Add browned beef tips. Put in casserole dish, cover, bake at 350 for approx. 2 hours. Serve over either cooked rice or cooked noodles.

Serves 6
