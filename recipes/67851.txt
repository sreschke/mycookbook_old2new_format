8 cups diced peeled potatoes
1/3 cup chopped onion
3 cans chicken broth
can cream of chicken soup
1/4 tsp pepper
8 oz pkg cream cheese, cubed
1/2 bound sliced bacon, ciiked an crumbled (optional)

In a slow cooker, combine the first five ingredients.  Cover and cook on low for 8-10 hours or until potatoes are tender.  Add cream chese, stir unti blended.  Garnish with bacon and chives, if desired.
