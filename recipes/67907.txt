
Monday Ham Balls 
serves 6 
3 cups ground cooked ham 
1/2 cups dry bread crumbs, finely crumbled 
1 egg 
1/4 cup oil 
1 - 14 ounce can pineapple chunks 
3 tablespoons soy sauce 
pinch ginger 
1 tablespoon brown sugar 
2 tablespoons cornstarch 
1/4 cup vinegar 
2 large green bell peppers, cut into 1/2 inch chunks 
1 large onion, cut into 1/2 inch chunks 
Mix ground ham with bread crumbs and egg. Shape into 12 large ball. Heat oil in heavy skillet. When hot, brown balls,
turning to cook evenly. When nicely browned, remove with slotted spoon and set aside. Drain syrup from pineapple, reserve
juice. To the juice add the soy sauce, ginger, sugar and enough water to make 4 cups of liquid. Stir this into the drippings left
from the ham balls. Cook and stir, briefly, cover skillet and let simmer 15 minutes. Mix cornstarch with vinegar. Stir into the
simmering liquid and cook until clear and thickened. Add ham balls, drained pineapple chunks, peppers and onions. Cook 5
minutes. Serve over rice.
