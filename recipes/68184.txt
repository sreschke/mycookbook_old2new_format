1/2 Tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
1 pound red snapper fillets
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 lemon
1/8 cup parsley
1 tbsp. dry breadcrumbs
1 clove garlic
1/3 cup almonds
1/2 tsp. paprika
1/8 cup olive oil (2 tbsps.)
1/4 cup tomato sauce
1/4 cup white wine

Brush bottom of a rectangular glass baking dish with the olive oil.  Lay fish fillets in the dish to coat, and flip them to coat the other side.  Sprinkle both sides of fish with salt and pepper.  Fit into dish, folding under the thin end of the fish.  Cover wirth lemon slices.  Mix together and pat on top the following:  parsley, bread crumbs, garlic, allmonds, paprika and olive oil.  When patting onto fish, leave a margin of 1 inch all around the edge.  Mix together tomato sauce and wine and pour over fish.  (You can cover and refrigerate  overnight at this point.  Return to room temperature before baking.) Bake at 425 * uncovered for 15 minutes.  Do not baste.
