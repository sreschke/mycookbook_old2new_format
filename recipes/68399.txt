
1 cup shortening
1 cup white sugar
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 cups sifted flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 cup oatmeal
1 cup cornflakes, crushed
1 cup chopped walnuts

Cream shortening and sugars together; add sugar gradually and cream
thoroughly.  Blend in well beaten eggs and add vanilla.  Sift flour,
baking soda and salt together and add to creamed mixture.  Stir in
oatmeal, cornflakes and nuts.  Shape into a long roll about 2 1/2
inches in diameter.  Chill overnight.  Slice and bake for 10 minutes
at 350* F.  Makes 3 dozen cookies.
