1 loaf round shepherd's bread
2 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
3 (6 1/2 oz.) can chopped clams, drained, reserving 1/4 cup liquid
3 Tbs. grated onion
1 Tbs. beer
2 Tbs. Worcestershire sauce
2 Tbs. lemon juice
1 tsp. hot pepper sauce
1/4 tsp. salt
Raw vegetables or crackers for dipping

With sharp knife, cut top from bread; set aside.  Hollow out loaf, leaving 1 1/2 to 2 inch thick shell.  Cut removed bread into cubes nad toast in oven last 5 minutes of baking time.

In large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth; stir in remaining ingredients except vegetables and crackers.  Blend well.  On baking sheet, make a large cross with two sheets of aluminum foil to cover loaf.  Center bread shell on foil.  Pour clam mixture into shell; replace top; cover with foil.  Bake in preheated over 250 degress for 3 hours.  Remove top.  Serve loaf surrounded by the bread cubes and raw vegetables for dipping or for spreading on crackers.
