8 slices white bread
4 oz cream cheese
2 Tbs. orange maramalade
1 egg
milk
vanilla

Mix cream cheese and orange marmalade together.  Take one slice of bread.  Spread some cream cheese with marmalade on it and top with other slice of bread.  Repeat this with the remaining slices of bread.  Slice each sandwich in half.

Mix one egg and mil together adding a dash of vanilla.  Dip one half of sandwich in egg mixture and fry in a little oil.  Turn and brown on other side.  Repeat this until all the halves are cooked.

Serve with maple syrup or a syrup of your choice.

Serves 4
