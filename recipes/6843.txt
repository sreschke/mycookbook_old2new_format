Buttermilk Pancakes
Ingredients: 

2 cups Bisquick (OK so it's a store bought mix!) 
1 cup buttermilk 
2 eggs 
1/4 tsp. vanilla extract 

Directions:
Mix all ingredients together with a whisk. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto hot griddle. Cook until bubbles appear and turn over. Cook until golden. 
Yields 15 pancakes. 
Georgia Pecan Pancakes
Ingredients: 

2 cups flour 
3 tablespoons sugar 
4 teaspoons baking powder 
1/2 teaspoon salt 
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon 
2 eggs 
1 1/2 cup milk 
1 teaspoon vanilla 
1/2 cup chopped pecans 
Directions:
Mix the dry ingredients in a large bowl. Combine eggs, milk and vanilla in a smaller bowl and add to dry ingredients. Stir in pecans. Drop batter by the spoonful onto a well greased griddle and turn when bubbles appear. Garnish with chopped pecans and pecan flavored syrup. 
Serves 6.

1 cup all-purpose flour 
1 Tbs. sugar 
1 1/4 cups whole milk 
2 large eggs 
1/4 tsp. salt 
1 Tbs. unsalted butter 
Confectioner's sugar 
Slices of fruit or berries
