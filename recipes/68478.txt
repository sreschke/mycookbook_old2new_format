1     6-8 lb beef roast rump
1     cup vegetable oil
3/4   cup soy sauce
1/2   cup lemon juice
1/4   cup Worchester sauce
30    garlic cloves, cut in half
1 tbl ground pepper
Garnish: Cherry tomatoes

Combine oil and ingrediants, pour
over roast, seal and chill 24-48 
hours. Remove roast, discard 
marinade, sprinkle with pepper and 
bake at 350 deg for 1 1/2 hoours or
until thermoter registers 145 deg
rare - 160 deg medium.
