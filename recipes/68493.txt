1 pound Chinese radish (sliced)
1 1/2 quarts water 
3 small cloves garlic (minced)
1 tsp. sugar 
1 dried red pepper 
1/2 tbsp. salt 
2 scallions (sliced) 

In a large jar or large container, combine all ingredients cover, and let sit at room temperature for 2 days. Serve cold. The kimchi may be stored, chilled, for up to 1 week.
