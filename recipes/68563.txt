3 tbsps. fresh lemon juice
4 tbsps Tamari
2 tbsps tomato paste
4 tbsps sesame oil
1 tsp chopped garlic
1 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp salt
pepper to taste
 4 8 0z fish steaks @ 1 inch thick

 Combine marinade ingredients.  Turn fish steaks in marinade to coat evenly.  Turn every so often and marinate 2 hours at room temp.
Preheat broiler.  Place steaks on broiler pan and broil 5 minutes a side basting with the marinade 2 or 3 times till done.
