5 tomatillos raw diced or 1/2 can green salsa if you cannot find tomatillos
1 C diced sweet onion
1/2 jalapeno, chopped
1 1/2 T cilantro
1 T tequila
1 T lime juice
1 T sugar

Mix together and let stand for 1 hour
Serve with tortilla chips or a side dish for chicken or other meats
Adjust the hotness by using more or less jalapeno
