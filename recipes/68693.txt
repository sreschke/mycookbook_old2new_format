
Double-Rich Oyster stew
by: Chef Bubba/Cookbook Author

Ingredients:

1 quart of heavy whipping cream
1 pint of oysters
2 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce
3 Tbsp butter
Â« tsp salt

Directions:

Drain the oysters and save the liquid. Place the drained oysters in a
2-quart or larger pot. Add the Worcestershire sauce and salt. Cook the
oysters over medium heat until the edges start to curl up. Add the
butter, cream, and reserved oyster liquid. Bring the pot to a simmer
(do not boil) and allow to simmer for about 10 minutes. Serve hot with
oyster crackers, croutons, or saltines.
