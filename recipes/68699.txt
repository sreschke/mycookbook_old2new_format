3 cups cooked rice (any)
5 slices bacon (cooked and diced)
1 cup onion (diced)
2 bullion cubes (melt in cup of hot water)
1garlic clove
2 cups celery (diced)
1 cup green pepper (chopped)
2/3 cup mayannase
1 Tablespoon lemon juice
2 medium tomatoes (cut small)
1 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon tobasco sauce

      Fry bacon and onion until bacon is tender and onion is crisp.Cook rice. Add all vegetables to rice except onion and bacon.Mix rice, vegetables,mayannase,lemon juice,bullion,salt and tobasco sauce.Then add bacon and onions plus 
drippings. Bake in large baking dish at 350 for 25-30 minutes.
