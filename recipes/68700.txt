1 lb. 	Large shrimp (peeled and de-veined)
1/4 C	Shallots (chopped)
1/4 C	Green onions (chopped)
2 lg.	Garlic cloves (smashed and chopped finely)
1/4 lb	Butter (1stick)
1/4 C	Parsley (chopped)

Melt butter in pan. Saute shallots, onions, parsley and garlic. Add shrimp and cook only until shrimp are bright pink and curl. Watch out for overcooking, the shrimp will becme mushy.) Salt and pepper. Server over over pasta or on a bed of lettuce.
