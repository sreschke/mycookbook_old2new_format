2 lbs. lean ground beef
1 Tlbs. oil
1 large onion, chopped
1-2 cloves garlic, crushed
oregano, basil, salt & pepper, to taste
1 4 oz. can mushrooms
16 oz. pkg. large pasta shells
8 oz. shredded cheddar cheese
8 oz. shredded mozzerella cheese
1 32 oz. jar spaghetti sauce

Saute onion, and garlic in oil until clear. Add beef and cook until no longer pink. Drain. Meanwhile, cook shells as directed, drain, and set aside. Add mushrooms, seasonings, and cheeses to beef. Mix well. In 13x9 pan, spread a thin layer of sauce in the bottom. With a spoon, fill pasta with meat mixture, and place in pan. Pour remaining sauce over shells, and bake covered (with foil) at 350 for 30-45 mins. Serves 8-10.
