      1  can of Cream of Chicken
1 1/2  can of water
      1  can of Tuna
1 1/2  cup of Maccaroni Noodles
          Desired seasoning
          Celery

-Pour Cream of Chicken and water into frying pan
-Mix water with soup on HI at first, then turn down to MED when start to boil
-Drain water off of tuna and pour into pan and break up the tuna
-Season with:  pepper, salt, parsley, celery, oregano, seasoning salt, spices
-Add noodles
-Cover and ready when noodles are done
-15 - 20 minutes
