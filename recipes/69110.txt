9 inch unbaked Pie shell
2 eggs slightly beaten
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 tbs. flour
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon ginger
2 cups cooked fresh pumpkin(instructions will follow)
14 ounce can evaporated mil
whipped cream (for topping if desired) yummy

To cook pumpkin:
Select bright colored medium/small size pumpkins.
Three pounds raw pumpkin makes 3 cups cooked pumpkin
approximately.
Cut pumpkin in half remove seeds and all stringy portion. Cut into small pieces and cut peel off.
Place in about 1 inch boiling slightly salted water
and cover and cook for approx. 25 to 30 minutes, until nice and tender. Drain Well, and then mash, drain excess as needed.
Now your ready to add to your recipe.

Preheat oven 450 degrees.
Combine eggs, sugars, flour, cinnamon, salt, nutmeg and ginger.Mix well. Blend in pumpkin, and gradually add evaporated milk. Mix well, mixture will be runny.

Turn into an unbaked 9 inch pie shell. Bake at 450 degrees for 10 minutes, then bake at 350 degrees for 40 to 50 minutes, until a knife inserted about half way between center and outside of filling comes out clean.
To keep crust from over cooking, wrap strips of aluminum foil around crust about 20 minutes into baking.
Enjoy!!!!!! Serve warm with whipped topping! yummy
