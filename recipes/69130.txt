1-1/2# boneless chicken, cooked
1/2 cup ch. onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 tsp. chili powder
1 tsp. cumin

4 tbsp. margarine
4 tbsp. flour

3 cups skim milk
l/2 tsp. pepper
2 tsp. lemon juice

2 cups low-fat monterey jack cheese
16 tortillas
1 sm. can ch. chili peppers (diced)
8 tbsp. cheese (for tortillas)

Preheat oven 350 degrees.  Cut chicken into 1/4" cubes.  Mix next 4 ingred. with chicken.  Heat tortillas until soft.  Put 1/4 cup chicken mix on each tortilla and top with 1 tbsp. cheese.  Roll tortillas up and set in baking dish seam side down.

Over med. heat melt butter and add flour.  Cook 2 min. Whisk in milk, pepper and lemon juice.  Bring to boil.  Add cheese and stir until cheese is melted, pour over tortillas.  Top with peppers (optional).  Bake 350 degrees 20 to 25 minutes.

Serves 8 persons (2 per person)
