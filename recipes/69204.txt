2   pounds broccoli cut into small flowerets
1   pound bacon , fried crisp, crumbled
1   cup craisons
1   cup cashews
1   small red onion finely chopped
1   cup mayonaise  (I use Miracle Whip)
2   tablespoons sugar
2   tablespoons white vinegar

Combine first five ingredients in a large bowl.

In a small bowl mix mayo,sugar and vinegar, blend well.  Toss broccoli mixture with dressing, cover and refrigerate for approximately 1 hour before serving
