This is a treat for grown-ups as well as kids. Before whipping the cream, place bowl and beater in the freezer to get cold.

1 pint fresh ripe strawberries
2 tablespoons granulated sugar
1 cup heavy cream 
2 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 package of 6 dessert shells or Mary Jane cakes

Slice 6 large strawberries and set them aside.  Slice remaining berries into medium-size bowl; stir in 2 tablespoons sugar and 1 tablespoon water. 
Let stand 10 minutes until berries are syrupy. Beat cream, confectioners' sugar and vanilla in a medium size bowl with mixer at high speed until
stiff peaks form; set aside.  Drain berries, reserving syrup.  Brush dessert shells with reserved syrup. Spoon on sugared berries. Swirl on
whipped cream. Sprinkle on reserved sliced berries. Repeat with remaining ingredients. Refrigerate up to 2 hours.  Makes 6 servings.
