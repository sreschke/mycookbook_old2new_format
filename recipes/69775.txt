Here's an easy way to make homemade applesauce. It will make your house smell absolutely wonderful--and you can control the amount of sugar and cinnamon you add. If you prefer your applesauce smooth rather than chunky, place all the ingredients in a food processor or blender and blend to desired texture before placing them in the 
crackpot. Serves eight. 

8-10 large cooking apples, peeled, cored, and       chopped in pieces of desired size 
1/2 cup water 
1/2 cup (or less) sugar 
1 teaspoon cinnamon, or to taste 

Combine all ingredients in large crockpot. Cover; cook on low setting eight hours.
