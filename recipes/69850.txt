2 EGGS
1 CUP MILK
1 CUP FLOUR
SALT AND PEPPER TO TASTE

MIX ABOVE INGREDIENTS TOGETHER AND BEAT WELL.  POUR ABOUT 1 TSP OIL OR BACON FAT INTO GREASED MUFFIN PANS AND PUT PAN IN 400 DEGREE OVEN UNTIL SMOKING HOT.
POUR EGG MIXTURE INTO HOT MUFFIN MOLDS AND PUT BACK IN HOT OVEN FOR 10-15 MINS UNTIL PUFFY AND GOLDEN
