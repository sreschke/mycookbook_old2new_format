9 c. boiling water
4 tea bags
2 c. sugar
12oz. frozen orange juice
12oz. frozen lemonad
2 c. brandy

Put the boiling water in large bowl. Steep tea bags in water for a few minutes. Add rest of ingredients & mix well. Put in freezer until frozen. Fill glass about half way with slush & add either 7 up or 50/50.
