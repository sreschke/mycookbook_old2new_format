10 medium apples, peeled and sliced
1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
2 T flour
1/2 cup milk
1/2 cup water
Mix all the above. Place in 9X13 pan
TOPPING:                                            1 1/3 cups flour
1 cup Quick Oats
1/2 cup sugar
1 t salt
2 t cinnamon
2 packages cook&serve Butterscotch Pudding,not inst.   
1/2 cup margarine, melted
Mix all the above and sprinkle over apple mixture
Bake at 350 degrees 45 to 50 minutes
