12 (6-inch) flour tortillas
2 boneless chicken breasts, cut into bite size pieces
1 pound Monterey Jack Cheese, shredded
1 pound Mild Sharp Cheese, shredded
1 large onion, diced
1 pound Bacon, cooked
6 Tablespoons Olive Oil (I use hot and spicy veg. oil)
6 Tablespoons lime juice
6 cloves garlic, minced
2 teaspoons Cilantro
1/2 teaspoon salt

Combine Olive oil, lime juice, garlic, cilantro and salt. Cut chicken into bite size pieces.
Mix together chicken and olive oil, lime juice, garlic, cilantro and salt. Set aside. Fry
bacon. In skillet, cook chicken mixture till chicken is done. Take one tortilla at a time, put
both cheeses in middle, onion, crumble 1 peice of bacon, and a spoonful of chicken
mixture. Fold tortilla in half. Cook on grill or skillet till cheese melts.

For dipping sauces use Salsa, Sour Cream or Ranch Dressing.
