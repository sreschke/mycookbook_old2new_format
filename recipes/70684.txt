  This is a very easy to make recipe. You pretty much just dump in all the
  ingredients, mix and bake. 

                    Preparation Time: 0
                    Oven temp: 350
                  
                   
  Ingredients
  2 cup(s) flour
  3/4 cup(s) sugar 
  2 tsp baking powder 
  1 tsp salt
  1/2 tsp baking soda 
  1/2 tsp cinnamon
  1 cup(s) nuts or raisins or both (
  I use both ) 
  1 egg (beaten) 
  1 cup(s) med. thick apple sauce 
  2 tbsp oil
  Preparation Steps
  1) mix all ingredients well
  2) Bake in greased loaf pan at 350 for 1 hour.
