2 tablespoons unsalted butter
1/2 cup medium or short grain white rice
4 cups milk
Pinch salt
Scant 1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla

Preheat oven to 300 degrees.  Heat butter in a 4 quart saucepan.  When frothy add rice and cook for a minute.  Slowly add milk, pinch salt, sugar, vanilla, and bring to a boil.

Transfer to a 9 inch square baking pan and set in oven.

Bake uncovered for 1 1/2 hours stirring every 15 minutes.  Then bake undisturbed for about 2 more hours, until very thick and creamy.  

VARIATIONS:

You can add  1/2 cup of heavy cream in the last half hour of baking.  

Or if you want a crusty topping, reduce sugar to 1/4 cup.  Once out of oven, crumble 1/2 cup of dark brown sugar mixed with 2 tablespoons of butter over rice  pudding and put under the broiler until melted and bubbly. 

Yield: 6 servings  It is so good I double the recipe.
