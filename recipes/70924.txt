This is a quick dish to prepare when you are not prepared for anything else.
 
Makes 4 servings
 
2 teaspoons paprika 
1/4 cup oil 
2 cans red beans -- 15 oz. cans 
2 cloves garlic - cut into slithers 
1 teaspoon cumin 
1/2 teaspoon turmeric 
8 large eggs 
1/2 lemon - peeled and sectioned 
2 tablespoons lemon juice 
water 
 
Mix paprika with oil and let stand.
Meanwhile, drain and rinse red beans. Place in a 4 quart pot. Add sliced garlic, cumin, and turmeric and lemon sections. Stir oil and paprika mixture and add to beans along with lemon juice. Add water to the top of beans and bring to a boil, reduce to a medium and cook for 5 minutes. 
Add eggs one at a time on top of beans, each in its 
separate location and continue cooking until eggs are thoroughly poached and an unctuous sauce remains, about 15 minutes.
