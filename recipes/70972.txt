SALISBURY STEAK
1 (10.5 OZ) can condensed French Onion Soup
1-1/2 lb ground beef
1/2 c bread crumbs
1 egg (lightly beaten)
pepper
1 can Beef gravy
1 can mushrooms

Mix 1/3 cup of the Onion Soup with the ground beef, bread crumbs,egg, and pepper.  Shape into 6 patties.
Cook in large skillet.  Cover with gravy, and mushrooms.   
Cook covered 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
