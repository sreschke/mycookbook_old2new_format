 10 large egg whites, rm. temp.
 1 c. sugar
 1 tsp.cream of tartar
 1 c. all-purpose flour
 1/4 tsp.salt
 1 tsp.pure vanilla extract
     Preheat oven to 375*. Using electic mixer, medium speed, beat whites w/ ceam of tartar & salt until soft peaks form. About 2 mins. Add sugar and vanilla & beat until stiff peaks form, about 2 mins. Add flour & beat for 2 mins, scraping down sides.  Pour batter into pan, bake until golden brown. (30-35mins.) Remove from oven turn upside down on the neck of a glass bottle. cool completely
