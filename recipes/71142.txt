1/2 cup sugar
1/3 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 tea. ground cinnamon, if desired
4 cups blueberries
1 Tab. lemon juice
2 tab. butter or margarine

Heat Oven to 425.  Prepare double crust
Mix sugar, flour and cinnamon.  Stire in blueberries.  Turn into pastry-lined pie plate.  Sprinkle with leomon juice and dot with Butter.  Trim crust.
Bake 34 to 45 minutes or until crust is brown and juice begins to bubble through slits in crust.  Serve warm.
