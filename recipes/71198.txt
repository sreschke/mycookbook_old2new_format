1/2c butter
1c sugar
2 eggs
1c sour cream
2c sifted flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
1 tsp baking pwdr
1 tsp baking soda

Cream butter till light & fluffy.  Add eggs, one @ a time, beating well after each.  Sift flour, soda, powder & salt.  Add dry ingredients alternately with sour cream, starting & ending w/flour.  Stir in vanilla.  Pour 1/2 batter in greased 9 x 9 pan.  sprinkle with 1/2 of sugar topping & add rest of batter, top w/remaining sugar topping.  Bake @ 325 for 40 minutes.

SUGAR TOPPING
1/2 c brown sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 c white sugar
