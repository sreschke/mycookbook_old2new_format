1/2 c. margarine
1/3 c. granulated sugar
1/3 c. brown sugar
2 Tbls. water
1 c. sifted flour
1 1/4 tsp. baking powder
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1/2 c. chopped nuts
1/2 c. coconut
1 c. (6 oz.) semi-sweet chocolate chips

Oven 350*.  Grease 9" square pan.

Melt margarine in 2-qt saucepan.  Remove from heat.  Mix in sugars and water.  

In separate small bowl, mix flour and baking powder. Add to margarine mixture.  Add egg and vanilla, beating well.  Add nuts, coconut and chocolate chips, stirring lightly to just combine.  Spread into pan and bake for 25-30 minutes.  Do not overbake.  Cool in pan, then cut into bars.
