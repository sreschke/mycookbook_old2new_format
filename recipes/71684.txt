2 Pkg. yeast soaked in a little hot water in cup
1 Cup Sweet Milk
1 Cup Whipping Cream (1 carton doesn`t quite make one cup so add sweet milk to make it come out to 1 cup.
1 Cup Sugar
1 Tsp. Salt
2 Eggs
1 Cup Raisins

Beat eggs, add salt, sugar, and butter real good. Add rasins, milk, cream and yeast. Than stir enough flour in to make a soft dough. Let raise till double .(Put a cloth over top of pan so dough don`t get hard.) Than roll out and cut into pieces and cut a slit in the middle. Let them raise again some. Have covered over with plastic or cloth. When risen enough fry in deep fat in skillet(Hot Lard)
