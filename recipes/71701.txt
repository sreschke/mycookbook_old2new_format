Crab Or Lobster Imperial

2 lb backfin crab meat or lobster
4 oz butter
1/2 cup finely chopped green pepper
1/2 cup finely chopped red pepper
1 cup finely chopped onion
4 Tbsp flour
3 cups half and half 
1 tsp salt
1/2 cup finely chopped parsley
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
Paprika

Clean crab meat of shell and set aside. Allow to warm to room temperature. 
Melt butter but do not allow to brown. Saute onions, green pepper and red 
pepper, about 2 minutes, on low heat. Add flour and then half and half, 
blending with a whisk until smooth. Cook slowly until sauce becomes medium 
thick. Remove from heat and add all remaining ingredients except crab and 
paprika. Fold in the crab gently and fill imperial dishes with mixture. Dust 
with paprika. Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven about 15 to 20 minutes. 
Yield: 10 servings.This freezes well. Freeze after baking. To serve, defrost 
and reheat in the oven or microwave.
