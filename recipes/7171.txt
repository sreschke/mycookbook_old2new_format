8 chicken pieces
cornflour
1 cup oil
2 onions, finely chopped
3 cloves garlic, crushed
1 tbls chilli sauce
1 tsp grated ginger
1 tsp chilli powder
2 tsp cornflour, extra
1/2 cup chicken stock
1/4 cup dry sherry
1 tbls soy sauce
1 tbls vinegar
2 shallots (green onions) sliced
1 red chilli, seeded and sliced

1.  Dust chicken in cornflour, shaking off excess.  Heat oil in wok.  Fry a few pieces of chicken at a time for 5 mins or until golden and cooked through.  Drain on crumpled kitchen paper.
2.  Drain off all except 2 tbls oil.  Stirfry onion, garlic, chilli sauce, ginger and chilli powder for 2mins.  Add chicken.  Stirfry for 3mins.
3.  Blend cornflour with stock, sherry, soy and vinegar until smooth.  Add to wok.  Stirfry until sauce boils and thickens.
4.  Simmer, stirring, for 3mins.  Mix through shallots and sliced chilli.  Served with rice or boiled egg noodles.
Serves 4.
