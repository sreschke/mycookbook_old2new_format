Yield: 6 serving

1 	lb		Crab Meat Fresh
1	med.	Green pepper, fiely chopped
1/2	teas.	Celery seed or chopped celery
1/2	C		Mayonnaise
1	T		Mustard
1	t		Seafood seasoning
1	t		Vinegar
1	t		Sugar
Dash tobasco
Carnishes:  parley, tomato chilled.

Remove any shell or cartilage from crab meat. Combine all ingredients; mix well.  Shape mixture in a mound on serving dish: garnish with parsley and tomato and serve
