2 c.   wild, brown or white rice
1 pkg. Lipton onion soup mix
1 can  cream of mushroom soup
2 lbs. chicken thighs, breasts, or legs (remove all skin)

1.  Place all of the rice in the bottom of a casserole dish.
2.  Place chicken pieces on top.
3.  Sprinkle onion soup mix over the chicken.
4.  Spoon the mushroom soup on next.
5.  Pour water over the soup (I fill the empty soup can 2X)
6.  Cover the dinner with either a lid, or with foil.
7.  Cook at 375 F and DON'T PEEK for 2 hours!

This will serve a large family.
Serve with cooked broccoli or with a big salad.  Our kids love this dinner.
