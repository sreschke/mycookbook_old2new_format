1/2 cup butter
1 cup flour
11 cups water
3 chicken bouillon cubes
2 lbs boneless chicken breast, cut into 1/2 inch chunks
2 bunches broccoli (trimmed and chopped)
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 cup light cream
3 cups cheddar cheese

In a 5 qt pot milt butter over medium heat. Add flour, stirring constantly until aq thick paste forms. Remove from pot and set aside. In same pot, combine water, bouillon cubes, chicken, broccoli, salt, and pepper. Bring to boil oever high heat. Reduce heat to medium low and simmer for 45 minutes or until broccoli is tender. Stir in flour a little at a time until soup thickens. Simmer 5 minutes. Reduce heat. Stir in cream. Mix well. Add cheese 1 cup at a time.
