6-8 lean pork chops, about 3/4" thick
1/2 c. flour*
2 tsp. salt*
1 1/2 tsp. dry mustard*
1/2-1 tsp. garlic powder*
2 Tbls. oil
1 (10.5 oz.) can chicken & rice soup, undiluted

*If you want to mix these dry ingredients and coat chops before browning, you can, OR just brown chops without coating.

Place chops in lightly greased crock-pot and pour soup over.  Cook on low 6-8 hours or high 3-4 hours.

Really good served with mashed potatoes!
