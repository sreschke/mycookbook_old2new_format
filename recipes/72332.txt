Dressing; (good on any fresh greens, but not tomato!) Mix all and reserve.
1/4 cup white vinegar      
2-3 tbsp oyster sauce
2-3 tbsp hoisin sauce
1 tbsp honey
1 large clove garlic, minced
1/2 teasp red pepper flakes

Salad;
3 boneless, skinless chicken breast pcs. (1/2 breast ea.), poached, cooled & slivered
1 large bell pepper, julienned
1/2 c. very thin-sliced celery
(optional)1/2 c. thin-sliced fresh mushrooms
1 c. unsalted peanuts, crushed
1/2 c. fresh parsley, rough-chopped
4-6 fresh mint leaves (important ingtredient), finely chopped

2-3 c. bean sprouts, washed & drained

Combine all ingredients -except bean sprouts. Chill. Not more than 1 hour before serving, toss with sauce. Just before serving add bean sprouts.
