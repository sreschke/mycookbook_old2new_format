Grease 9" x 13" pan
1st Layer:  8 slices bread, cubed
2nd Layer:  2 c. sharp cheese, grated
3rd Layer:  1 lb. bacon, browned and crumbled
      OR    1 lb. sausage, cooked
      OR    1 lb. ham, cut into small cubes
Mix:  4 eggs
      2 c. milk
      3/4 tsp. prepared mustard
      Salt and Pepper
Beat slightly and pour over the 3 layers.  Refrigerate overnight.

Next Day:
Mix i can cream of mushroom soup and 1 1/2 cup milk.  Pour over all and bake uncovered 1 1/2 hours at 300 degrees.
