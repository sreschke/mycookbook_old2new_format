1 cup sugar
1/2 cup shortening
1 beaten egg
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 cup chopped nuts
1 tsp ground cloves
3/4 cup cold strong coffee
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup raisins
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour

Cream shortening and sugar. Add egg and mix well. Sift flour with baking soda, salt, baking powder and spices.
Add to sugar mixture alternately with coffee. Add additional flour if needed to make a drop cookie dough.
Drop onto cookie sheet. Bake at 400 15 minutes or just until brown around edges.
