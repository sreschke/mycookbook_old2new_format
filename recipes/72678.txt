1	lb	Fresh Crab Meat
1/2 C	Chopped Celery
1	T	Grated onion
1	T	Pimento, minced
1/4	t	Salt
2	T	Lemon juice
Mayonnaise to taste

Combine crab, celery, onion and pimento. Sprinkle with salt and lemon juice. Add mayonnaise. Just enough to moisten. Mix well. Serve on bed of lettuce leaves, sprinkle with minced parsley. Serve with additional mayonnaise.
