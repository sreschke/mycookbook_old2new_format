SHRIMP FETTUCCINI

1 T. plus 1 t. margarine
1 c. sliced mushrooms
1 garlic clove, minced
1 c. shrimp, peeled, deveined
1 egg
1/4 c.  Ricotta cheese
1/2 c. cream
2 oz. Grated Parmesan cheese
Dash each salt & pepper
2 c. cooked fettuccine (hot)
2 T. chopped fresh parsley

In 12" skillet, heat margarine until bubbly and hot; add mushrooms and
garlic; saute briefly, about 2 min.  Stir in shrimp, cook until pink;
set aside.  In small bowl, combine egg and ricotta cheese; stir to
combine.  Sprinkle with salt and pepper; add to skillet, stirring
constantly.  Bring to a slow simmer; cook for about 2 min.  Add
fettuccine, toss to combine; serve sprinkled with parsley.
