One bite of these luscious and lemony bars and your sweet tooth will be satisfied - without the guilt!
 
Makes 9 servings
 
2 medium lemons 
1 cup flour (all-purpose) 
1/4 cup confectioners sugar 
1/3 cup margarine - used reduced-calorie stick margarine 
such as Promise Buttery Light, divided (5 Tbs) 
1 cup sugar 
1/2 cup egg substitute 
 
Preheat oven to 350 deg. F. 
From lemons, grate 1 teaspoon zest and squeeze 1/4 cup juice. 
Coat 9" square baking pan with cooking spray. 
In a bowl with bastry blender combine flour, 1/4 cup confectioners sugar, margarine and zest. Press mixture into pan. 
Bake 18 minutes. Cool on a rack. 
Combine granulated sugar, egg substitute and lemon juice. Pour over crust; bake 25 minutes.
