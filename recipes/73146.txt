2 each 7-8oz. Halibut filet
flour to dredge
2 tbsp. chopped macadamia nuts
2 tbsp. fresh chopped parsley
4-5oz. frangelico
4-5oz. whole butter
1 banana (1/4 lengthwise)

1.  lightly flour the halibut filet and saute in a little butter until golden brown.
2.  remove fish and in the same pan add nuts, banana, & parsley.
3.  flame with franjelico and add the whole butter and blend, spoon over fish.
