This recipe serves 2 people

1/2 lb ground beef     1/3 C. tomato sauce
1/2 tsp. salt                1/3 C. bread crumbs
1/8 tsp. pepper           2 tsp. onion flakes
1 egg                          1 T. brown sugar
1 tsp. parsley flakes     1/4 tsp. Worchestershire 
                                                   sauce

Preheat oven to 450 degrees.  Combine all ingredients and shape into 2 small loaves.  Place in shallow baking dish.  Bake for 20-30 minutes, or until desired doneness.
