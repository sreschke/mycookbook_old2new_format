GRILLED VIDALIA ONIONS:
               1 Vidalia onion, sliced
               crosswise 1/2-inch thick (do
               not separate into rings)
               Olive oil, for brushing
               Kosher salt and freshly
               ground black pepper

               Preheat a gas or charcoal grill to high. 

               Brush onions with olive oil on both sides and season with
               salt and pepper. Grill until golden brown, 3 to 4 minutes on
               each side.
