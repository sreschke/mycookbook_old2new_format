Lemon Snowflake Cookies 
    
1 box lemon cake mix (may substitute other flavors) 
2 cups Cool Whip 
1 egg 
powdered sugar for rolling

Mix together the first three ingredients. Roll the dough into 1-inch balls, and then coat with powdered sugar. Bake 10 min. at 350, until edges are golden and the cookies take on a snowflake appearance.
