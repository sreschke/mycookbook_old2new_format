
Peanut Better Cookies

1/2 cup of shortening
1/4 cup of peanut butter
1/4 cup of sugar
1/2 cup of brown sugar
2 eggs
4 tablespoons of powdered milk
1/2 cup of water
1/2 teaspoon of vanilla
1/2 teaspoon of almond extract
2 cups of flour
1 1/2 teaspoons of baking powder
3/4 cup of salted peanuts

Mix everything together thoroughly in a large bowl. 

Place each rounded teaspoonful 2 inches apart on 
a lightly sprayed or greased cookie sheet. 

Bake 15 to 20 minutes at 350 degrees.

Serving: 4 to 4 1/2 dozen
