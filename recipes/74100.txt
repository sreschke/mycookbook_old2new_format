INGREDIENTS
=======================================
1 Flaky Pie Crust (1 dough disk; see recipe)
2 large red-skinned sweet potatoes (yams; about 
  1 3/4 pounds)
3/4 cup packed dark brown sugar
5 large egg yolks
2 TB fresh lemon juice
1 ts vanilla extract
1 ts gound cinnamon
1/4 ts salt
1/4 ts gound nutmeg
1 cup whipping cream

sweetened whipped cream

DIRECTIONS
=======================================
Position rack in bottom third of oven and preheat to 375 F. Butter 10 inch diameter glass pie dish. Roll out dough on floured surface to 13-inch round. Transfer crust to pie disk. Trim edge to 1/2-inch overhang if necessary. Fold overhang under, forming edge. Crimp edge. Freeze 15 minutes.

Line pie crust with foil. Fill with dried beans or pie weights. Bake until edge of crust is pale golden, about 25 minutes. Remove foil and beans. Bake until bottom is pale golden, about 5 minutes. Cool on rack. Reduct oven temp to 325 F.

Meanwhile, pierce sweet potatoes with toothpick. Cook in microwave on high until tender, turning once, about 10 minutes. Cut open; cool. Scrape flesh into medium bowl; discard skins. Mash potatoes. Measure enough potatoes to equal 2 cups.

Combine 2 cups mashed sweet potatoes, brown sugar, yolks, lemon juice, vanilla, cinnamon, salt and nutmeg in processor; puree until smooth. Transfer to bowl; mix in 1 cup cream. Pour into crust.

Bake pie until filling is set in center and top is no longer shiny, about 50 minutes. Transfer to rack; cool completely. 

(can be prepared 1 day ahead. Refrigerate until cold, then cover and keep refrigerated)

Serve pie with whipped cream.
