 Potato Burgundy Stew


             Metric
                                           Ingredient
                                                                           Imperial
             25 ml
                                        Margarine or butter
                                                                             2 tbsp
             5
                                       Medium onions, sliced
                                                                                5
             1.5 L
                                         Sliced mushrooms
                                                                            6 cups
             1.5 Kg
                              Beef stew meat, cut into 1-inch (2.5 cm) cubes
                                                                              3 lbs
             2
                                     Cloves garlic, finely chopped
                                                                                2
             10 ml
                                              Salt
                                                                              2 tsp
             5 ml
                            Chopped fresh or 1/2 tsp (2 ml) dried marjoram leaves
                                                                              1 tsp
             5 ml
                             Chopped fresh or 1/2 tsp (2 ml) dried thyme leaves
                                                                              1 tsp
             1 ml
                                             Pepper
                                                                            1/4 tsp
             750 ml
                                            Beef broth
                                                                            3 cups
             45 ml
                                         All-purpose flour
                                                                             3 tbsp
             875 ml
                                          Red Burgundy
                                                                         3 - 1/2 cups
             4
                                     Medium red potatoes, diced
                                                                                4
             -
                                           French bread
                                                                                 -

             Directions
             Melt margarine in 3-quart (3 L) saucepan over medium heat. Cook onions and mushrooms in
             margarine about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until onions are tender. Remove vegetables
             from saucepan and reserve.

             Cook beef and garlic in same saucepan over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until beef is
             brown. Sprinkle with salt, marjoram, thyme and pepper.

             Mix broth and flour until smooth and pour over beef. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil
             and stir 1 minute.

             Stir in Burgundy. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer 1-1/2 to 2 hours, stirring in onions,
             mushrooms and potatoes 15 minutes before end of simmer time. Serve in bowls with bread for
             dipping into sauce.

             Yield: Serves 8.
