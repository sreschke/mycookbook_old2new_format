3 cups (1-1/2 12 oz. bags ) Hershey`s semi- sweet chocolate chips
1 (14 oz.) can sweetened condensed milk
dash salt
1 cup chopped walnuts
1-1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
line a 13 x 9 x 2 pan with foil, extend over short sides
set aside, In heavy saucepan over low heat, melt chips with condensed milk and salt, Remove from heat, stir in walnuts, and vanilla. Spread evenly into prepared pan. Refridgerate 2 hours or until firm. use foil to lift fudge out of pan, peel foil off when cool, cut with cookie cutters store tightly in covered bowl, in cool dry place
