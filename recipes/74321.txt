1/2 C butter
8 oz shredded sharp cheddar cheese
1 1/4 C flour
Olives  (green or black - whichever you desire)

Melt butter and cheese and mix with flour.  Roll olive into the middle of a small ball of the mixture.  Place balls on a pan and refrigerate for one hour.  Bake at 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes.
