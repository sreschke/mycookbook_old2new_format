2-20 oz. cans of pineapple chunks
3 cups christian bros. brandy,
on the Friday after Thanksgiving, pour pineapples and juice into 1 gallon jar, add brandy,
marinate for at least 4 weeks.
on Dec. 24th pour contents into large punch bowl, add 2 bottles cold duck, and 1- 2 litre bottle of 7-up, float slices of 1 orange on top, when serving, explain to guests they are to eat pineapples and "chase with juice", Please be advised this is a "sneaky pete", so please use caution when serving, I wouldn`t advise serving more than two, "God Bless and Happy Holidays"
