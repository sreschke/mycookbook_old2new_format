2- 10 oz. pkg. frozen broccoli spears
4 tbls. butter
6 tbls. flour
2 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup whipping cream
3 tbls. dry white wine
3 chicken breasts, halved and cooked
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese 
cook broccoli, drain, melt butter, blend in flour, 1/2 tsp. salt, and dash pepper, add chicken broth, stir till mixture thickens, stir in cream and wine, place broccoli crosswise in 12x 7 x 2 pan pour 1/2 the sauce over, top with chicken, to the remaining sauce, add cheese, pour over chicken, sprinkle with parmesan cheese, bake @ 350* 20 mins. then broil till golden brown about 5 mins.
