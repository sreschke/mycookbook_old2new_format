1 1/2 lb. browned and drained Jimmy Dean Regular sausage
3/4 cup chopped onion
1  dozen eggs
8 oz. grated cheddar cheese
1/2 pint whipping cream
1 small can mushrooms

Butter sides and bottom of 9X13 pan
Crack eggs in the pan.  Break yolks and add  salt and pepper.  Sprinkle in 1/2 of the cheese.  Pour cream over the cheese.

Sprinkle meat and mushrooms over that.  Add remaining cheese on top.  Refrigerate 10-12 hours or over night.  

Bake 40 min. at 350 degrees.
Serves 8-10 people.
