1 tbsp vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
1 green pepper, chopped
2 cloves garilic, minced
1 lb lean ground beef
1 can (19oz/540ml) kidney beans, drained
1 can tomato sauce (14oz/398ml)
1 tbsp chili powder
1/4 tsp pepper
2 cups elbow macaroni
1/2 cup light sour cream or thick plain yogurt

In skillet, heat oil over med heat; cook onion, green pepper, and garlic for 5 min.  Add beef; cook
over med-high heat, for 5 min. or until no longer
pink.  Spoon off fat.  Add beans, tomato sauce, chili powder, and pepper; cook for 3 min.
Meanwhile, in large pot of boiling water, cook macaroni until tender but firm; drain well and return to pot.  Add sauce, toss to coat well.  Serve topped with sour cream or yogurt.
Makes 4 servings
