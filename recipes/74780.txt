4 cups fresh cranberries
2 medium green apples, unpeeled
2 small oranges, unpeeled
1 small lime, unpeeled
1/2 cup pecans
1 3/4 cups sugar, or to taste
3 Tbsp Grand Marnier

Seed oranges and apples, cut everything into big chunks, run through food processor until all is very finely chopped. Refrigerate overnight.
