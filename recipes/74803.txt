Southwest Potato Corn Chowder 
 

Prep Time:  10 mins 
Total Time:  30 mins
Serves:  8 

 
Ingredients 
4 slices OSCAR MAYER Bacon, chopped  
3 cups cubed peeled potatoes (about 1-1/4 lb.)  
1/2 cup chopped onion  
1/2 cup sliced celery  
1 can (10-3/4 oz.) condensed cream of chicken soup  
3 cups milk  
8 oz. HOFFMAN'S Hot Pepper Pasteurized Process Cheese Food, cubed  
1 pkg. (10 oz.) frozen whole kernel corn, thawed  
Salt and pepper  
 
Preparation 
Cook bacon in large saucepan until crisp, stirring frequently. Drain, reserving 2 Tbsp. drippings 
in pan.

Add potatoes, onion and celery to saucepan. Cook and stir 2 minutes.

Stir in soup and milk; bring to boil. Reduce heat to low; simmer 8 minutes.
 Add process cheese food and corn; cook an additional 5 minutes or until potatoes are tender.
 Season to taste with salt and pepper.
