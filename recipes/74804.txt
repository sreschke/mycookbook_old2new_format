
Yield: 4 servings
3 	tbsp	Olive oil	
1 	cup	Onion -- chopped	
28 	oz	Crushed plum tomatoes in	
		Puree	
1/2 	cup	Sun-dried tomatoes --	
		Chopped	
1/4 	cup	Wine	
		Pepper	
1/3 	cup	Heavy cream	
		Parsley	
		Parmesean cheese	
Procedures 
Serve over tortellini or ravioli. 
Heat olive oil in skillet. 
Add onion and saute over medium heat for 3-4 minutes. 
Add tomatoes in puree, sun-dried tomatoes and wine. 
Bring to a boil and lower heat. 
Simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. 
Add pepper, cream and parsley. 
Simmer for 3-4 minutes.
