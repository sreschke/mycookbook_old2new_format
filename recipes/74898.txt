2 cups flour
1 cup sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
1/4 cup butter or margarine
1 egg, beaten
1 teaspoon grated orange peel
3/4 cup orange juice
1 1/2 cups light raisins
1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries, chopped

Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and baking soda into a large bowl.  Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly.  Add egg, orange peel, orange juice all at once, stir just until mixture is evenly moist.  Fold in raisins and cranberries.  Spoon into greased 9x5x3 inch loaf pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour and 10 minutes.  Remove from pan; cool on wire rack.  If you choose, you may substitute cranberries for the raisins to have an all cranberry bread.
