Scalloped Potatoes

                
  Ingredients:
               6 to 8 thinly sliced potatoes 
               1-2  onions  sliced
               1 can cheddar cheese soup 
               1 cup velveeta cheese 
               1-1/2 cups grated sharp cheddar cheese 
               1 can (12 oz.) evaporated milk 
               Salt and pepper 
  Directions:Mix soup with milk, heat. Add velveeta cheese and stir till melted. Add cheddar and stir till melted. Layer in greased casserole dish, potatoes, onions and cheese mixture - end with cheese mixture. Cover and bake
