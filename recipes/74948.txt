Cook 2 pkgs frozen chopped spinach according to directions.  Drain well and squeeze out excess liquid.
Spoon spinach evenly into bottom of shallow casserole.  Sprinkle with 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 c chopped onion, 2 tbs butter, melted and 1/2 c grated cheddar cheese.
Quickly rinse and dry 1 lb fresh mushrooms.  Saute in 4 tbs butter until just crisp, tender.  Spoon mushrooms over cheese layer in casserole.  Sprinkle lightly with 1/4 tsp garlic powder and top with 1/2 c grated cheddar cheese.  
Bake at 350 for 20-25 min or until bubbly.
