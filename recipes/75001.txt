Honey Ham
 
2 cup Honey 
1/3 cup Cider vinegar 
2 tsp Ground cloves 
5 lb Ham; boneless 
2 cup Brown sugar; packed 
2 tsp Ground cinnamon 

Combine all ingredients except ham, well.  Over low heat, heat sauce
to just 
before boiling point.  Remove from range.  Slice ham halfway through
into 
desired thickness for servings.  Tie with string and place in a
roasting pan.  
Pour sauce over ham, cover and marinate in the refrigerator for 24
hours.  Two 
hours before baking, remove from refrigerator to bring to room
temperature.  
Bake at 275*F for about 1 hour (or more) to heat through.  Glaze top
under 
broiler if desired. 

**Basting is recommended while cooking. 
**Carefully monitor while under broiler so as not to burn. 
**Garnish as desired.
