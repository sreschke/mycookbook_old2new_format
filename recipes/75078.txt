24 Lg fresh mushrooms (1-1/2" to 2" diameter)
1/4 C minced green onions
1 clove minced garlic
1/4 C butter
2/3 C fine dry bread crumbs (progresso garlic/herb)
1/2 C (2 oz) crumbled bleu cheese

Rinse and drain mushrooms.
Remove stems and chop to make 1 cup.
Melt butter in medium pan; saute stems, onions, garlic, until tender.
Stir in cheese and bread crumbs.
Stuff into mushroom caps.
Bake at 450 degrees for 8 to 10 minutes.

Sausage version:
Omit butter. Cook 1/2 lb Italian sausage with the mushroom stems, onions and garlic.
Drain fat.
Use 2 T grated parmesean instead of bleu cheese.
Add only 2 T bread crumbs.
