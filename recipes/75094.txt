1 pound ground beef 
1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese 
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup 
1/4 cup ketchup 
1/2 cup milk 
1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated biscuit dough 
 

 Directions    

 
1 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). 
2 In a large skillet over medium-high heat, saute the ground beef for 5 minutes, or until browned. Drain excess fat and set aside.  
3 In a medium bowl, combine the cream cheese, soup, ketchup and milk. Mix well and stir in the ground beef. Pour into a 9x13-inch baking dish.  
4 Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 15 minutes, place the biscuits on top and bake for 15 minutes or until biscuits are golden brown.
