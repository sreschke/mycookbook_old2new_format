2 tablespoons vegetable oil 
1 pound lean ground beef 
1/2 cup chopped onion 
1 teaspoon salt 
1/4 teaspoon pepper 
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder 
2 cups chopped cabbage 
1 can cream of mushroom soup 
2 8 oz. cans refrigerated crescent rolls 
1 8 oz. package shredded Cheddar cheese 
1 8 oz. package shredded mozzarella cheese 


Directions: 
Place vegetable oil in skillet. 
Brown hamburger in oil. 
Add onion, salt, pepper, and garlic. 
Add cabbage and cook until tender. 
Stir soup into hamburger. 
Press eight rolls into bottom of 9- by 13-inch glass baking dish.
Pour hamburger mixture on rolls. 
Top with both cheeses. 
Cover with eight more rolls. 
Bake at 350Ã¸ for 20 minutes.
