Ingredients: 
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese 
1/2 cup finely chopped onion 
4 large eggs, slightly beaten 
1/2 teaspoon baking powder 
1 clove finely chopped garlic 
1 cup flour 
1/2 cup oil 
2 Tablespoons parsley 
1 teaspoon salt 
1/4 teaspoon oregano 
dash pepper 
3 cups zucchini
Mix all ingredients well. Spread in greased baking pan and bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes. Cut into squares and serve warm.
