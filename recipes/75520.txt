2 large ripe avocados
2-3 T lemon or lime juice
1 clove garlic, minced
1-2 T chopped fresh cilantro
2-4 canned green chiles, chopped
1 med tomato, peeled & chopped
Minced jalapenos (optional)
salt

Pit avocados & scoop out pulp. Mash pulp coarsely with a fork. Blend in lemon or lime juice, garlic, cilantro, green chiles and tomatoes. If desired, add jalapenos to taste. Season to taste with salt.
