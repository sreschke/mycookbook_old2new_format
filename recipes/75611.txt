Categories    : Crockpot                         Chicken
                Main Dishes

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
   8      large         Chicken breasts
   2      can           Cream of chicken soup
   2      can           Cream of clery soup
   2      can           Cream of mushroom soup
     1  cup             Diced celery
   2      cup           Minute rice

Mix in crock-pot the 6 cans of soup and rice. Place the chicken on top of the
mixture, then add the diced celery. Cook for 3 hours on high or 4 hours on
low.  Makes 8 servings. half recipe to feed 4
