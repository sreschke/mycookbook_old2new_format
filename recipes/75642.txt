1 1/2 lb. beef tips (or good quality stewing beef)
1 envelope Lipton Onion Soup
1 can Campbells' Golden Mushroom Soup
1/2 cup red wine (you can omit or substitute beef stock or water)
1 can drained mushrooms

Combine all ingredients in crockpot and cook all day on low.  Serve with rice, noodles or mashed potatoes.  Everybody's favorite.
