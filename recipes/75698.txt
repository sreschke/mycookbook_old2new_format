1 - 16oz bag noodles
1 - can Cream of Mushroom Soup
1 - can Cream of Chicken Soup 
1 - Can Chunck White Chicken (Swanson)
1 - Stick Butter


Boil water and add noodles.  Take off heat and cover.  Let stand for 15 min.  Then Drain.  Add butter till melted.  Add both soups and chicken
with 1 can of milk.  Stir together and pour into
pan.  Top with crushed potato chips.  Bake at 350
for about 20 minutes or until hot.
