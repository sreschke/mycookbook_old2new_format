1 lb. ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
salt and pepper to taste
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can (5.33 oz.) evaporated milk
1 can (4 oz.) chopped green chiles
1 can (10 oz.) Rotel tomatoes with diced green chiles
1 pkg. (8 oz.) Doritos
1/2 lb. American or cheddar cheese, grated

Preheat oven to 350 degrees.

Brown ground beef, onion, salt and pepper.  Heat the soups, milk, chiles and Rotel tomatoes.  Spead Doritos in the bottom of a 9 X 13 inch pan.  Put meat mixture over Doritos and top with grated cheese.  Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
