1 tbsp vegetable oil
4 skinless bone-in chicken breast halves
1 medium onion, chopped
6 garlic cloves finely chopped
1 tsp salt
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp curry powder
1 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp crushed red pepper flakes (or to taste)
1 (14-1/2 oz) cans stewed tomatoes, undrained
1-1/3  lbs (  about 4 medium) potatoes, cut into 1 inch dice
1 cup baby-cut carrots
 1/2 cup raisins
2 tbsps lemon juice

In a non-reactive Dutch oven or large saucepan heat oil.  Add chicken;  brown all sides. (about 8 minutes)  Remove chicken from pot,  set aside.  In same pot cook and stir onions and garlic still onions are translucent.  Stir in salt, cinnamon, cumin, curry powder, ginger and red pepper.  Cook and stir about 30 seconds till fragrant.  Add tomatoes, potatoes, carrots and raisins and heat until tomatoes just come to a boil. (Mixture will look dry.)    Return chicken to pot.  Cover tightly, reduce heat and simmer 35 to 45 minutes or until  vegetables and chicken are tender.  Stir in lemon juice before serving.  Serves 4.
