This is the BEST recipe I've found for these Christmas favorites!  Roll in red sugar for Valentine's Day, too!

1 c. peanut butter, creamy OR crunchy
1 c. shortening (solid, not oil)
1 c. light brown sugar
1 c. granulated sugar
1/4 c. milk
2 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
3 1/2 c. flour
2 tsp. soda
1 tsp. salt
11 oz. bag of chocolate stars or kisses (UNWRAPPED) 
Extra granulated sugar for rolling cookies, (use colored sugar, if you want)

Oven 375*; ungreased cookie sheets.

In large bowl, mix peanut butter, shortening and brown and granulated sugars; stir in milk, vanilla and eggs.  

Sift together flour, soda and salt; add to peanut butter mixture.

Shape dough into 1" balls, roll in extra sugar and place on cookie sheets about 2" apart.  Bake for 8 minutes: remove from oven and press chocolate candy on the center of each cookie.  Return to oven and bake an additional 3 minutes.  Remove to racks to cool.
