1/2 cup butter, room temperature
2 - 8oz pkgs cream cheese, softened
2 cloves garlic, pressed, or 1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp oregano
1/4 tsp basil
1/4 tsp thyme
1/4 tsp marjoram
1/4 tsp dill weed
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
a variety of crackers

Bledn cream cheese and butter until smooth.  Add seasonings and mix well.  Chill overnight.  Serve at room temperature with crackers.
