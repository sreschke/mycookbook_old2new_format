Chicken Marsala 

skinned and boned chicken breasts 
2 tablespoons flour 
1/4 teaspoon fine seasalt 
freshly ground pepper 
1/4 teaspoon 
Sweet Hungarian Paprika 
finely snipped green onion tops or chives 
2 tablespoons real butter
1-2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
sliced fresh mushrooms 
2-3 tablespoons Marsala wine

Wash and dry chicken breasts and pound each one out to an even thickness. 
Combine the flour, salt, pepper, paprika, and chives and dredge each chicken 
breast in mixture. Heat saut‚ pan over medium heat with the butter and oil. 
When hot, add the chicken and saut‚ on each side until nicely browned and 
chicken is done. Remove chicken to a platter and cover to keep warm. Briefly 
cook and stir the mushroom slices in the hot pan and then add to the chicken. 
With the pan over medium heat, add the wine and cook and stir for several 
minutes, bringing up any browned bits clinging to the pan. Pour the sauce 
over the chicken, garnish, and serve.
