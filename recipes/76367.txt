Grilled London Broil

 
Prep: 10 minutes
Refrigerate: 4 hours to overnight
Cook: 20 minutes
Servings: Serves 6 
1-1/2 lbs. top round steak of flank steak, cut 1-1/2 inch thick 
5 large cloves garlic 
1 tsp. salt 
1/4 cup dry red wine 
1/4 cup balsamic vinegar 
1 Tbsp. soy sauce 
1 tsp. honey 

  
 
 
 Place all ingredients, except beef, in a blender or food processor
and blend until smooth. 
 In a heavy-duty sealable plastic bag, combine the steak and the
blended marinade. Seal bag, pressing out excess air. Place in a
shallow dish and marinate in the refrigerator, turning the bag
occasionally, for at least 4 or up to 24 hours. 
 To grill, remove steak from marinade allowing excess to drip off and
place on an oiled grid set 5-6 inches over medium coals. Cook for
14-18 minutes for medium-rare (145øF internal temperature), turning
once. 
 Transfer steak to a cutting board and let stand for 10 minutes. 
 Holding knife at a 45ø angle, cut steak across the grain into thin
slices to serve.
