Quick Beef Burgundy

 
Prep: 30 minutes
Servings: Serves 4 
1 package fully cooked pot roast 
1 pound small mushrooms 
2 small onions, cut into wedges 
2/3 cup red wine 
3/4 tsp. marjoram leaves 
1 Tbsp. cornstarch dissolved in 3 Tbsp. water 
-- egg noodles 

  
 
 
 Transfer liquid from pot roast into Dutch oven. Add mushrooms,
onions, wine and marjoram leaves to liquid in pan. 
 Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, 7-8 minutes until
vegetables are almost tender; stir occasionally. 
 Cut pot roast into 1-inch pieces; add to pan. Bring to a boil; cook
2-3 minutes until beef is heated through. 
 Stir in cornstarch. Bring to a boil; cook and stir until thickened. 
 Serve over prepared noodles.
