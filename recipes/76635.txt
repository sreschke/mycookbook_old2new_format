Cheddar Cheese - Broccoli Soup
 
2 cups whole milk 
1-1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese 
1 teaspoon black pepper 
1/2 cup butter 
1 teaspoon salt 
1 cup finely chopped broccoli 

Melt butter and cheese. Add broccoli, salt and pepper. Add milk, one cup at a time, allowing to heat up before adding more. Let simmer before serving.
