1-1/2 cups oil
2 cups sugar
3 eggs
2-1/2 cups sifted flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp soda
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp vanilla
3 cups peeled and grated apples
1 cup chopped pecans

GLAZE:
2 Tbsp margarine, melted
2 Tbsp milk
1 cup powdered sugar
1 tssp vanilla
1/4 tsp lemon extract

Grease and flour tube pan.  Mix oil, sugar and eggs and beat well.  In a separate bowl, sift together flour, salt, soda, baking powder and cinnamon.  Gradually add flour mixture to creamed mixture.  Add vanilla and fold in apples and pecans.  Pour into tube pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.  After cake has cooled, invert onto serving plate.  

For the glaze, combine and mix all ingredients together and drizzle over cake.  Punch holes in cake with a toothpick so that glaze will soak into cake.
