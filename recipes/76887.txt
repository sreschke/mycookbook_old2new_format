
Makes: About 2 cups

8 cups loosely packed basil leaves, washed and dried
4 to 6 cloves garlic
1/2 cup pine nuts
1/2 cup Pecorino Romano, broken into bite-size pieces
2 tablespoons olive oil

Place basil in bowl of food processor and pulse until volume is reduced by half. Add garlic, pine nuts, and cheese; pulse until blended. With processor running, pour in olive oil to form a thick paste. Refrigerate or freeze.
