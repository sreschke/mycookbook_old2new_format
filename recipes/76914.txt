Recipe By: CrockeryKitchen
Categories: Beef

1/2 pound ground beef, browned and drained
2 tablespoons bacon bits
1 small onion, chopped
15 ounces tomato sauce
1/2 cup water
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
salt and pepper
2/3 cup long grain rice
8 ounces whole kernel corn, drained
1/2 cup chopped green bell pepper

Place beef in bottom of crockpot.  Top with 
bacon bits and onion.  Combine the tomato 
sauce, water, chili powder, salt and pepper.
Pour half of the mixture over the ingredients
in crockpot.  Sprinkle rice over top. Add in 
the corn and green pepper.  Pour remaining 
tomato mixture over top.  Cover; cook on low 
for 5-6 hours or until rice is done.
