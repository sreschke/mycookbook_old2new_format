Light Cooking - Pasta Cookbook pg 90

2 tsp olive oil
4 cup thinly sliced sweet onions
3 oz goat cheese
1/4 c skim milk
6 oz bow tie or other small pasta - cooked
1 clove garlic
2 tbsp dry wine or chicken broth
1/2 tsp sage leaves
salt & pepper
2 tbsp chopped toasted walnuts

Carmelize onions about 20-25 minutes. Add garlic. Set aside.

Combine goat cheese and milk.  Set aside.

Cook wine, sage, salt & pepper til moisture evaporates.  Add onion & cheese mixture to pasta and toss.  Add walnuts to top

Makes 8- 1/2 cup servings

150 cal, 5 g fat 2g fiber 3 WW pts
