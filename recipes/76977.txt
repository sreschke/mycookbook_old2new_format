2 c cooked, shredded chicken
2 c shredded lettuce
1 can sliced water chestnuts, drained
1 c bean sprouts
1 tbs fresh ginger, grated
1/4 c low sodium soy sauce
1 tbs sesame oil
2 green onions, chopped
chopped peanuts to garnish (optional)

Combine chicken, water chestnuts, bean sprouts and green onion in large bowl.  Set aside.  Mix ginger, soy sauce and sesame oil until well blended.  Pour ginger marinade over chicken mixture.  Marinate 1 hour.  Line salad bowl with shredded lettuce and spoon chicken into center.  Garnish with chopped green onion and peanuts
