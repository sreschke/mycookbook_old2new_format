200g Marscapone Cheese
1 carton thickened light cream
1 Packet Savoiardi Biscuits
Two tablespoons of instant coffee and a splash of Frangelico dissolved in 2 cups boiling water
Cocoa

Mix Marscapone and cream in a bowl. Spread a thin layer over the bottom of a medium sized bowl (Wide enough to fit the savioardi). Dip each savoiardi biscuit in the coffee mixture to soak, then place a single layer over the cheese mixture. Cover with another layer of cheese mixture. Continue until last layer is remainder of cheese mixture. Sprinkle top with cocoa to decorate. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving. Serve with coffee.
