2 1/2 stick margarine, softened
  3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  1/2 cup granulated sugar
    1 egg
    1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
    1 tsp baking soda
  1/2 tsp salt (optional)
    1 tsp ground cinnamon
  1/4 tsp ground nutmeg (optional)
    3 cups Quaker Oats (uncooked)

1.  Heat oven to 375.  
2.  Beat margarine & sugars until creamy; then beat in egg and vanilla.
3.  Add combined flour, baking soda, salt, & spices.
4.  Mix well, then stir in oats.
5.  Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet.

Bake 8-9 min for a chewy cookie
Bake 10-11 min for a crisp cookie

Cool 1 min on cookie sheet; remove to wire rack.
Yield: 4 1/2 dozen
