6 cups (16oz) ziti, uncooked
3 cups (about 28oz jar) spaghetti sauce, divided
1 3/4 cups (15oz) ricotta cheese
2 cups (8oz) shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley or 1 tbsp. dried parsley flakes
1 egg
1 tsp. dried oregano leaves
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
3 tbsp. grated Parmesan cheese

Heat oven to 375. Cook pasta according to pkg. directions for 9 minutes; drain. In large bowl, stir together hot pasta, 1 1/2 cups spaghetti sauce, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, parsley, egg, oregano, garlic powder, salt and pepper. In a 13x9x2 inch baking dish, spoon pasta mixture; top with remaining 1 1/2 cups sauce. SPrinkle with Parmesan cheese; cover with foil. Bake 30 minutes or until hot and bubbly.

10 servings

Make ahead: Cover and freeze up to 2 months. Thaw in fridge 24 hours. Cook as above but add about 10 minutes.
