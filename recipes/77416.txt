1 cup chopped nuts
2-1/2 cups sugar
5 cups flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1-1/2 cups vegetable oil
3 eggs
3 bananas, mashed
1 teaspoon lemon juice
1 can crushed pineapple, drained

Mix together dry ingredients. Mix together liquid ingredients, combine.
Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
