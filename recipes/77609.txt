1 stalk celery
1 3-1/2 lb. roasting chicken
1/2 tsp. dried thyme
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tbsp. butter, softened, divided
1 onion, quartered

Preheat oven to 475. Slice celery. Sprinkle inside chicken cavity with thyme and salt; add 1 tsp. butter, sliced celery and onion. Rub outside of chicken with remaining butter. Tuck wing tips under back of chicken. Place chicken breast side down on rack in roasting pan. Add enough water to cover bottom of pan. Roast for 10 min. Reduce temp. to 375. Roast 20 min. longer. Turn chicken breast side up. Roast untilchicken is browned.

Make gravy to accompany chicken directly in the pan. Stir 1 tbsp. flour for each cup of pan juices and a small amount of water into the roasting pan. COok over med. High heat, stirring continually until thickened.

Serve with roasted potatoes!
