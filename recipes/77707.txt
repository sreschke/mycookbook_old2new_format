Prep: 18 minutes
Cook: 4 minutes
Tield: 4 servings

4 cups bowties or other fun shaped pasta
4 tsp butter, cut into pieces
2/3 cup grated gruyere cheese
freshly ground pepper

Boil pasta approximately 3 to 4 minutes, not to saoft but firm. Then spread in a microwave safe shallow  casserole dish; dot with butter pieces. Microwave on high for 3 minutes.
Sprinkle the grated gruyere cheese on top of the pasta; toss and microwave another minute to melt cheese. Season with fresh ground pepper if desired. 

306 calories
