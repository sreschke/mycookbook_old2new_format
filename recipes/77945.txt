TACO MEATLOAF

1lb. ground beef
2T. minced onion
3/4c. taco sauce
Doritos
package of taco seasoning
4-6 cheese slices(american)

Mix together ground beef, minced onion, taco sauce, and taco seasoning in a large bowl, add Doritos(crushed) until mixture holds together. Divide mixture into 2 equal parts, press first half into the bottom of a greased bread pan, cover with slices of cheese, then cover with other half of meat mixture. Cook in 350 degree oven for one hour, half way through cooking, spread some taco sauce on top and finish cooking. 
Enjoy, we love it!!
