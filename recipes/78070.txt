3 large garlic cloves, finely chopped
1/8 tsp. crushed red pepper
1 lb. frozen, peeled, deveined shrimp, thawed
2 tsps. olive or veggie oil
3 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
4 green onions, sliced in 1 inch pieces
1/2 medium red bell pepper, cut into strips
1/4 cup dry white wine
salt and pepper to taste
2 cups hot, cooked rice

In a 10 inch skillet, cook and stir garlic and crushed red pepper in oil over medium high heat for 1 minute. Add shrimp, stir fry for 1 minute. Stir in mushrooms, onions, red bell pepper and wine. Stir fry until shrimp are pink and veggies are hot, about 2 minutes longer. Season with salt and pepper. Serve over rice. About 3/4 cup shrimp mixture per serving and 1/2 cup rice.
