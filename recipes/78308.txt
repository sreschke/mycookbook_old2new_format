Spiced Chicken

Ingredients:
3-4 pounds broiler-fryer chicken (turkey may be used instead) 
1/2 cup low-sodium soy sauce 
1/4 cup chopped onions 
1 clove of garlic, crushed 
1 teaspoon fresh ginger root, minced 
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon 
1/4 teaspoon allspice 
1/8 teaspoon cloves pepper 

Preparation: 
Cut chicken into serving pieces. Marinate the chicken for about 3 hours in soy sauce,onions, garlic, and ginger root in the refrigerator. Arrange chicken in a shallow pan, sprayed with cooking spray. Mix the spices, sprinkle over chicken, and bake at 325 degrees for 45 minutes to 1 hour. Test for tenderness; turn once.
