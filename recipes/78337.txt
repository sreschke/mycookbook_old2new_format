Easy Chicken

1/2 bottle French Dressing
1/2 envelope Lipton's Onion Soup Mix (if you don't like onion use the 
mushroom soup mix)
1/2 cup water
1 whole chicken cut into serving pieces (or equal chicken parts)

Oven Method~ Mix dressing, soup mix and water. Pour over chicken (place skin 
side up) in greased casserole dish. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes. Turn chicken 
and bake 30 minutes longer.

Crockpot method ~ Mix dressing, soup mix and water. Pour over chicken (place 
skin side up) in crockpot. Cook on low for 7 hours.
