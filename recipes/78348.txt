1 C FLOUR
1 C SUGAR
1 TSP BAKING POWDER
1 EGGBEATER
4 LARGE APPLES, PARED & SLICED
2 TBSP SUGAR
1 TSP CINNAMON
1 TSP NUTMEG
1 TBSP BUTTER
1/4 C WATER

SIFT TOGETHER FLOUR, 1 C SUGAR & BAKING POWDER. ADD EGGBEATER. SLICE APPLES INTO GREASED BAKING DISH. COVER WITH FLOUR MIXTURE. COMBINE 2 TBSP SUGAR, CINNAMON & NUTMEG. SPRINKLE OVER FLOUR MIXTURE. DOT WITH BUTTER & SRINKLE WITH WATER. BAKE AT 350 FOR 40 MINUTES
