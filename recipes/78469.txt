Linda's Turtle Cake 

1 box german chocolate cake mix
1 can sweetened condensed milk
1 jar caramel toppping
Cool Whip
chopped nuts, (pecans)

Bake cake as directed in a 9x13" baking pan. Remove from oven. 

While still warm, take a wooden spoon, and poke holes all over. Pour condensed milk over top, and into holes. 

With spatula, spread caramel topping over top of cake. 

Let cool, and spread cool whip and sprinkle with nuts.
