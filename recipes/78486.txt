Dough:
2 pkg yeast
1/2 Cup Warm Water; 105ยง - 115ยง
1/3 Cup + 1/2 teasp Sugar
4 - 5 Cups Flour
1 teasp Salt
1 Cup Milk; Scalded then cooled to 110ยง
1/3 Cup Oil
2 Eggs; room temperature

Filling:
1/2 Cup Butter
1 Cup Brown Sugar
1/2 Cup Sugar
2 Tbls Cinnamon

Icing:
1 Cup Confectionary Sugar
2-3 Tbls Warm Milk
1 teasp Vanilla

 Disolve Yeast, 1/2 tsp sugar in Water for 5 minutes.  In mixer bowl combine 3 Cups of Flour, remaining 1/2 Cup Sugar and Salt. At low speed gradually beat in milk, oil, eggs and yeast mixture; beat until well blended. Beat in additional Flour  (about 1 1/2 Cups) until dough pulls away from side of bowl. On floured surface knead dough until smooth and elastic, 8-10 minutes. Place in greased bowl, turning to grease top. Cover and let rise until double in bulk, about 1 hour.

Filling: In mixer bowl beat all ingredients until smooth; set aside.

 Grease 2 round 9-inch cake pans. On lightly floured surface roll dough into  an 18x10-inch rectangle. Spread with filling. Roll tightly from long side. Cut into 1 1/4-inch slices. Place 1 roll cutside up in center of each pan. Arrange remaining rolls in a circle of 6 around the center roll. Cover and let rise until double in bulk, about 30-40 minutes.
Preheat oven to 350ยง. Bake 25 - 30 minutes, until golden brown. Cool in pans 10 minutes. Invert onto wire racks, then invert again to cool.

Icing: Wisk all ingredients and drizzle over cooled rolls.
Makes 14 rolls.
