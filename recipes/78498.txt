Linda's Buck Eyes 

2 c. peanut butter
3 c. confectionary sugar
1 stick margarine
3 1/2 c. rice crispies
1 12 oz. pkg. semi sweet chocolate bits
1/2 stick of parafin wax
crushed nuts (optional)

Mix together first 3 ingredients, then add rice crispies, mix and roll into balls. Refrigerate 1 hr. 

In a double boiler melt chocolate and parafin wax.

Put tooth pick into ball and dip into melted chocolate. 

If you like you can dip into crushed nuts. 

Cool and refrigerate.
