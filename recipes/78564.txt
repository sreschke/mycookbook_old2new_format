Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
   8      slices        bacon
   8      stalks        green onions, -- sliced thin
   2      pound         fresh mushrooms, -- sliced
                        salt and pepper -- to taste
     1/2  teaspoon      celery salt
   5      cups          shredded Monterey jack cheese
   16      large         eggs, -- well beaten
   2      cup           milk

Cook bacon crisp; drain and crumble.  Reserve bacon drippings to saute onions,
then mushrooms.  In a large mixing bowl, combine bacon, onions, mushrooms,
seasonings, cheese, eggs and milk.  Pour into a greased 4 quart casserole.
Bake, uncovered, at 350Ã¸F for 45 to an hour.
