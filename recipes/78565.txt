Fisherman's Casserole
 
8 Oz. Pkg. Macaroni
1/2 Cup Chopped Onion
2/3 Cup Milk
1 Can Cream of Celery Soup
1/2 Lb. Grated Cheddar Cheese
1 Tsp. Salt
1/2 Tsp. Dry Mustard
1 Box (approx. 8 oz.) Frozen Peas
1 Lb. Can Salmon
2 Tbsp. Butter
Bread Crumbs (approx. 1 Cup)


Cook macaroni according to package directions; drain. Mix with soup,
milk, cheese, onion and seasonings. Add frozen peas and salmon. Place
in a buttered 9" x 13" pan. Cover mixture with approximately 1 cup of
bread crumbs; drizzle with 2 tablespoons melted butter. 

Bake at 350 deg. F for 45 minutes.
