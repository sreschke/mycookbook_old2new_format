CURRY SAUCE
1/2 c mayo, 2 tbs milk, 1 tbs curry powder, 1/4 tsp tobasco.  Combine all ingred, refrigerate
MUSTARD SAUCE
1/4 c mustard, 1/4 c mayo, 1 garlic clove crushed, 1/4 tsp worcestershire, salt and pepper, combine, refrigerate
HORSERADISH SAUCE
1/2 c sour cream, 1 tbs horseradish, 1/4 tsp lemon juice, 1/4 tsp worcestershire, salt and pepper.  Combine and refrigerate
SAUCE REMOULADE
1 c mayo, 1/4 c finely chopped onion, 1 hard boiled egg, chopped, 2 tsp mustard, 2 tsp snipped parsley. Combine, refrigerate
