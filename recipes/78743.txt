2 - 6oz can tuna - drained
1 - 8 oz box Cremettes - cooked & drained
1 - med Onion - finely chopped & sautted
1 - 15 oz can evaporated milk
  potato chips
  paprika to taste.

Cook noodles - drain. Mix milk, onion, tuna  and noodles.

In prepared casserole dish, layer tuna noodle mixture, then crushed potatoe chips, tuna mix, crushed chips.  Ending with crushed chips.  Sprinkle with paprika and bake 25 min at 350 degrees.
