Outrageous brownies 

1 lb (4 sticks) unsalted butter 
1 lb plus 3 cups semisweet chocolate chips 
6 oz unsweetened chocolate 
6 large eggs 
2 tbs plus 1-1/2 tsp powdered instant espresso 
2 tbs vanilla extract 
2-1/4 cups sugar 
1 cup sifted all-purpose flour 
1 tbs baking powder 
1 tsp salt 
3 cups chopped walnut pieces 

Heat oven to 350Ã¸F. Grease and flour a 12-by-18-inch jelly roll pan. Set aside. Melt together the butter, pound of chocolate chips and unsweetened chocolate until smooth in the top of a double boiler. Cool to room temperature. Combine, but do not whisk, the eggs, powdered espresso, vanilla and sugar. Stir in the cooled chocolate mixture and set aside. 
Sift together the flour, baking powder and salt. Mix into the batter. Finally, fold in the remaining chocolate chips and the walnuts. Pour into the greased pan. Bake about 30 minutes or until a tester just comes out clean. Do not overbake. Cool thoroughly and cut into squares.
