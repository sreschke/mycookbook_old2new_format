2 large tomatoes diced (2 cups)
1/4 cup fresh basil
l garlic clove, minced
  freshly ground pepper
1/2 French or Italian bread or 1 French baguette
1 garlic clove, halved
1 tablespoon olive oil
2 tablespoons parmesan cheese

In small bowl combine tomatoes, basil, minced garlic, salt and pepper. Cover and refrigerate to blend flavours. Cut bread into 1" slices. Broil on one side until golden. Rub halved garlic over unbroiled side of bread slices. Brush bread slices with olive oil.Top with tomato mixture. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Broil for 1 minute or until hot and bubbly. Serve immediately.
Yield: 6-8 servings

This is excellent.
