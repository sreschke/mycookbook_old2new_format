      Microwave Party Mix
 
               from To Market, To Market...
             A Collection of Kentucky Recipes
            ___________________________________
 
 INGREDIENTS:
 
 6 tablespoons butter
 4 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
 1 teaspoon celery salt
 1 teaspoon onion salt
 2 teaspoons garlic salt
 2 cups Corn Chex cereal
 2 cups Rice Chex cereal
 2 cups Cheerios cereal
 2 cups thin pretzels
 1-1/2 cups mixed nuts
 
 TO PREPARE:
 
 1.  Place butter, Worcestershire, and salts in 13 x 9 x 2-
 inch baking dish.  Microwave 3 minutes on Medium High 
 (until butter melts).
 2.  Add cereals, pretzels, and nuts.  Stir to coat 
 thoroughly.  Microwave on High for 3 minutes.
 3.  Stir; microwave on High for 3 more minutes.  Cool.
 
 HINT:  For additional bite, sprinkle with more garlic salt.
 
 YIELD: 9-1/2 cups
