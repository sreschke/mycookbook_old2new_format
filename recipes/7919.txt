3 eggs
16 oz. mashed sweet potatoes
1/2 cup brown sugar
1-1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon allspice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 can milk, heated until hot
1 pie shell, unbaked

Beat eggs, sweet potatoes, sugar, allspice, and salt.  Gradually stir in milk.  Pour into pie shell.  Bake at 400 degrees for 40 to 45 minutes.
