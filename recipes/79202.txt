2 tubes refrigerated crescent rolls (8 oz. ea.)
1C shredded Co-Jack cheese
2/3C fat-free cream of chicken soup
1/2C chopped fresh broccoli
1/2C chopped red pepper
1/4C chopped water chestnuts
5 oz. can white chicken, drained
2T chopped onion

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Arrange rolls on a 12" pizza pan, forming a ring with pointed ends facing outer edge and wide ends overlapping.

Mix rest of ingredients; spoon over wide ends of rolls. Fold pointes over and tuck under wide ends. The filling will still be visible.

Bake 20-25 minutes.

Serves 8
7 WW points per serving
