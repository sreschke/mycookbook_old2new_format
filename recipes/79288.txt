White Christmas Pie

Soften 1 tbs gelatin in 1/4 c cold water

In sauce pan mix: 1/2 C sugar, 4 tbs flour, 1/2 tsp salt, 1 1/2 C milk. Cook over low heat stirring constantly until boil. Boil 1 min. Cool. When partially set beat until smooth. Blend in 3/4 tsp vanilla, 1/4 tsp almond extract. Fold in 1/2 C whipped cream, stiff. Fold in meringue of 3 egg whites, 1/4 tsp cream of tarter, 1/2 C sugar. Fold in 1 C coconut. Pile into cooled baked pie shell. Sprinkle with coconut. Can be served with a strawberry topping.
