8oz Lasagna Noodles
1/2 Cup Chopped Onions
1/2 Cup Green Peppers
3 Tbs Butter or Margarine
1 Can Cream of Chicken Soup
1/3 Cup Milk
1 Can Sliced Mushrooms
1 1/2 Cups Cottage Cheese
8oz Mozzarella Cheese
3 Cups Diced Chicken (Canned may be used)
2 Cups Grated Cheddar Cheese
1/2 Cup Parmesan Cheese

Cook onion and green pepper in butter until tender.  Stir in soup, mushrooms and milk.  Layer: Sauce, noodles, sauce, cottage cheese, chicken and cheeses.  Repeat until all ingredients are used.  Bake at 350 for 1-1/12 hours.  Let set 10 minutes before serving.  Serves 6-8
