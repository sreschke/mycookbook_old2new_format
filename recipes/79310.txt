CINNAMON COFFEE CAKE  

1 pkg. yellow cake mix
1 pkg. instant vanilla pudding
4 eggs
3/4-cup water
3/4-cup oil
1 t. vanilla
1 t. butter flavoring

Filling:
1/4-cup sugar
2 t. cinnamon
1/4-cup nuts (chopped)

Glaze:
1 cup powdered sugar
1/2 t. vanilla
1/2 t. butter flavoring
1 T. water

Combine ingredients in mixing bowl.  Beat until smooth.  Pour 1/3 batter into greased Bundt pan.  Sprinkle in 1/2 filling.  Pour 1/3 batter.  Sprinkle in remaining filling.  Pour in remaining batter.
Bake 350 degrees for 40-50 minutes.
Cool before adding glaze.
