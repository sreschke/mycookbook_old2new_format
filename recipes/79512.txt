1 3/4 C. noodles
1 can whole kernal corn, drained
1 can mushrooms, save liquid
6 T. margarine
1/4 lb. grated cheddar cheese
6 T. flour
3 C. milk
1 can tuna, drained
salt and pepper

Combine cooked noodles with corn, tuna, and mushrooms, drained.  Melt butter, add flour and cook about 1 minute.  Add milk anbd liquid from mushrooms.  Cook until thickened.  Add cheese (save enough for topping) and stir.  Season with salt and pepper.  Add to noodle mixure.  Mix lightly and sprinkle with cheese.  Bake about 50 minutes @350.
