1 - 9" frozen deep dish pie shell
4 - eggs
1 - cup light cream or whole milk
1 - 6 or 7 1/2 oz can crab meat
12 oz shredded cheddar cheese
1/4 cup finely chopped onion
6 strips cooked bacon
salt & pepper to taste

Beat eggs & milk together. Add crab meat, cheese, onion and crumbled bacon. Salt & pepper to taste. Mix all ingredients thoroughly. Pour into pie shell & bake at 350 degrees for 50 to 60 minutes.
