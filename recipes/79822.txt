6 (6-8 oz) frozen rock lobster tails, thawed
1/4 c salad oil
1 clove garlic, very finely chopped
1/2 lb pork shoulder, ground
2 tbs cornstarch
1/4 c soy auce
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 1/4 c boiling water
2 eggs
1/2 c slivered green onions
With kitchen shears, cut shell away from lobster meat.  Remove meat in one piece, and cut it crosswise in 2" sections.
Heat oil in large skillet with cover.  Add garlic and pork; saute stirring until pork is no longer pink about 10 minutes.  Meanwhile, in small bowl, make a smooth mixture of cornstarch and 1/3 c water.  Stir into pork the soy sauce, sugar, salt, pepper, boiling water and cornstarch mixture.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat, simmer stirring until thickened and translucent about 10 minutes.  Add lobster pieces, cook covered and over low heat until lobster is tender about 8 to 10 minutes (do not overcook)  In small bowl, beat eggs slightly with fork.  Stir, all at once, into lobster mixture (eggs will form shreds)  Add green onion.  Serve at once.  Serves 6
