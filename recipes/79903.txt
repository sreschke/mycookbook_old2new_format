Cheese Noodles   
  
1 cup sour milk 
1 cup cottage cheese 
1 teaspoon baking soda 
1/2 stick butter 
3 eggs 
flour 
sugar, for sprinkling 
cinnamon, for sprinkling 

Make a soft dough of the first 6 ingredients. Roll the dough out flat, to about 1 inch thickness. Cut the dough into 4 inches by 1 inch. Fry the dough pieces in deep hot fat. Sprinkle the pieces with sugar and cinnamon. 

From: Pennsylvania Dutch Cook Book 
By: J. George Frederick 
ISBN: 0-486-22676-X
