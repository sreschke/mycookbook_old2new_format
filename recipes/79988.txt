1 lg pink grapefruit, peeled,sectioned
1 lg navel orange, peeled, sectioned
1 tbs sugar
1 lg sweet red pepper, julienned
1 sm red onion, sliced and separated into rings
1/3 c honey
1/4 c cider vinegar
2 tbs poppy seeds
2 tsp grated onion
2 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp salt
1 tsp ground mustard
1/2 tsp grated lemon peel
4 c torn salad greens
2 c sliced fresh mushrooms
In a bowl, combine the grapefruit and orange sections, sprinkle w sugar.  Add red pepper and red onion.
In a blender, combine the honey, vinegar, poppy seeds, grated onion, lemon juice, salt, mustard and lemon peel; cover and process until blended.  Pour over fruit mixture and toss to coat.  In a serving bowl, toss greens, mushrooms and fruit mixture.  Serve immediately.  6 servings
