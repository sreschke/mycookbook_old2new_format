8 large eggs
2/3 cup cottage cheese
3 tablespoons chopped mint
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pepper
1/4 cup Parmesan
2 tablespoons olive oil
20 oz asparagus, cut in 3/4" pieces
6 green onions, chopped 

Preheat broiler. Whisk first 5 ingredients and 4 tablespoons Parmesan together. Heat oil ina nonstick broilerproof skillet over medium. Add asparagus, toss. Cover and cook until asparagus is crisp-tender. Add onions; stir 30 seconds. Stir in eggs.  Cover, turn heat to low and cook til almost set, about 8 minutes.

Sprinkle  with remaining Parmesan. Broil til top is set and starts to brown. Slide spatula around sides to loosen; slide out on a plate and serve. 

For 4.
