3 c medium peeled, thinly sliced tart apples
1 c raising
34 c sugar
1 c plus 3 tbs all purpose flour divided
2 tbs water
1 tbs lemon juice
1 tsp cinnamon
2/3 c firmly packed brown sugar
2/3 c butter or margarine
1 c rolled oats
Heat oven to 375.  In 9" square pan, bombine apples, raisins, sugar, 3 tbs flour, water, lemon juice and cinnamon.  In large bowl, combine remaining 1 c flour and brown sugar.  Usling pastry blender or fork, cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs.  Stir in oats and raisin mixture.  Bake at 375 for 40 minutes or until lightly browned.  Serve warm or at room tem. with ice cream or whipped cream.  6 servings
