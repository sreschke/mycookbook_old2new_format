INGREDIENTS:
4 c. peeled, finely sliced carrots
1 med. onion
1 Tbsp. butter
1 can cream of celery or cream of onion soup
1/2 c. grated cheddar cheese
1 1/2 c. bread crumbs
1/4 c. melted butter
1/2 tsp. salt
pinch of pepper

METHOD:
1.  Cook onion in butter until soft.
2.  Stir in soup, salt, pepper, cheese and carrots.
3.  Place in greased casserole.
4.  Toss bread crumbs with melted butter.
5.  Sprinkle over casserole mixture.
6.  Bake at 350F for 30 min.
