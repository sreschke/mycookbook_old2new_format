Ingredients

  1/2 lb. mild Italina turkey sausage
  cooking spray
  2 Tbl butter
  3 Tbl all-purpose flour
  2 cups 1% skim milk
  6 cups hot cooked ziti (about 4 cups uncooked)
  3/4 cup (3oz.) fresh grated Parmesan cheese
  1/2 cup (2oz.) shredded part-skim mozzarella ch.

Directiions:

  Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  
  Remove sausage from casings.  Place a large skillet with cooking spray over medium heat; cook sausage until browned, stirring to crumble.  drain well; set aside.  Wipe drippings from pan with paper towel.

  Melt butter in pan ver medium heat.  Add flour, stir with whisk.  Gradually add milk, stirring with a whisk until smooth.  cook until thick ( about 8 min.); remove form heat.  Combine sauce, dausage, pasta, Parmesan, and salt in a large bowl.  Spoon mixture into a 11x7-inch baking dish coated with cooking spray.  Sprinkle with mozarella.  Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes or until lightly browned.

Yield: 4 servings
