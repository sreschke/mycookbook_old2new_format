Preheat oven to 325 degrees.

In a saucepan over low heat put:

1 stick (1/4 lb.) butter
1 stick (1/4 lb.) margarine
1/2 cup sugar

On a cookie sheet, with sides, arrange:

48 graham cracker quarters (break large cracker into quarters as marked) with 3 oz. slivered almonds placed over graham crackers.

Turn up heat on sauce pan, stirring contents constantly.
Bring to a boil and boil for 2 minutes, continuing to stir.

Spoon mixture over graham crackers and almonds.

Bake for 12 minutes.

Quickly remove cookies from cookie sheet with spatula and place on waxed paper to cool.
