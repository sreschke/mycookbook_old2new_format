2 lb beef
3-4 potatoes
1 can cream corn
1 beef stock cube
6-8 carrots
2 onions 
1 can tomato soup
10oz frozen peas

Cut meat and veges into large pieces. Combine all ingredients except peas in crock pot. Stir well.  Cook on low 8-10 hours, high 3-4 hours. 
Add peas half an hour before serving
