Vispipuuro (Whipped Porridge)

*4 cups fruit juice*
3/4 cup cream of wheat
*1/2 cup sugar (ONLY if the fruit juice is unsweetened.)*  

In a large pan, heat the fruit juice and the cream of wheat. Stir until the mixture thickens. Pour the porridge into a bowl. Whip the porridge with an electric mixer until the porridge is fluffy and light. Pour into individual serving dishes and put into the refrigerator to chill. Serve with your favorite whipped topping. 

Cooking Note: *Cranberry, grape, cherry, lingonberry, or apple may be used. If the fruit juice is unsweetened, you can add 1/2 cup sugar.*

Additional Note: This recipe has come from Finland.
