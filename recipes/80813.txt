 
Football Sandwiches
serves 4
 
2 pounds of sirloin steak
1 cup of Italian dressing
2 tablespoons oil
1 thin sliced red or brown onion
1 green pepper, cut into strips
Salt and pepper
4 French rolls or crusty Italian rolls
 
The night before, slice steak into thin strips, place in bowl and cover with dressing. Refrigerate overnight.
Next day sautâ€š the onion and pepper in oil, until crisp tender. Meanwhile warm rolls in 250 degree oven.
Drain meat and add to the pan with onion and peppers, cooking over high until medium rare. Salt and pepper
to taste. While the meat is cooking, cut off one end of the roll and scoop out the bread. Fill rolls with meat mixture.
