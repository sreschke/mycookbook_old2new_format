Preheat skillet for 5 to 10 minutes so crab cakes will sear quickly.  

1/4 to 1/3 cup fine dry breadcrumbs
1 lb. fresh jumbo lump crabmeat, drained
1/3 cup mayonnaise
3 tablespoons sour cream
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
1 1/2 teaspoons Old Bay seasoning
1/4 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1/4 teaspoon hot sauce
1 tablespoon fresh parsley

6 tablespoons butter
1 tablespoon blackening spice
  
Sprinkle breadcrumbs over crabmeat in a med bowl.

Stir together mayo and next 7 ingredients in a small bowl; pour over crabmeat mixture, and toss gently.  Shape mixture into 5 (3 inch) patties.  (Mixture will be very soft.)  Cover and chill at least 1 1/2 hours.  

Brust crab cakes with 2 tablespoons butter.  Sprinkle evenly with blackening spice.  Cook crab cakes in 1/4 cup clarified butter in hot skillet over med heat 3 to 4 minutes on each side or until golden.
