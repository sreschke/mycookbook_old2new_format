1-1/2 cups biscuit mix
1 tbs parsley flakes
1 can (17 oz) whole kernal corn, drained
1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
8 oz. thinly sliced cooked beef (from deli), chopped
1 pkg (1-1/2 oz) sloppy joe seasoning mix

In a large bowl combine biscuit mix and parsley.  Add enough water (about 1/3 cup) to moisten; stir.

Reserve 3/4 cup of the dough.  With lightly floured hands pat remaining dough on to the bottom and     1-1/2 inches up the sides of a greased 2 quart au gratin dish or an 8x8x2 inch baking dish.

In a saucepan combine corn, tomato sauce, beef and seasoning mix; heat through.  Spoon mixture over dough.  Drop the reserved dough by small spoonfuls around the edge of the dish.  

Bake in a 400 degree oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until dumplings are golden.

Serves 6.
