2-3lb chuck roast(can also use cubed stew meat)
1 (12oz) can beer
1 envelope onion soup mix
1 envelope brown gravy mix
1/3 cup flour.

 In crock pot mix beer with onion soup mix and brown gravy mix. 
 Put flour in plastic bag and add either chuck roast or stew meat. Shake until well coated. 
 Add beef to crock pot and gentle stir to coat beef with liquid.
 Cover and cook on low for about 8 hours- or until done.
