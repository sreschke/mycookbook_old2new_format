2 bricks softened cream cheese
1/2 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/2 tsp vanilla
4 squares of semi-sweet chocolate, melted
1 ready-to-use chocolate crumb crust(9")

Mix cream cheese with sugar, eggs, and then vanilla.
Add melted chocolate.  Pour mixture into pie crust.
Bake at 350 degrees for 40 min. or until centre is
almost set.  Refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
Garnish to your heart's desire.
