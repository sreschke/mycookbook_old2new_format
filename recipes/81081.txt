CRUST:
3/4 C graham cracker crumbs
1 T butter
1 T sugar

Mix, well place in bottom of 10" spring form pan; pat down well on bottom & high enough on the side to cover the pan's joint.

FILLING:

3 8 OZ. packages cream cheese (may use Neufchatel)
4 Eggs
1 1/2 C sugar
1 T vanilla

Beat chream cheese well.  Beat in sugar, vanilla, and eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.

Pour into pan over graham cracker crust.

Bake 350 for  50 minutes.  Turn off oven and let rest in oven for 20 minutes.  Chill before removing from pan.
