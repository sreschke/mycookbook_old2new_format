BEAN  AND  HAM  CASSEROLE

1 (40 oz.) can Great Northern beans, undrained
1 1/4 c. ketchup
1 1/2 tsp. prepared mustard
3/4 c. brown sugar
2 tbsp. minced onion
1 c. diced cooked ham
4 strips bacon, if desired

Mix all ingredients together except the bacon.  Pour into 2-quart size
baking dish.  Place bacon strips over top.  Bake 40 minutes at 350
degrees.  Place under broiler for a few minutes to brown bacon.
