Jack Daniel's Honey Mustard 
1/2 cup honey
1/2 cup dark vinegar
1/2 cup dark brown sugar-packed
2 eggs
2 Tbsp. flour
2 Tbsp. yellow mustard
1/2 cup Jack Daniel's whiskey
1 9 oz. bottle kraft's horseradish cream sauce 

Put honey, vinegar, sugar, eggs and flour in blender a few seconds to
blend at high speed until smooth. Transfer to a 2-quart saucepan. Cook
on medium high, whipping briskly with whisk to prevent it from
sticking to bottom of pan. It will thicken just as it comes to a boil.
Quickly add mustard and whiskey. Continue to cook and stir briskly
only 1/2 minute. Remove from heat. Add the horseradish. Beat well.
Cool completely. 

Bottle and cap tightly. Can store refrigerated 6-8 weeks. Freezes for
months. Recipe can be halved.
