2 1/2 c. PREPARED macaroni & cheese, I prefer homemade, but you can use the "blue box", too!
1 # ground beef
1/2 c. sliced celery
1/4 c. chopped green pepper
2 Tbls. chopped onion (or 2 chopped green onions)
1 (15-16 oz.) can whole kernel corn, DRAINED
1 (6 oz.) can tomato paste
1/2 c. water
Salt and pepper to taste

Oven 350*.

If using boxed dinner, prepare as directed.  Set aside.

In large skillet, brown beef, drain; add celery, green pepper and onion; cook till vegetables are tender.

Add all other ingredients, including macaroni and cheese; mix well.  Pour into 2-quart, greased casserole and bake 15-20 minutes or till heated through.
