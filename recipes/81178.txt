3 eggs
1/2c. margarine, melted
1/2t. salt
1 box Jiffy corn muffin mix
1-8oz. sour cream
1-16oz. whole kernel corn,drained
1-16oz. cream style corn
    Beat eggs with margarine,add salt,sour cream and
corn muffin mix.  Beat well.  Bake in glass baking dish at 350 for 30-35 minutes.
