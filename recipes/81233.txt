Leftover chicken noodles(polish wedding style)
1 can cr.of chicken soup
1 sm can mushrooms
1 can of spam (or 1/2 depending on how many leftover noodles you have), cubed
1/8-1/4 tsp. dried thyme
ritz crackers
parmesan cheese

Mix together. Crumble crackers over top;then sprinkle parmesan cheese over that.
Bake at 350 till crackers are toasted and noodles are warmed through.
