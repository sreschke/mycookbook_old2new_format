3 C. frozen hashbrowns (thawed)
1/4 c. flour
1 Tab minced onion
2 tsp seasoned salt, dash pepper
3 slightly beaten eggs
1/4 c. milk
2 Tab oil
Combine above & let stand 5 min.
Drop batter in 1/4 cup portions on hot, greased griddle, turn to brown on both sides.
Serve with syrup or applesauce.
