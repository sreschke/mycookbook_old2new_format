Ingredients 
1 pound ground pork sausage 
1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent roll dough 
8 eggs, beaten 
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese 
2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese 
1 teaspoon dried oregano 
 
  
 
Directions 
1    Place sausage in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Drain and set aside.
 
2    Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Lightly grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
 
3    Line bottom of prepared baking dish with crescent roll dough and sprinkle with crumbled sausage.. In a large bowl, combine beaten eggs, Mozzarella and Cheddar cheese. Season with oregano and pour over sausage and crescent rolls. 
 
4    Bake in prepared oven for 25 to 30 minutes, until eggs have set and top is golden.
 
Makes 8 servings
