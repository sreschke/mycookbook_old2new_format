1 large 30 oz pkg frozen hash browns
2 cans cream of potato condensed soup
1 pint sour cream
4 oz grated cheddar cheese
1 small onion diced
Parmeson cheese


 Mix together in large bowl - soup, sour cream, cheddar cheese and onion.  Stir in hash browns - place in a greased casserole (loaf cake pan works well) top with grated cheese - At this point casserole may be baked or refrigerated until later - top with grated Parmeson - bake at 350 for 30 minutes or until top is lightly browned - Serves 6
