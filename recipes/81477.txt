6  Tablespoons minced green onion (including some green tops)
3/4 cup tequilla
1/2 cup vegetable oil
4 Tablespoons fresh squeezed lime juice
3 serrano peppers, seeded, minced
2 Tablespoons minced garlic
1 teaspoon kosher salt
1-1/2 pounds flank steak
1/2 cup sour cream
1/3 cup fresh snipped cilantro
12 flour tortillas
Salsa

Combine first 7 ingredients; refrigerate 1/2 cup.  Arrange flank steak in storage bag, pour remaining marinade over meat, seal bag and refrigerate several hours or overnight, turning the bag several times.  Combine sour cream and cilantro, refigerate.  

Prepare grill, spray with cooking spray.  Using direct heat method arrange steak on grill rack 5 inches from hot coals.  Grill 10-12 minutes for medium rare, turning and basting with reserved 1/2 cup marinade.  Cut steak diagonally into thin slices.

To Serve:  Spread sour cream mixture over warm tortillas.  Top each with 2 ounces of sliced steak and salsa.  Fold in half.

Makes 12 tortillas.
