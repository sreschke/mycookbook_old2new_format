Carole Kotkin is Food Editor for Wine News Magazine, Manager of The Cooking School at Ocean Reef Club in Key Largo. She is co-author of
MMMiami?Tempting Tropical Tastes for Home Cooks Everywhere, Henry Holt Publishers. 

1 lemon
2 tablespoons fresh rosemary leaves, or 1 tablespoon dried
rosemary leaves, crumbled
1 garlic clove, finely minced
1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 teaspoon salt 
2 tablespoons olive oil
4 whole chicken breasts, boned, halved, skin on
rosemary sprigs and lemon wedges for garnish

Remove lemon zest with a grater and squeeze lemon. Combine zest, lemon juice, rosemary, garlic, pepper, salt, and olive oil in a shallow dish. Wash chicken under cold running water. Pat dry with
paper towels.  Add chicken to marinade and toss to coat well.  Marinate at least one hour or refrigerate overnight, turning occasionally. Light a charcoal grill.  Pat chicken dry and discard
marinade. Grill over medium heat, for 7 to 10 minutes each side, until skin is golden and meat is tender.  Cool to room temperature. Can be prepared two days ahead and refrigerated. 
Garnish with rosemary sprigs and lemon wedges.  Serve with garlic pita chips.  Serves 8.
