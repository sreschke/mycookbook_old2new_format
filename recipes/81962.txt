1 stick butter, melted
1 pkg Ore-Ida hash brown, defrosted
1 can cream of potato soup
1 8ox ctn sour cream
Topping: 2 c crushed cornflakes. 1/2 stick butter melted
Mix all the above and put into a baking dish.  Put crushed cornflakes on top of casserole and pour melted butter over them.  Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.  This freezes well
