As served at the St. Francis hotel in   San Francisco

1 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup ketchup-based chili sauce
1/4 cup minced scallion
2 tbsps minced pitted green olives
2 tsps. fresh lemon juice
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1 tsp. bottled horse radish
 salt and pepper to taste.
  
Whisk all dressing ingredients together.  Pick over 1-1/2 lbs jumbo lump crabmeat, then divide among 4 plates lined with shredded  lettuce.  Garnish with capers, wedges of tomato, hard-boilded egg and lemon.  Serve napped with dressing.
