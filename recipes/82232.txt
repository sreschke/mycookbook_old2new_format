2 pounds stewing beef, cubed
1 envelope Sloppy Joe seasoning
8 oz. can tomato sauce
1 green pepper, chopped
1/2 cup water
2 tbsp. vinegar
salt and pepper to taste

Place all ingredients in crock pot. Stir to mix up and cover. Cook on low for 8-9 hours or high for 4 hours. Serve over rice or noodles.
