1 enevelope onion soup mix
2 lbs all purpose potatoes cut into large chunks
1/2 cup olive or vegetable oil.
Preheat oven to 450.  In large plastic bag or bowl, add all ingredients.  Close bag and shake, or toss in bowl, until potatoes are evenly coated.  In 13 x 9 inch baking pan or roasting pan, arrange potatoes; discard bag. Bake uncovered, stirring occasionally, 4o minutes or until potatoes are tender and golden brown.  Makes about 4 servings.
