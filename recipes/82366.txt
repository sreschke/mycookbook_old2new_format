
Prep/Cook Time: 20 min.

1 tbsp. vegetable oil
4 boneless pork chops, 3/4 inch thick
1 can (10 3/4 oz.) Campbell's Cream of Mushroom or 98% Fat Free Cream of Mushroom Soup
1/2 soup can milk
1 pkg. (1 oz.) ranch salad dressing mix
Paprika
Ranch-Style Rice


HEAT oil in skillet. Add chops and cook until browned.
ADD soup, milk and 1/2 pkg. salad dressing mix. Heat to a boil. Cover and cook over low heat 10 min. or until done. Sprinkle with paprika.
SERVE with Ranch-Style Rice. Serves 4.
 Ranch-Style Rice: Add remaining salad dressing mix to water when making rice.
