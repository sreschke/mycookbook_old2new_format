3 or 4 lb beef chuck roast
water to cover
1 large chopped onion
1/2 can tomato sauce
salt and pepper to taste
minced garlic
2 tbsp pickling spices tied in bag
cabbage
French bread
mint sprigs

Place all ingredients except cabbage, bread, and mint in large pot. Simmer 3-4 hours.  During last half hour of cooking, add cabbage and cook until tender. 

Place a slice of French bread in a large bowl. Put a sprig of mint on bread. Pour broth, meat and cabbage over bread.  DELISH!!!!!!!
