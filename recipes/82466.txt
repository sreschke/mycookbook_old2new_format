Di's PORK CHOP CASSEROLE 


6 Pork Chops 
2 medium onions 
5 medium potatoes 
2 cans Cream of Mushroom Soup 

Brown Pork Chops in a small amount of oil. Drain. Put your pork chops on bottom of large baking dish (13X9). Add your onions, thickly sliced on top of chops. Then add your potatoes thickly sliced on top of onions. In small bowl, add one can of water to your two cans of soup. Mix well, then pour over casserole. Cover with foil and cook for one hour at 350 degrees. (Or until potatoes are soft)
