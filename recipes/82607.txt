6 oz small shell pasta, cooked and drained
1 can (15oz) black eyed peas, rinsed and drained
1 c sliced green onions
3/4 c diced seeded peeled cucumber
3/4 c diced green pepper
3/4 c diced seeded tomato
1 small jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped
DRESSING
3 tbs canola oil
1/4 c red wine vinegar or cider vinegar
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp dried basil
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp hot pepper sauce
1/2 tsp seasoned salt
In a salad bowl, combine the first seven ingredients.  In a jar with a tight fitting lid, combine the oil, vinegar, sugar, basil, chili powder, hot pepper sauce and seasoned salt; shake well.  Pour over salad and stir to coat. over and refrigerate for at least 2 hurs before serving.
