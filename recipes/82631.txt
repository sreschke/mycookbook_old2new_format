1 can(6 1/2 or 7oz) tuna
1 can (10  1/2 oz) condensed cream of celery soup
3/4 cup precooked rice
2 egg yolks- slightly beaten
1/4 cup milk
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 tablespoons chopped canned pimiento
2 teaspoons instant miced onion
2. egg whites stiffly beaten
1/2 cup milk
1 tablespoon snipped parsley

Drain tuna; break in chunks. Combine with half the soup, rice, egg yolks, 1/4 cup milk, lemon juice, pimiento and onion. Fold in egg whites. Turn into a greased 10x6x1 1/2 baking dish. Bake in a moderate oven (350) for 20 to 25 minutes, or till set

Serve with parsley sauce; Heat remaining soup with 1/2 cup milk and the snipped parsley Makes 6 servings
