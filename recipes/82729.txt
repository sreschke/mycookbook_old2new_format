Chicken Paprikash (The Jewish Cookbook by Pauline Frankel) 

1/4 cup chicken fat
3 onions, chopped
4-lb chicken, cut up
2 Tbs Hungarian sweet paprika
salt to taste
1 green bell pepper, thinly sliced
1 tomato, seeded and diced
1 tsp tomato paste 

Saute onions in chicken fat in a 4-quart pot until golden brown. Lower  heat to medium and add chicken, paprika, and 1 teaspoon salt. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes. Stir several times, and add a little water only if necessary to keep from scorching. Add green peppers, diced tomato, tomato paste, and 1 cup water. Cover pan and simmer for 50 minutes, stirring and adding water if needed to prevent from scorching or sticking to pan. Adjust seasonings. Serve hot. 
Serves 4
