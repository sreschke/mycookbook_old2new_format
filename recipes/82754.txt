4-6 Dried Chinese mushrooms, soaked
52 g. bean thread noodles, soaked
oil for frying
2 garlic cloves, chopped
2 red chilies, seeded and chopped
8 oz. mjinced pork
2 oz chopped cooked prawns
1 tsp. sugar
1 carrot, finely shredded
2 oz bamboo shoots, chopped
2 oz bean sprouts
2 spring onions, chopped
1 Tb. chopped fresh coriander
2 Tb. flour
Phyllo

freshly ground black pepper
spring onions, carrots and red chilies, cut in slivers to garnish
sweet chili sauce, to serve

Drain and chop the mushrooms.  Cut the drained noodles into short lengths, aboutg 2 inches.

Heat 2 Tb. oil in a wok or frying pan; add the chopped garlic and chilies and fry for 30 seconds.  Add the minced pork, stirring until the meat is browned.

Add the noodles, mushrooms and prawns to the wok or frying pan.  Season with fish sauce, sugar and freshly ground black pepper.  Tip the mixture into a bowl.  Mix in the carrots, bamboo shoots, bean sprouts, spring onions and chopped fresh coriander until well combined.

Layer two sheets of phyllo on top of one another.  Cut into quarters.  Brush layers with melted butter.  Place a spoonful of the pork mixture on the phyllo and fold over to encase filling and roll up.  Freeze at this point or bake in 375 convection temperature oven for 15-20 minutes.  Serve with sauce.

SAUCE
1 part fish sauce
2-3 parts sugar
1 part vinegar - seasoned rice vinegar
grated carrots
chili flakes
