Asparagus with Italian Walnut Sauce

3 hard boiled egg yolks
1 cup walnuts toasted and chopped
3/4 cup chopped Italian parsley
2 large cloves garlic. finely minced
1 cup extra virgin olive oil
salt and pepper to taste
4 Tbs Mediterranean style black olives, chopped and pitted
1 Tbs capers

Whisk together all ingredients in a bowl. Serve over steamed asparagus. 
Makes 2 cups, serves 4. 
Note: You can add lemon juice or vinegar for flavor. For a vegan dish, use chopped almonds instead of eggs.
