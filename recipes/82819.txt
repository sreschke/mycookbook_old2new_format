Zucchini Pudding - Dairy 

3-1/2 lbs zucchini, grated
1 medium onion,  minced
1 garlic clove, minced
olive oil cooking spray
1 small carrot. peeled and grated
3 shredded fresh basil leaves 
1 tsp dried basil
1 Tbs chopped Italian parsley
3/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
4 Tbs fresh grated KLP Parmesan 
4 large egg whites, lightly beaten
1/2 cup matzo meal

Place grated zucchini in a colander, lightly sprinkle with 1/4 teaspoon salt. Allow to drain 10 minutes. 
Heat an 11'" non-stick skillet and coat with cooking spray. Saut‚ onion until soft, add garlic, cook one minute. Add carrot, season to taste with salt and pepper, cover and cook 5 minutes. Drain zucchini, add to pot, cover and cook until very tender, about 20 minutes. Uncover, cook and stir to evaporate any excess moisture. Remove from heat, add 2 tablespoons matzo meal, basil and parsley, cool. 
Add lightly beaten egg whites and 3 tablespoons parmesan. Place matzo meal in processor for 30 seconds to refine. Preheat oven to 450ø F. Oil a 9" square baking dish, sprinkle with half the remaining matzo meal. Pour in zucchini mixture, sprinkle top with remaining matzo meal and 1 tablespooon parmesan. Bake for 20 to 30 minutes. 
Serve hot or at room temperature. 
Serves 8
