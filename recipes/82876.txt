1 can (29 ounces) sliced peaches, drained
1/2c butter or margarine, softened
1 package white or sour cream white cake mix
1/2c flaked coconut
1/2c sugar
1 t cinnamon
1c dairy sour cream
2 egg yolks or 1 egg

Heat oven to 350*.  Drain the peaches thoroughly on paper towels.  Cut the butter into the cake mix (dry) until crumbly.  Mix in the coconut.  Pat the mixture lightly into an ungreased 13x9x2 cake pan, building up the edges slightly.  Bake 10 minutes.
     Arrange the peach slices on the warm crust.  Mix the sugar and the cinnamon; sprinkle over peaches.  Blend the sour cream and egg yolks; drizzle over the peaches. (Topping will not completely cover the peaches.)  Bake 25 minutes or until dthe edges or light brown.  (Do not overbake.)
Serve warm.  12 servings.
