6 lean pork chops 
1-1/4 oz. dry spaghetti sauce mix 
1 can (15 or 16 oz.) tomatoes 
1 tbsp. brown sugar 
1 green pepper, cut in rings 
Directions: 
Spray crock pot with Pam. brown the pork chops and season as desired. Place chops in bottom of crock 
pot. Mix the dry spaghetti sauce and brown sugar with the canned tomatoes. Pour the sauce mix over the top then cover with the green pepper rings. Cover. Cook on high for 1 hour, turn to low for 6-8 hours. The pork chops can be cut with a fork and the sauce is delicious! 
Makes 4-6 servings.
