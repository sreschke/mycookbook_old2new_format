1 1/2 lb fresh or 1 can long Asparagus, drained
4 boneless chicken breast
2 Tbs. oil
1/2 teas. salt
1/4 teas. pepper
1 can cream chicken or mushroom soup, undiluted
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 teas. lemon juice
1/2 teas. curry powder
1/2 - 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (optional)

Place cooked or canned Drained Asparagus into a greased baking dish (9" sq.).  Salt & pepper chicken. In oil, brown chicken on both sides.  Place chicken over asparagus.  Combine soup, mayonnaise, lemon juice and curry powder; pour over chicken. 
Cover and bake at 375 for 40 min. or until tender and chicken juices run clear.
Sprinkle with cheese.  Let stand 5 min.
