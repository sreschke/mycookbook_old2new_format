1 c. uncooked whole grain rice
1 1/4 c. sugar
1 1/2 t. vanilla
8 c. milk
   raisins (optional)

In large oven-safe bowl, combine all ingredients.  
Bake at 350 degrees for about 2 hours.
Stir occasionally, when brown film forms on top.
Serve in bowl with a little milk.    Delicious!!
