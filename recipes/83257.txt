Put a chuck roast in a crock pot.
Cover with a 2 liter of Coke.
Cook until it falls apart.
Drain off liquid
Pull apart in shreds

You can also do this with pork roast.

Add BBQ sauce:

Add equal amnts. of ketchup & Open Pitt(original recipe)
To that add:
Honey
Worchestershire sauce
Soy Sauce
Liquid smoke
Tabasco sauce

This sauce is also excellant to use on ribs while you are grilling them; & also chicken wings.

Simple & superb!
