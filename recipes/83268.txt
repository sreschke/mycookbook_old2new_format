4 c. low fat milk
2 c. diced, peeled baking potatoes
1 t. Cajun seasoning for seafood
1/4 t. salt
1/4 t. pepper
2 cans (14 3/4 oz each) cream style corn
1 lb. large shrimp, peeled and deveined

1. In a dutch oven, coine the milk, potatoes, seasonings: bring to boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes. Stir frequently.
2. Stir in the corn and ring to a boil.
3. Add the shrimp and cook for 2 minutes or until the shrimp are done.
Makes 4 servings.
Serve with pumernickel brand and green salad.
