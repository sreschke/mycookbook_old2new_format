1 pkg beef short ribs (can use chicken)
7 eggs
1/2 (scant) cup milk
1 cup butter
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
3-1/2 cups flour (add additional flourto make stiff dough if necessary)


Beat eggs in blender.
Combine flour, salt, baking powder.  Mix well.
Make well in flour.  Pour in beaten eggs.
Squeeze butter into eggs.  
Mix well working flour in slowly.
Roll out and cut with noodle cutter.
Spread out, sprinkle flour lightly over noodles and let dry about two hours.
Drop into boiling stew water (requires a large pot).
Cook on medium heat about 30 minutes.
