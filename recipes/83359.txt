1 pkg boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 t. margarine
1/2 c. chopped onion
2 cloves garlic
1/2 c. chopped artichoke hearts
2 T. dry bread crumbs
2 T. parmesan cheese
1/4 t. pepper
1 egg white

Heat oven to 375. Spray pan w/nonstick spray. Cook onion & garlic i margarine. Add artichokes until hot. Remove & add remaining ingredients (except chicken). Rinse chicken, dry & pound to 1/2" thickness. Spread about 2T. stuffing onto each and roll up. Secure w/toothpick. Bake about 45 min.
