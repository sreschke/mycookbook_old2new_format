  8 oz.   broad noodles, broken up
1/2 cup   chopped walnuts
  1 cup   chopped apples
1/2 cup   raisins
1/2 tsp.  cinnamon
1/4 tsp.  nutmeg
  3       eggs - separated
  3 Tbsp. sugar
1/2 tsp.  salt

- Cook noodles as directed. Drain and rinse in cold
  water.
- Combine nuts, apples, raisins and spices together.
- Beat egg yolks with butter, sugar and salt until
  well blended.
- Fold the egg yolk mixture into the noodles, then
  fold in the nut/apple/raisin/spice mixture.
- Beat egg whites until stiff.
- Fold into the noodle mixture.

Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
