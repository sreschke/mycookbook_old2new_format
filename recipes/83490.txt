Pad Thai in a Hurry

Ingredients:

2 Tbs. vegetable oil
1/4 cup sugar
1 Tbs. white vinegar
1 Tbs. soy sauce
2 tsp. crushed red pepper
2 pkgs. Ramen noodles, cooked (discard flavor packet)
1 Tbs. peanut oil
2 garlic cloves, minced
1/4 cup sesame seeds

Preparation:

Mix vegetable oil, sugar, vinegar, soy sauce and red pepper in a bowl to make the sauce. In a large skillet, brown noodles, peanut oil, garlic and sesame seeds. Toss the two parts together and garnish with chives and peanuts.
