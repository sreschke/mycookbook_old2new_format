2 cups sugar
pinch of salt
1 stick butter
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/4 cup cocoa
3 cups oatmeal
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp. vanilla

In 2 quart saucepan, mix sugar, salt, butter, cocoa, milk, peanut butter on medium heat until it boils. Wait one minute, then add oatmeal and vanilla. Drop by spoon on wax paper. Cool until not soft.
Enjoy!!
