Baked Breadloaf
serves 4
 
1 small unsliced round loaf of French bread
3/4 pound ground beef
1/2 chopped onion
1/2 green pepper, chopped
Bread crumbs, from the inside of the loaf
4 tablespoons chili sauce
1/2 teaspoon chili powder
Garlic powder
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
Salt and pepper
Parmesan cheese
 
Slice a shallow top off the loaf of bread. Scoop out the center leaving a crusty shell. Reserve the crumbs.
Brown the ground beef in a skillet, drain off fat. Add onions and peppers and continue cooking until onion is limp
and tender crisp. Add the bread crumbs, chili sauce, chili and garlic powders, seasoning, salt and pepper to taste.
Blend well and stuff mixture into the bread shell. Cover with cheese and replace the top.
Wrap the loaf in foil, sealing tightly. Place in a 425 degree oven and bake for 15 to 20 minutes.
Cut into 4 fat wedges.
