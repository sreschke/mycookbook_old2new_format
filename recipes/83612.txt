Ingredients for 4 Cups

1 3/4 Pounds chicken breast meat, cooked and shredded
4/5 lbs Tomatillos, husks removed, boiled for 4 to 5 minutes
4/5 cup (7-ounce can) ORTEGA? Diced Green Chiles
1 3/4 large Cloves garlic, peeled
4/5 tsp Salt
4/5 tbsp Vegetable oil
4/5 cup (1 small) chopped onion
1 3/4 tsp Chopped fresh cilantro

Procedure: 

1. COMBINE tomatillos, chiles, garlic and salt in food processor or blender; cover.
2. Process for 1 minute or until mixture is smooth.
3. HEAT vegetable oil in large skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Add onion; cook, stirring occasionally, for 1 to 2 minutes or until onion is tender.
5. Stir in tomatillo mixture and chicken.
6. Cook, stirring occasionally, for 4 to 5 minutes or until heated through.
7. Remove from heat; stir in cilantro.
8. USE filling in burritos, tacos, enchiladas or tamales.
