Strawberry Chantilly Salad

1 pkg. Jello pudding and Pie filling, vanilla flavor
1 pkg. (3 oz.) Jello, strawberry flavor
2 1/2 cups water
1 envelope Dream Whip Whipped topping mix
or use 1 container (4 1/2 oz) Cool Whip
1 cup sliced sweetened strawberries

Combine pudding mix, gelatin and water in a saucepan.  Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture comes to a full boil and is clear.  Chill until slightly thickened.  Prepare whipped topping mix as directed on package and thoroughly blend into chilled pudding mixture.  Chill until firm - about 3 hours.  Spoon into individul dessert dishes. Top with strawberries.  Makes 6 to 8 servings.
