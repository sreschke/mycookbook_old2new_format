Arrange in ungreased 9 x 13 pan: 1 angel food loaf cake cut in 1" slices.
Beat together: 1/2 cup cold milk
1 (5.1 oz) instant vanilla pudding
Add and beat in: 1 pint softened vanilla ice cream
Pour above over cake and chill.
Dissolve 6 oz pkg strawberry jello in 1 cup boiling water.  
Stir in: 2 (10 oz) pkgs frozen strawberries
Chill till partially set and spoon over pudding mixture. Chill till firm. 12 servings
