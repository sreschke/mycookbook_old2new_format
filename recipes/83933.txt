Pour 1 1/2 cups boiling water over 1 cup quick oats let stand for 20 minutes.

Cream together the following:
1/2 cup oleo
1 cup Sugar
1 cub Brown Sugar
2 Eggs
1 tsp. Vanilla

      Sift together and add to above mixture.

1 tsp. Salt
1 1/2 cups Flour
1 tsp. Soda
1 tsp. Baking Powder

      Mix in oats to above mixtures.

TOPPING:

1/4 lb. Softened Oleo
2/3 cup Brown Sugar
1 cup Coconut
1/4 cup Cream or Canned Milk

      Mix well and spread on baked cake, put under broiler and let brown--nuts if desired.
