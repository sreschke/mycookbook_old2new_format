2 cups Peanut Butter, creamy
3 cups Rice Krispies
1 Stick Oleo
3 cups Powdered Sugar

DIPPING CHOCOLATE:

1 (12 oz.) pkg. Chocolate Chips
1/2 Stick of Paraffin

      Mix together well and form mixture into balls with the hands.  Freeze.  Melt the chips and paraffin, dip the balls and store in the refrigerator.
