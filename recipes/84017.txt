1 can Refrigerated Biscuits
1 Egg White, slightly beaten
4 tsp. Poppy Seed or Sesame Seed

      Cut each biscuit into quarters; shape into pencil-like strips.  Dip in egg whites.  Place on ungreased cookie sheet.  Sprinkle with poppy seed or sesame seed.  Bake at 400' for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
