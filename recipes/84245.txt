1 1/4 c. crushed pineapple
1 c. sugar
1 pkg strawberry-banana jello (small box)
1 c. grated carrots
1 c. chopped pecans
1 c. whipped topping
1 c. hot water

Dissolve jello in hot water.  Boil together the crushed pineapple and sugar.  Add this while hot to jello.  Let cool.  Add carrots and pecans; place in refrigerator.  When almost set, fold in whipped topping.  Keep in fridge.
