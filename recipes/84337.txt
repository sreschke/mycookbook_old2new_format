          2 cups cooked, mashed sweet potatoes;  about 2 medium sweet potatoes
          3/4 cups sugar
          1 Tbs. all-purpose flour
          1/2 tsp. baking powder
          1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg
          1/4 cup butter or margarine
          1 egg; slightly beaten
          1 Tbs. lemon juice
          1 tsp. vanilla
          1 1/4 cup milk or evaporated milk
    

          Pre-heat to 425^     Combine sugar, flour, baking powder, and  nutmeg.  Add the remaining ingredients. and beat until well blended. Bake 15 minutes.  Reduce heat to 350^.   Bake until the knife comes out clean from the center or about 45 minutes.
