15 chicken wings
4 tbsp. soy sauce
1 tbsp. sesame oil
2 tbsp. oyster sauce
1 tbsp. dried basil
2 tsp. garlic salt
2 tsp. onion powder
1 tsp. black pepper

In bowl, combine soy sauce, sesame oil, oyster sauce, garlic salt, onion powder, pepper and basil.  Add chicken and cover in refrigerator overnight.  Stir the next day before grilling.  On heated grill, place chicken wings one inch apart and cook till juices run clear.  Marinate between turning chicken, discard the rest of marinade.
