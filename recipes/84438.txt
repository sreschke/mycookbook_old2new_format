Mix:
1 pkg Hidden Valley Ranch dressing
2 (8 oz) cream cheese

Spread evenly over 10 tortilla shells (soft shell)

Chop:
1 bunch green onion
1 (4 oz) jar diced (red) pimentos
1/2 c chopped jalapeno's
1 snall can chopped black olives

Mix above ingredients together and spread evenly over the soft shell.  Roll up and wrap in plastic wrap and chill 1 hour.  When ready to serve, slice about 1 inch thick.  Can be served with crackers.
