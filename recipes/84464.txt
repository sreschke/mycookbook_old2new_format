Spicy Chicken Wings

                                                                                       
                                     2 lbs (1 kg) chicken wings, tips removed
                                     2 Tbsp (30 mL) Canada Corn Starch
                                     1/4 cup (50 mL) Mazola Corn Oil
                                     1/3 cup (75 mL) Heinz Ketchup
                                     1/3 cup (75 mL) corn syrup
                                     1/4 cup (50 mL) soy sauce
                                     1 tsp (5 mL) minced fresh ginger*
                                     1 clove garlic, minced
                                     1 to 2 tsp (5 to 10 mL) crushed red pepper flakes
                                      
                                     Cut wings in half at joint. In large bowl, toss chicken with corn starch.

                                     In large frypan heat corn oil over medium-high heat. Add wings & stir-fry until
                                     cooked & browned on all sides, about 10 to 15 min. Drain off excess fat.

                                     Combine ketchup, corn syrup, soy sauce, ginger, garlic & hot pepper flakes.
                                     Add to frypan & continue stir-frying until wings are well glazed.

                                     Makes 4 servings.

                                     *Or substitute 1/2 tsp (2 mL) ground dried ginger.
