1 lb gound beef
4 cups peeled, cubed potatoes
1 small onion, chopped
3 cans (8oz) tomatoe sauce
4 cups of water
1 tsp salt
11/2 tsp pepper
1/2 tsp hot pepper sauce
In a dutch oven or a big kettle brown ground beef. Drain off fat, add potatoes, onion and tomato sauce. 
Stir in water, salt, pepper, and hot pepper sauce;
Bring to a boil, turn down heat, simmer 1 hour or until potatoes are tender and the soup is thickened. 

Yield:  6 to 8 depending on appetite      (2 quarts)
