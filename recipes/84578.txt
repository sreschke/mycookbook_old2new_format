1 pound dry split peas
2-1/2 qts water
1 meaty ham bone
1-1/2 c chopped onion
1 c each diced celery, carrots and potatoes
1 tsp dried parsley flakes
1/2 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp garlic salt
1/4 tsp dried marjoram
Salt to taste

In a Dutch oven or soup kettle, place the peas, water and ham bone; bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 2 hours, stirring occasionally.  Stir in the remaining ingredients.  Bring to a boil.  Reduce heat; cover and simmer for 30 min or until vegetables are tender.  Remove ham bone; when cool enough to handle, remove meat from the bone.  Chop ham and return to the soup; heat through.  Yield:  (3 qts)
