Serves 4
Preparation time: 5 minutes
Cooking time: 20 minutes (mostly unattended)

4 chicken breast fillets, about 500g
1/3 cup honey
2 tablespoons grainy mustard
1 teaspoon crushed garlic

Spray or brush an overproof dish with a little oil. Place chicken fillets in a single layer. Mix honey, mustard and garlic together and spread over chicken. Cover with baking paper or aluminium foil. 

Bake chicken at 180oC for 10 minutes. Uncover, baste with the juices, and cook for a further 10 minutes or until tender. Serve with steamed or microwaved snow peas, corn and baked jacket potatoes. 

Nutrition score per serve 4.7g fat, 5.2g fibre and 1660 kilojoules (397 Calories).
