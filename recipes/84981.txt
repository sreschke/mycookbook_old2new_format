Beat: 
1 C butter
1 1/2 C sugar
Add:
4 eaggs, one at a time and beat after each egg
Stir in:
1 t almond extract and 2 C flour, unsifted
Spread in 12 x 15 pan. With tip of knife, mark off the batter in 18 equal pieces. Spoon equal portion of chery pie filling in center of each square. Bake at 350* for 35-40 minute Dust with powder sugar
If using bluebery or apple pie filling use 1 t vanilla instead of almond
Joni Schaffeld-Delgado
