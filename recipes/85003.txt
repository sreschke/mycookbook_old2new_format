1 White Cake Mix
Oreo Cookie Crumbs or Crushed Oreo Cookies
Cool Whip
Chocolate Sauce (Hershey's)


Prepare white cake mix according to package directions.  Add 3/4 C. crushed Oreo Cookies or crumbs (stir in).  Spread in 9 X 13 inch pan and bake according to package directions.  Let cool completely.  Frost with 1 bowl cool whip.  Drizzle with chocolate sauce.
