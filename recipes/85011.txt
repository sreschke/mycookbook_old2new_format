 
  Sauce:
 Prepare Lawry's enchilada sauce mix according to package directions, using tomato paste and 3 cups water.
 Each recipe uses 8 corn tortillas.

 Filling:
 Combine all ingredients except 3/4 cup cheese used for topping.

 Ingredients for filling;
 1 pound beef, browned, fat drained
 1 teaspoon lawry's seasond salt
 2 1/2 cups grated Monterey jack or Cheddar cheese
 1 can sliced ripe olives

 To Assemble:
 Pour 1/2 cup prepared enchilada sauce into a 12x8x2" baking dish. Dip each tortilla into enchilada sauce. Place about 1/2 cup filling in center of each tortilla. Fold sides over the filling;place in dish,seam side down. Pour remaining sauce over enchiladas, top with reserver cheese. Bake in 350 oven 20 minutes. Sour cream and olives can be used as garnish.
