2 cans Old El Paso green chile enchilada sauce
2 shredded boneless chicken breasts
12 corn or flour tortillas
1 cup mozzarella or Mexican blend cheese, shredded

Heat oven to 350 deg.  Cook and shredd chicken.
Combine chicken and one can of sauce. Spray or
lightly grease baking dish.

Fill tortillas with chicken mixture and roll tightly and place side by side in baking dish.

Pour remaining sauce and second can of sauce over
tortillas.  Cover with cheese.

Bake for 30 minutes until cheese is melted.
