Chicken Salad Sandwich - San Jose California - # 14

1 cup chicken, chopped and cooked 
1/2 cup celery, chopped
2 Tablespoons mayonnaise
1/8 teaspoon onion powder
1/8 teaspoon tarragon 
1 dash garlic powder

Combine the mayonnaise and seasoning in small bowl. Stir in the chicken and the celery and combine well. Spread the mixture on sandwich bread and top with lettuce. Makes 4 sandwiches.

Note: This was found in San Jose, California.
