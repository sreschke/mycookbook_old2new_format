1 lb ground beef
1 c chopped onion
1 c celery, diced
3 tbs margaine
1 6 or 8 oz pkg. noodles, cooked and drained
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
 1 c milk
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper

Brown beef, onions and celery in margarine.  Arrange beef mixture and noodles in layers.  Combine soups, milk and seasonings.  Pour over beef and noodles.  Cover and bake at 325 for 1 hour.
