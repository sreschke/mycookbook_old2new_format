1 pound ground beef
1 frozen package (10 ounces) spinach, drained and cooked 
1 onion, chopped
4 eggs, beaten
1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
*2 cups mushrooms, cooked* (optional)
salt to taste
pepper to taste
*1 teaspoon basil, dried* (optional)

Brown ground beef and chopped onions in the skillet and season with salt and pepper. Drain off the grease. Add the spinach and mushrooms* and mix well. Pour in the beaten eggs and sprinkle with the *basil*. Stir while cooking. Remove from the heat when the eggs are set. Sprinkle with the parmesan cheese. Serve while hot. Makes 4 servings.

HISTORY NOTE: This California dish (Joe's Special) originated in San Fransico, California in the 1970's.
