3 Tbls. butter or margarine
1/2 (of 18.25 oz package) white cake mix, dry      (about 2 cups)
1 1/2 cups mini marshmallows
6 oz. chocolate chips
1 1/3 cups (3.5 oz.) coconut
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 can (14 oz.) sweetened condensed milk

Lightly spray sides of pan first, then in 350* oven, melt butter in 9 x 13 pan; tilt pan to completely cover the bottom with butter.  Sprinkle cake mix over butter, then the following ingredients as listed, ending with pouring the milk evenly over all.  

Bake about 30 minutes or till just golden brown.  Immediately run a buttered knife around edges to loosen.  Let cool completely before cutting.
