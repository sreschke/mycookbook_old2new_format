Greek Potato Salad 

3 medium potatoes 
1-1/2 oz sun-dried tomatoes, chopped 
1 cup sliced seedless cucumber
1/2 cup red onion; sliced 
4 oz feta cheese, crumbled (or more) 
1/2 cup Greek olives, pitted
1 clove garlic, minced
1 Tbs fresh oregano (1 tsp dried)
salt and pepper to taste 

Lemon Dressing
1 cup extra-virgin olive oil
2-1/2 Tbs lemon juice
4 cloves garlic, minced 
1/4 cup chopped fresh oregano (2 tsp dried)
salt and pepper 

Slice potatoes 1/4" thick and boil until tender. Mix with salad ingredients. Mix dressing ingredients, pour over salad and toss gently so as not to break up potatoes too much.
