2 cups onions, thinly sliced  
3 Tablespoons FRENCH'S? WORCESTERSHIRE Sauce  
4 hero rolls, split in half and toasted  
3/4 pound sliced roast beef  
1 container (4 ounces) garlic-flavored cheese spread 

Melt 1 tablespoon butter in medium skillet over medium-high heat. Saute onions for 5 minutes until tender. Add Worcestershire; cook 2 minutes.

Spread about 1 tablespoon cheese on each half of hero roll. Broil 30 seconds until cheese begins to brown. Layer roast beef and onions on bottom halves of hero rolls. Cover with top halves
