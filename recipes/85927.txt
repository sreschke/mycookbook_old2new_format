4 large egges, lightly beaten
2 Cups milk
2 Cups all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp salt
2 tbsps hot beef drippings, plus more to grease pans

Preheat oven to 450F. Generously grease a 13x9 baking pan or 12 popover pans; heat pans. Ina large bowl whisk together the eggs and milk.  Gradually mix in the flour and salt; stir in the beef drippings. Pour batter into prepared pans.  Bake 10 mins; reduce heat to 375F and continue baking 10 mins for popovers or 15-20 mins for baking pan.  Serve immediately with roast beef.
