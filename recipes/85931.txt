4 medium red potatoes, thinly sliced
1 small onion, thinly sliced and separated into rings
3 Tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder

Place half of the potatoes in a greased 2 qt. or 11/7/2 in. baking dish.  Top with onion and remaining potatoes; drizzle with butter.  Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, salt, pepper and garlic powder.  Bake uncovered at 450 degrees for 25-30 minutes or until potatoes are golden brown and tender.

Yield 4 servings
