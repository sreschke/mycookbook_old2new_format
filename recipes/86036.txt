1 1/2 tsp unflavored gelatin
1/3 c fro. sweetened gr. juice, thawed and undiluted
    3 c   fresh blueberries

Sprinkle gelatin over grape juice concentrate in a large saucepan, and let mixture stand 1 minute. Add blueberries, and cook over low heat, stirring constantly, until gelatin dissolves and mixture thickens slightly. Let mixture cool.

Pour jam into airtight containers, and chill until set. Store jam in refrigerator.
