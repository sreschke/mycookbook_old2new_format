Corral Barbecue Sauce 

1 tsp olive oil
3/4 cup chopped green onions
3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
14 oz crushed tomatoes
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup water
1/4 cup orange juice
2 Tbs cider vinegar
2 tsp chili sauce
1/8 tsp Worcestershire sauce 

Heat oil in pan over medium heat. Add onions and garlic. Cook and stir 5 minutes until onions are tender. Stir in remaining ingredients. Reduce heat to medium-low. Cook 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Makes 2 cups 


White Barbecue Sauce 

2 cups mayonnaise
2 Tbs black pepper
2 Tbs salt
6 Tbs lemon juice
6 Tbs vinegar
4 Tbs sugar 

Combine all ingredients. Mix well. Brush on meat during last 15 minutes of grilling. Makes 2 cups sauce. 


Smoky Sweet Barbecue Sauce 

1/2 cup ketchup
1/3 cup brown mustard
1/3 cup light molasses
1/4 cup Worcestershire Sauce
1/4 tsp liquid smoke flavoring 

Combine all ingredients. Mix well. Brush on meat during last 15 minutes of grilling. Makes approximately 1-1/2 cups.

Cocoa-Cola Barbecue Sauce
 
1 cup Coca-Cola 
1/4 lb margarine 
2 cups Worcestershire sauce 
1 Tbs salt 
1-1/4 cups ketchup 
4 tsp sugar 
1 to 2 Tbs freshly ground black pepper 
Combine all ingredients in pot and bring to boil over low heat. Cook, stirring frequently, at least 30 minutes until sauce thickens. Brush onto foods as they are grilled.

1-1/2 cups ketchup
1/8 cup packed brown sugar

Whisk together all ingredients. Brush on poultry while grilling. Makes 2 cups. 

Jamaican Jerk Sauce 

1/4 cup fresh chopped cilantro
2 Tbs coarsely chopped fresh ginger
2 Tbs black pepper
2 Tbs lime juice
3 cloves garlic
1 Tbs ground allspice
1/2 tsp curry powder
1/4 tsp ground cloves
1/8 tsp ground red pepper 

Combine all ingredients in food processor or blender; process until thick and pasty. Makes about 1/4 cup. Rub sauce onto chicken. Refrigerate several hours before grilling over low heat.

Jack Daniels BBQ Sauce 

1 tsp onion powder
1 Tbs Tabasco 
2 Tbs red wine vinegar
1/4 cup Jack Daniel's
2 cups brown sugar
1/4 cup water 
2 large beef bouillion cubes (or 4 small) 
2 Tbs worcestershire sauce 

Combine all ingredients in a small sauce pan. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes. Allow to cool and use as a sauce on any grilled meat or fish --especially salmon!

FIERY BBQ SAUCE FOR CHICKEN 

10-3/4 oz apricot jam
1 cup ketchup
1/4 cup cider vinegar
1 Tbs soy sauce
1/3 tsp ginger
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 tsp cayenne pepper 

In medium non-aluminum saucepan, warm jam over medium heat, stirring until melted, about 2 minutes. Stir in remaining ingredients and simmer, stirring frequently, 5 minutes. Let sauce cool before using to marinate. 
Use for basting chicken. Store sauce in covered jar in refrigerator for up to 7 days.
