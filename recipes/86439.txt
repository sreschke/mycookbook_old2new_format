Strawberry cake 

8 oz minature marshmallows 
1 box (2 layer size) white or yellow cake mix 
4 cups fresh strawberries 
1 box strawberry gelatin 

Grease oblong pan. Put marshmallows in bottom of pan. Mix cake mix as directed. Mash strawberries and dry gelatin together. Pour strawberries over top of batter. Bake as directed on cake mix box.
