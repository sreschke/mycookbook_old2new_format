Baked Feta Cheese Apetizer 

1 block feta cheese
1/3 cup herb flavored olive oil
1/2 tsp crushed red pepper
1/4 tsp finely grated lemon zest
3 cloves of garlic, pressed
1/4 tsp seasoned salt, or regular salt
1/4 tsp freshly ground pepper
3-5 Tbs fresh oregano leaves 

Place all ingredients except feta in a medium size plastic zip-lock bag. Tip the bag back and forth to mix contents, then add feta. Zip bag, break feta into large chunks while turning. Marinating for 3-5 hours at room temperature. Preheat oven to 375Ã¸F. Place feta and marinating ingredients into a pretty baking/serving dish. Bake for 10-15 minutes until feta becomes soft and tawny. Serve with crusty bread and your favorite wine.
