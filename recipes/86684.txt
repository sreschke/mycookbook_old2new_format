1 dozen Roma tomatoes
2 toes garlic, thinly sliced
3 green onions (tops only), thinly sliced
1 bunch fresh basil
olive oil
salt (preferably Kosher)
white pepper
balsamic vinegar (optional)
1 T capers (optional)
1/8 cup toasted pine nuts

Cut Romas in half lengthwise. With a spoon,
scrape out and discard pulp. Dice and put into
bowl. Add sliced garlic, green onions, capers. 
Stack clean, dry basil leaves, slice thinly and
add to mix. Lightly drizzle with olive oil. Add
salt, white pepper (go easy!) and balsamic vinegar
to taste. Top with pine nuts.

Serve with warm mozzarella cheese bread.
