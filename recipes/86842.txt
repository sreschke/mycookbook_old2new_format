Willy's Stroopwaffels
Recipe By : Willy Dean
Serving Size : 24 
Preparation Time :1:00
categories : Desserts Tried & True
4 cups flour
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup butter
2 eggs
1 package yeast
1/2 cup water -- 
warm SYRUP:1-1/2 cups brown sugar
1 cup butter
1 teaspoon cinnamon
6 tablespoons dark corn syrup

Dissolve yeast in warm water. Cut 1 cup butter into flour. Mix in sugar, eggs, and yeast mixture. Mix well. Set aside to rise 30-60minutes. Roll dough into balls and bake in pizelle iron. Make syrup. Boil all ingredients till at soft-ball stage. Split waffles in half and spread cut sides with warm syrup. Then put the halves back together.
