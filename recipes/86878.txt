2 # tomatoes, chopped (may be blanched/peeled) 
2 # sugar
2   lemons, thinly sliced
1 oz preserved ginger, minced

Mix tomatoes and sugar and let sit til juicy.
Pour into large pan, bring to a boil. Lower heat to         simmer.  Add lemon slices and ginger.
Simmer one hour or until tomatoes/lemons are translucent and syrup has thickened.
