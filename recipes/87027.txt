4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 pkg. (12 oz.) extra wide noodles
1 can (26 1/2 oz.) Del Monte Mushroom Spaghetti Sauce
1 cup (4 oz.) shredded mozzarella cheese
1/4 cup (1 oz.) grated Parmesan cheese

1.  Preheat over to 450 degrees.  Season chicken with salt and pepper, if desired.  Arrange chicken in 11x8-inch baking dish.  Bake, uncovered, 20 minutes or until chicken is done; drain.  Remove chicken from dish.  Keep warm.

2.  Cook noodles according to package direction; drain.  Reserve 1 cup spaghetti sauce and toss noodles with remaining sauce; place in baking dish.  Top with chicken.  Pour reserved sauce over chicken and sprinkle with cheeses.

3.  Bake 10 minutes or until heated through and cheese is melted.  Sprinkle with chopped parsley; if desired.
