1 can (19 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
1 sweet red pepper, diced
1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
1/2 cup diced celery
3 Tbsp. red or white wine vinegar
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 tsp. hot pepper sauce
1/2 cup chopped cucumber
1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

In large bowl, toss together beans, red pepper, cheese and celery.  In small bowl, whisk together vinegar, oil, garlic and hot pepper sauce; pour over bean mixture, tossing to coat (can be prepared to this point, covered and refrigerated for up to 2 days).  Stir in cucumber and parsley just before serving.  

Serves 4.
