

   4      lb            pork roast
   3      medium        apples
   2      cups          apple juice
     2/3  cup           brown sugar

Rub roast with salt and pepper.  Place apple quarters
in the bottom of the crockpot.  Place the roast on top
of apples.  Combine apple juice and brown sugar. 
Spoon over roast.  Cook on low for 8-10 hours. 
Thicken juice the last 30 minutes.
