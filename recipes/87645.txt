1 can Healthy Request cream of mushroom soup
8 oz. shredded cheese blend
1/3C grated Parmesan cheese
1C milk
1/4t pepper
4C cooked spiral pasta

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Mix soup, cheeses, milk and pepper in a 1 1/2-qt. casserole. Stir in pasta. Bake for 20 min. Serves 4.

9 Weight Watchers Points per serving
