6 lbs pork ribs
1 1/2 cups maple syrup
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
4 tbls catsup
2 tbls cider vinegar
2 tbls Worcestershire 
1 tsp salt
1 tsp dry mustard
   
Place ribs in a stock pot, cover with water. Simmer, covered, about 1 hour. Drain when tender, and put in shallow dish.  In a small pan, mix maple syrup, brown sugar, ketchup, vinegar, Worcestershire, salt, and mustard. Bring to a boil. Pour over ribs, and marinate in the fridge for a couple of hours.  

Prepare grill.  Transfer ribs to grill, reserve marinade. Cook over med about 20 minutes, or until tender and glazed; baste with marinating liquid, and turn occassionally. For 8 very hungry people.
