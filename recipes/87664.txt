1 3/4 cups flour
1/3 cup sugar
2 1/2 tsps baking powder
1 cup fresh or unthawed frozen bluberries
3/4 cup milk
1 egg
1/3 cup fat-free yogurt ( or melted margarine)

In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, and baking powder. Stir in blueberries. Add milk, egg and yogurt. Mix just until dry ingredients are moistened. Batter will be lumpy; do not over beat.

Spoon batter into 12  2 1/2-inch greased muffin cups and bake at 400 degrees for 25 minutes or until tops spring back when lightly touched. Serve warm. These may be frozen. Warm at room temperature, then put in microwave for 11 seconds; turn over and do another 11 seconds.
