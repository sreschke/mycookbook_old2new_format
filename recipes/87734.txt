3/4 cup chicken broth
1/4 cup soy sauce
2 garlic cloves,minced
2 tbs. cornstarch
3/4 tsp ground ginger
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
6 boneless skinless chicken breast halves, cut      into 1-inch pieces
1 tbs cooking oil
1 each medium green, yellow and sweet red peppers, cut into 1-inch pieces (may use all green if you prefer)
1/4 cup water

In a bowl, combine broth, soy sauce, garlic, cornstarch, ginger and cayenne pepper; mix well.  Add chicken, stir to coat.  Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.  Add chicken; cook and stir constantly until chicken is done.  Reduce heat to medium.  Add peppers and water and chicken marinade; cook and stir for 5-8 minutes or until peppers are tender.  Serve with rice. 6 Servings
