9 strips bacon, chopped
4 cups fresh corn kernels
1 large onion, chopped
1 red bell pepper, seeded, chopped
2 Tbsp. minced parsley
1 Tbsp. milk
Salt, pepper to taste

Cook bacon in large, heavy-bottomed skillet til bacon is crisp.  Remove to dish using slotted spoon. Add onion to drippings, saute til crisp-tender, about 4 minutes.  Add corn and rd pepper.  Saute tul corn is crisp-tender, about 5 to 10 minutes.  Stir in parsley, milk, salt, and pepper.  Garnish with crisp bacon pieces.
