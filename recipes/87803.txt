Spicy Stuffed Zuchinni
Serve this recipe as is for a side dish or add cooked ground beef
to create an entree. Serves 4

2 large Zucchini
1 1/2 teaspoons Oil
1/2 cup Onion, diced
1 teaspoon Garlic, minced
2 Tablespoons Jalapeno, diced
1/2 cup Tomatoes
1 1/2 cups tvp (textured vegetable protein) * not necessary will use 
ground beef as a filler*
1 tablespoon Chili powder
3/4 cup Monterey jack, grated
Salsa

Split the squash lengthwise. Scrap out most of the pulp. Reserve.
Heat the oil in a skillet. Add the onion and garlic. Cook until just
soft. Toss in the remaining ingredients and the reserved pulp.
Stuff the squash shells with the mixture. Bake at 350 for 40
minutes or until lightly browned. Garnish with salsa.
