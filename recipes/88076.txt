Grandma's Shortbread

2 cups butter (do not substitute margarine) 
1-1/4 cups brown sugar
1/4 cup powdered sugar
2 teaspoons vanilla
3-1/2 cups flour (or more)
1/2 cup cornstarch

Preheat oven to 300Ã¸F. Cream butter. Add sugars and vanilla. Add flour and cornstarch and knead like bread. Pat out on a floured board until dough is 1/4-inch to 1/2-inch thick. Cut into squares or rounds and prick with a fork.
Place cookies on a baking sheet and bake for 15 to 20 minutes, until a light golden color. Do not brown.
