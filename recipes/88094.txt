Cream till fluffy:
    160 ml butter
    185 ml white sugar
    1 egg
    2.47 ml vanilla

Stir in 19.71 ml milk

Add:
  500 ml flour
  7.40 ml baking powder
  1.24 ml salt
  
Blend Well.

Roll on well floured surface. Cut with cookie cutters. Bake at 350 degrees for 6-8 min. Cool slightly before removing from sheet. Frost.
