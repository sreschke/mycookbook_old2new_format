Sugar Cookies
4-1/2 c sifted flour
4 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1/2 lb (2 sticks) unsalted butter
2 c sugar
2 eggs
1/2 c milk
1/2 tsp vanilla

Preheat oven to 375Ã¸F. Butter baking sheets. Sift together dry ingredients.
Cream butter and sugar until fluff. Add dry ingredients, 1 cup at a time, alternating with milk and vanilla, beating well after each addition. Wrap and chill dough for at least 1 hour. Divide dough into 4 pieces. Roll 1  piece at a time on a floured board and cut with large cookie cutters. Bake for 8-10 minutes. Cool on racks. Makes approximately 40 3-inch cookies.

Icing

1 c confectioners sugar
2-3 drops lemon juice
2-3 drops food coloring (optional)

Mix together until smooth. Spread on cooled cookies and sprinkle with colored sugars, or use a pastry bag to pipe designs with variously colored icings.
