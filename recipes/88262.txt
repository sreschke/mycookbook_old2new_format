Preheat oven to 450F

Note:For tender, flaky biscuits, cut in the shortening with two knives, a fork or a pastry blender. Knead the dough gently, but do knead it - this improves the texture.

l cup golden crisco shortening
5-l/4 cups all purpose flour *
7-l/2 teaspoons baking powder
2-l/4 teaspoons salt
2-l/4 cups milk

l.Cut shortening into flour, baking powder and salt until mixture resembles fine crumbs. Stir in just enough milk so dough leaves side of bowl and rounds up into a ball. (Too much milk makes dough sticky)

2.Turn dough onto lightly floured surface. Knead light l0 times. 

3.Roll l/2 inch thick. Cut with floured biscuit cutter. Place on ungreased cookie sheet about l inch apart. 

4.Bake until golden brown. Remove from cookie sheet immediately.

Yields: 3 dozen biscuits

*If using self-rising flour, omit baking powder and salt.

Buttermilk Biscuits: Decrease baking powder to 6 teaspoons and add 3/4 teaspoon baking soda to the flour mixture. Substitute buttermilk for the milk.

Cheese Biscuits: Stir in l-l/2 cup shredded sharp cheese.

Herb Biscuits: Add 3-3/4 teaspoons caraway seed, l-l/2 teaspoons crumbled leaf sage and 3/4 teaspoon mustard to the dry ingredients.

Sour Cream-Chive Biscuits: Substitute 3 cups dairy sour cream for the milk, stir in 3 tablespoons plus 3 teaspoons snipped chives with the sour cream.
