2/3 c fat-free sour cream
1/2 c water
2 tbsp flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp sage
1/4 tsp black pepper
4 (6 oz) bone-in center-cut pork chops, trimmed
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 black pepper
1 tsp olive oil
1/2 c chopped onion
1 (8 oz) pkg presliced mushroomss
1/2 whiskey

Combine first 6 ingredients in a small bowl.  Sprinkle pork with 1/4 tsp salt and 1/8 tsp pepper.  Heat the oil in a large skillet over med-high heat.  Add pork; saute 5 min on each side or until golden.  Remove poik from pan.  Add onion and mushrooms to pan; saute for 3 min.  Carefully add whiskey to pan, cook for 1 min or until liquid almost evaporates.  Stir sour cream mixture into pan.  Return pork to pan; spoon sauce over pork.  Transfer to oven dish and bake at 300 degrees for approximately 1 hour.
