1 pkg lemon Jello
1 pkg lemon cake mix
4 eggs
3/4 cup water
3/4 cup oil
Mix and bake at 350 deg. in 9 x 13 greased and floured pan 40 minutes or until done.
GLAZE:  Mix 2 cups powdered sugar with juice of 2 lemons.  Punch holes in cake with large fork.  Pour glaze over cake while cake is still warm.
