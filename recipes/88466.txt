Texas Tornado Cake

1 1/2 c. sugar
2 eggs
2 cups fruit cocktail w/ syrup
2 tsp. baking soda
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 cup chopped nuts

Mix sugar, eggs, fruit and juice, soda and flower. Put in greased 13 x 8 x 2 pan. 
Mix nuts and sugar, sprinkle on batter. Bake at 325 for 40 min. 

Icing:
1 stick butter
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup evaporated milk
1 cup flaked coconut

Combine all ingredients, boil 2 min. and spread on WARM CAKE.
