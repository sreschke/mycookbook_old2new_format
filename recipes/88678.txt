1 chicken breast
1tbsp tobasco
veg oil
corn tortilla strips
salt
4c romaine lettuce shredded
1/2 red pepper cut in strips

Dressing;
4tbsp toasted sesame seeds
2tbsp white wine vinegar
1tbsp dijon mustard
1/2c veg oil
s and p

Cut chicken breast into strips and toss with tobasco.  Fry tortilla strips until crispy.  Combine all ingredients for the dressing until smooth.  Place lettuce, peppers and chicken in a bowl and toss with dressing and tortilla strips.
