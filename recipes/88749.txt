1 pkg. imitation crab (300 g.)
1 cup mayonnaise
1/3 cup chopped onion
1 tin chopped artichokes (13.75 oz}
3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
1 pkg. cream cheese (8 oz)

Soften cream cheese and mix with mayonnaise.
Add remainder ingredients  and put in 13 x 9" pan.
Bake 375 for 15 to 20 minutes till hot and a little brown on top.
Serve hot with crackers or french bread.
