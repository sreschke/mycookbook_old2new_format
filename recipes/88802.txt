Yield: 4

Ingredients:

Spicy Garlic Shrimp:
1 pound of shrimp, cleaned with shells removed, tails on
2 tablespoons of olive oil
2 cloves of garlic, minced
A few shots of Tabasco, to taste

The Dressing:
« cup olive oil
¬ cup Raspberry vinegar
Reserved grapefruit juice
1 shallot, thinly sliced
Zest of one lemon
Salt and pepper

The Salad:
2 ruby red grapefruits, peeled with pith removed and sectioned (reserve excess juice in a small bowl)
« red onion, julienned
1 yellow pepper, julienned
Spicy garlic shrimp (see above)
10 cups lightly packed mixed greens, rinsed and dried

Directions:

Saut‚ing the Garlic Shrimp:
1. Heat a wok or a large frying pan over high heat and add olive oil.
2. Quickly toss in garlic and saut‚, stirring constantly for 10 seconds.
3. Add shrimp and toss together with garlic, stirring constantly.
4. Season with a few shots of Tabasco sauce and saut‚ for 3-4 minutes.
5. Reserve in a bowl to cool.

Making the Vinaigrette:
1. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, raspberry vinegar, grapefruit juice, shallot, lemon zest, salt and pepper.

Assembling the Salad:
1. In a large salad bowl toss together the mixed greens, spicy shrimp, grapefruit, red onion and yellow pepper and toss gently with the vinaigrette.
