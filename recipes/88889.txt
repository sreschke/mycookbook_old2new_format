4 chicken breasts (skinned and deboned) cut into strips.
2 TBsps. lime juice
1 Tbsp. unsalted butter
2 Tbsp. olive oil
1 yellow onion diced
1/2 tsp. turmeric
2-3 Tbsps. curry powder (or to taste)
1/2 cup chicken stock
1 cup coconut milk
S & P

Remove any excess fat from chicken. Pat dry and marinate in lime juice 15 minutes.
Dry chicken pieces with paper towel. In a large frying pan saute chicken in oil and butter for 2-3 minutes. Transfer to a plate.
Pour of most of fat and saute onion 2 minutes. Add curry powder, saute further 2 minutes. Add chicken stock and stir in. Simmer 5 minutes.
Add coconut milk and simmer until slightly thickened
Return chicken to pan and simmer gently for 15 minutes
Season to taste
