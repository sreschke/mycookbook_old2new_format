Sponge Cake 

5 eggs, separated
3/4 cup Splenda
pinch salt
3/4 tsp baking powder
pinch cream of tartar (otional) 
1/2 tsp brown sweet and low (optional) juice of 1/2 lemon 
2 tsp vanila
1 cup almond flour 

Beat egg whites with salt until frothy, add 1/2 the Splenda, baking powder, and cream of tartar, and beat until very stiff. Beat yolks with remaining Splenda, vanilla, brown sweet and low and lemon juice for about 5 minutes until light and fluffy and increase in volume (about 1/3 of egg whites). Sift almond flour and gently fold together with whites and yolk mixure untll there are no lumps of white left. Pour into a greased 
9"x13" baking pan and bake at 350Ã¸F for 20-25 minutes until cake begins to pull away at the edges, and is brown on top.
