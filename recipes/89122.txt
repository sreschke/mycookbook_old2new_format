QUICK CHILI-RICE DINNER 

3/4 lb. ground beef
1/3 c. chopped onion
1 Tbsp. chili powder
1/2 tsp. dry mustard
1 (10 oz.) pkg. whole kernel corn
1 c. diced green pepper
1 (15 oz.) can tomato sauce
1/2 c. water
1 c. Minute rice
1/2 c. shredded Cheddar cheese

Brown beef and onion in skillet. Add spices, corn,
green pepper, tomato sauce and water. Cover and bring to a
full boil, stirring occasionally. Stir in rice, reduce heat,
cover and simmer for 5 minutes. Sprinkle with cheese. Serves 4.
