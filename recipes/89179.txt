1 pkg sugar cookie dough
8 oz. cream cheese
1/2 cup peanut butter
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 apples, sliced
Jar Caramel Ice Cream topping
peanuts

Spread cookie dough over a jelly roll pan or round pizza pan.
Bake according to pkg. directions.  Cool.
Mix together cream cheese, peanut butter and brown sugar.  spread over cooled crust.
Toss sliced apples with fruit fresh or just mix with caramel topping.
Sprinkle with peanuts.
Store in refrigerator.
