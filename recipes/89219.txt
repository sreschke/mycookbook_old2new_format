4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 egg, beaten
3/4 cup of Italian Seasoned Bread Crumbs
1 jar Pasta Sauce (26 ounces)
1 cup, shredded Mozzarella cheese

Preheat oven to 400 degrees
Dip chicken in egg, then in bread crumbs
Put chicken into 13 x 9 baking dish, arrange chicken. Bake uncovered 20 minutes.
Pour sauce over chicken; top with cheese. Bake for 10 more minutes or until chicken is no longer pink.. Cooking time..30 minutes total.  (Serves 4)

Reprinted from recipe for "Ragu 10 minute prep, no frying chicken parmesan"
THIS RECIPE IS VERY SIMPLE & IS DELICIOUS!
You don't have to use Ragu sauce. You can use any brand you like.
