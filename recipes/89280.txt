Ragged Robins  

2 cups cornflakes 
1 cup chopped walnuts 
1 cup chopped dates 
1/2 cup sugar 
1 teaspoon vanilla 
2 eggs 

Beat the eggs until they are lemon colored. Gradually add in the sugar and the vanilla and continue to beat until the mixture is well blended. Add in the dates and the chopped nuts. Fold in the cornflakes into the mixture. Shape into balls that are 1 inch in diameter. Place the balls 2 inches apart on a greased baking sheet. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes at 350 degrees F. Makes 4 dozen cookies. 

Recipe Source: Mrs. D. H. Reimer, Altona, Man., Canada 

Taken From: Pennsylvania Dutch Cooking--A Mennonite Community Cookbook 
By: Mary Emma Showalter 
ISBN: 0-517-16213-X 

Note: The recipes in this cookbook came from most of the Mennonite communities in Canada and the United States Of America.
