1 lb stewing meat or any kind you choose(i chose veal since very low in fat
2 jars fat free brown gravy
1 c brown gravy mix(like mccormick
1/2 c cornstarch
1 c hot water
1 onion, chopped
1 lb cooked potato chunks
1 or 2 carrots cut in very small pieces
this dish can all be combined in the crock pot or just simmered on the stove top till ready to serve.
it is so versatile the ways it can be prepared. raw home cooked veggies or canned veggies from the store
-- it's your creation.
