1 lb sausage 
2 (8 oz) pkgs cream cheese 
2 cups shredded cheddar 
1 cup chopped green onions 
4 cups Bisquick 
8 eggs, beaten 
3 cups milk 
 
Heat oven to 350F. Lightly grease a Bundt pan. Cook sausage over medium. Drain on paper towels and crumble.  Mix sausage, cream cheese, cheddar, green onion and baking mix. In another bowl, beat eggs and milk. Add to flour/cheese mixture, stir just to moisten. Pour in pan. Bake for 45 to 55 minutes til a toothpick comes out clean. Remove immediately from pan and cool slightly. Serve warm.
