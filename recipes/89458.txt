Catch a King Salmon - the bigger the fish, the more people you can
feed.  Fillet the fish Lay the fish meat side down on the grill (a BBQ
or a pit fire) Cook until fish is cooked thru 1/3 of the thickness of
fillet Flip fish so that skin side is down Brush with secret sauce
(recipe follows) Cook until done (the flesh will turn opaque) - don't
over cook salmon Slide a spatula between the fish and the skin and
lift the fillet off the grill leaving the skin behind.

Secret Sauce
Melt equal parts of butter and brown sugar together
Add a squirt of lemon juice
