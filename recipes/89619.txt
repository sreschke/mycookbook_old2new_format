Toss together:
5 C oats
1 C wheat germ
1 C sliced almonds
1/2 C sesame seeds
1 C whole wheat flour

Warm: 
1 C honey  (Lisa Foley says needs about 1/4-1/2C more honey)
3/4 C oil

Drizzle over oat mixture and toss together. Lay out mixture on ungreased cookie sheets and bake at 300F. Toss every 15 minutes, done when golden brown.  

Toss in:
1 C raisins

Yield approx 10 C
