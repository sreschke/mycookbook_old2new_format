1 lb. fusilli, rotini. or shell pasta
8 oz. Italian or ranch salad dressing
4 Tbsp. Salad Supreme seasoning -- McCormick
4 - 5 cups chopped assorted veggies like broccoli, carrot, red onion, cherry tomato, or bell pepper
 
Cook pasta, rinse and drain.  In a large bowl, combine pasta, salad dresssing and Salad Supreme Seasoning.  Toss in Veggies.  Cover and refrigerate 4 or more hours.  Stir before serving.
Makes 8 servings.
For main dish -- stir in 12 oz. tuna or chicken.
