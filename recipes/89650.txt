Cook 4 large potatoes. Mash and add 1/4 lb. grated Jack cheese.  Cook 1/2 box lasagna noodles and drain. Grease an oblong pan with butter.  Pour in a little milk to prevent      sticking.  Slice 2 large onions. Fry in butter. Layer noodles and potatoes.  Top with onions. Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes.
