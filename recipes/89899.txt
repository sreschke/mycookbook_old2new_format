 Cola Chicken

Recipe By     : Betty
Serving Size  : 4    Preparation Time :8:00
Categories    : Chicken

  Amount  Measure       Ingredient -- Preparation Method
--------  ------------  --------------------------------
   1      whole         chicken -- 1 1/2 lbs to 2 lbs
   1      cup           ketchup
   1      large         onion -- thinly sliced
   1      cup           cola -- coke, pepsi,rc, etc

Wash and pat chicken dry.  Salt and pepper to taste.  Put chicken in 
crock-pot with the onions on top.  Add cola and ketchup and cook on LOW 6 to 
8 hours.  Enjoy
