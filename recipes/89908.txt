Crockpot Lazy Day Stew

1 lb stew meat
6 potatoes, cut in chunks
4 carrots, cut in chunks
2 stalks celery, cut in chunks
1 cup frozen peas.
1 can tomato soup
1 envelope of onion soup mix

Place all into crockpot.  Stir to coat all vegetables with soup.  Cook
on
low for 8-10 hours.
