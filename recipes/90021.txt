1 TB butter
1 onion chopped
1 can chopped green chilies, drained
1 8oz soft cream cheese
3 1/2 c chopped, cooked chicken
8 flour tortillas
2 8oz pkg of Monterey Jack cheese
2 c whipping cream


Melt butter and cook onions.  Add green chilies and heat through.  Stir in cream cheese and chicken; stirring continuously til cheese is melted.

Spoon 2 or 3 TB of chicken mixture in center of each tortilla and roll.  Place seam side down in lightly greased baking dish.  Sprinkle with Mont. cheese and drizzle with cream.

Bake 350 for about 45 min.
