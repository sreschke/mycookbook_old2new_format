3 lbs. of hamburger meat
1can of chicken soup
1can of mushroom soup1chopped onion
1[8ozs.] pkg. cheese
1lcan biscuits
 
In large skillet, brown ground beef ;drain. Add soups, onion, and cheese.
Put in a large casserole dish and top with biscuits. Bake at 400 DEGREES
until biscuits are done. More cheese can be added if desired.
