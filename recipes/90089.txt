5 lg tomatoes, sliced
1 red onion, thin sliced
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tbls balsamic vinegar
1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
1/4 cup black olives, pitted
1 tbls chopped parsley 

Arrange tomatoes on a platter. Top with onion slices. Drizzle on oil and vinegar. Season with salt and pepper. Let stand 1 hour. Sprinkle with cheese, olives and parsley. Serves 6.
