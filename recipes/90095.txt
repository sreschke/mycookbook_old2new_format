 
Filled Meat Roll   
 
1/2 cup bread crumbs
1/2 cup milk
1-1/2 lb ground beef
2 eggs beaten
2 Tbsp onion, chopped
1 1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
3 Tbsp cheese, grated
1/4 cup cracker crumbs
2 cup hot mashed potatoes
1 cup cheese grated
1 (8 oz ) can tomato sauce 
Soak crumbs in milk until soft; combine with meat, eggs, onion, salt, pepper & 3 T cheese. 
Sprinkle cracker crumbs on waxed paper. 
Put meat mixture on the paper & form into 8 x 12" rectangle. Add remaining cheese to mashed potatoes & spread over meat. Roll up like a jelly roll. Press ends to seal. Put in shallow baking dish. 
Bake 350Ã¸ for 30 minutes. 
Pour tomato sauce over meat. Bake another 20 minutes
