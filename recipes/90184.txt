Crunchy on the outside, but chewy on the inside!

2 c. flour
1 c. granulated sugar
1 c. brown sugar
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 c. shortening
2 eggs
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 c. quick oats
1/2 c. chopped walnuts, optional

Oven 375*.

Stir together in a large bowl the flour, sugars, baking powder, soda and salt, mixing well.

Add shortening, eggs, vanilla; beat well.  Stir in oats and nuts.

Form into 1" balls, dip in extra granulated sugar and bake on UNGREASED sheets for 10-12 minutes.
