Serves 4
1 cup red lentils
1 T olive oil
1 large onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1 T curry powder
1 T ground coriander
1 t ground cumin
1 stick celery, chopped
2 cups chopped pumpkin
4 medium potatoes, peeled, diced, cooked
1 T soy sauce
black pepper to taste
1/2 cup sour light cream
1 cup grated reduced fat cheese

Place red lentils in saucepan, add enough water to cover and bring to boil.  Reduce heat, cover and cook for about 15mins until lentils are tender.  Drain.  In another saucepan, heat oil.  Add onion, garlic and spices.  Cook for a further 2 mins until onions are soft.  Add celery and pumpkin.  Saute until vegetables are tender.  Add cooked potato, lentils, soy sauce, pepper and sour cream.  Mix well to combine.  Transfer potato mixture into a shallow casserole dish.  Sprinkle with grated cheese.   Bake at 200c for about 15mins until cheese is golden.

NOTE:  Microwave shortcut - cook onion, garlic, celery and spices on high for 1 min.  Set aside.  Cook potatoes and pumpkin on high for 4 mins, stir and cook for further 4 mins.  Mix all ingredients except cheese in casserole dish, sprinkle with grated cheese and bake as above.
