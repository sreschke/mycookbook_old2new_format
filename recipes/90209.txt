1 cup sugar 
1 cup corn syrup 
24 ounce jar of peanut butter (chunky or smooth) 
5  cups corn flakes 
3 cups of semi sweet chocolate chips (can use milk chocolate) 

Mix together sugar and cornsyrup and heat until it just starts to boil. Add peanut butter (It is easier to heat the peanut butter in the microwave before adding but not required) and mix until smooth. Add cornflakes and mix together. Melt chocolate chips in microwave 1-2 minutes. In 11x15 baking pan pour peanut butter mixture. Use wax paper to even out mixture.  Press down. Spread melted chocolate over top. Refrigerate about 15-20 minutes or until chocolate gets hard. Cut into squares.
