2 eggs beaten
2 - 15 oz cans cream style corn
2/3 C milk
1 C cracker crumbs (about 24 soda crackers)
1/2 C chopped onion
1/2 C chopped green pepper (optional)
1 C grated Cheddar cheese
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper

Heat oven to 375 degrees.  Pam a 2 qt. casserole.
In a large mixing bowl, stir together the eggs, corn,  milk, cracker crumbs, onion, green pepper, cheese, salt & pepper.  Pour into the casserole.  Bake uncovered for about 50 minutes, until corn is set in the middle and begins to pull away from the sides of the casserole.  8-10 servings.
