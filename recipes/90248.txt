  1 jar marrachino cherries; drained
  2 (8oz.)cans pineapple tidbits; drained
  1 (11-15 oz.) can manarin orange segments
  2/3 cup seedless grapes (red or green)
  1 large apple, cut into chunks
  1 (16oz.) bag walnut pieces
  
Chill all fruit; just before serving, drain canned fruit thoroughly; place all ingredients
in large bowl.  Pour dressing over mixture and mix until well coated. Keep refrigerated until ready to serve.

Sour Cream and Honey Dressing
  
Blend together:

1 cup sour cream
2 Tablespoons honey
2 Tablespoons orange juice.  
(If not sweet enough, just add honey and orange juice to taste.)
