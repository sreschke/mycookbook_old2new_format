4 slices of bacon
1 lb ground chuck
1 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup chopped green pepper
1 lb can tomatoes (broken up)
1 can kidney beans (red)
1 8oz can tomato sauce
1 tsp salt 
t Tbsp chili powder
1 tsp ground cumin
2 cloves garlic (Minced)
1 bay leaf (optional)
1/2 tsp oregano
1/4 cup dry red wine
1 or 2 jalapena peppers (or serve on side)

In a heavy skillet, cook meat, garlic, onion and
green pepper until meat is lightly browned and onion is tender . Stir in remaining ingredients, cover and simmer for 1 hour. Remove bay leaf- Makes 4 servings
