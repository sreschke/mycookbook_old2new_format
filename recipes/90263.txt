Ginger-Miso Dressing

Makes 1 3/4 cups
1/2 cup white miso paste
1/4 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup plain rice wine vinegar
1/3 cup chicken stock 
1/4 cup sugar
1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger
togarashi to taste 
Combine all ingredients in a small bowl and whisk to blend. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
