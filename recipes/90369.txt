      Title: C/P CHICKEN CACCIATORE
 Categories: Crockpot, Poultry
      Yield: 4 Servings
 
MMMMM--------------------JUDY GARNETT PJXG05A-------------------------
      2    Med Onions; sliced thin
      3 lb Chicken; cut up
      2 c  Garlic; minced
      1 c  Tomatoes; 16 oz
      1 ts Salt
    1/4 ts Pepper
  1 1/2 ts Dried oregano; crushed
    1/2 ts Dried basil; crushed
      1    Bay leaf
    1/4 c  Dry white wine
           Cooked spaghetti
 
  Place onions in slow cooker. On top of onions place chicken pieces,
  garlic, tomatoes, tomato sauce, salt, pepper, herbs and wine. Cover
  pot. Cook on low 6 to 8 hours, or high 3 to 4 hours. Serve chicken
  with its sauce on spaghetti which you have prepared.
