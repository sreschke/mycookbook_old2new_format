
This unusual salad is as good to eat as it is beautiful to look at.
Toasting the cumin seeds in a dry pan brings out the flavor in the seeds. 

5 teaspoons cumin seed
1-1/2 large mangoes cut in julienne (thin strips)
1-1/2 medium jicamas cut in julienne (thin strips)
1-1/2 bunch watercress, trimmed, rinsed, and dried
3 tablespoons lime juice
1/2 cup peanut oil
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon freshly ground pepper

Toast the cumin seeds in a hot dry saute pan for about 3 minutes, or until browned.  Combine the mango, jicama, and watercress in a salad bowl.  Add
the cumin seed and toss well.  Mix together the lime juice and peanut oil and pour over the salad. Toss well.  Season to taste with salt and pepper.
Serves 6.
