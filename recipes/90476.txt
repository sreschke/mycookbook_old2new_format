1/2 cup butter
1/4 cup sugar
1/4 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup flour

1 8 oz. pkg.cream cheese
1/4 cup sugar
1 egg
1/2 teaspoon vanilla

1/3 cup sugar
4 cups peeled and sliced apples
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 cup sliced almonds

Cream 1/2 c. butter and 1/4 c. sugar.  Add vanilla and flour.  Spread mixture on bottom and sides of 9-inch springform pan.

Cream cheese and sugar together.  Add egg and vanilla.  Pour over crust.

Combine 1/3 cup sugar and 1/2 tsp. cinnamon.  Toss with apples.  Spoon over cream cheese mixture.  Sprinkle with almonds.

Bake at 450 degrees F for 10 minutes.  Reduce heat to 400 degrees F. and bake for a further 25 minutes.

Loosen and let cool before removing from pan.

YIELD:  8 to 10 servings.
