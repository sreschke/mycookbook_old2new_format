 This is a fragrant and tasty bread machine recipe.
 Prep  Time: approx. 5 Minutes. Cook Time: approx. 3 Hours .
 Ready  in: approx. 3 Hours 5 Minutes. Makes 1 - 2 pound loaf (15  servings). 
 Printed from Allrecipes, Submitted by Jenny Kernan
  		
1-1/3 cups and 1 tablespoon water
 3 tablespoons olive oil
 1 teaspoon minced garlic
 4 cups bread flour
 3 tablespoons white sugar
 2 teaspoons salt 
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 teaspoon dried basil 
1 teaspoon garlic powder 
3 tablespoons chopped fresh chives 
1 teaspoon coarsely ground  black pepper 
2-1/2 teaspoons bread machine yeast
 	
Directions 1 Place ingredients in the bread machine pan in the order  suggested 
by the manufacturer. 2 Select Basic or White Bread cycle, and press Start.
