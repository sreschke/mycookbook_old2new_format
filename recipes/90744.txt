1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/8 tsp. salt
1/4 cup sugar
1 1/2 tsps. ground ginger
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. ground cloves
2/3 cup unsweetened applesauce
1/3 cup molasses
3 tbsps. vegetable oil
1 egg, lightly beaten
vegetable cooking spray

Combine first 7 ingredients; stir well. Combine applesauce, molasses, oil and egg in a small bowl; add to flour mixture, stirring well. Spoon batter into 9 inch square baking pan coated with cooking spray. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 minutes or until pick comes out clean. serve warm.
