Blue Cheese Dip
Made on TRODS by Janette and Christina
3/4 cup sour cream
4 ounces crumbled blue cheese 
(I like Maytag Blue and Roquefort)
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
Salt
Freshly ground black pepper
Combine all ingredients in a bowl, mashing
the cheese with a fork until well combined.
If the dressing is too thick, add a little more
vinegar.
Makes about 1 1/2 cups
