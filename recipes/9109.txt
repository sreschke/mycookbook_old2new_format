2 Eggs 
1 cup vegetable oil
1 1/2 cup milk
2 cups yellow or white corn meal
2tsp baking powder
salt to taste 
2 cans drained white corn 
2 cups shedded longhorn cheese
7 oz can Hatch whole green chiles hot or mild depending on taste drained and cut in strips or roast your own fresh 
Preheat oven to 400 in a medium mixing bowl, combine eggs,oil,milk using a mixer.Slowly add cornmeal,baking powder and salt.Stir in corn Pour half the batter in a ungreased 9/13 pan.Layer with chile strips and shredded chesse .Add remaining batter .Bake at 400 for 30-40 minutes or until golden brown
