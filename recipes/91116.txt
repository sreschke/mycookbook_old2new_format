2 cups all-purpose flour
2 t baking powder
1 t baking soda
1/4 t salt
10 T butter or margarine
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 cup brown sugar
3 t vanilla
1 cup sugar
2 eggs, lightly beaten
1 cup ripe bananas
1/2 cup sour cream

Sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside. Combine 2 T butter with walnuts, brown sugar, and 1 t vanilla. Mix with fork until crumbly. Set aside. Cream together remaining butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Combine eggs, bananas, sour cream, and remaining vanilla. Blend into butter and sugar mixture. Reserve 1/4 cup of brown sugar & nut mixture for later use. Put remainder in an even layer in a greased 9x5x3 inch loaf pan. Top with batter, spreading smoothly. Sprinkle with reserved sugar/nut mixture. Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 50 minutes. Cool in pan on wire rack 10 minutes. Remove from pan and cool.
