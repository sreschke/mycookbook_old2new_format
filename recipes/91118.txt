
Garlic Shrimp 

Serve this delicious dish over pasta or rice to create an entree.

Serves 2

3/4 pound of Shrimp, large, raw, peeled, and deveined
3 tablespoons Olive oil
1 1/2 teaspoons Garlic, minced
1/8 teaspoon Crushed red pepper flakes
Dash Salt
Chopped parsley

Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp
and cook until just pink. Toss in the garlic, crushed pepper, and
salt. Remove the skillet from the flame and allow to sit for 4
minutes. Place the pan over medium-low flame and re-heat for about 2
minutes. Garnish with the parsley. Serve warm.
