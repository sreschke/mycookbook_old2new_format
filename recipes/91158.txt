3 cups sour cream 
2 cups milk 
1 large cucumber, peeled and grated 
2 med cloves garlic, crushed 
2 tbsp olive oil 
3 tbsps finely chopped dill 
1 tbsp finely chopped mint 
juice of 1 lemon
salt and pepper  
4 drops Tabasco sauce 
thin sliced cucumber 
 
Put sour cream in a bowl, whisk in milk til smooth. 
Stir in the cucumber, garlic, olive oil, 2 tbps dill, mint, lemon juice, salt, pepper and Tabasco. 
Chill til ice cold. Garnish with a little of the remaining dill and a slice of cucumber.
