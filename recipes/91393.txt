Sherry D. Johnston
Casual Snacks and Appetizer Category
Fort Lauderdale, FL
Prep time: 20 minutes
Ready in one hour and 10 minutes

1 Pillsbury Refrigerated Pie Crust
(from 15 ounce pkg.) softened as directed
on package
1 /2 cup Progresso Italian Style Bread Crumbs
1 /3 cup chopped fresh basil
1 /4 cup Romano cheese
1 /4 teaspoon salt
1 /4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
1 cup part-skim ricotta cheese
3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil or olive oil
3 Italian plum tomatoes, seeded and diced
Garnish with 1 Italian plum tomato, sliced, if desired
8 fresh basil leaves, if desired

   Heat oven to 400 degrees F. Remove crust from pouch; unfold crust. Place on ungreased cookie sheet; press out fold lines. With rolling pin, roll to form 12-inch round.

    In medium bowl, combine bread crumbs, chopped basil, Romano cheese, salt, pepper, ricotta cheese and oil. Mix well. Stir in diced tomatoes. Spoon and spread over crust to within 3 inches of edge. Fold edge of crust 3 inches over filling; crimp crust slightly.

     Bake at 400 degrees for 25 to 35 minutes or until golden brown. Cool 15 minutes. Cut into wedges. Garnish with tomato slices and basil leaves. Serve warm or cool. 12 servings.
