2 Tbs olive oil
8 anchovy filets, drained well 
3 cloves of garlic, crushed and chopped
28 oz can plum tomatoes 
2 Tbs capers, rinsed and drained
1/2 cup black olives, halved
2 Tbs fresh basil, finely chopped
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes 
salt & pepper
2 Tbs Italian parsley, finely chopped
1 lb Spaghetti 

Heat a skillet over medium-high, add the olive oil. Add the anchovies and stir, shaking the pan, til they have disolved. Lower heat, add garlic and saute til it begins to color slightly. Don't burn. 
Add tomatoes, crushing as they go in, then the capers, black olives, basil and red pepper flakes. Bring to a simmer and taste for salt and pepper.  
Cook for twenty to thirty mins, til the sauce has reduced and thickened. In the meantime, cook pasta.
Drain pasta, ladle sauce over the pasta and garnish with parsley. Serves four.
