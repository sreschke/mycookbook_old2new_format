These are delicious filled with sauteed onions, cheddar cheese and mashed potatoes.

Pirohy

3 to 4 cups flour
1 egg
1 to 2 teaspoons oil
Salt
1/2 to 1 cup warm water

Place 3  to 3 1/2 cups flour in food processor workbowl.  Add egg, 
oil, and salt; process until granular-mixed.  Add about 1/2 cup 
water, more as necessary, & process until a moderately stiff dough 
is formed.  Should not be sticky.  Remove from bowl, cover and let 
rest at least 30 minutes.  Roll thinly, cut into approximately  3" 
squares or 2 1/2" circles, fill with desired filling (about 1 to 2 
teaspoons, depends on filling).  Pinch to seal; boil gently 5 to 7 
minutes.  Toss with butter & onions.
