2 Tbs olive oil
2 Tbs seasoned garlic salt (or Mrs. Dash)
1 tsp corriander. 
6 large red potatoes cut in wedges.
Preheat oven to 450 Degrees.  Line shallow baking pann with foil.  Combine olive oil and spices in bowl.  Add potatoes and toss to coat.
Spread in single layer in foil lined pan.  Bake 40-45 minutes umtil browned and crisp.
