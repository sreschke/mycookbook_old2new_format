2 cups sifted flour
2 tsps baking powder
1 tsp salt
1/3 cp. margarine
1/2 cp milk
Sift dry ingredients together.  Rub in the margarine.  Add enough milk to make a soft dough, as for biscuits. Roll thinner than 1/16 inch.

Cut in 1 1/2 inch squares or rectangles.  Have your broth boiling at high heat. Drop in dumplings two at a time.  Keep the broth boiling. When all are in, reduce heat to low and cover.  Boil for 12 minutes.

Cooking utensil must always have a tight fitting lid or dumplings will be heavy.  Don't allow the broth to boil over after covering utensil, or the dumplings will fall.
