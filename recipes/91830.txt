3 Cups Sugar
6 Tbsp. cocoa
2 tsp. vanilla
3/4 Cup butter
1 & 1/2 tsp. soda
3 Cups flour
1 & 1/2 Cups water
3/4 cup oil
3/4 cup buttermilk or sour milk
3 eggs
Walnuts

Mix sugar, cocoa, & flour in large bowl.  Bring to boil water, & butter & oil.  Pour over the dry ingredients.  Beat all together.  Add buttermilk and soda together. Add eggs & vanilla, beat. Add chopped up walnuts.  Bake 18 minutes at 350 degrees.

Cool brownies for about 1/2 hour.  Mix the frosting
and put unto the brownies.
         Frosting
6 Tbsp. butter
6 Tbsp. milk
1 & 1/3 Cup sugar
   Boil together for one minute
Add:
6 oz. choc. chips (equals one cup chips)
Beat together and frost brownies.  Put walnuts on top if desired.
