 
 6 Cups all-purpose flour
 1 Cup Corn meal
 1 Cup Rolled Oats
 1 Cup Whole wheat flour
 1 Tablespoon baking powder
 1 Tablespoon baking soda
 1 Tablespoon salt
 2 Teaspoons sugar
 6 whole eggs
 6 Cups buttermilk
 3 /4 Cups canola oil
 Milk to thin if necessary
 
    Mix dry ingredients, mix wet ingredients and mix together.  Cook on hot greased griddle.  Serve with applesauce or any fresh fruit in season and Ohio Maple Syrup.
