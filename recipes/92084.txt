6 - 8 good quality sausages (Hint - choose any of the gourmet low-fat sausages available)
425g can tomato soup
1/2 t mustard
2 t vinegar
1 clove garlic, crushed
1 onion, sliced
1 t curry powder
1 t brown sugar

Preheat the oven to 180C.  Parboil the sausages.  Remove from heat, drain and set aside to cool.  Once cool, carefully peel off skins and cut the sausage into chunks.  Place the sausage in a casserole dish.  NMix the remaining ingredients together in a bowl.  Pour over the sausages, cover and cook in the oven for about 40mins.  Serve with fluffy white or brown rice.
