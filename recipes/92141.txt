Ingredients 
2 cups mashed potatoes 
2 cups finely chopped ham 
1 cup shredded Swiss cheese 
1/2 cup mayonnaise 
1/4 cup chopped onion 
2 eggs, beaten 
1 Tablespoon dry mustard 
1/2 teaspoon salt and pepper 
3-1/2 cups crushed corn flakes
Combine all ingredients except corn flakes. Shape into 1-inch balls. Roll in crushed corn flakes. Place on lightly greased cookie sheet. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 30 minutes.
