
1 & 1/2 cups chopped  
Calimyrna Figs, stems removed
3 cups chopped green apples
2 teaspoons grated orange peel
1/3 cup orange juice
1/4 cup plus 2 tablespoons granulated 
sugar, divided
1 cup plus 2 tablespoons flour
1/8 teaspoon salt
8 tablespoons margarine, 
softened
1 cup apricot fruit spread
1 teaspoon brandy (optional)

For filling, combine chopped figs, apples, orange peel, juice and 1/4 cup sugar in large saucepan. Cover, bring to boil and simmer 10 minutes or until apples are tender. Uncover and cook briefly to evaporate any remaining liquid. Cool.

For pastry, combine flour, 2 tablespoons sugar, salt and margarine. Stir with pastry blender or fork until crumbs can be clumped together in a ball between the fingertips. Press in bottom and sides of  10-inch Quiche Dish or 2-quart Round Dish. Bake at 425Ã¸F for 10?12 minutes or until light golden. Cool.

Sieve apricot spread to make a smooth glaze. Mix with brandy, if desired. Spread filling in baked shell. Spoon apricot glaze evenly over top. Serves eight.
