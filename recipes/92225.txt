Mixed Vegetable Casserole
                                                    
3 packages (10 ounces each) frozen mixed vegetables
1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cups crushed round crackers (butter crackers, I.E. Townhouse, Ritz, etc.)  about 38 crackers
1 cup chopped celery
1 cup chopped onion
1 cup mayonnaise or salad dressing
1/4 cup margarine or butter, softened
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Cook vegetables according to package directions and drain. Combine with cheese,celery, onion, pepper, and mayonaise or salad dressing. Turn mixture into a 13 x 9 x 2 inch baking dish. Combine crackers and margarine or butter. Sprinkle over vegetable mixture. Bake uncovered in a 350 F. oven for 20-25 minutes or till heated through. Makes 10 to 12 servings.
