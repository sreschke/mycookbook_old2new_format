4 cubes of beef boullion
2 smashed garlic cloves
1 pnd of beef (cut up)
1 celery root
1 onion (sliced)

In each serving bowl:
1/2 juice lime
2 dashes of red pepper flakes
1 pinch of msg
1/4 cup chopped cilantro
1 tbsp. fish sauce
1 green onion (chopped)

Boil cubes in a pot full of water.  Add garlic cloves, beef, celery root, and onion.  Simmer till tender and beef is done (about an hour).  Spoon out soup mixture into prepared serving bowls.
