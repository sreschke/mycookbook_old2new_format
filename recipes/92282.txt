1 large zucchini, chunked
3 carrots, sliced
2 stalks celery, thin sliced
1 t salt 
1 1/2 cups water 
1 can creamed corn 
1 onion, chopped 
4 T butter 
4 T flour 
1 1/2 cups milk 
1/2 lb shredded sharp cheese 

Cook veggies in salt water for 10 minutes. Do not drain. Add corn and set aside. Melt butter and blend in flour til smooth. Add milk gradually and stir til thickened. Add veggies and cheese. Heat hot. DO NOT BOIL!
