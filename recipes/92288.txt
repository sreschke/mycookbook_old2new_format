Peanut Butter Oatmeal Treats

1 jar (16 ounces) peanut butter
2 cups of regular old fashion oats
1 1/4 cup flour
1 cup butter
1 cup chocolate chips
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 teaspoon baking soda 
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 eggs

In a small bowl mix together flour, baking soda, salt and set aside. In a large bowl, cream butter, peanut butter, and both sugars. Beat in eggs and vanilla. Stir in flour mixture and oats and chocolate chips. Do not over beat mixture. Place mixture in an ungreased 15 1/2" x 10 1/2" x 1" inch pan. Press the cookie dough down slightly to fill the pan. Bake at 350 F. for 15 to 17 minutes.

Recipe Source: Sheila Bratcher
