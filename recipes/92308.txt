Ingredients:
1 package sliced ham (about 6 slices)
1/4 teaspoon prepared mustard
1/2 teaspoon horseradish
1/8 seasoned salt
4 oz cream cheese, softened

Mix all ingredients except ham.  Spread on ham slice and then layer spreading on each slice after.  Wrap tightly in plastice wrap and chill overnight.  Then slice into squares and serve with toothpicks.
