2 Tbs vegetable oil
1 chopped onion
1 clove minced garlic
4 cups cubed peeled squash
3 cups chicken stock
1 bay leaf
1/2 cup soymilk
1/2 tsp each salt & nutmeg
1/4 tsp black pepper

1. Cook onion and garlic, in oil over med heat until softened, 5 minutes. 

2. Add squash, chicken stock and bay leaf. Boil, reduce heat and simmer, covered, for 20-30 minutes until squash is tender. Discard bay leaf.

3. Puree soup until smooth. Add milk, salt, pepper and nutmeg.  Reheat, do not boil.
