(about 5 dozen)

Roll these or use the cooky press.

Blend in blender or cuisinart
1 cup very soft butter
2 hard-cooked egg yolks
3/4 cup sugar
2 raw egg yolks
Juice of 1/2 lemon
Grated lemon peel of 1/2 lemon

Blend until thoroughly mixed.

Place in mixing bowl

3 1/2 cups sifted flour
1/4 teaspoon salt

Stir liquid mixture into dry ingredients.  Chill about an hour or until dough is stiff enough to put through cooky press or to roll in small portions.

Shape as you like, or roll on floured and sugared board, and cut.

Bake at 375 degrees F. for 10 minutes or until done.
