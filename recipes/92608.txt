1  cake mix any flavor           2 tablespoons water
1/2 cup oil                            2 eggs
coconut, chips, nuts, or rolled oats, optional

Combine the cake mix, oil, water, eggs and any optional ingredients.  Mix well and
drop by teasopoons onto a baking sheet.  Bake at 350 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes.
*Cookies are soft and chewey*
