POTATO CASSEROLE

8 med. potatoes
1/4 cup butter
1 can cream of mushroom soup
3 chopped green onions
2 cups grated cheddar
1 1/2 cups sour cream
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1/2 cup crushed corn flakes

Cook potatoes in skins until barely tender, cool, peel and grate.
Blend soup, butter, sour cream, spice, onion and 1 1/2 cups of the
cheese.  Mix with potatoes and put in buttered 2 1/2 qt.  casserole.

Bake uncovered at 350 degrees for 30 min.  Combine remaining cheese
and corn flakes and sprinkle over top and return to oven for 10-15
min.  longer.
