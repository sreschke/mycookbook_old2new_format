5 tsp. cornstarch (divided)
4 tsp. soy sauce (divided)
1 TBSP. dry sherry
1 tsp. sesame oil
3 boneless chicken breasts, cut into pieces
1 TBSP. fermented black beans
1 tsp. minced garlic
1 tsp. minced ginger
1/2 cup chicken broth
1 TBSP. oyster sauce
1 medium onion, cut in wedges
3 TBSP. vegetable oil
1 lb. asparagus cut into 1" pieces
2 TBSP. water

Combine 2tsp. cornstarch, 2 tsp. soy sauce, sherry and sesame oil. Add chicken, stir well. Let stand 30 minutes.
Place beans in a seive, rinse under cold water. Finely chop beans and add ginger and garlic.
Combine remaining cornstarch, soy, chicken broth and oyster sauce.
Cut onion in wedges.
Heat 2 TBSP. oil in wok. Add chicken mixture. Stir fry 3 minutes until chicken is no longer pink. Remove from wok.
Heat remaining oil, add onion and asparagus. Stir fry 2 minutes. Add water, cover and cook 2 minutes.
Return chicken to wok. Stir in broth mixture. Add bean mixture.
Cook until sauce boils and thickens.
