1 stick butter
16 oz  broccoli cuts
2 cans cream of corn
2 cans whole kernel corn
Chicken-in-the-Bisket Crackers 
Pepper to tast

Cook all ingredients (half time).  Melt butter in cassorole dish.  Mix ingredients in dish and top with crushed crackers.  
Bake at 325 degrees for 1/2 hour.
