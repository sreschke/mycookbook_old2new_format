Yummy Ribs!

Serves 2-4 as an appetizer. Recipe can be doubled.

1 lb pork ribs, cut up
1/4 cup white vinegar

Sauce:
1/2 cup ketchup
1/4 cup soya sauce
1/4 cup water
1/4 cup honey
1 garlic clove, minced
dash salt

Place ribs, vinegar, and water to cover in a large pot.  Cover.  Boil
on medium for one hour.  Meanwhile, in medium pot- mix all sauce
ingredients and cook over medium heat for one hour.  Drain ribs.  Add
to sauce and put in a crock pot on high for 3 hours.  Or- baste ribs
with sauce and BBQ on medium for 20 min.  Sauce is also good on
chicken wings
