1/3 cup margarineor butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup white sugar
1 egg beaten slightly
1 cup flour
1/2 ts baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1-1/2 tsp cinnamon

Cream margarine and sugar
Add egg and mix well
Combine flour, baking soda, taking powder and cinnamon. Add these dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternately while mixing in buttermilk.
Beat well and spread in a greased 8" square pan.

TOPPING
1/4 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup chopped nuts
1/4 tsp cinnamon
1/8 tsp nutmeg
Combine all ingredients and sprinkleover the batter. COVER and refrigerate overnight.
Bake uncovered in a 350 degree oven for 40 to 45 min.
