5 medium potatoes, thinly sliced
1 medium onion,sliced
6 Tbs. butter
1/3 c. shredded cheddar cheese
2 Tbs. minced parsley
1 Tbs. Worchesterchire sauce
salt and pepper
1/3 c. chicken broth

Place the potatoes and onion on a lg piece of heavy-duty foil( 20 x 20 ); dot with butter. Combine the cheese, parsley,salt, pepper and sauce; sprinkle over potatoes. Fold foil up around potatoes and add broth.  Seal the edges of the foil well. Grill, covered, over medium heat for 35 to 40 minutes or until potatoes are tender.

4 to 6 servings
