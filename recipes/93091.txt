2 lb. ground beef
1 Large Onion, chopped
1 Med Green Pepper, chopped
1 Can Cream Style Corn
1 Can Tomato Soup (undiluted)
1 Can (8oz) Tomato Sauce
1 Jar (2oz) Sliced Pimentos
2 Tbsp. Chopped Jalepeno Peppers
1-1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp Chili Powder
1/4 tsp Ground Mustard
1/4 tsp Pepper
1 Pkg (8oz) Med Egg Noodles, cooked and drained
1 Jar (4oz) sliced mushrooms
1-1/2 cups Shredded Cheddar Cheese

  Cook beef, onion and green pepper until meat is done and the vegetables are tender. drain
  Add next nine ingredients and mix well. Stir noodles in and mushrooms. Transfer to 13x9x2 baking dish. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake uncovered at 350 for 45 min or until heated through.
