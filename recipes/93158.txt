Turkey Chilli

1 pound lean ground raw turkey
1 can (16 ounces) red kidney beans, drained
1 can (16 ounces) tomatos, cut up   
1 can (8 ounces) tomato sauce
1 can (6 ounces) V-8 juice
1 cup water
1 cup chopped celery
1/2 cup green pepper
2 tablespoons minced onion
1 teaspoon dried basil
1/2 teaspoon beef bouillon
1/2 teaspoon cumin
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1 bay leaf 
                                      
Spray skillet with non stick coating spray and add turkey and celery. Cook till no longer pink. Add in the rest of the ingredients and bring to a boil. Reduce heat. Add extra chilli powder if you want. Simmer uncovered for 20 minutes. Discard bay leaf. Serve with cornbread or with corn chips. 

Recipe Source: Sheila Bratcher
