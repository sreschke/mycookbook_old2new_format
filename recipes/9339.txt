1 1/2 lbs stew meat
2 Tbsp. butter or margarine
1 1/2 C. beef bouillon
2 Tbsp. ketchup
1/8 tsp. garlic salt
1 tsp. salt
1 can sliced mushrooms
1 medium chopped onion
2 Tbsp. flour
1 C. sour cream
3 to 4 C cooked noodles
1 Tbsp. butter or margarine

Cook and stir beef in 2 Tbsp. butter in large skillet over low heat until brown.  Reserve 1/3 C bouillon.  Stir Remaining bouillon, ketchup, garlic, and salt into skillet.  Heat to boiling- reduce heat, cover and simmer till beef is tender, 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
Stir in mushrooms and onions.  Cover and simmer till onion is tender, approximately 5 minutes.  Mix remaining bouillon and flour.  Gradually stir this mixture in.  Heat to boiling, stirring constantly.  Boil and stir 1 minute.  Reduce heat stir in sour cream- heat through.  Toss Noodles with 1 Tbsp. butter.  Serve beef mixture over hot noodles
