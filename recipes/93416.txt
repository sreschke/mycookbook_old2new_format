1 c white sugar
1 c dark corn syrup
1 c butter or margarine
1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
1 tsp vanilla

Line 8 inch square pan with foil & butter foil generously; set aside.  Combine sugar, corn syrup & butter in 3 qt saucepan.  Bring to boil over medium heat, stirring constantly.  Boil slowly for 4 min without stirring.  Remove from heat & stir in milk.  Reduce heat to medium-low and cook until candy thermometer reads 238 degrees F (soft ball stage), stirring constantly.  Remove from heat & stir in vaanilla.  Pour into prepared pan.  Cool.  Remove from pan & foil & cut into 1 inch squares.  Wrap individually in waxed paper, twist ends.  Makes 64 pieces.  

*I have also done it in a 9x13 pan.  Makes approx 7 doz.
