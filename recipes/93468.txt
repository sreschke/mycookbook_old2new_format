In a medium bowl, mix 1 lb. ground beef, 1/2 cup uncooked rolled oats, 2/3 cup of evaporated milk, 2 tablespoons minced onion, 1/8 teaspoon salt, and 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper.  Let stand for a few minutes until milk is absorbed, and shape into 8 patties.
In a small bowl thoroughly mix 4 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce, 2 tablespoons vinegar, 4 teaspoons granulated sugar, 2/3 cup ketchup, and 1/4 cup chopped onion;  set aside.
Heat 3 tablespoons vegetable oil in a medium skillet over medium heat, and fry the patties until brown on both sides.  Pour the sauce in with the patties, and reduce heat.  Continue cooking about 15 minutes.
