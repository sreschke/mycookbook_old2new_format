4 slices bread, crumbs
2 lb. ground round
2 eggs
1 C. chopped onions
1/2 C. bell pepper, chopped
1 1/2 tsp. salt
4 Tbsp. Worcestershire Sauce
1/2 tsp. garlic salt
1/2 tsp. celery salt
1/2 tsp. dry mustard
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/4 C. milk
3/4 C. catsup

Mix altogether.  Cover meatloaf with the following topping:
 
3/4 C. catsup 
2 Tbsp. butter, optional
1 Tbsp. prepared mustard
1 Tbsp. Worcestershire Sauce
2 Tbsp. brown sugar 

Bake 30 minutes at 350 degrees.

NOTE:  I usually bake this in two smaller sized loaf pans or two 8x8 pyrex dishes.  Freezes well, and I like to keep on hand for a quick sandwich.
