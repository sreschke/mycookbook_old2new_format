Preparation Time : 30 min - hour

3-4 Chicken Breasts
1 Bunch of Broccoli
1 Cream of Mushroom
1 Cream of Chicken
1lb of Colby or Cheddar Cheese
Fresh Sliced Mushrooms(Not in the can)
Thyme
Garlic Powder
Salt

Boil Chicken in Thyme, Garlic Powder, and Salt until done.
Steam or Boil Broccoli to your taste.

Mix Broccoli, Creamed Soups, 1/2 of the lb of cheese, and some mushrooms in
baking dish big enough for the chicken breasts.  Place Chicken Breasts in dish
and smother in mix.   Sprinkle the rest of the cheese on top with some mushrooms 
and bake at 350 until cheese is melted.
