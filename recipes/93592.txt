Bonnie Stern's Double Lemon Squares

Crust:
1/2 c. butter
1/3 c. brown sugar
l c. a.p. flour

Cream butter and sugar until light. Mix in flour and knead ingredients until they come together into a dough. Press evenly into the bottom of 8" baking dish lined with parchment paper. Bake in preheated oven at 350 for 20 min. or until lightly browned.

Filling:
2 eggs
l c. sugar
2 tbsp. a.p. flour
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 c. lemon juice
2 tbsp. grated lemon peel
sifted icing sugar

Beat eggs with sugar in a medium bowl. Combine flour with b.p. in a small bowl and stir into eggs. Add lemon juice and peel. Pour over base.
Bake 15 min. or until slightly browned and set. Cool. Sprinkle with icing sugar. Run a knife around the edge of the cake to loosen and gently remove from the pan using the parchment paper to help you lit. Cut into squares. Makes 25 squares.
