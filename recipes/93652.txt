Chocolate-Pecan Rugelach 

1-1/4 cups(2 1/2 sticks) butter or margarine, divided 
1(8-ounce) package cream cheese, softened 
2 cups all-purpose flour 
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup ground walnuts or pecans
1/2 cup granulated sugar
1/2 cup ground semisweet chocolate
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional) 

In medium bowl, cream 1 cup butter with cream cheese. Add flour and salt. Blend until dough begins to hold together. Gather into a ball and transfer to floured surface; knead until smooth. Divide into 6 parts; wrap each in plastic wrap and chill. 
In small bowl, combine nuts, sugar, chocolate and cinnamon (optional). Set aside. 
Roll each dough portion into an 8-inch circle. Melt remaining 1/4 cup butter; brush on each dough circle. Sprinkle 2 to 3 tablespoons nut mixture over each circle. 
Cut each circle into 12 wedges. Starting at wide end, roll up each wedge. Roll each cookie in remaining walnut mixture to coat on all sides. Place on lightly oiled, foil-lined baking sheet. 
Bake in a preheated 350Ã¸ oven 15 to 20 minutes, or until golden brown. 
Yield: About 6 dozen cookies.
