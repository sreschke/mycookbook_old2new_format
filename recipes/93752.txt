1 cup butter
2 tbsps. garlic salt
2 tbsps. paprika
salt and pepper to taste
1 (12 oz.) can beer, any kind
1 whole chicken

Preheat grill

In a small skillet, melt 1/2 cup butter or margarine. Add 1 tablespoon garlic salt, 1 tablespoon paprika and salt and pepper to taste. Pour out 1/2 of beer in can. Add 1/2 cup butter or margarine to beer can then add 1 tablespoon garlic salt, 1 tbsp. paprika and salt and pepper to taste. Place beer can on disposable baking sheet, set chicken on/over can, inserting into cavity. Baste with melted seasoned butter or margarine. Place baking sheet with beer and chicken on grill. Cook over low heat for 3 hours or until chicken is thoroughly cooked.
