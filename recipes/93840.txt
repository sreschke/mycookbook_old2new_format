

1/2 c. whole wheat flour    1 beaten egg
1/4 c. all-purpose flour    1/4 c. molasses
1/4 c.yellow cornmeal       2 TB. sugar
1/2 tsp. baking powder      2 tsp. cooking oil
1/4 tsp. baking soda   3/4 c.buttermilk or sour milk
1/4 tsp. salt               2 TB. raisins

Stir together flours, cornmeal, baking powder,soda & salt.Combine egg,molasses,sugar, & oil. Add flour mixture to molasses mixture alternately with buttermilk;beat well.Stir in raisins. Turn batter into 2 well-greased 16 oz. vegetable cans.Cover cans tightly with foil.Place cans in crockery cooker.Cover & cook on high-heat setting for 3 hours. Remove cans from cooker;cool 10 minutes in cans. Serve warm. Makes 2 loaves.
