1 1/2 cups egg whites (12 large eggs)
1 cup powdered sugar
1 cup cake flour (sift powdered sugar and flour together 5 times)
1 cup granulated sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cream of tartar
1 tsp. vanilla
1/8 tsp. almond extract

Beat egg whites on high speed with salt. Add cream of tartar when foamy. Gradually add granulated sugar, a small amount at a time when whites form soft peaks and continue beating until stiff. Fold in vanilla and almond. Fold in flour and powdered sugar and put in a 10 inch tube pan. Bake at 425 degrees for 15 minutes, then turn oven back to 375 degrees and bake 15 minutes longer. Invert pan to remove cake
