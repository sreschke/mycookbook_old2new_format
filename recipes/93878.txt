1 package (3 oz.) cream cheese, softened 
6 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened 
1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour 
1 tablespoon sugar 
1/4 teaspoon salt 

Blend together the cream cheese and butter. Stir in the flour, sugar and salt until the dough comes together easily. Form the dough into a round disk. Wrap in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. 

Roll the dough out on a lightly floured surface to a 1/4-inch thickness. Transfer it to a 9-inch glass pie plate, and refrigerate until ready to fill. 

Sweet Potato Filling 
2 cups mashed sweet potatoes (about 2 1/4 pounds), pureed in a food processor or blender 
1 cup sugar 
1/4 cup brown sugar 
3 tablespoons butter, melted 
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice 
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg 
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 tsp. all spice 
1 teaspoon vanilla extract 
1/2 teaspoon salt 
1/2 teaspoon grated lemon zest 
3 large eggs, beaten 
1/2 cup half-and-half 

Preheat the oven to 400ø. Remove the piecrust from the refrigerator. Combine all the filling ingredients in a large bowl. Using an electric mixer, blend the filling until smooth. Pour the filling into the prepared crust. Bake for 20 minutes, then reduce heat to 325ø. Bake until the outer edges are puffed and the center of the filling is still gelatin-like, about 20 minutes longer. Cool completely before slicing. 

Serves 6-8.
