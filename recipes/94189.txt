Bourbon Chicken

2lbs boneless skinless chicken breast-cut into bite sized cubes

1-2 Tbls olive oil

1 clove garlic, crushed

1/4 tsp. ginger

1/2 tsp. crushed pepper

1/4 cup apple juice

1/3 cup firmly packed light brown sugar

2 Tbls. catsup

1 Tbls. cider vinager

1/2 cup water

1/3 cup soy sauce

1 Tbls. corn starch

1 Tbls. water

Heat oil in large pot then add chicken pieces and lightly brown for about 6-7 minutes. Remove chicken from pot and put aside.Ã¿ Put remaining ingredients(garlic thru soy sauce) in pot and heat over medium heat until sugar is dissolved. Add chicken and bring to a boil and then simmer for about 20 minutes. In a small bowl mix cornstarch and water and add to chicken and sauce just before serving to thicken. Serve over rice.
