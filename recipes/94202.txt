1 lb. ditalini or elbow macaroni
2 15 oz. cans tomato sauce
1 can chicken broth
1 can cannellini, great northern, or navy beans
1 oz. extra virgin olive oil
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1/8 tsp. oregano
1/8 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. crushed red pepper or ground black pepper

In 2 qt. saucepan, saute garlic and oregano in olive oil over medium heat for about 30 seconds.  Do not brown.  Add tomato sauce, chicken broth, and beans.  add salt and peeper, and simmer for 10 minutes to blend flavors.  Cook pasta according to package directions.  Drain pasta and return to pot.  Add tomato/bean mixture and serve.  May sprinkle with Parmesan cheese if desired.

Serves 6.
