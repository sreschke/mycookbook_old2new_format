You can make this with white kidney beans or cannellini beans.  You can also add a sprig of chopped fresh resemary for a true Tuscan treat.  Serve as is or under grilled vegetables or tofu steaks.  This is great hot or chilled.

1  (16 oz) can white beans, drained and rinsed
1  T extra-virgin olive oil
2  cloves garlic, thinly sliced
1/2  t crushed red pepper flakes
Juice of 1 lemon
Salt, to taste

Place beans in a nonreactive mixing bowl.  Heat oil in a small skillet and saute garlic about 45 seconds until lightly browned. Remove from heat and add pepper flakes.  Add garlic mixture to beans with lemon juice and salt.

Makes 2 servings
