Mrs Geller's Simple Spagetti 
jammerr 

1 28 oz can whole tomatoes drained and chopped 
1/2 cup fresh basil leaves cut in strips 
1/4 c. olive oil 
4 cloves minced garlic 
salt and pepper to taste 
I get my spaghetti squash going in the micro wave first. Place everything but pepper in sauce pan over medium heat. Simmer 10 minutes until liquid evaporates and tomatoes form rough sauce. Add pepper. Serve with spaghetti squash and fresh grated cheese.
