4 tbsp. unsalted butter
3 Tbsp. honey
1 cup cornmeal
1 cup all purpose flour
1 Tbsp. baking powder
3/4 tsp. salt
2 eggs
11/2 cups milk
3/4 cup shredded Cheddar or Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup finely chopped hard Italian sausage, such as suppressata or salami

Preheat oven to 425 degrees, lightly grease an 8-inch square baking pan.
 Melt butter and honey together and set aside to cool.
In a large bowl. beat eggs and milk together. Stir in cooled butter mixture. Add liquid ingrediants to dry ones and stir to combine, fold in cheese and sausage.
pour batter into the prepared pan. bake for 25 mins. or until bread is golden brown. Remove from oven and let cool for 10 mins.
