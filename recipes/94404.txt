Five-Minute Frosty Frappuccino 

stkates98 

First- Prepare about one cup of instant or brewed decaf coffee. Pour into ice cube tray; freeze overnight. Remove cubes and store in freezer in a ziploc. 

To Make the Frappuccino: 

Place in blender: 4-5 coffee-ice-cubes, 1 cup skim milk, 2 packets of stevia powder (optional), 1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla or almond extract (optional). Blend until smooth and frothy. Serve immediately. You may want to adjust amounts of ingredients depending on personal taste.
