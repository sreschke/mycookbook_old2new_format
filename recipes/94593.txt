6 tblsp. olive oil
5 large carrots, thinly sliced
2-1/2 cups thinly sliced onions
1 tsp dried thyme
1 tsp. brown sugar
1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg
4 cups chicken broth
1/4 cup orange juice

Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat.  Add carrots & onions; saute 4 minutes.  Add thyme, brown sugar & nutmeg; saute until vegetables are tender, about 6 minutes.  Add chicken broth.  cover pot; simmer until carrots are very soft, about 25 minutes.  Using slotted spoon, transfer vegetables to processor.  Add 1/4 cup cooking liquid.  Puree vegetables until smooth.  Return puree to pot.  Stir in orange juice.  Season to taste w/salt & pepper.  

6 Servings
This is delicious either hot or cold.
