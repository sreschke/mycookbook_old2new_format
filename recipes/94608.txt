3 cups chicken broth
1 cup peanut butter
1.2 ts salt
1/8 ts sugar
1 1/3 cup cream or koffiemelk
Chopped Peanuts

Heat chicken broth to boiling; add peanut butter, stirring until smooth.  Season to taste salt.  Add sugar.  Stir in cream or koffimelk.  Heat; do not boil.  Top with chopped peanuts.  Serves 6.
