
Chili

1 lb. hamburger (I use the extra lean ground
beef with only 4 grams of fat)
2- 16 oz. cans light kidney beans
2- 14 1/2 oz. cans diced tomatoes
1- 8 oz. can tomato sauce
1 onion, chopped
1/2 green pepper, chopped
2 tsp. garlic ( I use minced)
2 tbs. chili powder
2 tsp. salt ( I use seasoned salt)
1 tsp. pepper ( I use cracked)
Dash of BBQ spice
Dash of cumin
Dash of Italian seasoning
Dash of oregano
2 tsp. sugar ( I use Equal)

Brown beef, onion and green pepper that garlic has been added to.  Add
other ingredients.  Cook in crockpot for 8-10 hours.
