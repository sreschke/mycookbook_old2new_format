1 PKG BLUEBERRY MUFFIN MIX
1/4 C. BUTTER
1 CAN FRUIT PIE FILLING
1/2 TSP CINNAMON
1/2 TSP ALMOND EXTRACTR
1/4 C. CHOPPED NUTS

oven 350.  In med pan melt butter. set aside to cool slightly. Empty blueberries into strainer and rinse under cold running water. drain. Pour fruit pie filling into an ungreased 9inch square pan. sprinkle cinnamon & almond extract and drained blueberries over pie filling.  Add dry muffin mix and nuts to cooled, melted butter. combine with a spoon or fork til crumbly. Distribute evenly over fruit in pan. bake 30-40 min til golden brown.
