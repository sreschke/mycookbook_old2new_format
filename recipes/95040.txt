    Ingredients:

2 lbs. ground beef (may substitute ground turkey)
2 (14 1/2 oz.) cans of diced tomatoes
1 cup water
2-4 T. chili powder
1 large green pepper (chopped)
1 large onion (chopped)
2-3 cups of shredded cheddar cheese (may substitute 
     Mexican blend of cheese)
2 packages of corn tortillas

Combine beef, green pepper and onion, saute. When meat is cooked, add tomatoes, chili powder and water. Bring to a boil and cover, simmer for 20 minutes.

Line a 9x13x2 inch baking pan with one-third of the tortillas. Top with 1/3 meat mixture and 1/3 of the cheese. Repeat layering with the remaining ingredients, making two more layers.

Bake uncovered in 350 oven for 45 minutes. Serve with salsa, shredded lettuce, and diced tomatoes, and sour cream on the side.

* This dish freezes well after cooked.
