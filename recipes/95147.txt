Serves 12  Prep Time: 20 min.  Baking Time: 45 min.
2 sticks (1 cup) unsalted butter, softened		
1 cup peanut butter, smooth or chunky
1/2 cup firmly packed light brown sugar		
3 large eggs
8 ounces cream cheese, softened		
6 ounces bittersweet chocolate, chopped
1 1/2 cups granulated sugar			
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup sifted all-purpose flour
Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Butter 13- x 9-inch baking pan. In bowl, with electric mixer, cream 1/2 stick of butter with 1 cup peanut butter and 1/2 cup brown sugar until light. Beat in 1 egg and 8 oz cream cheese, a little at a time, until mixture is smooth. In top of double boiler over barely simmering water, combine 1/2 stick butter, cut into pieces, with 6 oz chocolate and heat, stirring, until melted and smooth. Let mixture cool. In another bowl, with electric mixer, cream together remaining 1 stick butter with 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the remaining 2 eggs, 1 at a time, until incorporated. Stir in melted chocolate mixture, 1 teaspoon vanilla, and 1 cup flour until combined. Pour batter into prepared pan.  Drop dollops of peanut butter/cream cheese mixture into batter, swirling it in with tip of knife to make marble pattern. Bake brownies in middle of oven 45 min., or until brownies pull away slightly from sides of pan and tester inserted in center comes out with crumbs adhering to it. Let brownies cool before cutting into bars.  *Be sure not to overbake them and let them cool fully before cutting. Don't double the recipe: Make 2 separate batches.
