2LBS GROUND BEEF
3 CLOVES GARLIC DICED
1 BUNCH GREEN ONIONS
1/2 BELL PEPPER CHOPPED
1 EGG
1 HANDFULL SALTINE CRACKERS (CRUSHED)
SALT AND PEPPER

IN A LARGE BOWL COMBINE ALL INGREDIENTS. MAKE INTO ROUND BALLS. BROWN IN A SMALL AMOUNT OF OIL ON ALL SIDES. PLACE IN BAKING DISH. FINISH IN THE OVEN ON 350F FOR ABOUT 30 MIN OR BOIL IN TOMATOE SAUCE FOR SPAGATTIE.
