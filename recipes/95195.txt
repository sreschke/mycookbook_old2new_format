1 cup all purpose flour
1/4 cup sugar
4 tsp baking powder
3/4 tsp salt
1 cup yellow corn meal
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1/4 oil

Conbine flour:  sugar, baking powder, and salt:  stir in corn meal.  Add eggs, milk, and oil.  Beat with electric beater till ust smooth, about 1 minute.  Do not over beat.  Pour into grease 9x9x2 inch pan.  Bake in hot overn 425 for 20- 25 minutes.

Corn stick or muffins:  2/3 full @ 425 for 12 to 15 minutes
