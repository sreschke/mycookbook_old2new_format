  
Make this on a day when you will be able to cook it for only 6 to 7 hours. Cooking longer, will make it mushy and the chicken will be falling off the bones. It will still 
taste good if cooked longer but will not look as appealing.
 
3 each: bone in chicken breast
halves, thighs and legs

Salt and grounf black pepper
2 tablespoons sweet
Hungarian paprika

1 onion, sliced

2 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup sour cream
 
Wash chicken pieces: trim off excess skin. Rub all over with salt, pepper and 
paprika. Place in slow cooker. Scatter onion over the chicken. Pour in broth. 
Cover tightly; cook on high setting for 
30 minutes. Turn to low setting and continue cooking for 6 to 7 hours or until 
chicken is tender. Just before serving, stir in the sour cream. Serves 
6.
