1 head cabbage, shredded
pepper to taste
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1 lb. hamburger
1/2 cup regular rice(uncooked)
1 onion, chopped
1 can(10 3/4 oz) tomato soup
1 soup can water
     Grease 9x13 pan. Lay cabbage in bottom. Brown meat and onions then drain. Stir in garlic powder, pepper and rice. Put on top of cabbage. Combine soup and water and pour over casserole. DO NOT stir. COver tightly with foil and bake in 350 degree oven for 1 hour.
