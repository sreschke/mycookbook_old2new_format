1 c. shortening (we use butter)
2 c. sugar
1 c. sour cream
2 eggs
1 t. vanilla
4 t. baking powder
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t. soda
4&1/2 c. flour

Mix together flour, soda, salt, powder, in separate bowl mix remaining ingredients, slowly add flour, mixing on low until smooth. Refrigerate for an hour, then roll out and cut  shapes. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes. Do not cook until brown - they are to remain white.
