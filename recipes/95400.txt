6-8 oz spaghetti noodles
6-8 cups boiling water
one cup spaghetti sauce

Start boiling water.  Add noodles.  Allow to cook 10-12 minutes.  Cook til al dente.  Drain noodles.
Add oil to prevent clumping if desired.  Divide noodles into two bowls.

In Microwave heat spaghetti sauce.  Pour half of sauce over noodles.  Do it again over other bowl of noodles.
