1/2 c. butter
2 c. white choc. chips
2 lg eggs
1/2 c. sugar
1 c. flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp almond extract
1/2 c. seedless raspberry jam
1/4 c. toasted sliced almonds
heat oven to 325
Grease & sugar 9 " square pan

Melt butter in med micro bowl on high for 1 min. Stir. Add 1 c. chips. let stand, do not stir.
Beat eggs in lg mixer bowl ti foamy. Add sugar, beat tillight lemon colored, abut 5 min. Stir in chip/butter mixture. Add flour, salt & extract. Mix at low speed til combined. Spread 2/3 of batter into pan. Bake 15-17 min. Remove from over to wire rack. Heat jam in small micro. dish on high 30 sec. spread jam over warm crust. Stir remaining chips into remaining batter. drop spoonfuls of batter over jam. sprinkle with almonds Bake 25-30 min or til edges are browned. Cool completely in pan
