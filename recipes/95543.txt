1 corned beef brisket
1 quart apple juice
1 cup brown sugar
1 tsp. prepared mustard
8 small red potatoes
2 med. carrots, pared and cut into chunks
1 onion, peeled and cut into eighths
1/2 head cabbage, cut into chunks

Place meat, juice, sugar and mustard in a large stockpot.  Bring to a boil,reduce heat and simmer, covered for 40 minutes per pound of meat.  Add vegetables for last 30 minutes of cooking.  Remove meat and vegetables from pot.  Slice meat thinly across the grain.  Serve with vegetables and some of the cooking liquid.

Method for slow cooker;  Place all ingredients in a large slow cooker (cut meat in half if necessary); stir to mis.  Cook on high for 4 to 5 hours or on low for 8 - 10 hours.
