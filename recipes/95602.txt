1/2 cup white vinegar
1/2 cup chicken broth
10 large garlic cloves, chopped
2 1/2 tbls soy sauce
1/4 tsp pepper
4 skinless boneless chicken breast halves
2 tsps Mrs. Dash 
1 tbls sesame oil
3 large bay leaves
Cooked white rice 

Whisk vinegar, chicken broth, garlic, soy sauce and pepper to blend. Season chicken breast halves on both sides with Mrs. Dash. Heat oil in  skillet over med-high. Add chicken and cook til golden, about 4 minutes per side. Pour vinegar mixture over chicken. Add bay leaves.  Turn heat to low, cover and simmer til chicken is just cooked
through. Put chicken on a plate. Boil pan juices  2 minutes. Season  with salt. Spoon over chicken, discarding bay leaves.

Serve with rice. 

Serves 4.
