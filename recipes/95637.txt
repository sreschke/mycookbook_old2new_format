2 cups flour 
1 cup sugar 
2 teaspoons baking powder 
1/2 teaspoon baking soda 
1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon 
1 teaspoon salt 
1 cup buttermilk (or I cup milk with 1 tbls vinegar)
1/4 cup oil 
2 eggs 
2 teaspoons vanilla
2 tablespoons sugar 
1 teaspoon cinnamon 
2 teaspoons butter
 
Heat oven to 350F. Grease a 9x5 loaf pan. Measure flour, 1 cup sugar, baking powder, soda, cinnamon, salt, buttermilk, oil, eggs, and vanilla into large mixing bowl. Beat 3 minutes. Pour in loaf pan. Smooth top. 
                  
Topping: Combine 2 tablespoons sugar, 1 teaspoon cinnamon, and butter, mixing until crumbly. Sprinkle smoothed batter. Using knife, cut in a light swirling motion to give a marbled effect. Bake about 50 minutes. Test with toothpick. Remove from pan to cool.
 
1 loaf.
