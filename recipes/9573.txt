1 Lb. penne or mostaccioli
1 tbsp. olive oil
3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
1/4 tsp. red-pepper flakes
1 28 oz. can plum tomatoes, drained
1/2 cup grated romano cheese
1/4 cup finely chopped frest parsley, Italian leaf
Salt and fresh ground pepper to taste

Cook penne until al dente.  Meanwhile, heat oil in large nonstick skillet over low heat.  Add garlic and red pepper flakes; cook, stirring, until the garlic is golden, about 1 minute.  Add tomatoes, crushing them roughly with a fork.  Bring to a simmer over low heat and cook until slightly reduced, about 5 minutes.  When pasta is ready, drain it and return to pot.  Stir in the sauce and put the pot over high heat.  Add chesse and parsley; toss well.  Taste adn adjust seasonings; serve immediately.  Serves 4
