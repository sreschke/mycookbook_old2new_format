1 pkg nut bread mix
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1 c. water
1 egg
FILLING
8 oz cream cheese, softened
1/4 c. sugar
1/4 tsp almond extract
1/4 c. raspbenny preserves
oven to 350
Greas bottom only of loaf pan. In lg. bowl, blend loaf ingredients, stir 50-75 strokes til dry particles are moistened. Pour into prepared pan. Bake 45-55 min. Cool 15 min then remove from pan. cool completely.
In small bowl, combine cream cheese, sugar & almond extract, mix well. To assemble, split loaf horizontally into 3 layers, Place first layer on serving plate, spread with half of cream cheese filling. Carefully spread 2 tbsp of preserves over fililng. Repeat with second layer. Top with thrid layer. Cover tightly with plastic wrap or foil and refrig for 2 hours or til filling is firm. Slice before serving, Store tightly covered in frig.
