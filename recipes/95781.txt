2 c sliced mushrooms
1/4 c chopped green onion
2 Tbsp flour
1/4 c water
2 Tbsp dry sherry
1/2 c plain nonfat yogurt
1/4 tsp salt(opt)
1/8 tsp pepper
1 tsp instant chicken bouillon
1 lb skinless,boneless chicken breasts
1/4 tsp paprika

Preheat oven to 350. Saute mushrooms and onions in skillet that has been sprayed with nonstick cooking 
spray. Shake flour and water together to prevent lumps. Add to mushrooms along with sherry,yogurt,
salt,pepper and bouillon. Cook,stirring constantly,
until thickened. Spray a 9" quare pan with cooking 
spray and arrange chicken in dish. Pour sauce over
chicken and sprinkle with paprika. Bake for 30 minutes or until chicken is no longer pink.

Serves 4;205 cal
