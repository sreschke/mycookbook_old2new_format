1 box (7 oz.) spaghetti
broken in 2 in. pieces
2 cups cooked cubed ckicken
1cup shreaded cheddar cheese
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 medium chopped onion
2 cans (4oz. each ) sliced 
mushrooms drained
1/3 cup milk
1/4 cup chopped green pepper
1 2oz. jar chopped pimientos, drained
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon pepper
additional cheadar cheese optional


Cook spaghetti acording to package directions; drain. Transfer to a large mixing bowl; add the next 10 ingredients and mix well. Spoon into a greased 2-1/2-qt. casserole; sprinkle with cheese if desired. Bake uncovered, at 375 Degrees for 40-45 minutes.

Yield: 6-8 servings

From: Rick Maurer
