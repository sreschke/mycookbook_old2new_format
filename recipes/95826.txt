meat of 1 green coconut
1 cup hot water
3 cups boiling water

In a blender, puree the coconut meat with 1 cup hot water. When liquified, pour into a bowl and add boiling water. let stand 30 minutes. Strain the liquid over a bowl, discarding the solids. Chill in the refrigerator. Pour over crushed ice and serve, or use in a cocktail
