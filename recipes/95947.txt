Classy Chicken

3 chicken breasts, skinned and deboned.
1/4 tsp. pepper
3 tbsp. oil
10 oz. pkg. frozen asparagus or broccoli (fresh is even better).
10 oz. can cream of chicken soup
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 tsp. curry powder
1 tsp. lemon juice
1 cup grated cheddar cheese

Cut chicken into 2"x4" pieces and sprinkle with pepper. Saute slowly in oil over medium heat until white and opaque (about 6 minutes). Drain. Cook asparagus or broccoli until tender crisp.Drain and arrange in bottom of a buttered 7" diameter casserole dish. Place chicken on top. Mix soup, mayonnaise, curry and lemon juice together and pour over chicken. Sprinkle top with cheddar cheese and bake uncovered at 375 degrees for 30-35 minutes. Note: this is really easy. Goes great with rice. Serves 4-6.
