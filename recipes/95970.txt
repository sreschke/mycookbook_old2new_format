4 sq Baker's unsweetened Chocolate
3/4 c butter
2 c sugar
3 eggs
1 t vanilla
1 c flour
1-1/2 c chopped walnuts

Microwave chocolate and margarine in large bowl on HIGH 2 minutes or until butter melts. Stir until chocolate is melted. Stir sugar into chocolate until well blended. Mix in eggs and vanilla. Stir in flour and nuts. Spread in 13 x 9 greased and foil lined pan.  Bake 30-35 minutes. Toothpick test--fudge crumbs will stick. Do not overbake.  Cool in pan.
