3/4 c chocolate fudge topping, divided
1 graham cracker crust
1 tub (8 oz) whipped topping, thawed
1-1/4 c cold milk
1/2 c creamy peanut butter
2 pkg (4 serving size) vanilla pudding, instant

Spoon 1/2 cup fudge sauce into bottom of pie crust. Gently spread 1/2 of the whipped topping over the fudge; place in freezer 10 minutes. Pour milk into large bowl; stir in peanut butter until smooth. Add pudding mixes. Beat with wire whisk 2 minutes or until smooth.  Immediately add remaining whipped topping. Spoon over layers in pie crust.  Refrigerate 3 hours oruntil set. To serve, drizzle with reaining fudge topping.
