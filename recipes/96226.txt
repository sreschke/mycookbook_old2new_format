Fruit Cocktail Cake

1 cup flour
1 cup sugar
1 tsp. soda
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
1 egg, slightly beaten
2/3 cup fruit cocktail juice
1 cup drained fruit cocktail

Put in 8 inch square pan.

Sprinkle over top of cake:
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 cup chopped nuts

Bake at 350* for 40 - 45 minutes
