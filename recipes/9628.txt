4 stalks of celery
1 Large can of tomatoes
1 large zucchini 
1 large can of tomato juice
1 large bell pepper
1 large onion
1 Tblspn. of oregano
1 Tblspn. of parsley
1 lb. italian sausage
salt & pepper to taste

saute the onion & celery untill tender. Brown the sausage & drain off the grease. Add the sauteed vegetables & sausage & all the other ingredients except the bell pepper to a large soup pan.Let this simmer for about a half hour. Add the bell pepper & simmer on low for another half hour.


This is superb!
