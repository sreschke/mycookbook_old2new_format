1 T minced dired onions
2 T dried green pepper flakes
1 T dired clery flakes
1 t instant chicken bouillon granules
1/2 t salt
1/8 t garlic powder
dash of crushed red pepper

Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Stir until evenly distributed. Put mixture into foil packet or plastic bag. Label with date and contents. Store in a cool, dry place. Use within 6 to 8 months.. Make 1 package spanish rice seasoning mix.

To make Spanish rice:

1 c long grain rice
1 (16 oz) can stewed sliced tomatoes
1 c water
1 pkg. spanish rice seasoning mix

Spray a large skillet with cooking spray. Add uncooked rice. cook over moderate heat, stirring constantly, until rice is lightly browned. Add tomatoes, water and seasoning. Stir to combine. Bring to boil.  Cover and reduce heat. Simmer 20 minutes. Makes 4 sevings.
