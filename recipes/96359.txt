8 oz ground 90% lean beef
1/2 cup chopped onion
3 cups cooked noodles, rinsed and drained
1/3 full cup (2 ounces) thin-sliced pepperoni, chopped
1 15oz can Hunts Chunky Tomato Sauce
1 4oz can sliced mushrooms, drained
1 tsp pizza spice or Italian seasoning
3/4 cup (3 ounces) Kraft shredded reduced-fat mozzarella cheese

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a large skillet sprayed with olive-flavored cooking spray, brown meat and onion. Stir in noodles, pepperoni, tomato sauce, mushrooms, and seasoning. Mix well. Pour into ungreased 8x8in baking dish. Bake 20 minutes. Sprinkle top with cheese. Bake an additional 5 minutes or until cheese melts.

Hint: 2 2/3 cups uncooked noodles usually makes 3 cups.
Use Turkey Pepperoni and save a few more fat grams.

282 Calories, 11 gm fat, 3gm fiber
