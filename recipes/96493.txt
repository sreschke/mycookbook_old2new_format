Rhubarb Cake

1 1/2 cups brown sugar
1/2 cup margarine
1 egg
1 cup sour milk (1 cup milk and 1 Tbl. vinegar)
1 tsp. baking soda
2 cups flour
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 cups rhubarb, cut up

Topping:
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon

Cream margarine and sugar; beat in egg.  Add sifted dry ingredients with sour milk and vanilla.  Fold in rhubarb.  Pour into greased 13x9-inch baking pan.
Mix sugar and cinnamon topping; sprinkle on cake.

Bake st 350* for 35 to 40 minutes.
