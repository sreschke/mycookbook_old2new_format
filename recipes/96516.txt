1 pound butter
8 eggs
3/12 cups sugar
8 tablespoons carnation can milk
3 1/2 cups Swansdown flour
1 tablespoon vanilla flavor

Cream butter and sugar.
Add eight (8) eggs - one at a time.
Add milk and flour, alternately.
Add vanilla flavor.

Pour into greased tubepan.

Bake at 325 degrees for one (1) hour and 25 minutes.

From the kitchen of Hattie Simmons
