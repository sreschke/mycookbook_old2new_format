Yield: 4 servings

Nutritional Information: 
Total calories: 800 
Calories per serving: 200 (based on 4 ounces of cooked breast meat) 
Nutrient % Grams 
Carbohydrate 15 9 
Protein 65 32 
Fat 20 4 


Ingredients 
4 chicken breasts, skinned 
1 cup water 
2 chicken bouillon cubes 
2 Tbs Dijon (spicy) mustard 
1/2 cup white wine (optional) 
2 Tbs flour 
1/2 cup evaporated skim milk or low-fat milk 



Directions 
1 In a large saucepan, put the chicken, water, bouillon, mustard, and wine (optional).  
2 Cover and bring to a boil; simmer over medium-low heat for 20 minutes, or until the juices run clear when the chicken is poked with a fork. 
3 Mix the flour with the milk. (Evaporated milk offers a richer flavor.) 
4 Skim off any fat on top of the broth. Thicken the broth into a nice sauce by slowly stirring in the flour and milk mixture. 
5 Cover and cook over low heat for 5 minutes to allow the flavors to blend.
