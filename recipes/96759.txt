Cook pasta and drain.
Cook some bacon in pan until crisp.
In a mixing bowl place spaghetti (or pasta of your choice).
Sprinkle some good oil over.

Add some:-
salt and a good grind of black pepper.
finely chopped parsley.
freshly grated parmesan.

Then add the bacon and its juice and toss gently.

Place in serving bowls.
Separate an egg and mix yolk through spaghetti and serve with some more parmesan if you wish.
