 1 1/2 TBLS CORNSTARCH
1 CUP SUGAR 
2 CUPS MILK 
3 BEATEN EGGS
1 TSP VANILLA
MIX TOGETHER SUGAR AND CORNSTARCH.

MIX TOGETHER MILK AND EGGS! AND HEAT TO BOILING THEN ADD SUGAR MIXTURE, AND VANILLA.
COOK UNTIL THICK, STIRRING CONSTANTLY.
PLACE IN LAYERS OF EACH VANILLA WAFERS, BANANAS AND "GUMP".
