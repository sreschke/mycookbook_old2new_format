2-CANS (32 OZ) SWEET POTATOES, DRAINED
1/4-CUP (1/2 STICK) BUTTER, MELTED
1/4-CUP ORANGE JUICE
1/2-TSP GROUND CINNAMON
15-JET PUFFED MARSHMALLOWS

HEAT OVEN TO 350 DEGREES

MIX ALL INGREDIENTS EXCEPT MARSHMALLOWS TIL WELL BLENDED. SPOON INTO 1-QT CASSEROLE DISH; TOP WITH MARSHMELLOWS.

BAKE 20 MINS OR TIL MARSHMALLOWS ARE LIGHTLY BROWNED.

FROM THE KITCHEN OF PAM HUGHES
