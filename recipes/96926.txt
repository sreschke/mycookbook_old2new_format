1 lb. stew beef
1/2 C chopped onion
1/2 lb. sliced mushrooms
2-3 chopped garlic cloves
2 tbsps. olive oil
1 can beef broth + 1 can water
1 sm can tomatoe sauce
1/4 C vinegar/red wine
1/4 tsp. allspice
2-3 bay leaves

Brown beef in olive oil; add onion, garlic and mushrooms browning until onions are opaque or lightly browned.  Add broth, water, tomatoe sauce,
vinegar/wine and spices.  Salt & Pepper to taste.
Simmer for about 1 hour.  Serve over wide egg noodles or home made spaetzel.
