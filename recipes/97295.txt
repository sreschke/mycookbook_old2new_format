1 broiler-fryer chicken, cut up
3 onions, chopped
1 carrot, sliced
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 tsp oregano
1/2 tsp basil
1 tsp salt
1/8 tsp ground black pepper
1 tbs paprika
1 can (16 oz) diced tomatoes
1 lb shelled, raw shrimp
2 cups cooked rice

In crock-pot, combine chicken, onions, carrots, garlic, paprika, oregano, basil, salt, pepper and tomatoes.

Cover and cook on low 4 to 5 hours.

Turn control to high. Add rice and shrimp.  Cover and cook on high for 30 to 40 minutes or until shrimp is done.

Serves 6.
