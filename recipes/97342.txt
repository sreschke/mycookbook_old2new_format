4 chicken breasts or legs
2 T. lemon juice
1/4 C. apricot preserves
1 T. minces gingeroot
1 T. dijon mustard
1/4 T. each s+p
2 T. sesame seeds
1 T soy sauce
2 t. sesame oil
1 t. grated orange rind

In small bowl, mix all ingredients.Brush over chicken. Bake in 425 F oven, skin side down, for 35 min. Turn over 1/2 way through cooking.
