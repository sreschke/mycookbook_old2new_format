TWINKIE SUPRISE

1 box Twinkies
1 large cool whip
1 10 oz. Reese's pieces (may use M&M's in place of Reese's )
1 large vanilla instant pudding

Cut Twinkies in half lengthwise and layer in bottom of 9x13 inch pan. Mix vanilla pudding according to directions and pour over Twinkies. Crush Reese's pieces and put 3/4 bag on top of pudding. Spread cool whip and sprinkle rest of Reese's pieces and refrigerate overnite.
