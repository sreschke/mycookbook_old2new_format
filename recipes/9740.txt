RED LOBSTER'S GARLIC CHEESE BISCUITS 
  
2 c. baking mix, such as Original Bisquick            
2/3 c. whole,low fat or skim milk                       
1/2 c. lightly packed shredded Cheddar cheese    1/4 c. butter/margarine
1/2 tsp garlic powder
  
       Spoon baking mix into cup to measure it. (Otherwise, it packs too much if scooped into the cup) In a large bowl with a wooden spoon toss together baking mix and cheese until well mixed. Add milk, stirring until soft dough forms. Beat vigorously with spoon 30 seconds. Drop dough by heaping tablespoonfuls onto ungreased baking sheet. Bake in preheated 450 degree oven 8 to 10 minutes or until golden brown. Melt butter. Stir in garlic powder. Brush over warm biscuits before removing from baking sheet. Serve warm.
