36 large marshmallows
1-1/2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 cup chunky peanut butter
2 tablespoons butter or margarine

Line a 9-inch square pan with foil; butter the foil.  Arrange marshmallows in pan.  In a double boiler or microwave-safe bowl, melt chocolate chips, peanut butter, and butter.  Pour over the marshmallows.  Chill completely.  Cut between marshmallows.

Yield:  3 dozen

Source:  Taste of Home Magazine, Dec/Jan '95 issue
