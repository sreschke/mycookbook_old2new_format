4 large potatoes
1/3 cup Parmesan cheese
1/3 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. salt
fresh ground pepper to taste
1 tsp. paprika
Non-stick cooking spray

Wash potatoes and cut into 1/2 inch strips, about the size of large homestyle French-fries.  

Combine cheese, garlic powder salt and pepper in a bowl mixing well.  Spray Non-stick cooking spray on cookie pan and then arrange potato slices on pan.  

Spray potatoes lightly with Non-stick spray and sprinkle cheese mixture over potatoes.  

Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 45 minutes.
