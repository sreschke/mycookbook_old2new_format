3/4 cup chopped pecans or halves
25 ounces frozen yeast roll dough, unthawed
3/4 cup butter, melted (1-1/2 sticks)
1 3.5 ounce package cook-and-serve vanilla pudding (NOT instant)
3/4 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup white sugar
2 teaspoons cinnamon

Butter bundt or tube pan. Sprinkle pecans over bottom. Place frozen rolls over pecans. Drizzle butter over rolls. 

Combine pudding, sugars and cinnamon; sprinkle over rolls.

Let rise overnight (do not cover, as will rise above the pan.) In morning, place pan in heated 350-degree oven and bake for 25 minutes. Invert onto plate and serve.
