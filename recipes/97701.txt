Ingredients:
3 cups  fresh rhubarb, cut into 1/2 inch pieces
1/2 cup  sugar
3/4 cup  water
2/3 cup  sugar
1/3 cup  shortening
1 tsp.  grated orange peal
1  egg
1 cup  sifted enriched flour
1 tsp.  baking powder
1/4 tsp. salt
2/3 cup  milk
1/2 tsp.  vanilla

Method:
1. Combine rhubarb, sugar, and water.  Cover and simmer 10-15 minutes until rhubarb is tender.
2. Cream second sugar and shortening till fluffy.  Add orange peel and egg and beat well.
3. Sift together: flour, baking powder, and salt.  Add to the creamed mixture alternately with milk, stirring until smooth.  Add vanilla.
4. Pour batter into greased 8x8x2 pan.  Pour hot rhubarb sauce over batter.
5. Bake in moderate oven (350 degrees) for 30-40 minutes.
6. Serve hot or cold.  Yields 6 servings.
