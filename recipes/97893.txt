STRAWBERRY PIE

1 small pkg strawberry Jello
1 1/2 cups boiling water
1 pkg instant vanilla pudding
1 cup fresh or frozen strawberries
1 baked pie crust

Dissolve Jello in boiling water. Stir in instant pudding, while Jello is still hot, until dissolved. When it is cool and starts to thicken, add strawberries and pour into pie crust. Serve with whipped cream
