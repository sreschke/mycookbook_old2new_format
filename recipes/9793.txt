Broccoli & Cheese Casserole 

2 packages of frozen broccoli
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 can creme of celery soup
8 oz. cheese
1 small onion (chopped fine)
1 egg
6 crumbled Ritz crackers 

Cook broccoli according to directions on box. Combine egg, soup, cheese, mayonnaise, and crackers in small bowl. Place cooked broccoli in greased casserole dish. Pour mixture over broccoli and bake for 30 minutes on 350 degrees.
