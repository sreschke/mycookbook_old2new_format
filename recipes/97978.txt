Amount Measure Ingredient -- Preparation Method
-------- ------------ --------------------------------
1/2 C Tomato Puree
2 Tsp Lemon Peel -- Grated
2 Tbsp Lemon Juice
1 Tbsp Rice Vinegar
2 Tsp Fresh Ginger -- Finely Chopped
2 Tsp Soy Sauce
1 Tsp Honey
1/2 Tsp Cinnamon
1/4 Tsp Tabasco Sauce
1 Clove Garlic -- Finely Chopped
1/2 C Beef Broth
2 Tsp Cornstarch

Cook all ingredients except broth and cornstarch in a 1 quart saucepan over medium heat 2 minutes, stirring occasionally. Mix broth and cornstarch; stir into hot mixture. Cook about 2 minutes or until thickened. Baste pork the last 10 minutes of cooking time.
