Choco-Nut Meringue Clouds 
ÿÿÿÿÿÿÿÿÿÿ
2 large eggs whites, room temperature 1 tsp vanilla extract 
1/4 tsp cream of tartar
1/4 tsp salt
2/3 cup sugar
1 cup pecans chopped 
1 cup mini semi-sweet chocolate chips 

Preheat oven to 275øF. Grease baking sheets. In a large mixer bowl at high speed, beat egg whites, vanilla, cream of tartar and salt until soft peaks form. Gradually add sugar and beat until very stiff and glossy and sugar is almost dissolved. By hand, fold in pecans and chips. Drop by well-rounded teaspoonfuls about 1" apart on baking sheets. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until set and dry to the touch. Cool cookies on baking sheets for 1 minute. Carefully transfer cookies to wire racks to cool. Store in an airtight container in a cool place. Yield: 5 servings.
