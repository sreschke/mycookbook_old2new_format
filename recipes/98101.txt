1 (6 oz) pkg butterscotch pieces
1/2 c peanut butter
3 oz chow mein noodles
1 c marshmallows
1/2 c Planters peanuts

Melt butterscotch chips and stir in peanut butter.  Add chow mein noodles, marshmallows, and peanuts.  Toss until coated.  Drop by teaspoon on wax paper and chill in refrigerator.
