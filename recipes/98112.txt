Pecan Sweet Potato Pie
2 cups yams (1 40 0z, can or about 8 fresh sweet potatoes)
1/3 cup butter-melted 
1/2 cup sugar 
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 eggs - beaten
1 tsp. lemon juice

1 tsp. vanilla (goes in at end)
2 tsp. flour (goes in at end)

Topping:
1 cup chopped pecans 
3/4 cup brown sugan 
1/3 cup flour 
1/3 cup melted butter

Mix the first six ingredients together.  Then add vanilla and 2 tsp. Flour

Mix topping ingredients together. Place on top of casserole and bake 30 min. at 350 degrees,
