4 1/2 Cups all-purpose flour 

5 Teaspoons Baking powder 

1 Teaspoon Salt 

5 Tablespoons Solid shortening 

3 Tablespoons Unsalted butter -- cut up 

In a large bowl whisk together dry ingredients. 
With electric mixer on low or #2 of a 3 speed mixer, cut in fats until uniform in texture and fat particles are no longer visible. Remove from mixer. 
Divide among 3 air tight containers. 
Yield 3 batches mix, scant 2 c. ea. Keep refrigerated for up to one month. 

Recipe is easily doubled, tripled, etc. Best biscuit mix you'll ever use, guaranteed.
