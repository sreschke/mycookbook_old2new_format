1 can tomato sauce
2 teaspoons lime juice
1-1/2 tsp chili powder
1-1/2 tsp oregano
1 lb boneless skinless chicken, cut in strips
2 cups corn kernels
1-1/2 cups shredded cheese

optional toppings such as lettuce, chopped tomatoes, sour cream, etc.


Heat tomato sauce, lime and seasonings in a large frypan over medium heat.  Add chicken, stir and cook for 3 minutes.  Stir in corn.  cook 2 minutes or until chicken is cooked and no longer pink.  Reduce heat.  Stir in shredded cheese. If you prefer a creamier recipe, add some velveeta cheese until melted. You can serve this over nachos or wrap in soft tortillas.
