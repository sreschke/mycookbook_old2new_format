3 cups warm buttermilk
2 eggs
2 cups sugar
1 1/2 cups honey
4 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. salt
5 cups wheat flour
1/4 tsp. nutmeg

Beat eggs well.  Add sugar and honey.  Mix well.  Add buttermilk.  Combine dry ingredients and then mix into egg mixture till smooth.  

Grease & flour 3 loaf pans and bake @ 350 degrees for 50 minutes.

Enjoy!  

This is a bread from the Netherlands.  It's Dutch name is pepercoek.
