2 pounds extra lean ground beef

1-can cream of mushroom soup

1-can grated cheddar cheese

1-(2 pound) package frozen Tater Tots.


Put ground beef in bottom of geased 13x9x2 inch pan. Spread soup over meat and cover with cheese. Top with Tater tots.

Bake covered 45 min. on 350 deg.
Then bake uncovered for another 15 min.
