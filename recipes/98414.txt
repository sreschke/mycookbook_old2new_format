1 pkg (8 oz) cream cheese
1 tsp Italian seasoning
1 pkg (8 oz) shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
1/2 cup pizza or spaghetti sauce

Wheat thins

Beat cream cheese and Italian seasoning until well blended.   Spread onto bottom of 9-inch pie plate.  Top with layers of 1 cup of the mozzarella cheese and pizza sauce;  sprinkle with remaining 1 cup mozzarella.  Bake at 350 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes or until mixture is thoroughly heated and cheese is melted.  Serve with crackers.  Makes 3 cups

Can top with 1/3 cup chopped pepperoni before baking
