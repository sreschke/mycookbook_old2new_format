Preheat over to 350
Butter a casserole dish
In a bowl, add the following in order without mixing.
2 cups buttermilk
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 egg
2 cups corn meal

Mix quickly.
Pour into casserole.  Cook in over 40 minutes.
If double receipe, increase cook time to 55 minutes.
