4 sheets aluminum foil
1 pnd. boneless beef sirloin steak
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. pepper
4 red potatoes (cut up)
1 green bell pepper (cut up in strips)
1 can cream of mushroom soup (undiluted)
1 1/2 tbsp. worcestershire sauce

Sprinkle beef strips with garlic and pepper.  Combine beef with potatoes, green peppers, soup, and worcestershire sauce. Center mixture on each foil piece.  Bring up foil sides.  Double fold top and ends to seal packet.  Bake 30 minutes on a cookie sheet in preheated 450 degrees oven or in a covered grill for 15 minutes.
