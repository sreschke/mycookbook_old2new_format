Crock Pot Chicken in Mushroom Gravy

3 whole chicken breasts, halved
1/4 cup dry white wine or chicken broth
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 4-ounce can mushrooms, sliced
Salt and pepper

Place chicken pieces in crock pot. Season with salt
and pepper. Mix wine and soup. Pour over chicken. Add
mushrooms. Cover and cook on low for 7 to 9 hours or
high 3 to 4 hours. Recipe may be doubled in 5-quart
crock pot.
