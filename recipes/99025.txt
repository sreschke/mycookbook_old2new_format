1/3 c. bourbon
1/3 c. water
1 T. sherry
2 or 3 T. Chopped Jalapenos
2 or 3 T. minced garlic
1 T. minced ginger
2 pork tenderloins
1/4 c. molasses
1/4 c. ketchup
1 T. oil for brushing
salt & ground pepper

1.Combine the first 6 ingredients. Pour over the tenderloins and marinate at room temp for an hour.
2. Prehat the oven to 450 degrees. Remove the pork from the marinade and pat dry.
3. In a small saucepan, boil the marinade until reduced by 1/3. Add the molasses and ketchup and cook over moderate heat until thickened--about 5 minutes.
4. Heat the oil in an ovenproof skillet. Brush the pork lightly with oil. Sear the pork in the skillet over high heat, turning until browned all over. 3 to 4 minutes.
5. Pour the BBQ sauce over the meat and roast for about 12 minutes or until cooked through. Turn the meat in the sauce.
6. Transfer the pork to a platter. Cover with foil and let stand 5 miutes. Slice meat and serve with any remaining BBQ sauce.
