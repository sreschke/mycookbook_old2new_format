12 corn tortillas
Salt(opt)

Preheat oven to 400. Spray 2 cookie sheets with nonstick cooking spray. Stack tortillas and cut into
six wedges. Place tortillas on prepared cookie sheets in single layer. Lightly spray with cooking spray. If desired,lightly sprinkle with salt. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until lightly browned and crisp.

Makes 72 chips and serves 12;65 cal;1g fat;0mg chol
