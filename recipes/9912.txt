1 pound lean ground beef
1 small onion, diced fined (optional)
1/2 cup diced celery (optional)
4 large potatoes, cubed
2 large carrots  sliced or cubed
1  32oz  package of frozen mixed vegetables
1 can kidney beans (optional) sometimes I use chili beans
1 quart of more of tomato juice or 2 cans of tomato soup and a can of tomato paste
1/2  Cup catsup
1 1/2 teaspoons sugar
Can use seasoning salt,  or chili powder..  Salt & pepper to your taste.
Cook the meat  with onion and celery until meat is no longer pink.  Add the other ingredients.  Use the amount of water you want to.  Bring to a boil and then lower heat and cook several hours.  It is better even after you warm it up.
