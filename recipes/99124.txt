1 cup sugar
3 cups water
1 small can frozen o.j.
1 small can frozen lemonade
1 tall can chunk pineapple
1 can mandarine oranges (drained)
at least 3 large bananas, sliced
1/2 cup marachino cherries, cut up
1 large bag frozen strawberries

Mix and freeze, stirring occasionally. Remove from freezer 1 hr. before serving.

Makes 3 Qts.
