Ingredients for 4 Servings

1/2 tbsp Vegetable oil
1/2 cup (1 small) chopped onion
4/5 cups (16-ounce jar) ORTEGA? Thick &amp; Chunky Salsa
3/5 cups (10-ounce can) ORTEGA? Enchilada Sauce
1/2 pkg (12) ORTEGA? Taco Shells, broken into pieces
3/4 cups (6 ounces) SARGENTO? ChefStyle Mild Cheddar or Taco Blend Shredded
Cheese, divided
Garnish suggestions: sour cream, ORTEGA? Pickled Jalape&ntilde;o Slices, chopped
avocados

Procedure: 

1. PREHEAT oven to 350Ã¸ F.
2. HEAT vegetable oil in large skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Add onion; cook, stirring occasionally, for 1 to 2 minutes or until tender.
4. Stir in salsa and enchilada sauce.
5. Bring to a boil.
6. Reduce heat to low; cook, stirring frequently, for 3 to 4 minutes.
7. LAYER half of taco shell pieces in ungreased 13 x 9-inch baking dish.
8. Top with half of salsa mixture and half of cheese; repeat layers.
9. BAKE for 10 to 15 minutes or until cheese is melted.
10. Garnish with sour cream, jalape&ntilde;os and avocado.
