LOADED BAKED POTATO SALAD

5# small unpeeled red potatoes, cubed
1tsp salt
«tsp pepper
8 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
1# sliced bacon, cooked & crumbled
2c(8oz)shredded cheddar cheese
1 med Vidalia or sweet onion, chopped
3 dill pickles, chopped
1«c(12oz)sour cream
1c mayonnaise
2-3tsp prepared mustard

Place the potatoes in a greased 15x10x1" baking pan; sprinkle with salt & pepper. Bake, uncovered, @ 425§ for 40-45 min or until tender. Cool in pan on a wire rack.

In a large bowl, combine the potatoes, eggs, bacon, cheese, onion & pickles. In a small bowl, combine the sour cream, mayonnaise & mustard; pour over the potato mixture & toss to coat. Serve immediately.

Yield:20 servings
