1 pkg seafood meat or crab meat
1/2 stick of butter
1/2 cup white cooking wine
2 cups milk
1/2 cup flour
dash cayanne pepper
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp chicken base
1 1/2 cup grated colby/jack cheese
flour tortillas

Make white sauce, melt butter add flour and then milk.  If too runny add more milk, if too thick add milk.  Add seasonings and wine, and half the cheese, add seafood, Soften shells in microwave, spoon mixture into shells and roll up. Pour remains over the top and top with cheese.
Bake 350 until hot and bubbly
