1 1/2 cup flour
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 salt
2/3 cup butter
1 1/2 cup sugar 
4T water
1 12oz bag chocolate chips
2 tsp vanilla
4 eggs

Combine dry ingredients
In saucepan melt butter,sugar,water, melt to boiling point add 1 12 oz chocolate chips stir until melted.
Add 2 tsp vanilla
4 eggs Stir in one more 12oz chips Pour into large oblong pan, Bigger than 9x13
Bake 325 40 minutes
