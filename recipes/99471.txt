The taste of the tomatooes, garic and herbs intensifies when tossed with hot paastaa or spooned over sizzling grilled fish.

3 lb. fresh, ripe tomatoes, peeled, seeded and diced
2 tbsp. garlic, finely minced
1/3 c. extra virgin olive oil
1/4 c. chopped basil or 1 tbsp. minced fresh oregano
2 tbsp. minced fresh parsley 
1/2 tsp. hot red pepper flakes
black pepper
1 tbsp. balsamic vinegar
1 tsp. coarse salt
Combine all ingredients except the salt in a large non-aluminum bowl.  Stir to blend well.  Let sit at room temperature for 30 min. Just before serving, add salt.
