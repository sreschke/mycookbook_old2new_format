500gr (1lb) chicken fillets (sliced very thinly)       
1/2 teaspoon sugar
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon sherry (optional)
1 tablespoon cornflour
Mix together in a bowl and let stand while preparing vegetables.
2 tablespoons oil
3 tablespoons Changs Black Bean Paste
2 tablespoons dark soy sauce
2 teaspoons sugar  
1 tablespoon chopped ginger
2 pkts Changs Oriental or Chow Mein Vegetables
Heat oil in wok or pan until very hot.  Add chopped ginger, then Black Bean Paste.  Stir fry for 30 seconds.  Add chicken and fry for 2 minutes.  Add the rest of seasonings and fry for 1 minute.  Add Changs Oriental/Chow Mein Vegetables and stir .  Cook until heated through, stirring constantly. 
Serve on a bed of Changs Fried or Crunchy Noodles.
