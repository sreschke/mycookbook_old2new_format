Ingredients 
1 pound angel hair pasta 
3 ounces sun-dried tomatoes, softened and chopped 
8 ounces tomato and basil feta cheese 
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese 
4 tablespoons olive oil 
4 cloves garlic, crushed 
1 bunch fresh cilantro 
salt and pepper to taste 
 
  
 
Directions 
1    Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and return to pot.
 
2    Mix in tomatoes; finely crumble feta into pot along with Parmesan and garlic. Pour in olive oil and mix well; add enough olive oil to ease the mixing. 
 
3    Stir in cilantro and season to taste with salt and pepper; serve warm with more grated Parmesan cheese. 
 
Makes 6 servings
