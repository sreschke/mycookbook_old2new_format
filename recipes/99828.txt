1 lb. lean ground beef
« onion, finely chopped
3 garlic cloves, minced
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. black pepper
« cup breadcrumbs
2 egg yolks
2 tbsp. olive oil
8 oz. mushrooms, halved
« cup sherry
2 cups chicken broth
2 tbsp. butter, softened
2 tbsp. flour
2 tbsp. chopped parsley
« cup sour cream

 Combine the ground beef, half of the chopped onion, garlic, salt, pepper, breadcrumbs, egg yolks and « cup of water in a large bowl. Mix well and form meatballs about 1" in diameter. Heat oil in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add meatballs in batches and cook until well browned. Transfer to paper towels to drain.
In skillet cook remaining onion until softened, about 5 minutes. Add mushrooms and cook about 10 minutes. Pour in stock and bring to a boil.
Combine butter and flour in small bowl until smooth. Add to the boiling sauce, whisking constantly for 3 minutes. Add meatballs, parsley, sour cream and cook over low heat until meatballs are just heated through. Season with salt and pepper and serve with noodles.
Serves 4 to 6.

Original recipe from "Sara Moulton Cooks at Home".
