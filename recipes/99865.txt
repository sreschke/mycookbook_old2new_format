6 chicken breasts, boneless & skinless
Salt
Pepper
Garlic powder
2 cans fat-free cream of chicken soup
1 can cheddar cheese soup

Rinse and season chicken. Layer in crockpot. Mix soups and put on top of chicken. Cook 6-8 hours on low or 4 hours on high. Serve over egg noodles or rice.

Serves 3
